개요Summary

The Cambodian National Police is the national police force of the Kingdom of Cambodia, under the Ministry of Interior. In the public IC corpus the agency is documented as the Cambodian counterpart for the bilateral 한국-캄보디아 공동 전담반 (Korea–Cambodia Joint Task Force for Korean nationals, "코리아전담반") MOU signed in Phnom Penh on 2025-11-10, and as the operational counterpart for the joint Korean-Cambodian field unit that produced the 42-person Cambodian tranche of the 2026-04-27 Korea-Cambodia-Philippines extradition cycle.

Cooperation Track Record (Public Sources)

  • 2025-11-10: Cambodian Police Chief Sar Thet 써 텟 signs the Korea–Cambodia Joint Task Force MOU with KNPA Acting Commissioner Yoo Jae-sung in Phnom Penh, in the presence of Korean Foreign Minister Cho Hyun and Cambodian Interior Minister Sar Sokha. (See korea-cambodia-joint-task-force-mou-2025.)
  • 2025-11 to early 2026: Cambodian National Police participates in 코리아전담반 (Joint Task Force) staffed by 12 Cambodian + 7 Korean officers (19 total per Korean tier-2 reporting); 7 rounds of joint operations through early January 2026 with 135 suspects arrested in the field and 3 Korean victims rescued.
  • 2026-02 to 2026-04: Cooperates with KNPA on the 73-person Korean scam-suspect extradition cohort, with 42 Korean nationals extradited from Cambodia (41 remanded into custody after arrival in Korea).
