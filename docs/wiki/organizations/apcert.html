임무: Asia-Pacific computer emergency response team cooperation network.

국제공조 역할

  • Provides regional CERT-to-CERT coordination context for incident response and cyber-security capacity building.
  • Connects national CERTs such as kisa / KrCERT and SingCERT-facing functions.

협력 기관

Kisa, Krcert Cc, Singapore Csa, First Forum Incident Response

개요Summary

APCERT is the Asia-Pacific CERT cooperation network referenced in the repository through national cyber-security agency profiles. It should be treated as an incident-response coordination forum rather than a law-enforcement arresting body.

Cybercrime Cooperation Role

APCERT matters to cybercrime IC because technical incident response often precedes or runs alongside criminal investigation. National CERTs can share indicators, malware samples, incident notifications, and mitigation guidance across borders while police and prosecutors handle legal process. The local KISA/KrCERT source set anchors APCERT through South Korea's national cyber-security ecosystem.

IC Relevance

Use this page for CERT-to-CERT regional cooperation in the Asia-Pacific. Use krcert-cc, kisa, or a national police page when the source concerns a specific incident response or criminal investigation.

Source Coverage

Current coverage is indirect through Korean CERT/KISA sources. A direct APCERT institutional source should be added when available.
