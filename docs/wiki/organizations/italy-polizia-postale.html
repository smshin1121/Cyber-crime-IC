개요Summary

The Servizio Polizia postale e per la sicurezza cibernetica is Italy's core national cybercrime police service within the Polizia di Stato structure. It is the main institutional anchor for cybercrime investigation, online child-protection enforcement, and cyber-enabled fraud work in Italy's law-enforcement system, and it provides the organizational home for CNAIPIC.

임무와 권한Mandate and Authority

Official Polizia di Stato normative material shows that, from 11 May 2024, the service's competences are exercised within the newly created central directorate for scientific police and cyber security. The service's legal references also trace back to the earlier Servizio Polizia postale e delle comunicazioni framework, showing organizational continuity rather than an entirely new institution.

Italian Interior Ministry material also places CNAIPIC inside the telecommunications-security branch of the Ministry of the Interior. That matters because it shows that Italy's critical-infrastructure cyber mission is not separate from the Postal Police tradition; it is embedded in it.

사이버범죄 국제공조 관련 구조Structure Relevant to Cybercrime IC

  • national service within the State Police system
  • coordinates specialized cyber investigative activity
  • supports cyber-security and communications-related law-enforcement missions
  • provides an institutional home for nationally coordinated cybercrime response
  • includes the national critical-infrastructure protection center CNAIPIC
  • bridges operational policing, incident response, and expedited cross-border assistance

국제공조 역량IC Capabilities

  • treaty-based and MLA-linked cybercrime support under Italy's Budapest Convention framework
  • operational cybercrime investigation capacity through the State Police system
  • experience in ransomware-linked, fraud, and exploitation investigations
  • critical-infrastructure alerting and coordination through CNAIPIC
  • participation in expedited cyber-assistance channels, including the High Tech Crime 24/7 environment referenced by the Interior Ministry

주요 작전 및 사건Key Operations and Cases

The official 2017 Operazione Criptolocker release provides a concrete example of Postal Police cybercrime enforcement against ransomware-linked extortion and computer fraud. The case demonstrates that the service is not only policy-facing, but also operationally active in complex cyber investigations.

The 2022 Interior Ministry page on the SINC3 project adds a second layer: it describes CNAIPIC as handling hundreds of attacks and alerts and receiving assistance requests through the G7 High Tech Crime 24/7 network. That makes the institution relevant not just to domestic investigation but also to expedited international cyber cooperation.

모순점 및 미해결 문제Contradictions & Open Questions
