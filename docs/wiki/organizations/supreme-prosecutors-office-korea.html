요약

대검찰청(대검찰청, SPO)은 서울 서초구에 본부를 두고 있는 대한민국의 최고 검찰 기관입니다. 1948년 대한민국 건국과 함께 설립되어 이재명 정부의 수사권 분리 개혁에 따라 2026년 10월 폐지까지 78년 동안 대한민국 검찰 중심 형사사법제도의 정점으로 운영되었습니다.

사이버 범죄에 대한 국제 협력을 위해 SPO는 역사적으로 다음과 같은 역할을 수행했습니다. - 검찰측 MLAT 채널 (법무부 국제형사과 공유) - APC-HUB 사무국 호스트(세계은행과 GFCE가 공동 자금을 지원하는 아시아 태평양 사이버 범죄 허브) - 디지털 포렌식 지원을 위한 NDFC(국가디지털포렌식센터) 운영자 - 유럽평의회 문어회의 / T-CY 참관인 참여 한국 대표단 주임

국제협력단의 자세한 사이버범죄-IC 프로필과 2026년 10월 2일 이후 어느 후임 기관(공소청 또는 중수청)이 이러한 기능을 물려받을 것인지에 대한 불확실성은 spo-international-cooperation를 참조하세요.

  • 기존 MLAT 서신 및 양자간 각서 "대검찰청"을 상대방으로 지정 - 외국 파트너가 새로운 계약을 체결해야 하는지 아니면 승계-연속 조항에 의존할 수 있는지 여부

  • 폐지가 한국의 부다페스트 협약 가입과 어떻게 상호 작용하는지 — Korea's Art. 35 24시간 연중무휴 연락 지점 지정은 후임 계약으로 인해 담당 기관이 변경되는 경우 업데이트가 필요할 수 있습니다.

⚠️ Status — Being Dissolved (effective 2026-10-02) Under the 공소청법 (Prosecution Service Act) and 중대범죄수사청법 (Serious Crimes Investigation Agency Act) enacted in early 2026, 검찰청 / 대검찰청 will be abolished effective 2026-10-02 after 78 years of existence (est. 1948) [1][2][3]. The 수사·기소 기능 (investigation and prosecution functions) will be separated into two new agencies:
  • 공소청 (Prosecution Service) under the Ministry of Justice — handles prosecution only, organized in three tiers (공소청·광역공소청·지방공소청). Inherits the professional prosecution function and is highly likely to inherit the existing MLAT/extradition international-cooperation functions previously housed in 대검찰청 국제협력단 [3][4].
  • 중대범죄수사청 / 중수청 (Serious Crimes Investigation Agency) under the Ministry of the Interior and Safety (행정안전부) — handles investigation of six serious-crime categories: anti-corruption, economic crime, defense-industry crime, narcotics, insurrection/treason, and cybercrime (사이버범죄) [1][3]. This is the first time cybercrime is explicitly designated as a dedicated category for a national-level criminal investigation agency in Korea.

As of 2026-04-10 the reorganization is enacted but not yet in force. 대검찰청 continues to operate under its existing structure through 2026-09-30. The April 2026 prosecutor personnel rotation was described in the press as "preparing for the October 공소청 transition" [5].

Assignment of specific cybercrime IC functions (APC-HUB Secretariat, NDFC digital forensics center, 국제협력단 MLAT desk) between 공소청 and 중수청 as of 2026-04-10 is not yet publicly confirmed. See spo-international-cooperation for the current best assessment.

개요Summary

The Supreme Prosecutors' Office (대검찰청, SPO) is South Korea's highest prosecution body, headquartered in Seocho-gu, Seoul. Established in 1948 alongside the founding of the Republic of Korea, it operated for 78 years as the apex of Korea's prosecutor-led criminal justice system until the October 2026 abolition under the Lee Jae-myung administration's 수사권 분리 (separation of investigation and prosecution) reform.

For international cooperation on cybercrime, the SPO historically served as: - The prosecutor-side MLAT channel (shared with the Ministry of Justice's 국제형사과) - The APC-HUB Secretariat host (Asia-Pacific Cybercrime Hub, co-funded by World Bank and GFCE) - The NDFC (국가디지털포렌식센터) operator for digital forensic support - The Korean delegation lead for Council of Europe Octopus Conference / T-CY observer participation

See spo-international-cooperation for the detailed cybercrime-IC profile of the 국제협력단 and the uncertainty about which successor body (공소청 or 중수청) will inherit these functions after 2026-10-02.

Korean Interactions (한국과의 협력)

Historically the SPO was the primary prosecutor-side Korean counterpart for foreign prosecution services and the main MLAT execution body alongside the Ministry of Justice's 국제형사과. After October 2026:

  • 공소청 will likely be the prosecutor-to-prosecutor channel for foreign counterparts
  • 중대범죄수사청 will be the investigator channel for serious cybercrime matters, sharing operational space with the KNPA cybercrime enforcement line — creating a new three-way Korean cybercrime enforcement ecosystem (경찰 수사국 사이버수사심의관 / 중수청 사이버범죄 / 공소청 prosecution) that has almost certainly no direct international precedent and whose coordination mechanics remain to be observed

모순점 및 미해결 문제Contradictions & Open Questions

  • Precise division of cybercrime IC functions between 공소청 and 중수청 after 2026-10-02 — not yet publicly confirmed
  • Fate of the APC-HUB Secretariat (Asia-Pacific Cybercrime Hub) hosted by the SPO — World Bank and GFCE partnership arrangements may require explicit successor designation
  • Fate of the NDFC (국가디지털포렌식센터) — whether it remains a shared facility, transfers wholly to 중수청, or is split
  • Existing MLAT correspondence and bilateral memoranda naming "대검찰청" as the counterparty — whether foreign partners must execute new agreements or can rely on successor-continuity clauses
  • Overlap with KNPA 사이버수사심의관 (under 수사국) — whether 중수청 cybercrime investigation supplants or coexists with KNPA cybercrime investigation is a major open governance question
  • How the abolition interacts with Korea's pending Budapest Convention accession — Korea's Art. 35 24/7 contact point designation may need updating if the successor arrangements change the responsible agency
