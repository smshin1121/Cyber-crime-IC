개요Summary

The Garda National Cyber Crime Bureau (GNCCB) is the cybercrime unit within An Garda Siochana. Use this page for cybercrime investigation and digital-forensics context; use the parent Garda page for broader policing context.

Cybercrime Cooperation Role

The local Garda source set is strongest for Operation Jackal / Operation Skein updates, which show Irish participation in international fraud and cyber-enabled financial crime enforcement. The Ghost platform page adds a second cooperation pattern: encrypted communications and organized-crime disruption.
