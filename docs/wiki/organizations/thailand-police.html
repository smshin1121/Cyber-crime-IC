개요Summary

"Thailand police" / "Thai cybercrime police" in cybercrime international cooperation sources almost certainly refers to one of the following:

  1. Royal Thai Police — Cyber Crime Investigation Bureau (CCIB) — the national cybercrime-dedicated bureau established September 2020. This is the primary referent in most post-2020 sources.
  2. Technology Crime Suppression Division (TCSD) — the earlier Thai cybercrime unit, active before and alongside CCIB. TCSD was part of the Central Investigation Bureau (CIB) of the Royal Thai Police.
  3. Department of Special Investigation (DSI) — Ministry of Justice unit handling some transnational cybercrime cases, especially those involving money laundering and organized crime.
  4. Royal Thai Police — Metropolitan or Provincial Police — local police units handling routine cybercrime cases.

When to Use This Page

This page should be referenced only when a source is ambiguous about which Thai unit was involved. In all other cases, editors should resolve the reference to the specific unit and link accordingly.
