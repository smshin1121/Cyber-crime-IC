개요Summary

The German Federal Ministry of the Interior (BMI) is Germany's federal internal-security ministry. In this corpus it is most important as the parent policy context for BKA cybercrime enforcement and as an official ministry-level source for Germany's participation in large international operations.

Cybercrime Cooperation Role

The local BMI source covers Operation Endgame, describing it as a major international law-enforcement operation against botnets and the ransomware-enabling malware ecosystem. The operational execution belongs to agencies such as germany-bka, zit-frankfurt, Europol, Eurojust, and partner-country investigators; BMI's role in this corpus is to provide official German federal framing and confirmation.
