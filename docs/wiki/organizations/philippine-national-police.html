개요Summary

The Philippine National Police is the national police force of the Republic of the Philippines, headquartered at Camp Crame, Quezon City. In the public IC corpus the agency is documented as the Philippine counterpart for the bilateral Korea–Philippines police MOU (originally signed 2007; second revision signed 2026-03-03 in Manila) and as the operational counterpart for the Korean Desk at the Philippine National Capital Region Police Office (established 2012, expanded 2026).
