Supreme Prosecutors' Office — International Cooperation Division

: 2026-04-10 : 9

개요

Supreme Prosecutors' Office — International Cooperation Division (대검찰청 국제협력단)은(는) prosecution-office으로 unknown년 설립되었으며 Seoul, South Korea에 본부를 두고 있다.

임무: Direct and support international cooperation affairs of the Korean Prosecution

상위기관: Supreme Prosecutors Office Korea

소속국: South Korea

⚠️ Status — Reorganized (effective 2026-10-02) The parent 대검찰청 is scheduled to be abolished on 2026-10-02 under the 공소청법 and 중수청법 enacted in early 2026 [5][6]. As a consequence, the 국제협력단 / 국제협력담당관실 — the unit this page documents — will cease to exist in its current form on the same date. Functions are highly likely to be split as follows:
  • Prosecutor-side MLAT, extradition, and multilateral engagement (Council of Europe Octopus Conference, T-CY, UN Cybercrime Convention) → 공소청 (Prosecution Service, under 법무부) — likely under a new 국제협력담당관 or equivalent
  • APC-HUB Secretariat (Asia-Pacific Cybercrime Hub) — uncertain; could follow prosecution to 공소청, could transfer to 중수청, or could become a joint-managed function
  • NDFC (국가디지털포렌식센터) — uncertain; investigative digital-forensic capacity may transfer to 중대범죄수사청 (under 행정안전부) since cybercrime is one of 중수청's six designated categories
  • Cybercrime prosecution oversight (prior 사이버·기술범죄수사부 functions) — likely to 중수청 for investigation and 공소청 for prosecution, requiring new coordination mechanisms

Also: historical sources have used both "국제협력단" (단 = bureau/team) and "국제협력담당관실" (담당관실 = officer's office) to refer to the SPO's international-cooperation unit. The current official structure (as confirmed by the SPO website) uses 국제협력담당관실 (International Cooperation Officer's Office) [7]. The earlier "국제협력단" term persists in academic literature and some press coverage.

The 2022 검수완박 amendment (형사소송법·검찰청법 개정) restricted prosecutor direct investigation to six categories; this did not abolish the SPO's international-cooperation function, but it is the precursor reform to the 2026 full abolition [8].

개요Summary

The International Cooperation Division of the Supreme Prosecutors' Office of the Republic of Korea (대검찰청 국제협력단) directs and supports international cooperation affairs of the Korean Prosecution Service. It is highly likely Korea's most consequential prosecutor-side channel for cross-border cybercrime cooperation, particularly through its co-located Cybercrime International Cooperation Center and the APC-HUB Secretariat, which coordinate Asia-Pacific regional cybercrime capacity building.

The SPO also operates the National Digital Forensic Center (NDFC, 국가디지털포렌식센터), launched in 2008, and the Forensic Science Investigation Department (2015), which support cybercrime investigations and international digital evidence cooperation.

Current Status (as of 2026-04-10)

Official current name: 대검찰청 국제협력담당관실 (International Cooperation Officer's Office, Supreme Prosecutors' Office) [7] Parent: 대검찰청 Status: Active but being dissolved effective 2026-10-02 (along with the parent SPO) Role: Prosecutor-side MLAT and extradition coordination; APC-HUB Secretariat; Council of Europe Octopus/T-CY observer participation; multilateral engagement on cybercrime

The unit continues normal operations through 2026-09-30. The April 2026 prosecutor personnel rotation has been described as "preparing for the October 공소청 transition" [9], suggesting the international-cooperation unit's personnel will be reassigned to successor entities rather than retained in a dissolved 대검찰청.

📝 Naming correction Academic and policy literature frequently refers to this unit as "대검찰청 국제협력단" (with 단 meaning division/bureau). The SPO's own website as of 2026 lists the unit as "국제협력담당관실" (담당관실 meaning officer's office) [7]. The two terms refer to the same operational function; the 단 form likely reflects an earlier organizational designation or a less formal rendering.

임무와 권한Mandate and Authority

The SPO's International Cooperation Division directs and supports international cooperation affairs of the Prosecution Service. The Cyber and Technology Crime Investigation Division of the SPO handles the substantive cybercrime investigative function, including:

  • Supervising cybercrime case investigation ordered by the Prosecutor General
  • Research and development on investigation techniques for cybercrimes
  • Securing, developing and operating equipment for cybercrime investigation
  • Implementing international investigation cooperation related to cybercrime
  • Training prosecutors and investigators on cybercrime matters

사이버범죄 국제공조 관련 구조Structure Relevant to Cybercrime IC

  • International Cooperation Division (국제협력단): Central coordination of cross-border cooperation.
  • Cybercrime International Cooperation Center & APC-HUB Secretariat: Located at the SPO, coordinates Asia-Pacific regional cybercrime capacity building in partnership with the World Bank and Global Forum on Cyber Expertise (GFCE).
  • Cyber and Technology Crime Investigation Division: Substantive investigative oversight.
  • National Digital Forensic Center (NDFC, 국가디지털포렌식센터): Established 2008; provides digital forensic support.

국제공조 역량IC Capabilities

  • APC-HUB leadership: The Asia-Pacific Cybercrime Hub provides capacity building to developing countries in the region; Korea's SPO serves as the secretariat.
  • MLAT processing: Korean central authority for mutual legal assistance in criminal matters (shared or coordinated with Ministry of Justice depending on the framework).
  • Multilateral engagement: Participates in Council of Europe Octopus Conference, T-CY meetings, and regional workshops under the Budapest Convention framework (Korea is not a party but participates as observer).

공조 실적Cooperation Track Record

The SPO's regional leadership via the APC-HUB Secretariat is likely Korea's most visible multilateral cybercrime cooperation contribution. Bilateral MLAT processing and extradition cooperation with the US, Europe, and Asia-Pacific partners is routine but not always publicly documented.

Korean Interactions (한국과의 협력)

As a Korean prosecution office, this unit is by definition the Korean counterpart for prosecutor-to-prosecutor cooperation on cybercrime. It works alongside the Korean National Police Agency Cyber Bureau (경찰청 사이버수사국) on police-led investigations that require prosecutorial direction.

모순점 및 미해결 문제Contradictions & Open Questions

  • Exact date the International Cooperation Division was established is unclear from open sources.
  • Division of labor between the SPO International Cooperation Division and the Ministry of Justice International Affairs Division (법무부 국제형사과) on MLAT processing requires clarification.
  • The operational scope of the Cybercrime International Cooperation Center vs. the broader International Cooperation Division is not fully documented in public sources.
  • Post-2026-10-02 successor assignments: Which successor body (공소청 or 중수청) inherits MLAT processing, APC-HUB Secretariat, NDFC, and bilateral partner relationships is not publicly confirmed as of 2026-04-10.
  • Bilateral MOUs and APC-HUB partner agreements naming "대검찰청" or "Supreme Prosecutors' Office of the Republic of Korea" as the counterparty — whether these automatically transfer to 공소청 or require renegotiation.
  • APC-HUB's World Bank and GFCE co-funding structure — whether donor partners treat the reorganization as a continuity event or trigger a re-evaluation of the hosting arrangement.
