개요Summary

AFRIPOL (African Police Cooperation Organization) is the African Union's continental police cooperation mechanism, established in 2017 and headquartered in Algiers, Algeria. AFRIPOL coordinates law enforcement cooperation among the 55 AU member states and partners with INTERPOL for joint operations targeting cybercrime and other transnational crime in Africa.
