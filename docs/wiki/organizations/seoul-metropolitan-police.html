개요Summary

The Seoul Metropolitan Police Agency (서울경찰청, SMPA) is the metropolitan police agency responsible for law enforcement in Seoul. SMPA has conducted bilateral cooperation with Chinese police on voice phishing cases, notably the Qingdao joint operation (2023) which was the first significant Korea-China voice phishing roundup.
