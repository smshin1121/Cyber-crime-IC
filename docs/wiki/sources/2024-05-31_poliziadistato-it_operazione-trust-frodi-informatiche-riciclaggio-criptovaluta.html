주요 발견사항

  • Italian Polizia Postale / Centro operativo per la sicurezza cibernetica della Lombardia, coordinated by the Milan Prosecutor's Office (procura di Milano), dismantled a transnational cryptocurrency-fraud and money-laundering organisation in collaboration with the Portuguese Polícia Judiciária and with support from Europol; the operation was codenamed "Trust".
  • Enforcement results: one arrest and 12 home/IT searches; seizure of numerous electronic devices and hundreds of thousands of euros in cryptocurrency.
  • Modus operandi: "Rip deal" scheme — perpetrators posed as international investment-fund representatives, lured victims to luxury restaurants/hotels, induced movement of crypto via digital wallets/mobile apps, then drained the wallets via technical-IT manipulation before victims realised the theft.
  • Network composition (as identified by investigators): Balkan-origin recruiters / interlocutors mostly resident in France; predominantly Asian crypto-launderers established in Italy. Victims came from Austria, Portugal, Romania, Spain, Switzerland and Italy. Italian search activity executed in Milano, Monza, Lodi, Roma, Torino, Cagliari and Nuoro.

개요Summary

Official press release by Italy's Polizia di Stato announcing the conclusion of Operation "Trust", a transnational investigation into cryptocurrency-fraud (Rip deal scheme) and crypto money-laundering. The case was coordinated by the Milan Prosecutor's Office and operationally led by the Centro operativo per la sicurezza cibernetica della Lombardia of the Polizia Postale, with formal LE cooperation from Portugal's Polícia Judiciária and support from Europol. Year-long international investigation yielded one arrest, 12 searches, and hundreds of thousands of euros in cryptocurrency seized in Italy. The release names the two cooperating LE bodies (Italian Polizia Postale's Lombardia cyber centre and the Portuguese Polícia Judiciária) plus Europol explicitly, satisfying the ≥2-country IC cooperation criterion.

IC Relevance

  • Demonstrates Italy–Portugal bilateral cybercrime cooperation channelled through Europol for a "Rip deal" / fake-investment crypto-fraud network with victims across six European countries.
  • Confirms continuing role of the Italian Polizia Postale's regional cyber-security operations centres (Lombardia C.O.S.C.) as lead investigative units for multi-jurisdictional crypto-fraud cases.
  • Reinforces the pattern of Polícia Judiciária as a recurring Italian partner on crypto-fraud and online financial-crime takedowns.
