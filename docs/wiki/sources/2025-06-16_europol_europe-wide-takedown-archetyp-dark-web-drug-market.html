주요 발견사항

  • Action days 11-13 June 2025 across Germany, Netherlands, Romania, Spain, Sweden — operation codenamed 'Operation Deep Sentinel'.
  • ~300 officers deployed across the 5 EU jurisdictions.
  • Archetyp Market platform infrastructure (hosted in the Netherlands) taken offline.
  • Administrator — 30-year-old German national — arrested in Barcelona, Spain.
  • 1 moderator + 6 highest vendors targeted in Germany and Sweden.
  • EUR 7,800,000 in assets seized.
  • Archetyp Market operated for 5+ years; 600,000+ users worldwide; EUR 250 million+ total transaction volume; 17,000+ listings; one of the few darknet markets that allowed fentanyl and other highly potent synthetic opioids.
  • Drug categories: cocaine, MDMA, amphetamines, synthetic opioids.
  • German lead: Prosecutor General's Office Frankfurt am Main — Cyber Crime Centre (Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Frankfurt am Main — ZIT) + Federal Criminal Police Office (Bundeskriminalamt / BKA).
  • Eurojust coordinated mutual legal assistance and European Investigation Orders during the action days and preliminary investigations.
  • Europol coordination meetings; US partners: Homeland Security Investigations (HSI), IRS Criminal Investigation (IRS-CI), US Department of Justice (USDOJ).

Source Summary

Europol newsroom press release announcing Operation Deep Sentinel — the 11-13 June 2025 takedown of Archetyp Market, the longest-running darknet drug marketplace, with German lead authorities (Frankfurt am Main ZIT + BKA) and 5-EU-state plus US cooperation. Administrator arrested in Barcelona; 1 moderator + 6 top vendors targeted in Germany/Sweden; EUR 7.8M assets seized; 600,000+ users / EUR 250M+ transactions / 17,000+ listings disrupted.

IC Pattern

This release establishes a fully-articulated 6-jurisdiction darknet-marketplace takedown pattern with explicit Eurojust EIO + MLA coordination during the action days:

  • Germany: lead jurisdiction (Frankfurt am Main ZIT + BKA).
  • Netherlands: infrastructure-hosting jurisdiction (platform infrastructure in NL taken offline).
  • Spain: arrest jurisdiction for the German-national administrator (Barcelona).
  • Sweden + Germany: action jurisdictions for 1 moderator + 6 highest vendors.
  • Romania: action partner (specifics not enumerated).
  • United States: HSI + IRS-CI + USDOJ analytical and operational partners.
  • Europol: coordination meetings + analytical support.
  • Eurojust: MLA + European Investigation Order coordination during action days and preliminary investigation.

Wiki Cross-Corpus Relevance

This case fills the 2025-Q2 darknet-marketplace takedown gap in the wiki and complements: - 150 arrested in dark web drug bust (2025) (Eurojust-coordinated drug-marketplace action) - Operation Raptor (270 arrests, dark web sting) - Empire Market (US prosecution)

The Archetyp Market takedown is the wiki's first record of Operation Deep Sentinel as a discrete operation name, and the most fully-articulated Germany-Spain-Netherlands-Sweden-Romania-US 6-jurisdiction darknet-marketplace takedown architecture.
