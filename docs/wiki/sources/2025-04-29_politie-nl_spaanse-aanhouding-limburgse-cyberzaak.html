주요 발견사항

  • Politie Limburg Team Cybercrime, under leadership of Openbaar Ministerie Parket Limburg, located a 33-year-old suspect in a Dutch telephone bank-helpdesk fraud case (Nationale Postcode Loterij impersonation scheme using AnyDesk-style remote-access malware).
  • Suspect was arrested in Estepona, Spain on 29 April 2025 by the Spanish Policia Nacional unit UDYCO Greco Costa del Sol (organised-crime/drug crime special unit), following a Dutch international cooperation request.
  • On the same day, coordinated house searches were conducted in Borne and Deurningen (NL, Eenheid Oost-Nederland) at the addresses of two other suspects; multiple phones and laptops were seized for further investigation. No additional arrests on the Dutch side at that point.
  • Investigation opened in September 2024 after a victim reported being defrauded of hundreds of euros via a fake lottery 'overpayment-refund' scheme using AnyDesk remote-access takeover; multiple victims confirmed but full scope (victim count, total loss) not yet public.

개요Summary

Tier-1 Dutch national police press release documenting a Netherlands-Spain bilateral cybercrime cooperation: Politie Limburg located a fugitive bank-helpdesk fraud suspect operating from Spain; on a Dutch request, the Spanish Policia Nacional UDYCO Greco Costa del Sol unit arrested him in Estepona on the same day Dutch officers searched two co-suspect addresses in Borne and Deurningen.

Why it matters for the wiki

Concrete bilateral NL-ES arrest cooperation in a bank-helpdesk fraud case — useful for the wiki's documentation of (a) Dutch outbound cooperation patterns in cyber-enabled fraud, (b) Spain as an executing jurisdiction for Dutch cybercrime warrants on the Costa del Sol, (c) the UDYCO Greco specialised-unit role in transnational cyber-fraud arrests.
