주요 발견사항

  • Action day 2025-06-16: Royal Thai Police (RTP) executed search warrants at a six-bedroom house on the outskirts of Bangkok operating as an investment scam 'boiler room'.
  • 13 men arrested: 5 Australian, 6 UK, 1 Canadian, 1 South African.
  • Charges: Section 210 Thai Criminal Code (conspiracy to commit an offence); max 5 years.
  • Operation period: April 2025 — June 2025 (boiler-room operations).
  • Scam method: Fraudulent representatives of international financial firms selling fake high-yield fixed-income bonds; high-pressure sales scripts; forged documentation; fraudulent use of an authentic financial services licence; online face-to-face meetings; victim funds transferred to cryptocurrency before moving to other accounts.
  • Estimated stolen: AUD 1,900,000+ in two months.
  • Operation context: AFP-led JPC3 Operation Firestorm, launched August 2024 — first Thailand takedown under this operation; three prior scam-centre takedowns in Manila.
  • AFP-RTP cooperation channel: AFP International Network alerted RTP after Australian authorities received credible information of Australian recruits in the syndicate.
  • Items seized: dozens of mobile phones and laptops; scam scripts; digital spreadsheet with list of thousands of potential Australian targets.

Source Summary

AFP press release dated 2025-06-17 reporting the 2025-06-16 RTP-AFP joint takedown of a Bangkok-based investment scam centre. The operation is part of the AFP-led JPC3 Operation Firestorm (launched August 2024) and represents the first Thailand takedown under Operation Firestorm, with three prior takedowns in Manila. 13 arrests across 4 nationalities (Australia, UK, Canada, South Africa); the scammers used fraudulent international-financial-firm representations to sell fake high-yield fixed-income bonds, with proceeds laundered through cryptocurrency.
