주요 발견사항

  • Korean Ministry of Justice (법무부, Minister Jeong Seong-ho) and Korean National Police Agency (경찰청) extradited a 40-year-old Chinese-national senior member (총책급) of an overseas hacking syndicate from Bangkok, Thailand to Incheon, South Korea on 2026-05-13.
  • Tier-1 own-domain primary source at moj.go.kr explicitly names cooperating foreign LE/prosecutor counterparts: 태국 대검찰청 (Royal Thai Office of the Attorney General), 태국 경찰청 (Royal Thai Police), and 인터폴 (INTERPOL — joint operation channel).
  • Defendant ㄱ○○ allegedly led a syndicate that hacked MVNO (알뜰폰) operator websites between Aug 2023 – Apr 2025, harvested personal information of Korean victims, and used it for unauthorised withdrawals from victims' financial and virtual-asset (crypto) accounts totalling over KRW 38 billion (≈ USD 25.5 million). Confirmed victims include 16 individuals: chairman of a major conglomerate, head of a venture firm, and BTS member Jungkook.
  • Cooperation timeline: May 2025 urgent provisional-arrest request (긴급인도구속) to Thai authorities; Jul 2025 dispatch of Korean prosecutors + investigators to Thailand for face-to-face meetings with Thai OAG and Royal Thai Police; Oct–Dec 2025 multiple video conferences between Korean MOJ and Thai OAG; May 13 2026 extradition transfer.
  • Second of two senior-leader-level extraditions from the same syndicate: the first (36 y.o. co-leader) was extradited from Thailand in Aug 2025 and is on trial in Seoul.
  • L24 strict compliance: multiple foreign LE/prosecutor agencies named verbatim. Quote: '법무부는 공조 중앙기관으로서 경찰청과 긴밀히 협력, 인터폴 합동작전을 통해 2025년 5월 태국 현지에서 총책급 공범인을 검거… 2025년 7월 태국 현지에 담당 검사, 수사관을 파견하여 태국 대검찰청 및 경찰청 관계자들과 면담을 진행.'

Korean side (lead + co-lead)

  • 법무부 (MOJ Korea) — central authority (공조 중앙기관); led extradition request, dispatched prosecutors and investigators to Thailand, executed transfer
  • 경찰청 (KNPA) — co-lead; accompanied defendant from Bangkok to Incheon
  • (Per related Korean media: cooperation channels run through MOJ International Criminal Affairs Division — 국제형사과)

Foreign LE / prosecutor counterparts (verbatim from MOJ release)

  • 태국 대검찰청 (Royal Thai Office of the Attorney General — OAG Thailand) — counterpart central authority; Jul 2025 in-person meetings; Oct–Dec 2025 video conferences
  • 태국 경찰청 (Royal Thai Police) — operational counterpart; Jul 2025 in-person meetings
  • INTERPOL — joint-operation channel (인터폴 합동작전) through which the May 2025 on-site arrest of the co-leader and the present defendant's apprehension at the same scene were carried out

Defendant nationality / venue

  • Defendant ("ㄱ○○"): 40-year-old male, Chinese national
  • Crime base: organised "in Thailand and other overseas locations" (태국 등 해외)
  • Forensic indictment: planned in Seoul (Korean territorial-personality jurisdiction over Korean victims and use-of-Korean-financial-system theory of harm)
