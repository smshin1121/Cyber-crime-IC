Seoul Metropolitan Police and Chinese police jointly arrest 16 voice phishing suspects in Qingdao

source 출처

Summary

Seoul Metropolitan Police and Chinese police jointly arrested 16 suspects for voice phishing scams operated from Qingdao, China, extorting KRW 2.7 billion (USD 2 million) from 68 victims. This was reported as the first significant roundup of voice phishing scammers in China under a Seoul-Beijing bilateral agreement.

Key Data

  • Operation type: Bilateral joint operation (Korea-China)
  • Timeframe: Investigation April 2023; China arrests August 24, 2023
  • Countries: South Korea, China
  • Arrests: 16 (3 Chinese nationals, 13 Koreans)
  • Financial losses: KRW 2.7 billion (USD 2 million)
  • Victims: 68
  • Crime types: Voice phishing
  • Scam base: Qingdao, China

Relevance

Landmark case as first major joint Korea-China voice phishing operation. Established precedent for larger-scale joint operations including HAECHI V voice phishing syndicate dismantling.
