주요 발견사항

  • Seoul Eastern District Prosecutors' Office Voice-Phishing Joint Investigation Unit (보이스피싱범죄 합동수사부, 부장검사 이태순) announces the on-site arrest of 4 Korean-national voice phishing call-centre operators in Clark, Philippines (2025-07-26), forced extradition to Korea, and indictment in custody on 2026-04-28.
  • Cooperation chain explicitly named in primary text: SPO Organised Crime Division-deployed prosecutorial investigators in the Philippines (대검찰청 조직범죄과 소속 파견 검찰수사관) → Philippine Bureau of Immigration FSU (Fugitive Search Unit / 수배자 추적대) → INTERPOL Red Notice + Korean passport invalidation → MOJ International Criminal Affairs Division (법무부 국제형사과) + Korean Embassy Manila police attaché-Consul (총경급 파견 영사) → forced repatriation.
  • Lead defendant: A (male, 37, call-centre 관리책 / manager) plus 3 co-conspirators. Charges: 통신사기피해환급에관한특별법위반 (Special Act on Refund of Damages from Telecommunications-Based Financial Fraud) + 사기 (fraud, KCC §347).
  • Modus operandi: 'review-task' lure scam (리뷰달기 미끼용 미션). Two-stage role-split: 1st-stage 'mission' counsellor (hotel review-writing, small payouts to build trust), 2nd-stage 'purchase-authentication team mission' counsellor (high-value extraction with debt-coercion script: 'mid-quit hurts the team, take a loan to participate'). Total fraud volume per the indicted offences: ≈ ₩130 million. Offence period: 2025-06 to 2025-07.
  • Verified operational timeline (primary source, dated): 2025-07-24 source-information intake by Philippines-stationed prosecutorial investigator → 2025-07-25 BI FSU joint operational planning (informant deployed as housekeeper for ID confirmation) → 2025-07-26 raid + Bicutan detention centre intake → 2025-08–09 INTERPOL Red Notice + passport invalidation → 2026-02–04 MOJ International Criminal Affairs + Manila Embassy + deployed prosecutors coordination for forced repatriation → 2026-03–04 indictment in custody.
  • Evidentiary recovery: 5 PCs plus mobile phones and tablets seized at the Clark site, preserved against destruction by deployed prosecutorial investigators in coordination with BI, and physically transferred into Korea alongside the suspects.
  • Korean-side organisational note: 합수부 was launched 2022-07-29 as a 합동수사단 (Joint Investigation Team), and was formally embedded into Seoul Eastern District Prosecutors' Office as a permanent 부 (department) on 2026-01-06. Cumulative outputs to 2026-03-31: 1,206 persons booked, 481 in custody.
  • Press release header lists four Korean cooperating agencies: 검찰 (Prosecution Service) / 경찰 (Police) / 관세청 (Korea Customs Service) / 국세청 (National Tax Service) — i.e., 합수부 is a four-agency Korean structure. The body explicitly identifies 법무부 국제형사과 (MOJ International Criminal Affairs Division) and 필리핀 대사관 파견 영사 (Korean Embassy Manila police attaché, ranked 총경 / Senior Superintendent) as additional Korean-side coordinators for the repatriation phase.

Source Summary

Tier-1 Korean-language primary press release issued by the Seoul Eastern District Prosecutors' Office (대검찰청 subordinate office) on 2026-04-30 (10:00 embargo), announcing the arrest, forced repatriation, and indictment-in-custody of a Korean-national voice phishing call-centre cell that had been operating in real time from a Clark (Philippines) office during 2025-06–07. The case was worked end-to-end by the 보이스피싱범죄 합동수사부 (Voice-Phishing Joint Investigation Unit, "합수부") in cooperation with Philippines-stationed Korean prosecutorial investigators (파견 검찰수사관) and the Philippine Bureau of Immigration Fugitive Search Unit (BI FSU / 수배자 추적대). Custody was secured through INTERPOL Red Notices and Korean passport invalidation; forced repatriation was coordinated with MOJ International Criminal Affairs and the Korean Embassy Manila police attaché.

Relevance to IC

This source documents a Korea-as-requesting-state cooperation pattern that is structurally distinct from the 73-person KNPA extradition (2026-04-27) already in the wiki:

  1. Prosecution-led, not police-led. The Korean lead actor is the SPO (대검찰청 → 서울동부지검 합수부), with prosecutorial investigators physically deployed in-country, rather than the KNPA-led 코리아전담반 model documented for Cambodia.
  2. In-country prosecutorial investigator + foreign immigration authority chain. The named partner agency is the Philippine Bureau of Immigration Fugitive Search Unit (FSU), exercising domestic immigration-violation detention at the Bicutan facility, which is then converted into a repatriation channel via INTERPOL Red Notice. This is a different cooperation channel from PNP-to-KNPA police-to-police cooperation and warrants its own mechanism tracking.
  3. Four-agency Korean-side composition. The 합수부 header lists Prosecution / Police / Customs / Tax Service together — i.e., the 합수부 is a four-pillar fusion structure for voice-phishing transnational fraud, not a single-agency unit.
  4. Verifiable, dated timeline. The press release supplies a fully dated 2025-07-24 → 2026-04-28 cooperation timeline with named legal mechanisms (INTERPOL Red Notice, passport invalidation, MOJ International Criminal Affairs coordination, Manila Embassy police attaché) — a level of mechanism specificity rarely available in tier-1 primary sources for Korean transnational cybercrime cases.
  5. New modus-operandi attestation. The 'review-task' (리뷰달기) two-stage mission scam — small-reward trust-building mission → high-extraction 'purchase-authentication team mission' with debt-coercion script — is presented as a new typology (신종 수법) in the primary text and is useful for the wiki's voice-phishing IC crime-type page.
