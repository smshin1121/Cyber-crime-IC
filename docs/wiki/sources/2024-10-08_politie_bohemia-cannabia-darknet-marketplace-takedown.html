주요 발견사항

  • Politie announced 2024-10-08 takedown of Bohemia/Cannabia darknet marketplace, described as the largest ever encountered by Dutch police.
  • Two administrators arrested: a 20-year-old British national at Schiphol Airport on 2024-06-27, and a 23-year-old Irish national in Ireland in August 2024.
  • More than EUR 8 million in cryptocurrency seized; database, crypto and web servers (most located in the Netherlands) seized for further analysis.
  • Marketplace metrics per Politie: ~82,000 daily ads, ~67,000 monthly transactions, EUR 12 million peak monthly turnover (September 2023).
  • Investigation conducted with Ireland, the United Kingdom and the United States since end of 2022. Eurojust/Europol coordination not explicitly cited for the original takedown.
