주요 발견사항

  • Xu Zewei (徐泽伟), 34, PRC national, extradited from Italy weekend of 2026-04-25 to 2026-04-26; first US court appearance Houston (S.D. Texas) on 2026-04-27.
  • Co-defendant Zhang Yu (张宇), 44, PRC national, remains at large.
  • 9-count indictment in S.D. Texas: conspiracy to commit wire fraud + 2 counts of wire fraud (max 20 years/count); conspiracy to cause damage / unauthorized access / wire fraud / identity theft (max 5 years); 2 counts of obtaining information by unauthorized access (max 5 years/count); 2 counts of intentional damage to a protected computer (max 10 years/count); aggravated identity theft (max 2 years).
  • Activity period: February 2020 — June 2021.
  • Direction: officers of the PRC Ministry of State Security (MSS) Shanghai State Security Bureau (SSSB).
  • Xu employed by Shanghai Powerock Network Co. Ltd. (Powerock) — one of many PRC enabling companies for state-directed hacking.
  • Phase 1 (early 2020) — COVID-19 research intrusions: targeted US universities, immunologists, virologists doing COVID-19 vaccine/treatment/testing research; example 2020-02-19 compromise of S.D. Texas research university network; 2020-02-22 SSSB officer directed mailbox targeting of virologists / immunologists.
  • Phase 2 (late 2020 — early 2021) — HAFNIUM Microsoft Exchange campaign: exploited Microsoft Exchange Server vulnerabilities; web-shell installations specific to HAFNIUM actors; targeted another S.D. Texas university + a worldwide-offices law firm including Washington, D.C.; search terms used in stolen mailboxes: 'Chinese sources', 'MSS', 'HongKong'.
  • HAFNIUM scale: 12,700+ US organizations compromised.
  • March 2021: Microsoft public disclosure of HAFNIUM; 2021-03-10 FBI/CISA Joint Advisory; April 2021 court-authorized DOJ remediation operation; July 2021 US + foreign partner attribution of HAFNIUM to PRC MSS.
  • Italy arrest: Milan Malpensa airport, July 2025.
  • DOJ OIA + Italian National Police (Cyber Division / Polizia Postale) coordinated arrest and extradition.
  • FBI lead office: Houston Field Office. Prosecutors: AUSA Mark McIntyre (S.D. Texas) + Deputy Chief Matthew Anzaldi (NSD National Security Cyber Section).

Source Summary

DOJ-OPA Press Release 26-404 dated 2026-04-27 announcing the weekend extradition from Italy of Xu Zewei (PRC national, 34) — a contractor for Shanghai Powerock Network Co. Ltd. who acted under direction of PRC MSS Shanghai State Security Bureau (SSSB) officers — and his first US court appearance in Houston on a 9-count S.D. Texas indictment for COVID-19 research intrusions (early 2020) and HAFNIUM Microsoft Exchange Server intrusions (late 2020 — early 2021). Xu was arrested at Milan Malpensa airport in July 2025; extradition coordinated by DOJ OIA + Italian National Police Cyber Division (Polizia Postale). Co-defendant Zhang Yu remains at large.

IC Pattern

This release establishes a US-Italy bilateral extradition pattern for Chinese state-sponsored hackers — the first wiki record of such a pattern. Notable features:

  • Italy as extradition source for a Chinese state-sponsored hacker — characterized as "rare" in third-party reporting; first wiki record.
  • PRC MSS / SSSB direction with private-contractor architecture (Shanghai Powerock Network Co. Ltd.): the PRC uses an extensive network of private companies and contractors to obscure state involvement in hacking, with contractors casting a wide net for vulnerable computers and selling stolen information back to the PRC government.
  • Two-phase activity attribution: COVID-19 research intrusions (Phase 1, early 2020) and HAFNIUM Microsoft Exchange campaign (Phase 2, late 2020 – early 2021) under the same SSSB direction.
  • DOJ OIA + Italian Polizia Postale Cyber Division as the operational extradition channel.
  • HAFNIUM scale: 12,700+ US organizations compromised — the wiki's first record of HAFNIUM-attributed intrusion-volume figures in primary IC documentation.
