  • DPRK IT workers earn up to USD 300,000 annually individually, generating hundreds of millions collectively each year for DPRK Ministry of Defense and weapons programs.
  • US State Department reward: up to USD 5M for information disrupting DPRK illicit financial activities.

Source Summary

DOJ Office of Public Affairs press release (25-680) dated 2025-06-30 (updated 2025-07-03) announcing the most comprehensive single-day enforcement wave in the DPRK RevGen: Domestic Enabler Initiative to date. The release packages two federal indictments (D. Mass. and N.D. Ga.), one separate plea/information (D. Mass.), one arrest (Zhenxing Wang), 29 laptop-farm searches across 16 states, 29 financial accounts and 21 fraudulent websites seized, and approximately 200 computers seized.

IC Pattern

This release establishes the most fully-articulated multi-jurisdiction DPRK-IT-worker enforcement architecture in the wiki to date. Notable features:

  • DPRK RevGen: Domestic Enabler Initiative — joint National Security Division + FBI Cyber and Counterintelligence Divisions effort prioritising US-based enablers of DPRK illicit revenue.
  • Cross-jurisdictional defendant base: 8 Chinese nationals, 4 DPRK nationals, 2 Taiwanese nationals, 2 US nationals; co-conspirator locations include US, China, UAE, Taiwan; DPRK travel-document use to enter UAE.
  • Atlanta + Serbia employer cohort: blockchain R&D company (Atlanta GA) + virtual token company (Serbia).
  • Tornado Cash + Malaysian fraudulent ID as the laundering chain — connecting DPRK virtual-currency theft to a previously-sanctioned mixer (Cryptex / Pm2BTC is a different mixer; Tornado Cash was earlier OFAC-designated and partially de-listed).
  • ITAR data theft from California AI defense contractor (2024-01-19 → 2024-04-02) as a national-security-relevant export-control violation embedded in an apparently economic-fraud scheme.
  • Multi-district laptop-farm actions by FBI Denver Field Office across 14 states in June 10–17, 2025 — distinct from the October 2024 prior action across 3 states.
