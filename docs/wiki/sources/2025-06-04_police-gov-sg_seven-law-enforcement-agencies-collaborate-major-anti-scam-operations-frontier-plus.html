주요 발견사항

  • Singapore Police Force own-domain release (police.gov.sg) on the FRONTIER+ first joint operation (28 Apr – 28 May 2025), Singapore-side counterpart of the Hong Kong-issued release P2025060300475.
  • Names all seven participating LE agencies with their full operational designations: SPF Anti-Scam Command (ASCom); HKSAR Anti-Deception Coordination Centre; RoK National Counter Scam Centre; Malaysia National Scam Response Centre; Maldives Anti-Scam Centre; Thailand Anti-Online Scam Operation Centre; Macao SAR Anti-Fraud Co-Ordination Centre.
  • Aggregate results confirmed: 2,784 officers; 1,800+ arrests; 33,900+ subjects investigated; 9,200+ scam cases; S$289M (USD 225M) victim losses; 32,600+ bank accounts frozen; S$26.2M (USD 20M) seized.
  • Singapore-specific figures: 545 investigated; 106 arrested; 1,300+ local scam cases; S$39.3M (USD 30.6M) losses; 714 frozen bank accounts; S$7.69M (USD 5.90M) seized — match the per-jurisdiction Annex in the HK release.
  • Documents a concrete cross-border recovery success: two Chinese Services Impersonation Scam victims transferred S$25K + S$45K; Anti-Scam Centre traced same-day outflow to Malaysian bank accounts; with assistance of Malaysia's National Scam Response Centre, S$19K+ recovered. Cited as proof of FRONTIER+ cross-border recovery effectiveness.
  • FRONTIER+ membership at time of this release listed as 10 jurisdictions (Singapore, Hong Kong SAR, Malaysia, Thailand, Maldives, Republic of Korea, Australia, Macao SAR, Canada, Indonesia). Brunei is NOT yet listed — matching the HK release of one day prior.
  • Quote from Director CAD David Chew: 'A transnational threat requires a transnational response. No single jurisdiction has an adequate answer, but we are collectively stronger together.'
  • Lists Singapore-side prosecution charges under PC s.417 r/w s.109, CMA s.3(1) r/w s.12, CDSA s.55A, CDSA s.51, CMA s.8A — first source to document the domestic-law basis on the Singapore side of FRONTIER+ enforcement.

개요Summary

Official Singapore Police Force own-domain press release (police.gov.sg/media-hub/news, 2025-06-04) issued one day after the Hong Kong release on the same operation. This is the Singapore-side counterpart of HKSAR Government Information Services Press Release P2025060300475 (2025-06-03), reporting the results of the first joint enforcement operation of the FRONTIER+ Cross-border Anti-Scam Collaboration Platform conducted 2025-04-28 to 2025-05-28.

The release enumerates all seven participating law enforcement agencies with their full operational unit designations, gives identical aggregate results to the HK release, supplies a Singapore-specific breakdown that matches the HK Annex per-jurisdiction figures, and documents a concrete cross-border-recovery case study (Chinese Services Impersonation Scam, S$19K+ recovered from Malaysia via Malaysia's National Scam Response Centre).
