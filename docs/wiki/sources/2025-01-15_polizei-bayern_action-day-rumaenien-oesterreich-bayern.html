주요 발견사항

  • Tier-1 own-domain joint press release of Bayerische Polizei (Polizeipräsidium Mittelfranken) and the Zentralstelle Cybercrime Bayern (ZCB) of the Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Bamberg, dated 2025-01-15, reporting on the cross-border action day of 2024-12-17 in Tirol (Austria), Bukarest and Râmnicu Vâlcea (Romania), and the Regensburg area (Bayern, Germany).
  • Explicitly names four jurisdictions' LE/prosecutor agencies as cooperating: Germany (ZCB Bayern of the Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Bamberg, Polizeipräsidium Mittelfranken / Kriminalpolizei Nürnberg), Austria (Landeskriminalamt Tirol), Romania (D.I.I.C.O.T - Directoratul de Investigare a Infracțiunilor de Criminalitate Organizată și Terorism), and Eurojust as coordinating EU body. Quote (de): 'gemeinsamen Ermittlungsgruppe mit den rumänischen Strafverfolgungsbehörden unter Beteiligung von Eurojust' / (en) 'joint investigative team with Romanian prosecutorial authorities under Eurojust participation'.
  • Verbatim cooperation framing: 'länderübergreifenden Strafverfolgung bayerischer, österreichischer und rumänischer Ermittler' / 'cross-border prosecution efforts by Bavarian, Austrian, and Romanian investigators'.
  • Quantified action day results: 13 locations searched; approximately 150 police officers deployed; 5 male suspects of 7 identified (ages 22-38) arrested under cross-jurisdictional warrants; at least 120 compromised merchant accounts; orders worth approximately EUR 110 million induced from victims; 381 identified victims with confirmed EUR 192,000 in losses for undelivered goods plus EUR 17,000 from at least 7 real-estate-fraud victims; approximately EUR 70,000 cash seized; 2 prosecutors and 2 IT-forensics specialists assigned.

Source Summary

Tier-1 joint press release of the Bayerische Polizei (Polizeipräsidium Mittelfranken / Kriminalpolizei Nürnberg) and the Zentralstelle Cybercrime Bayern (ZCB) of the Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Bamberg, dated 15 January 2025, reporting on a tri-country coordinated action day on 17 December 2024 against an internationally operating internet-fraud organised-crime group. The release explicitly enumerates the cooperating LE/prosecutor authorities (Germany: ZCB Bayern + Polizeipräsidium Mittelfranken; Austria: Landeskriminalamt Tirol; Romania: D.I.I.C.O.T; coordinating EU body: Eurojust) and the cooperation modality (Joint Investigation Team with Romanian prosecutorial authorities under Eurojust participation; cross-border prosecution by Bavarian, Austrian, and Romanian investigators).

Relevance to IC

Documents a Germany–Austria–Romania three-country action day coordinated through a Eurojust-backed JIT. Confirms the DIICOT role as the Romanian-side counterpart on the JIT and adds a Bavarian-state-level (rather than BKA-federal) ingest point for the German LKA-system mapping.
