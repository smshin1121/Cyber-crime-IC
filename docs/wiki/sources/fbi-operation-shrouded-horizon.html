개요Summary

The FBI news story documents Operation Shrouded Horizon, the 15 July 2015 takedown of the Darkode cybercrime forum. Key facts:

  1. Scale: 20 countries participated, ~70 individuals targeted through arrests, searches or equipment seizures
  2. US indictments: 12 individuals charged in the Western District of Pennsylvania
  3. Forum profile: 250-300 vetted members, invitation-only, English-language, ~10 years of operation
  4. Lead agency: FBI Pittsburgh Field Office
  5. Coordination: Europol EC3, NCFTA (private-sector partnership)
  6. Duration of investigation: 18 months of undercover infiltration
  7. FBI characterisation: "Largest-ever coordinated law enforcement effort directed at an online cyber criminal forum" (at the time of announcement)

Why this source matters

For the IC analysis on operation-shrouded-horizon this FBI press release is the primary (Tier 2) US source. It is especially significant because:

  • Precedent value: Shrouded Horizon provided the operational template for later forum takedowns (AlphaBay, Hansa, RaidForums, BreachForums)
  • Undercover infiltration of an invitation-only forum: One of the few publicly acknowledged FBI operations that gained access to a closed community — providing rare insight into covert cooperation methods
  • 20-country scale: An unusually broad coalition for 2015, predating the normalization of 20+ country operations that became common in 2019-2024
  • NCFTA involvement: An early example of formal private-sector/law-enforcement partnership in a takedown (NCFTA is a non-profit based in Pittsburgh focused on exactly this coordination)
