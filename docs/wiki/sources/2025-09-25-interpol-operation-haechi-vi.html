USD 439 million recovered in global financial crime operation - Operation HAECHI VI

source 출처

Summary

Operation HAECHI VI, the sixth Korea-initiated HAECHI iteration, ran April to August 2025 across 40 countries. Recovered USD 439 million total ($342M fiat, $97M physical/virtual assets). 68,000+ bank accounts blocked, ~400 crypto wallets frozen. Continued targeting seven cyber-enabled fraud types. Notable Korea-UAE BEC recovery of USD 3.91 million.

Key Data

  • Operation: Operation HAECHI VI
  • Timeframe: April – August 2025
  • Countries: 40
  • Assets recovered: USD 439 million
  • Crypto recovered: ~USD 16 million
  • Bank accounts blocked: 68,000+
  • Crypto wallets frozen: ~400
  • Crime types: Voice phishing, romance scams, BEC, investment fraud, e-commerce fraud, money laundering

Relevance

Sixth HAECHI iteration setting new financial recovery records. Korea-UAE I-GRIP sub-operation demonstrates rapid international fund interception critical for BEC and voice phishing cases.
