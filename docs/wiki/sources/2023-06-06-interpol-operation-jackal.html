Closing ranks on West African organized crime: more than EUR 2 million seized in Operation Jackal

source 출처

Summary

INTERPOL coordinated Operation Jackal from 15 to 29 May 2023, mobilizing law enforcement across 21 countries against Black Axe and similar West African organized crime groups responsible for the majority of the world's cyber-enabled financial fraud. 103 arrests, 1,110 suspects identified, 208 bank accounts blocked, EUR 2.15 million seized/frozen.

Key Data

  • Operation: Operation Jackal
  • Timeframe: 15-29 May 2023
  • Countries: 21 (Argentina, Australia, Belgium, Brazil, Canada, Cote d'Ivoire, France, Germany, Indonesia, Ireland, Italy, Malaysia, Netherlands, Nigeria, Portugal, South Africa, Spain, Switzerland, UAE, UK, US)
  • Arrests: 103
  • Suspects identified: 1,110
  • Bank accounts blocked: 208
  • Assets seized: EUR 2.15 million
  • Crime types: BEC, romance scams, money laundering, credit card fraud, advance payment scams
  • Target: Black Axe and similar West African organized crime groups

Relevance

First dedicated INTERPOL operation targeting Black Axe. 21-country scope across five continents demonstrates global nature of BEC enforcement.
