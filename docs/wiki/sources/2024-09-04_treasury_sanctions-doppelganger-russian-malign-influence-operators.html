주요 발견사항

  • On 2024-09-04, U.S. Treasury OFAC issued press release JY-2559 designating 10 Russian individuals and 2 Russian entities under E.O. 14024 (IEEPA) as part of the U.S. government's coordinated response to Moscow's malign influence efforts targeting the 2024 U.S. presidential election.
  • Designated entities: Autonomous Non-Profit Organization (ANO) Dialog (linked to Doppelgänger; operates War on Fakes website); ANO Dialog Regions (subsidiary).
  • Designated individuals (10): RT executives Margarita Simonyan, Elizaveta Brodskaia, Anton Anisimov, Andrey Kiyashko, Konstantin Kalashnikov, Elena Afanasyeva (last two also indicted same day in SDNY); RaHDit operatives Aleksey Garashchenko, Anastasia Yermoshkina, Aleksandr Nezhentsev; ANO Dialog Director General Vladimir Tabak.
  • Companion DOJ/FBI domain seizure (32 domains) targeting RRN/ANO Dialog and previously-designated Social Design Agency (SDA) is explicitly cross-referenced. State Department also concurrently designated Rossiya Segodnya/RT/Sputnik as Foreign Missions and offered up to $10M Rewards for Justice.
  • No same-day UK/EU/NATO/allied parallel sanctions package is announced. Treasury notes that the EU previously separately sanctioned ANO Dialog for War on Fakes — this is a prior (not coordinated) EU action.

Source Summary

U.S. Treasury OFAC press release JY-2559, dated 2024-09-04, announcing the designation of 10 Russian individuals and 2 Russian entities under Executive Order 14024 (Russia harmful foreign activities; IEEPA) as the financial-sanctions component of a coordinated U.S. government response to Moscow's foreign malign influence operations targeting the 2024 U.S. presidential election. The designations target two clusters: (1) RT executives (Simonyan and seven others) plus (2) ANO Dialog / ANO Dialog Regions and their Director General Tabak, identified by Treasury as linked to the Doppelgänger influence network and operators of the "War on Fakes" website and the "RRN" (Reliable Recent News) outlet. The release explicitly cross-references the FBI's same-day domain seizures targeting RRN/ANO Dialog and previously-designated Social Design Agency (SDA).

Relevance to IC

This source is the financial-sanctions companion to the DOJ Doppelganger 32-domain seizure + SDNY RT/Tenet indictment. It documents the operation page treasury-doppelganger-russian-malign-influence-sanctions-2024.

International cooperation scope is limited and unilateral:

  • The release names no foreign LE counterpart, foreign government, or international organization as a same-day cooperation partner. The cross-reference to a prior (separate) EU sanction of ANO Dialog is preserved as background context but is not coordination.
  • The operation illustrates the DOJ + Treasury + State Department same-day synchronization pattern that the U.S. now uses against state-directed cyber-enabled foreign malign influence when the host state (Russia, post-2022) is uncooperative — a comparative reference point for IC effectiveness analysis where formal MLAT / extradition / Budapest Convention pathways are unavailable.
