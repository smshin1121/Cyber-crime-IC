Operation Orion International: South America CSAM Crackdown

Source Overview

INTERPOL press release reporting on Operation Orion International, a region-wide initiative across 12 South American countries targeting transnational CSAM distribution on instant messaging applications. Launched from intelligence provided by Chile and Argentina.

Key Data Points

  • Operation period: May - September 2024
  • 12 South American countries participated
  • 144 arrests; 20 children rescued/safeguarded
  • 18 direct abusers arrested; 111 charged with possession/distribution
  • 7 Red Notice subjects arrested
  • Ecuador-Korea collaboration mentioned for one apprehension
  • UNODC provided support
