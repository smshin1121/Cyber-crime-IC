Source Summary

INTERPOL press release announcing the results of Operation HAECHI V (July-November 2024), the fifth iteration of the HAECHI series targeting cyber-enabled financial crime. The operation set records for the HAECHI series with 5,500+ arrests and USD 400M+ in seizures across 40 countries. A notable sub-operation involved Korean and Chinese authorities jointly dismantling a voice phishing syndicate responsible for USD 1.1 billion in financial losses.

Relevance to IC

This source is almost certainly one of the most significant examples of INTERPOL-coordinated financial cybercrime cooperation in 2024. It demonstrates: - Massive multi-country operational coordination (40 countries) - Effective bilateral cooperation within a multilateral framework (Korea-China sub-operation) - INTERPOL's role as the primary coordination body for global financial cybercrime enforcement - Korea's leading role in the HAECHI series
