주요 발견사항

  • KNPA-Cambodia 코리아 전담반 (Korea-dedicated Squad in Cambodia, opened 2025-11) conducted a series of joint arrests Dec 2025–Feb 2026, with the 2026-02-19 KNPA release reporting 6 INTERPOL Red Notice fugitives (관리자급 인물) arrested between 2025-12-22 and 2026-02-10. Of those, 2 were scam-organisation 총책 (masterminds) and one was the head of Korean-victim money laundering at the Taizi (태자) compound.
  • Three named individual takedowns in February 2026: (1) 2026-02-04 — scam-organisation manager arrested after a 500m foot chase, intelligence supplied through a Korean police attaché (경찰주재관) and real-time information sharing between the Korea-dedicated Squad and Cambodian police; (2) 2026-02-06 — major scam-organisation cadre arrested at hotel hideout in joint operation between Korea-dedicated Squad and Cambodian police, victim losses ~₩8.4 billion (~USD 6 million); (3) 2026-02-10 — investment-fraud organisation principal arrested after multi-day CCTV-based tracking originating from intelligence supplied through the Korean Embassy police attaché in Cambodia, victim losses ~₩10.6 billion (~USD 7.6 million).
  • The Korea-dedicated Squad has run 12 operations since opening in 2025-11; cumulative outcomes: 4 Korean nationals rescued (구출) and 140 organised-crime suspects arrested. Average pre-arrest in-country concealment by the 6 Red Notice subjects was ~1 year 10 months — explicit primary-source datapoint on transnational fugitive concealment duration.
  • The 2026-02-19 release explicitly names Cambodia National Police (캄보디아 경찰청) as the cooperating LE partner with KNPA, MOFA Embassy police attaché (주캄보디아 한국대사관 경찰주재관), NIS (국정원), and Seoul Metropolitan Police INTERPOL team (서울청 인터폴팀) named on the Korean side. Whole-of-government Korean construct: 범정부 「초국가범죄 특별대응 전담반(TF)」 (Presidential-Office-anchored Transnational Crime Special Response TF).
  • Quoted spokesperson: 이재영 경찰청 국제치안협력국장 (Lee Jae-young, KNPA International Police Cooperation Bureau Director-General). Quote on the balloon-effect risk: '캄보디아 내 조직이 거점을 이동하거나 운영 방식을 변화시키는 이른바 「풍선효과」 가능성까지 주시하며 국제공조를 강화하고 있다.'
  • Tier-1 IC verification per L24: explicit Korea LE + Cambodia LE acknowledgement in the primary release ('한국 경찰청 – 국정원 - 대사관 – 캄보디아 경찰청 간 유기적인 협력 체계' and '한·캄 경찰 간 합동작전' wording verbatim). No private-wire dependency: PDF attachment captured directly from korea.kr file 198362700.

Source Summary

KNPA press release dated 2026-02-19 (online/broadcast embargo lifted 2026-02-18 09:00) reporting a sustained joint Korea–Cambodia enforcement campaign against Korean-targeted scam organisations operating from Cambodian compounds, conducted through the Korea-dedicated Squad (코리아 전담반) jointly stood up by KNPA and Cambodian National Police inside Cambodia in November 2025. The release announces six INTERPOL Red Notice fugitives arrested between 2025-12-22 and 2026-02-10, plus three named February 2026 takedowns: a 500-metre foot-chase arrest of a scam-organisation manager (2026-02-04), a hotel-hideout arrest of a major scam cadre tied to ₩8.4 billion in victim losses (2026-02-06), and a CCTV-tracking arrest of an investment-fraud principal tied to ₩10.6 billion in victim losses (2026-02-10). The Squad has run 12 operations since opening; cumulative arrests = 140 organised-crime suspects, with 4 Korean nationals rescued. The Korean side names KNPA, NIS (국정원), the Korean Embassy in Cambodia (specifically the embedded police attaché 경찰주재관), and Seoul Metropolitan Police INTERPOL team; the Cambodian side is named only as the Cambodian National Police (캄보디아 경찰청). The release is signed by the KNPA International Police Cooperation Bureau (국제치안협력국) and quoted spokesperson is its Director-General 이재영, who flags the "balloon-effect" (풍선효과) of organisations relocating to weaker-enforcement jurisdictions.

Relevance to IC

This is a tier-1 Korean-language primary source confirming sustained, named-individual, joint Korea–Cambodia LE field cooperation through an embedded operational unit (Korea-dedicated Squad) inside the receiving jurisdiction. It is materially distinct from the existing Korea-Cambodia operations in the wiki:

  1. korea-cambodia-scam-repatriation — earlier (2025) cohort of repatriations.
  2. korea-cambodia-philippines-73-extradition-2026 — April 2026 73-person mass extradition (different cohort, different timeframe).
  3. knpa-breaking-chains-2nd-international-operation-meeting-2026 — 2026-02-05 multilateral meeting (different event, multilateral rather than bilateral, no individual takedowns named in February 2026).

The 2026-02-19 release supplies novel datapoints not in any existing wiki page: - 6 INTERPOL Red Notice fugitives arrested in a single 12-22 to 02-10 window (named count, not cohort total). - Three discrete February 2026 takedowns with named operational details (foot chase distance, hotel-hideout takedown method, CCTV multi-day tracking). - Direct quote from KNPA International Police Cooperation Bureau Director-General Lee Jae-young naming the balloon-effect (풍선효과). - Average ~1-year-10-month in-country concealment by Red Notice subjects — primary-source transnational concealment duration datapoint. - Whole-of-Korean-government bilateral architecture explicitly diagrammed: KNPA → NIS → Embassy → Cambodian National Police.
