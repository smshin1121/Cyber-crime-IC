574 arrests and USD 3 million recovered in coordinated cybercrime operation across Africa - Operation Sentinel

source 출처

Summary

INTERPOL coordinated Operation Sentinel from 27 October to 27 November 2025 across 19 African countries under the AFJOC umbrella. 574 suspects arrested, USD 3 million recovered. Over 6,000 malicious links taken down and 6 ransomware variants decrypted. Focused on BEC, digital extortion, and ransomware. Funded by UK FCDO and EU-Council of Europe GLACY-e project.

Key Data

  • Operation: Operation Sentinel
  • Timeframe: 27 October – 27 November 2025
  • Countries: 19 African countries
  • Arrests: 574
  • Assets recovered: USD 3 million
  • Estimated losses: USD 21 million+
  • Malicious links taken down: 6,000+
  • Ransomware variants decrypted: 6
  • Crime types: BEC, digital extortion, ransomware
  • Funding: UK FCDO, EU-Council of Europe GLACY-e

Relevance

Largest BEC-related enforcement action on the African continent. Demonstrates donor-funded model for building African cybercrime enforcement capacity.
