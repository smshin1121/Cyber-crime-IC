주요 발견사항

  • Indonesian National Police (Polri) Bareskrim arrested an Indonesian national identified by initials LCS at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport on 2026-05-03, executing an INTERPOL Red Notice tied to a cross-border online fraud network operating from Cambodia.
  • LCS allegedly served as operator of an online fraud platform branded 'abbishopee'; at least 23 separate police reports (Laporan Polisi) had been filed against the network across regions of Indonesia and were consolidated under Dittipidsiber Bareskrim Polri.
  • Three earlier-arrested co-defendants in the same network were already convicted by the South Jakarta District Court (Pengadilan Negeri Jakarta Selatan) prior to LCS's apprehension.
  • Senior Polri official on the record: Brig. Gen. Pol. Himawan Bayu Aji, Director of Cyber Crime (Direktur Tindak Pidana Siber) at Bareskrim Polri, who described the arrest as the result of cross-country cooperation and coordination.
  • Tier-1 primary source: published on Polri's official Tribrata News newsroom (tribratanews.polri.go.id) on 2026-05-05 09:59 WIB; verbatim mirror copies appear on multiple regional Polri portals (Polda Jatim, Polres Batu, Polda Sultra).
  • IC scope (≥2 countries): Indonesia (host / arresting state) + Cambodia (named operating base of the fraud network), with INTERPOL Red Notice as the named multilateral coordination mechanism.

Source Profile

This is an Indonesian-language press release on Tribrata News, the official news portal of the Indonesian National Police (Kepolisian Negara Republik Indonesia, "Polri"), at tribratanews.polri.go.id. It is dated 2026-05-05 09:59 WIB and is maintained by Polri's Public Relations Division (Divisi Humas Polri). Verbatim mirror copies appear on regional Polri sub-domains (e.g. tribratanews.jatim.polri.go.id, tribratanews.resbatu.jatim.polri.go.id, tribratanews.sultra.polri.go.id), confirming the canonical national release.

The release announces a single coordinated arrest action conducted at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport in Jakarta on Sunday, 2026-05-03, against an Indonesian national designated by the initials LCS. LCS had been on INTERPOL's Red Notice fugitive list and on the wanted-persons list (DPO) of Polri's Cyber Crime Directorate (Dittipidsiber, Bareskrim). He is alleged to have run a cross-border online fraud network operating from Cambodia (Kamboja), using a branded fraud platform called "abbishopee".
