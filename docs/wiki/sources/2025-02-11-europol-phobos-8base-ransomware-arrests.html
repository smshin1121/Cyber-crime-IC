Summary

Official Europol press release announcing a coordinated international crackdown on the Phobos and 8Base ransomware operations. The operation resulted in 4 arrests of Russian nationals (8Base leadership) and the takedown of 27 servers, with 400+ companies warned worldwide.

This source is highly likely the most relevant to the Korean context among the five verified sources, as it confirms the arrest of a Phobos administrator in South Korea in June 2024, followed by extradition to the United States in November 2024. This demonstrates active Korean participation in international ransomware enforcement.

The source describes a sustained investigation spanning from February 2019 to February 2025, with Europol organizing 37 operational meetings and exchanging nearly 600 SIENA messages.
