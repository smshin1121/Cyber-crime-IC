주요 발견사항

  • 4-country coordinated takedown announced 2025-05-07: Germany, Netherlands, Poland, US.
  • 4 administrators arrested in Poland, running 6 stresser/booter services: Cfxapi, Cfxsecurity, neostress, jetstress, quickdown, zapcut.
  • 9 booter-service domains seized in the United States.
  • Service activity period: 2022 — 2025.
  • Attack targets: schools, government services, businesses, and gaming platforms.
  • Polish lead authority: Central Cybercrime Bureau (Centralne Biuro Zwalczania Cyberprzestępczości / CBZC).
  • German support: BKA + Frankfurt am Main ZIT — identified one suspect and shared intelligence on others.
  • Dutch contribution: deployed fake booter sites as warnings; seized data from booter websites in NL data centres and shared with international partners including Poland.
  • US action: USDOJ, FBI, HSI, DCIS — seized 9 domains associated with booter services.
  • Operation context: continuing phase of Operation PowerOFF (long-running international DDoS-for-hire infrastructure-takedown campaign).
  • Service architecture: slick UIs requiring no technical skills (target IP + attack type + duration + fee → automated DDoS); industrialised via centralised rented infrastructure rather than infected device botnets.

Source Summary

Europol newsroom press release dated 2025-05-07 announcing the May 2025 phase of Operation PowerOFF — a 4-country coordinated takedown of a DDoS-for-hire empire running 6 stresser/booter services (Cfxapi, Cfxsecurity, neostress, jetstress, quickdown, zapcut) active 2022-2025. Polish-led; 4 admins arrested in Poland; 9 US booter-service domains seized; Dutch fake-booter-site warning campaign + Dutch data sharing; German BKA + ZIT identified suspects; Europol EC3 analytical and operational support.

IC Pattern

This release establishes a 4-country Polish-led DDoS-for-hire takedown pattern under Operation PowerOFF:
