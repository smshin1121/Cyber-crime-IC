주요 발견사항

  • Panama PGN (Procuraduría General de la Nación), led by procurador Luis Carlos Gómez personally, executed the Panama leg of 'Aliados por la Infancia VI' on 2026-04-28 with the collaboration of US Homeland Security Investigations (HSI).
  • Panama auxiliary forces named: DNIP (Dirección Nacional de Investigación Penal) and DIJ (División de Investigación Judicial), both arms of the Policía Nacional de Panamá.
  • Panama-side results: 7 arrests (aprehensiones) across multiple search locations in the province of Panamá and in Chiriquí, on charges of posesión y distribución de material de abuso sexual infantil.
  • PGN frames the operation as simultaneous across 15 countries, naming Argentina, Brasil, Costa Rica, España, Francia as partner jurisdictions ('entre otros').
  • The 7 Panama arrests figure directly corroborates the MPF CABA Argentina release's Panama-specific raid/arrest breakdown (7 raids in Panama under MPF CABA's tally).
  • PGN names HSI explicitly as the only US cooperation partner — narrower than the MPF CABA Argentina release which names US Embassy + DHS + MPF CABA as the tripartite IC arrangement (HSI is a sub-component of DHS, so the two are consistent).
