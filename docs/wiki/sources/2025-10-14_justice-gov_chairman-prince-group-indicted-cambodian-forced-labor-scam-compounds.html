주요 발견사항

  • EDNY federal grand jury indictment unsealed 2025-10-14 charging Cambodian national Chen Zhi (a.k.a. Vincent, 37) — founder/chairman of Prince Holding Group (Prince Group) — with wire fraud conspiracy and money laundering conspiracy. Defendant at large; max 40 years.
  • Largest forfeiture action in DOJ history: civil forfeiture complaint against approximately 127,271 Bitcoin (~USD 15 billion) currently in US government custody, previously stored in unhosted cryptocurrency wallets whose private keys the defendant personally held.
  • Activity period: since approximately 2015. Prince Group operates dozens of business entities in 30+ countries.
  • Predicate offences: forced-labor scam compounds in Cambodia perpetrating 'pig butchering' cryptocurrency investment fraud. Compounds housed vast dormitories surrounded by high walls and barbed wire; functioned as violent forced labor camps.
  • Brooklyn local network laundered millions of dollars on behalf of Prince Group from 250+ victims in New York and across the country.
  • Phone farms with thousands of phones and millions of mobile telephone numbers.
  • Violence: defendant possessed photographs depicting beatings and other methods of torture; communicated directly about beating individuals who 'caused trouble' but specifying not to be 'beaten to death'.
  • Laundering: 'spraying' + 'funneling' cryptocurrency disaggregation/reconsolidation; online gambling; cryptocurrency mining operations using stolen funds as operating capital.
  • Asset use: luxury travel + entertainment + watches + yachts + private jets + vacation homes + high-end collectables + rare artwork (including a Picasso painting purchased through an NY auction house).
  • Investigators: DEA New York Division + FBI New York Joint Asian Criminal Enterprise Task Force + FBI Virtual Asset Unit.
  • Prosecutors: AUSAs Alexander F. Mindlin, Andrew D. Reich, Benjamin Weintraub, Rebecca M. Schuman (EDNY) + Deputy Chief Christopher B. Brown (NSD NatSec Cyber) + AUSA Tanisha Payne (EDNY Asset Recovery for forfeiture).
  • International cooperation: DOJ Office of International Affairs; United Kingdom's National Crime Agency; Isle of Man Constabulary's Proactive International Money-Laundering Investigations Team; United Kingdom's FCDO.
  • Parallel concurrent action: OFAC + FinCEN + UK FCDO/OFSI sanctions (us-uk-prince-group-tco-huione-group-sanctions-2025) and Treasury Section 311 vs Huione Group.
  • Cryptocurrency investment fraud context (FBI IC3 2024 report): caused USD 5.8 billion+ in reported US losses in 2024 alone.
  • Tipline: PrinceGroupTips@fbi.gov.

Source Summary

DOJ-OPA Press Release 25-1,031 dated 2025-10-14 announcing the unsealing of an EDNY federal grand jury indictment against Cambodian national Chen Zhi (a.k.a. Vincent, 37, founder/chairman of Prince Holding Group / Prince Group) for wire fraud conspiracy and money laundering conspiracy directing forced-labor cryptocurrency-investment-fraud scam compounds across Cambodia. Concurrently, the U.S. Attorney's Office EDNY and the National Security Division filed a civil forfeiture complaint against approximately 127,271 Bitcoin (~USD 15 billion) currently in US government custody — the largest forfeiture action in DOJ history. Defendant at large; max 40 years.

IC Pattern

This release establishes the criminal-prosecution and asset-forfeiture track parallel to the OFAC + FinCEN + UK FCDO/OFSI sanctions track on the same Prince Group TCO. Notable IC mechanism classes:

  • Concurrent administrative-and-criminal action: OFAC sanctions + EDNY criminal indictment + civil forfeiture complaint + UK FCDO/OFSI sanctions all unsealed/filed on 2025-10-14.
  • Largest-ever DOJ forfeiture (127,271 BTC, ~USD 15 billion) — the wiki's first record of a single forfeiture exceeding USD 1 billion in any cybercrime-related case, let alone exceeding USD 10 billion.
  • DEA Trident Initiative + FBI New York Joint Asian Criminal Enterprise Task Force + FBI Virtual Asset Unit as the multi-agency investigative architecture for transnational crypto-fraud + forced-labor cases.
  • DOJ OIA + UK NCA + Isle of Man Constabulary's Proactive International Money-Laundering Investigations Team + UK FCDO as the international-cooperation chain for the criminal track.
  • 'Spraying' and 'funneling' cryptocurrency laundering techniques documented in primary IC documentation for the first time in the wiki.
  • Phone-farm criminal-infrastructure pattern (thousands of phones + millions of mobile numbers) recorded in primary IC documentation as a discrete scam-compound operational class.
