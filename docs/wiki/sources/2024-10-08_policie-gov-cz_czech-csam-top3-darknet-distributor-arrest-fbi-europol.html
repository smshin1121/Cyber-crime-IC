주요 발견사항

  • Prague Regional Police Directorate (KŘP hl. m. Prahy, 3rd Department) arrested an elderly Czech-American dual national who, at one point, was among the three largest worldwide distributors of male-child CSAM on the Dark Net.
  • Tier-1 IC acknowledgment: Czech police statement explicitly names U.S. FBI (long-standing intelligence holder over ~10 years) and Europol (coordinator of the international operation that revealed the suspect's identity) as cooperating partners — satisfying ≥2 distinct cooperating LE / prosecutorial agencies in primary source.
  • Suspect described in 1980s U.S. media as an 'electronic genius'; arrest followed >1 year of Prague police investigation enabled by Europol-coordinated international identification.
  • Several terabytes of originally encrypted material seized; full access obtained during investigation.
  • Charges under Czech Criminal Code: production and other handling of child pornography (felony) + exploitation of a child for production of pornography (misdemeanour); maximum 8 years' imprisonment on conviction.
  • No public operation codename disclosed; Europol-coordinated multinational identification operation is referenced only generically.

What this source establishes

  1. Cross-border identification of a Dark Net CSAM offender via Europol-coordinated international operation supported by long-standing FBI intelligence. The press release verbatim states that "the American FBI had information about this person and his activities for nearly ten years" but that the suspect's identity "was revealed only on the basis of an international operation coordinated by Europol."
  2. Czech lead executive action. Prague Regional Police Directorate's 3rd Department conducted >1 year of follow-up investigation enabled by the Europol-coordinated identification, secured full access to several terabytes of originally encrypted material, and effected arrest.
  3. Czech-American dual nationality of suspect. Suspect is a Czech citizen who also obtained U.S. citizenship in earlier life, described in 1980s U.S. media as an "electronic genius" — providing the contextual basis for FBI's long-standing investigative interest.
  4. Scale of distribution. Per the Czech police's own characterisation, the suspect was, "at a certain time, approximately among the three largest distributors of male-child pornography in the world."
