주요 발견사항

  • Korean National Police Agency arrested 5 Korean-national suspects of a Cambodia-Bavet-based web romance-scam ring on 2025-10-28 in three Vietnamese cities (Da Nang, Ho Chi Minh City, Khanh Hoa Province) through joint operation with Vietnamese Public Security (베트남 공안청 / Bộ Công an).
  • 192 Korean victims and ~₩46 billion (~USD 3.5M) in losses; modus operandi was app/SNS-based romance scam coupled with cryptocurrency-investment lures.
  • Bilateral KNPA–Vietnam Public Security cooperation explicitly acknowledged by KNPA International Cooperation Officer (이재영 국제협력관) as a tier-1 IC operation; coordination channels were the Korean police attachés (경찰주재관) in Da Nang and Ho Chi Minh City interfacing with Vietnamese provincial-level public security units.
  • Cambodia is identified as the operating base / origin jurisdiction of the criminal organisation but is NOT a participating LE jurisdiction in this discrete operation (per L24, adversary/origin states do not count as IC partners).
  • Operation is positioned in the KNPA pipeline of 2025-Q4 / 2026-Q1 / 2026-Q2 anti-scam-compound campaigns, predating both the knpa-cambodia-korea-task-force-redcorner-arrests-2026-02 (2026-02-19) and the korea-cambodia-philippines-73-extradition-2026 (2026-04-27) repatriations, and represents the discrete Korea-Vietnam bilateral cooperation thread within the broader Southeast Asian scam-compound enforcement architecture.

개요Summary

Korean-language tier-1 primary press release, issued 2025-11-04 by the Korean National Police Agency via the official Korea.kr 정책브리핑 channel (newsId 156724852), reporting the 2025-10-28 simultaneous arrest of five Korean-national suspects of a Cambodia-Bavet-based web romance-scam organisation, executed in three Vietnamese cities (Da Nang, Ho Chi Minh City, Khanh Hoa Province) through joint operation with Vietnamese Public Security (베트남 공안청 / Bộ Công an Việt Nam).

The release explicitly attributes the arrest outcome to bilateral Korean–Vietnamese law-enforcement cooperation, with KNPA International Cooperation Officer Lee Jae-young (이재영 경찰청 국제협력관) quoted: "이번 검거는 동남아 스캠(사기) 범죄 확산이 우려되는 상황에서 양국 간 긴밀한 국제 공조로 거둔 구체적 성과" and "베트남은 해외 범죄자에 대한 법집행 역량이 높고, 한국 경찰과의 상시 공조 채널을 통해 양국 협력체계가 안정적으로 작동하고 있다."

This satisfies the L24 ≥2-country IC requirement: South Korea (lead) + Vietnam (foreign LE counterpart, named in primary source). Cambodia is identified as the operating base / origin jurisdiction and is recorded only as prose attribution, not as a participating jurisdiction.
