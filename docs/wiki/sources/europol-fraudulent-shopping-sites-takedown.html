개요Summary

Europol's 5 December 2024 press release details a coordinated action against a region-sorted data marketplace and its upstream fake-shopping-site phishing network. Two suspects (Germany, Austria) arrested under European Arrest Warrants; 50+ servers and 200+ TB of digital evidence seized across six EU countries. The ~2-year investigation started in autumn 2022 following reports of bank-impersonation phishing.

The marketplace is distinctive in the 2024 enforcement landscape because it sorted stolen data by geographic region and account balance — a productization pattern suggesting mature criminal market segmentation. The upstream fake-shopping sites used e-commerce branding to capture payment credentials directly, bypassing the need for separate skimmer deployment.
