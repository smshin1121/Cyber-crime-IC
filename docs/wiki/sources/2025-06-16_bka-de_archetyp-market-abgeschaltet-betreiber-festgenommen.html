주요 발견사항

  • Same-day BKA-side joint press release (with Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Frankfurt am Main / ZIT) for Operation 'Deep Sentinel' — confirms German lead architecture (ZIT + BKA) and codifies Archetyp administrator as 30-year-old DE national arrested in Barcelona on 2025-06-11 by Spanish National Police special unit.
  • BKA release enumerates international partners explicitly: Niederlande (Politie), Rumaenien (IGPR), Schweden (Polisen), Spanien (Policia Nacional), USA (USDOJ + HSI + IRS-CI); plus Europol and Eurojust framed as supporting agencies.
  • Charges: bandenmaessiges unerlaubtes Handeltreiben mit Betaeubungsmitteln in nicht geringer Menge under Paragraphen 29a, 30a Betaeubungsmittelgesetz (BtMG).
  • Search-and-seizure detail beyond Europol release: Barcelona admin residence + Hannover + Minden-Luebbecke (Germany) + Bucharest (Romania); additional 20 objects in NRW/Niedersachsen/Hessen/Baden-Wuerttemberg (DE) + Sweden after admin arrest.
  • Sweden arrests enumerated: '7 weitere Personen wurden in Schweden festgenommen' — confirms 7 vendor/moderator arrests on Sweden side (Europol release said '1 moderator + 6 highest vendors targeted').
  • Seizures (verbatim totals): 8 mobiles + 4 computers + 34 data carriers (admin); 47 smartphones + 45 computers + narcotics + assets (other suspects); EUR 7.8 million in assets.
  • Marketplace turnover stats (verbatim): 17,000 listings; 612,000 customer accounts; 3,200 vendors; 2,800+ DE-origin narcotics listings; payment exclusively in Monero; >= EUR 250M turnover.
  • Verbatim cooperation framing: 'Den durch die ZIT und das BKA koordinierten Massnahmen im Zuge der internationalen Operation Deep Sentinel ... gingen langwierige und aufwaendige verdeckte Ermittlungen in den beteiligten Staaten voraus, welche von Europol und Eurojust unterstuetzt wurden.'
  • Quotes preserved: Carsten Meywirth (Leiter Abt Cybercrime BKA) — 'Mit Archetyp Market haben wir den aeltesten, noch aktiven Darknet-Marktplatz vom Netz genommen.'; Dr. Benjamin Krause (Leitender Oberstaatsanwalt der ZIT).
  • Tier-1 own-domain (bka.de) German national LE/prosecutor publisher — distinct from the Europol newsroom primary; satisfies L25 strict (national police agency + national prosecutor's office).

Source Summary

Joint BKA + Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Frankfurt am Main (ZIT) press release on the same day as the Europol newsroom announcement (2025-06-16) for the takedown of Archetyp Market under international Operation "Deep Sentinel". The release confirms the German-lead architecture from the German side, enumerates each named international LE partner with the agency's local name (Politie / IGPR / Polisen / Policia Nacional / USDOJ + HSI + IRS-CI), and adds operational detail that the Europol release does not enumerate (Bucharest search, 9-Polizeibehoerde German deployment, 47 smartphones + 45 computers further seized, EUR-7.8M asset total).

IC Pattern (BKA-side framing)

  • German lead-prosecution + lead-police architecture stated from the German side: ZIT (Frankfurt am Main) coordinates the prosecutorial side; BKA coordinates the police side.
  • Sequenced international action: Spanish National Police special unit makes the admin arrest in Barcelona on 2025-06-11; Dutch National Police seizes and shuts down server infrastructure in NL data centre; followed by internationally coordinated additional searches in DE and SE.
  • Europol + Eurojust supporting framing preserved verbatim: "von Europol und Eurojust unterstuetzt".
  • L24 verbatim IC quote: "Dieser erneute Ermittlungserfolg von BKA, ZIT und internationalen Partnerdienststellen zeigt, dass die Strafverfolgungsbehoerden in immer schnellerer Abfolge illegale IT-Infrastruktur wie Darknet-Marktplaetze abschalten und mutmassliche Verantwortliche festnehmen koennen." — explicit acknowledgment of cooperation with foreign-LE counterparts.
