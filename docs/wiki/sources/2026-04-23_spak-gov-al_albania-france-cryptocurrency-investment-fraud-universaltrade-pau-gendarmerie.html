주요 발견사항

  • On 2026-04-22 SPAK and the Pau Gendarmerie (France) Financial and Digital Investigations Division jointly conducted searches at a call centre and four residences in Tirana, Albania, in a joint investigation into 'Computer Fraud.'
  • Cooperation was initiated by Eurojust spontaneous information exchange, by which the Pau gendarmerie investigation section transmitted to the Albanian Special Prosecutor's Office data on a French preliminary investigation against the cryptocurrency investment platform UNIVERSAL TRADE (universaltrade.io).
  • 5 Albanian nationals were identified by initials (A.B., E.Sh., B.Z., F.B., G.P.); 1 (G.P.) was placed under detention order by SPAK and the order was executed by the Albanian National Bureau of Investigation (BKH).
  • Recorded fraud: EUR 845,400 against 4 named French complainants and EUR 1.1 million in aggregate against 9 additional identified French complainants over February 2023 – May 2023.
  • Seizures included 4 cold-wallet cryptocurrency wallets, computer units, storage media, mobile phones, a luxury vehicle, suspected forged identity documents, automatic money-counting equipment, business stamps, and currency in USD/CAD/EUR/GBP/ALL totalling approximately 5,000,000 ALL (≈ EUR 50,000).
  • Albanian charging vehicles cited: Articles 28/4 (commission in cooperation), 143/b (computer fraud), 333/a (structured criminal group), and 334 (commission of offences by criminal organisation and structured criminal group) of the Albanian Criminal Code; Albanian criminal proceeding no. 39/20255.
  • French side: Pau Public Prosecutor's Office is conducting preliminary investigations into cryptocurrency investment fraud and aggravated money laundering.
  • Methodology: Agents using fake French identities contacted French citizens and induced cryptocurrency investments through universaltrade.io; victim funds were transferred into cryptocurrency wallets opened by Albanian-resident wallet operators.
  • Domestic Albanian support agencies: Police Oversight Agency (Agjencia e Mbikëqyrjes Policore) and Albanian State Police (Policia e Shtetit).
