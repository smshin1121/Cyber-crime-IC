주요 발견사항

  • FBI San Diego Field Office–led Tai Chang scam-compound investigation produced the third Scam Center Strike Force seizure of a fraud domain (tickmilleas.com) on 2025-12-02, less than three weeks after the Strike Force's November 2025 launch.
  • FBI agents deployed to Bangkok are working alongside the Royal Thai Police in their War Room Task Force to investigate Tai Chang and other Southeast Asian scam compounds — explicit US-Thai LE cooperation.
  • Tai Chang compound is affiliated with the Democratic Karen Benevolent Army (DKBA) and Trans Asia International Holding Group Thailand Company Limited, both designated SDN by Treasury on 2025-11-12 for ties to Chinese organized crime.
  • Strike Force is a DOJ-led collaboration: USAO-DC, DOJ Criminal Division (CCIPS, Fraud Section, MLARS), FBI, US Secret Service, and USAO-RI; public-private cooperation with Google, Apple, and Meta (~2,000 Meta accounts removed).

FBI San Diego — Tai Chang tickmilleas.com domain seizure (2025-12-02)

개요Summary

On 2025-12-02 the FBI San Diego Field Office and the U.S. Attorney's Office for the District of Columbia announced the Scam Center Strike Force's seizure of tickmilleas.com, a third fake-cryptocurrency-investment domain operated from the Tai Chang scam compound (a.k.a. Casino Kosai) in Kyaukhat, Burma. The investigation rests on FBI San Diego casework, but the press release also formalises an embedded FBI presence inside the Royal Thai Police War Room Task Force in Bangkok — a working US-Thai cybercrime-investigation channel against Southeast Asian "pig butchering" compounds.
