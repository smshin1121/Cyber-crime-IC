USD 257 million seized in global police crackdown against online scams - Operation First Light 2024

source 출처

Summary

Operation First Light 2024 was a coordinated global crackdown on online scams from March to May 2024 across 61 countries. 3,950 arrests, 14,643 additional suspects identified, USD 257 million seized, 6,745 bank accounts frozen. Bolstered by AFRIPOL, ASEANAPOL, GCCPOL, and Europol. Utilized INTERPOL's I-GRIP mechanism for cross-border fund recovery.

Key Data

  • Operation: Operation First Light 2024
  • Timeframe: March – May 2024
  • Countries: 61
  • Arrests: 3,950
  • Additional suspects: 14,643
  • Assets seized: USD 257 million ($135M fiat, $2M crypto, ~$120M physical assets)
  • Bank accounts frozen: 6,745
  • Crime types: Phishing, investment fraud, fake online shopping, romance scams, impersonation scams

Relevance

Part of longstanding First Light series. 61-country scope is one of the broadest IC operations. I-GRIP mechanism demonstrated for rapid cross-border fund interception. Namibia trafficking rescue highlights intersection of online scams and forced labor.
