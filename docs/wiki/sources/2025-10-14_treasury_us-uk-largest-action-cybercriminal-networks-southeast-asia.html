주요 발견사항

  • OFAC + FinCEN + UK FCDO + UK OFSI complementary actions on 2025-10-14, described as the largest US-UK action ever targeting cybercriminal networks in Southeast Asia.
  • 146 OFAC sanctions targets within Prince Group Transnational Criminal Organization (Prince Group TCO), a Cambodia-based network led by Cambodian national Chen Zhi (38, Chinese émigré who renounced Chinese citizenship).
  • 117 Prince Group TCO–affiliated businesses + 1 associated official designated; majority offshore shell companies.
  • Concurrent UK FCDO/OFSI sanctions on 12 Prince Group entities and individuals.
  • Foundational US-UK instrument: January 2025 OFAC-OFSI MOU.
  • Criminal indictment unsealed in EDNY against Chen Zhi, coordinated by FBI + USAO-EDNY + UK FCDO.
  • FinCEN Section 311 final rule severs Huione Group (Cambodia-based financial services conglomerate) from US financial system.
  • Huione Group laundered $4 billion+ between August 2021 and January 2025; $37M+ from DPRK cyber heists, $36M+ from VC investment scams, $300M+ from other cyber scams.
  • Underlying scope: $16.6 billion+ in US online-investment-scam losses to date; $10 billion+ in 2024 alone (66% YoY increase) attributable to Southeast Asia–based scam operations including Prince Group TCO.
  • Prince Group TCO predicate offences: pig butchering / virtual currency investment scams, sextortion, money laundering, fraud, corruption, illegal online gambling, human trafficking, torture, and extortion of enslaved workers across at least 10 scam compounds in Cambodia.
  • FBI 2022 Jin Bei Group takedown identified 259 US persons with $18 million in losses — a single sub-network within Prince Group TCO.
  • Prince Group TCO also targets Palau via Grand Legend International Asset Management Co., Ltd. — 99-year lease on Ngerbelas Island, Kayangel, Palau — leveraging Palau-based facilitator Rose Wang (Wang Guodan).
  • Treasury context references prior 2025 actions: September 8 (Burmese + Cambodian cyber-scam facilitators); May 5 (Burmese Warlord Saw Chit Thu); May 29 (Funnull Technology Inc., Philippines-based).
  • Notable individuals targeted: Chen Zhi, Guy Chhay, Lei Bo, Ing Dara, Zhu Zhongbiao (a.k.a. Jack Zhu), Sin Huat Alan Yeo, Zhou Yun (a.k.a. Sandy Zhou), Chen Xiuling (a.k.a. Karen Chen), Wei Qianjiang, Thet Li, Wang Guodan (a.k.a. Rose Wang).
  • Notable entities targeted: Prince Holding Group; Prince Bank Plc; Prince Huan Yu Real Estate Cambodia Group Co. Ltd; Cambodian Heng Xin Real Estate Investment Co. Ltd.; Jin Bei Group Co. Ltd.; Golden Fortune Resorts World Co. Ltd. (Golden Fortune Science and Technology Park, near Chrey Thum, south of Phnom Penh); Warp Data Technology Lao Sole Co. (Laos-based bitcoin mining); Grand Legend International Asset Management Co., Ltd. (Palau).
  • Chen Zhi-controlled holdings span Mauritius, Taiwan, Singapore (managed by Karen Chen).

Source Summary

US Treasury press release SB0278 (2025-10-14) announcing the largest joint US-UK action ever targeting Southeast Asia–based cybercriminal networks: OFAC sanctions on 146 targets within Prince Group TCO (Cambodia, Chen Zhi-led), UK FCDO concurrent sanctions on Prince Group entities and individuals, FinCEN Section 311 final rule severing Cambodia-based Huione Group from the US financial system, and an unsealed EDNY criminal indictment against Chen Zhi. Underlying scope: $16.6 billion+ in cumulative US online-investment-scam losses; $10 billion+ in 2024 alone (66% YoY increase) attributable to Southeast Asia operations.

IC Pattern

This release establishes the most fully-articulated US-UK bilateral cyber-fraud enforcement cooperation pattern in the wiki's pre-existing Asia corpus. Notable features:

  • OFAC + FinCEN + UK FCDO + UK OFSI four-channel coordinated action under a January 2025 OFAC-OFSI MOU (foundational instrument).
  • Concurrent administrative-and-criminal action: OFAC/UK sanctions designations + EDNY criminal indictment unsealed against Chen Zhi the same day.
  • FinCEN Section 311 final rule as a discrete administrative-financial mechanism that severs Huione Group from the US correspondent banking system — a structurally similar pattern to the prior Cryptex sanctions (cryptex-pm2btc-sanctions) but applied here to a Cambodia-based scam-laundering conglomerate rather than a Russia-linked crypto exchange.
  • Cross-laundering nexus: Huione Group laundered both DPRK cyber-heist proceeds ($37M+) and Southeast Asia pig-butchering proceeds ($36M+), bridging the wiki's DPRK-cyber and Southeast-Asia-scam corpora.
  • Scam-compound architecture in Cambodia: 10+ scam compounds, industrial-scale human-trafficking + forced-labor + torture documented as the labor base for the cyberfraud operations.
  • Multi-jurisdiction shell-company web: 100+ shell and holding companies across Mauritius, Taiwan, Singapore, Laos, Palau.
