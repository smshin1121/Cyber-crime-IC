개요Summary

The actual TechXplore article (AFP wire copy) reports on Operation reWired, not Avalanche:

  1. 281 global arrests: 167 in Nigeria, 74 in the US, 18 in Turkey, 15 in Ghana, plus arrests in France, Italy, Japan, Kenya, Malaysia, UK
  2. Financial seizures: ~US$3.7 million globally; $169,850 + 92M naira recovered in Nigeria alone
  3. Operation launch: May 2019 joint FBI-EFCC initiative
  4. Target: BEC fraud (business email compromise), characterised by Nigerian officials as 'Yahoo Yahoo boys'
  5. Operational success narrative: EFCC director quoted describing 'tremendous successes'
