  • US DOJ (Central District of California), 2022-01-19: Evan Jaime Hernandez, 35, of Long Beach, was sentenced by United States District Judge David O.
  • US DOJ (Central District of California), 2022-01-19: Hernandez pleaded guilty in June 2021 to one count of distribution of methamphetamine and one count of laundering of monetary instruments.
  • US DOJ (Central District of California), 2022-01-19: In March 2018, Hernandez sold approximately 895 grams (1.97 pounds) of methamphetamine for $5,000 to an individual whom he thought was a money launderer, but who was working with federal law enforcement at the time.
