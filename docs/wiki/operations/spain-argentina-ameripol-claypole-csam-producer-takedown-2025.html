개요Summary

On 2025-12-23 the Spanish National Police (Policía Nacional) announced a Spain–Argentina coordinated detention against an international CSAM-producer / CSAM-distributor cell. The Spanish leg, led by the Policía Nacional Unidad Central de Ciberdelincuencia (UCC) with the Brigada Provincial de Policía Judicial de Alicante, opened from an NCMEC-tipped intelligence pipeline and arrested a Spanish national in Alicante province who used cloud storage services and instant-messaging applications to store and exchange large volumes of CSAM. Forensic analysis of his devices recovered "ingente cantidad de archivos de pornografía infantil," including newly-created CSAM allegedly depicting a minor to whom an Argentine counterpart claimed personal access.

Spain's UCC then activated the AMERIPOL hemispheric police-cooperation mechanism urgently via the Spanish National Police liaison officer at the AMERIPOL Centro Especializado en Cibercrimen (AC3). Argentine police — specifically the Dirección de Investigaciones Cibercrimen de la Policía de la Provincia de Buenos Aires — identified, located, and arrested the second suspect in the town of Claypole (Buenos Aires province), with a search of his residence. Two arrests resulted from this bilateral action; the investigation is reported as ongoing internationally.

배경Background

The Policía Nacional UCC maintains an upstream international-cooperation channel with the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC), which collects CSAM-related reports from internet-content industry partners and routes leads to the LE agencies of involved countries. From NCMEC-derived information, Spanish investigators identified a user inside Spain who was storing large volumes of CSAM in remote cloud servers — a typology described by the Policía Nacional as "muy común entre pedófilos de todo el mundo" who use cloud platforms to access, share, or simply archive digital CSAM.

This domestic lead matured into the Alicante arrest, after which forensic analysis of the seized devices revealed cross-border exchange chats with a counterpart in Buenos Aires province, Argentina — including images that the UCC's intelligence team classified as newly-created CSAM depicting a minor to whom the Argentine counterpart claimed personal access. That assessment converted the case from a CSAM-distribution investigation into a CSAM-production investigation requiring urgent transnational identification — the operational trigger that activated AMERIPOL/AC3.

법적 근거Legal Framework

The operation is framed in the Policía Nacional release as a bilateral police-to-police investigation routed through the AMERIPOL hemispheric cooperation mechanism. The release does not cite a formal Mutual Legal Assistance Treaty instrument, a Joint Investigation Team, or a specific Spanish or Argentine judicial-control identifier. The named legal-cooperation channels are:

  • The AMERIPOL "mecanismos de cooperación internacional" activated urgently through AC3, with the Spanish National Police liaison officer at AC3 as the routing point;
  • The "estrecha cooperación internacional" between la Policía Judicial de la policía Nacional de España y la Dirección de Investigaciones Cibercrimen de la Policía de la Provincia de Buenos Aires — described in the release as the operational frame within which the Argentine investigation, identification, location, and arrest were conducted;
  • Pre-existing public-private cooperation between the Policía Nacional UCC and NCMEC for CSAM tip-line referral.

The primary release does not enumerate friction points. The implicit operational tension — newly-created CSAM, a minor with claimed in-person access, and a transatlantic suspect chain — is exactly the use case AMERIPOL/AC3 was designed for, and is presented as the justification for the urgent escalation.

교훈Lessons Learned

  • AMERIPOL/AC3 + Spanish National Police liaison officer is, per this primary release, a functional and operationally-fast police-to-police channel for urgent CSAM identifications between EU and Latin American jurisdictions.
