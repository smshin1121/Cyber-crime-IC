개요Summary

The Mobdro illegal streaming takedown was a Spanish action against a mobile application used to stream professional football matches without authorization. Eurojust reports that more than 43 million users worldwide had downloaded the application and that it tapped into audiovisual and streaming services through servers and platforms.

Eurojust coordinated the action day and assisted with European Investigation Orders to Portugal and the Czech Republic and an MLA request to Andorra. Europol provided analytical support, while Spanish, Portuguese, and Czech authorities supported the operational actions.

결과 및 영향Results and Impact

  • One suspect was arrested in Spain.
  • Three other persons were questioned in Spain and Andorra.
  • Four places were searched.
  • Upload routes through Spain, Portugal, and the Czech Republic were blocked or placed under investigation.
  • Portuguese reporting identified a Portugal-hosted server that was deactivated, with data and hardware seized by Portuguese Judicial Police.
