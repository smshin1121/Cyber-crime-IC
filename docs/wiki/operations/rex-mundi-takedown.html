Rex Mundi Hacking Group Takedown

operation 작전 최종 수정: 2026-04-08 출처: 1건

Summary

UK, French, and Thai law enforcement, coordinated by Europol and the Joint Cybercrime Action Taskforce (J-CAT), dismantled the Rex Mundi hacking group that had engaged in large-scale customer data breaches and cyber extortion targeting British companies. The operation resulted in the arrest of 8 suspects, including a key member arrested in Thailand. The investigation ran from May 2017 to May 2018.

Participating Parties

Lead Agencies: - London Metropolitan Police - French National Police High Tech Crime Unit - Thai Royal Police

Coordinating Body: - Europol / J-CAT (Joint Cybercrime Action Taskforce)

Participating Countries (3): United Kingdom, France, Thailand

Results

  • 8 suspects arrested
  • Rex Mundi hacking group dismantled
  • Data breach and cyber extortion operations neutralized
