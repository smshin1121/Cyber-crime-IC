개요Summary

Voice phishing, vishing, and phone-enabled fraud are cyber-enabled fraud schemes in which criminals use telephone, VoIP, call-center, or remote-support channels to impersonate trusted parties and pressure victims into transferring funds, credentials, payment cards, or device access. In this corpus the category covers classic Korean 보이스피싱, European phone-phishing and bank-helpdesk fraud, INTERPOL HAECHI and First Light phone-scam operations, and CBI Operation Chakra call-center cases where the phone or remote-support channel is central to the fraud.

The category remains closely related to online-fraud-ic, bec-ic, money-laundering-ic, and malware-ic. It is kept as a separate SNA target because cross-border phone-enabled fraud has a distinct cooperation pattern: rapid call-center relocation, multilingual impersonation scripts, mule-account networks, and urgent cross-border fund blocking.

한국 상황Korean Context

South Korea is one of the clearest national drivers of international cooperation against voice phishing. The corpus shows three recurring Korean roles:

  • Korea as operation initiator or lead participant in the HAECHI series through knpa-cyber-bureau and INTERPOL channels.
  • Korea as victim jurisdiction for overseas call centers, especially China-based and Southeast Asia-based scam operations.
  • Korea as coordinating state for repatriation or transfer of suspects from scam-center jurisdictions.

Evidence And Data Notes

The workbook sample contains a narrow voice-phishing/vishing/illegal-call-center subset and a broader tech-support call-center subset. For reporting, keep those counts separate:

  • Narrow VOIP/보이스피싱 indicators: rows naming 보이스피싱, 비싱, 불법 전화센터, or 불법 콜센터.
  • Broader phone-enabled fraud indicators: the narrow set plus technical-support scam rows where the public source describes call-center or remote-support impersonation.

The current count reconciliation is maintained in voip-workbook-sna-count-reconciliation-2026-05-06.

This distinction prevents the SNA from silently merging all online fraud into voice phishing while still capturing cases where the phone or call-center channel is the relevant international-cooperation feature.
