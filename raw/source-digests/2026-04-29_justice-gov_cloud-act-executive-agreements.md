## Source Digest

DOJ guidance explains that CLOUD Act executive agreements remove legal restrictions so covered providers in one country can comply with qualifying lawful orders for electronic data issued by the other country. The Office of International Affairs serves as the U.S. Designated Authority under these agreements.
