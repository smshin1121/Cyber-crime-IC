## Source Digest

Singapore's Personal Data Protection Commission explains that the Personal Data Protection Act provides a baseline standard for protection of personal data in Singapore while allowing organizations to collect, use, and disclose data for legitimate and reasonable purposes. It is relevant to cybercrime cooperation where investigations interact with data-protection duties.
