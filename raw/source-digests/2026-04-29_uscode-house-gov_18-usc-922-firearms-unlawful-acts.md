## Source Digest

18 U.S.C. 922 contains federal firearms unlawful-act provisions, including restrictions on interstate commerce, prohibited persons, stolen firearms, altered serial numbers, and machineguns. It appears in cybercrime-adjacent records where online conduct intersects with weapons, trafficking, threats, or proceeds.
