## Source Digest

Senate Executive Report 110-13 covers U.S.-EU mutual legal assistance instruments and includes the U.S.-Netherlands agreement applying the 2003 U.S.-EU MLA framework to the 1981 U.S.-Netherlands criminal-assistance treaty. It supports treating the Netherlands page as an MLA framework node rather than a generic country link.
