## Source Digest

18 U.S.C. 1962 is the core RICO prohibited-activities provision, targeting enterprise conduct involving a pattern of racketeering activity or unlawful debt. In cybercrime cooperation records it is most relevant where prosecutors characterize a cyber-enabled fraud or marketplace group as an enterprise rather than as isolated transactions.
