DEA parallel agency press release announcing the seven-year sentence imposed on Waylan Graves of Rochester, Washington (Thurston County), for repeat drug trafficking with firearms. Graves was arrested twice in a four-month period in 2023 with distributable amounts of fentanyl, heroin, cocaine, and methamphetamine, plus a loaded firearm in his vehicle.
