The FBI announced Operation RapTor, a global coordinated operation involving the United States (130 arrests), Germany (42), United Kingdom (37), France (29), South Korea (19), and several other nations targeting darknet fentanyl/opioid trafficking. U.S. agencies (FBI, DEA, IRS-CI, HSI) worked alongside Europol, the U.K.'s NCA, and law enforcement from over a dozen countries. Operation RapTor resulted in $200M+ in currency/digital asset seizures, 2+ metric tons of drugs, and 144kg of fentanyl.
