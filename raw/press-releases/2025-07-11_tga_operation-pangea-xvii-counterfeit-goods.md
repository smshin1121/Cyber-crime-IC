## Summary-Only Archive

Australia's TGA announced that it facilitated seizure of millions of counterfeit and illegal therapeutic goods as part of INTERPOL's Operation Pangea XVII, described as a global initiative targeting the illegal trade of therapeutic goods.

The release reports that TGA assessed more than 9,500 imports referred by the Australian Border Force and facilitated seizure of more than 5.2 million units of unlawfully imported therapeutic goods, including substandard and falsified products. It also states that TGA works with international partners to share intelligence and resources against the global trade in illicit and counterfeit therapeutic goods.
