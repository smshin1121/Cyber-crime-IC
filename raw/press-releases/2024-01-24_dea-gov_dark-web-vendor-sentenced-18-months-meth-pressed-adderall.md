DEA San Francisco Field Division companion press release for the sentencing of Tony Tan, 28, of San Francisco, to 18 months in prison for operating dark-web vendor site "Adderall123" that sold counterfeit Adderall pills laced with methamphetamine. The release confirms inter-agency cooperation with IRS Criminal Investigation Oakland Field Office and U.S. Postal Inspection Service.
