## Summary-Only Archive

The UK MHRA announced its contribution to Operation Pangea, describing it as an INTERPOL-coordinated global initiative involving around 90 countries and targeting the illegal online sale and distribution of unlicensed and counterfeit medicines and medical devices.

The release reports that MHRA's Criminal Enforcement Unit seized 7.7 million doses of illicit medicines worth approximately GBP 17.2 million, coordinated arrest operations, denied gangs access to almost GBP 1.4 million in criminal profits, and removed 367 illegal websites and social media accounts.
