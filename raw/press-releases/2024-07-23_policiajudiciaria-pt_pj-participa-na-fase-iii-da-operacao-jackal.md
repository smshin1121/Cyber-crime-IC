## Summary

Portugal's Policia Judiciaria said it participated in phase III of Operation Jackal through its national cybercrime unit. The release stated that, in Portugal alone, the operation led to 31 detentions, the seizure of about EUR 1.4 million, the freezing of 50 bank accounts, identification of 48 suspects, and the opening of 28 investigations. It also confirmed the action window of 10 April to 3 July 2024 across 21 countries.
