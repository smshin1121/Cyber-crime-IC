## Summary-Only Archive

The Department of Justice announced a 62-count indictment charging 19 people alleged to have founded, moderated, or operated Shadowcrew, an online marketplace for stolen identity information, counterfeit documents, and stolen credit and debit card numbers. DOJ stated that Shadowcrew had approximately 4,000 members and that the defendants allegedly trafficked in at least 1.7 million stolen credit card numbers.

The release attributes the investigation to the U.S. Secret Service working with the District of New Jersey, DOJ's Computer Crime and Intellectual Property Section, other U.S. Attorneys' offices, and law-enforcement partners. It also records coordinated foreign arrests alongside domestic arrests.
