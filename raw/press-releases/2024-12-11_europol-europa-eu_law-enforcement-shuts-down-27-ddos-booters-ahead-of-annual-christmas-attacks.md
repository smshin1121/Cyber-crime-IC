Operation PowerOFF is an **ongoing, multi-phase international law enforcement operation** targeting DDoS-for-hire (booter/stresser) services. Launched in December 2018 and still active as of December 2024, it is coordinated by Europol's European Cybercrime Centre (EC3) and led operationally by the FBI, with the UK National Crime Agency (NCA) as a core partner. The operation has conducted at least six major enforcement waves, seizing over 142 domains, arresting 16 individuals, and identifying hundreds of users across 15 countries.
