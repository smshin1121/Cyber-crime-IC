## Summary-Only Archive

INTERPOL announced Operation Catalyst, a July to September 2025 operation jointly coordinated with AFRIPOL across Angola, Cameroon, Kenya, Namibia, Nigeria, and South Sudan.

The release states that 83 people were arrested and 160 persons of interest were identified. Arrest categories included 21 terrorism-related crimes, 28 financial fraud and money-laundering cases, 16 cyber-enabled scam cases, and 18 illicit virtual-asset cases. Authorities screened more than 15,000 persons of interest and entities, identified around USD 260 million in fiat and virtual currencies potentially linked to terrorism-related activity, and seized approximately USD 600,000.

The release also describes strategic cyber intelligence and private-sector data support from Binance, Moody's, and Uppsala Security, plus a cryptocurrency-based Ponzi scheme affecting at least 17 countries and more than 100,000 victims.
