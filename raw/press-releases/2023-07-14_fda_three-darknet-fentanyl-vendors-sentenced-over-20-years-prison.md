FDA Office of Criminal Investigations parallel press release confirming the EDVA sentencing of darknet fentanyl vendors Veronica Dittman, Rick Schiffner, and Devin Langer (all of Phoenix, Arizona). Schiffner sentenced to 150 months, Langer 84 months, Dittman 60 months.
