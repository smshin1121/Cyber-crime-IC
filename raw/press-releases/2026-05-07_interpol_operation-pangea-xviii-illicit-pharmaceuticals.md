## Summary-Only Archive

INTERPOL announced Operation Pangea XVIII, a 10-23 March 2026 operation across 90 countries and territories targeting the online sale of illicit pharmaceuticals.

The release reports 269 arrests, 66 criminal groups dismantled, 6.42 million doses seized, an estimated USD 15.5 million in seizures, 392 investigations, 158 search warrants, and approximately 5,700 criminal-linked websites, social media pages, channels, and automated bots disrupted.

INTERPOL also lists participating jurisdictions and support from national health regulatory agencies, OLAF, Europol, the International Narcotics Control Board, the Pharmaceutical Security Institute, the Transnational Alliance to Combat Illicit Trade, UNODC, the Universal Postal Union, the World Customs Organization, and the World Health Organization.
