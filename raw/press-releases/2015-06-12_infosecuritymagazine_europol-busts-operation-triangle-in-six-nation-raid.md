## Summary

Infosecurity Magazine reported that Operation Triangle dismantled a cybercrime ring responsible for roughly USD 6 million in losses through phishing, malware and money-laundering activity. The article restated the six-country arrest sweep and the role of Europol, Eurojust, Italian police, Spanish police and Polish investigators.
