## Summary

TechRadar summarized INTERPOL's HAECHI IV announcement and highlighted that the campaign targeted seven types of cyber-enabled financial fraud across 34 countries. The report emphasized the record 3,500 arrests, the USD 300 million seizure total, the 82,112 blocked accounts and INTERPOL's warning that AI voice cloning and fraudulent NFT sales were emerging scam vectors.
