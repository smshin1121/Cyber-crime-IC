DEA press release announcing that Shawn Ellis, 32, of Renton, Washington, was sentenced in the Western District of Washington to 13 years in prison for trafficking large quantities of fentanyl, methamphetamine, cocaine, and counterfeit Xanax pills while in possession of multiple firearms, body armor, and a homemade silencer. Ellis was a redistributor in a drug trafficking ring connected to Aryan prison gangs, and the case was the result of a March 2023 multi-agency takedown involving 10 SWAT teams and over 350 law enforcement officers across Washington and Arizona.
