## Summary

United States v. Konovolov et al. is the prosecution of 10 members of the GozNym cybercriminal network, which used GozNym malware to steal online banking credentials from over 41,000 victim computers and attempt approximately USD 100 million in theft. The case is *almost certainly* unprecedented in its use of simultaneous parallel prosecutions: while the US indicted 10 defendants in the Western District of Pennsylvania, Bulgaria, Georgia, Moldova, and Ukraine conducted parallel domestic prosecutions of their own nationals.
