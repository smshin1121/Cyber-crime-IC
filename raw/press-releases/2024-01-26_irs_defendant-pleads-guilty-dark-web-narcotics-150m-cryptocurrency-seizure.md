IRS Criminal Investigation (IRS-CI) announced the guilty plea of Banmeet Singh of Haldwani, India, for selling controlled substances (fentanyl, LSD, ecstasy, Xanax, ketamine, tramadol) on dark web marketplaces (Silk Road, Silk Road 2, Alpha Bay, Hansa). Singh was arrested in London in April 2019 and extradited from the UK in 2023. The seizure constituted the largest single cryptocurrency and cash seizure in DEA history at approximately $150M. Investigation involved IRS-CI, DEA, and the UK National Crime Agency (NCA).
