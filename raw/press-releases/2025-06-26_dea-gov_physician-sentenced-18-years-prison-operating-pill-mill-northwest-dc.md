Parallel DEA press release on the sentencing of Dr. Ndubuisi Joseph Okafor, 65, of Upper Marlboro, MD, to 18 years in federal prison for illegally distributing oxycodone and promethazine-with-codeine from his Northwest D.C. clinic. Investigation included undercover FBI operations.
