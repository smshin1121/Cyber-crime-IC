## Summary

DOJ-MD initial charging press release for Sheldon Kennedy (26, Lincoln Nebraska), arrested in Miami on conspiracy to traffic controlled substances and related charges. Federal complaint filed January 31, 2014 in M.D. Md., unsealed February 20, 2014. Allegations include selling cocaine and a pistol to undercover Baltimore agents through Silk Road, plus sale of counterfeit currency to a Maryland buyer. Investigation by HSI Baltimore and ATF.
