## Source Archive Record

This record preserves the source metadata and source-page digest for the
joint press release of the Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Berlin
(Berlin Public Prosecutor's Office, Press Release No. 273 of 2025-11-12)
and the Berlin State Criminal Police Office (Landeskriminalamt Berlin, LKA 253),
announcing the results of a cross-border seven-week action campaign against
the "Enkeltrick" (grandparent / shock-call) phone-fraud phenomenon. The
action ran from 15 September to 31 October 2025 across all 16 German federal
states, with participation of police authorities from Austria, Poland,
Switzerland, Slovakia and the Czech Republic, the German Federal Criminal
Police Office (BKA) and Europol, under the leadership of the EU-funded
"ISF-LUMEN" project coordinated by the LKA Baden-Württemberg.

## Source Digest

- Title: "Gemeinsame Meldung mit Polizei - Länderübergreifender Einsatz zur Bekämpfung des Enkeltricks"
- Publisher: Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Berlin (Berlin Public Prosecutor's Office)
- Press release no.: 273 / 2025-11-12
- Lead investigative authority: Landeskriminalamt Berlin (LKA Berlin), Dezernat 253 ("Schockanrufe / Enkeltrick")
- Prosecutorial lead: Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Berlin, Abteilung 285
- Action period: 2025-09-15 through 2025-10-31, organised in seven "Aktionswochen" (action weeks)
- Framework: EU-funded "ISF-LUMEN" project (Internal Security Fund), coordinated by LKA Baden-Württemberg
- Foreign authorities named: Austria, Poland, Switzerland, Slovakia, Czech Republic; plus Europol and the German BKA
- Verbatim cooperation clause (German): *"Der Verbundeinsatz von Polizeibehörden aus allen 16 Bundesländern, des Bundeskriminalamts, Österreich, Polen, Schweiz, Slowakei und Tschechien sowie von Europol erfolgte im Auftrag der zuständigen Fachabteilungen der jeweils verantwortlichen Staatsanwaltschaften."*
- Arrests: 16 (one 26-year-old female caller; three male callers aged 24, 25, 49; twelve money couriers aged 22-63)
- Call-centre infrastructure dismantled: 3 (Frankfurt am Main, Austria, Warsaw / Poland)
- Cases prevented: 44 attempted "Enkeltrick" cases
- Aggregate damages prevented: EUR 2,007,330
- Modus operandi: emotionally charged shock-call scripts (impersonation of family members in fake accidents, followed by impersonation of police or prosecutors demanding "bail" payments), exploiting telephone-directory targeting of elderly victims
