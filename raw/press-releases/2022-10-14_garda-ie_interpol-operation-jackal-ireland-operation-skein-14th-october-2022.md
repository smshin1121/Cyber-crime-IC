## Summary

An Garda Siochana described the Irish leg of Operation Jackal as Operation Skein and reported that enforcement activity began in mid-August 2022 ahead of a planned international action week starting on 26 September 2022. The release said nine premises were searched in Dublin and Clare, 11 people were arrested under organized crime legislation, and 23 more were arrested for charging, producing 62 money-laundering charges and three organized-crime charges. Garda also explained that the investigation focused on Black Axe-linked business email compromise and romance-fraud proceeds being laundered through Irish bank accounts.
