## Summary

US Immigration and Customs Enforcement (Homeland Security Investigations) press release announcing federal charges against Sheldon Kennedy of Lincoln, Nebraska, for selling drugs and guns on Silk Road. HSI Baltimore investigated; complaint unsealed in M.D. Md. February 20, 2014. Allegations include underground sale of cocaine and a pistol to undercover agents and counterfeit currency sales to a Maryland customer. Kennedy also allegedly mailed a package containing 110 grams of DMT to a buyer in England in May 2012.
