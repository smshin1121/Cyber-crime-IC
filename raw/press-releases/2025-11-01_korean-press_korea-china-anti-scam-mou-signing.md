# 한-중 경찰 보이스피싱·온라인 사기 범죄 대응 공조 양해각서 (2025-11-01)

**Source: KNPA + Chinese Ministry of Public Security joint announcement of bilateral MOU signing on 2025-11-01 in Korea, observed by both heads of state during the APEC Korea 2025 summit (Gyeongju, October 31 – November 1, 2025). Verbatim body captured from Kyunghyang Shinmun (경향신문) coverage and corroborating tier-1 Korean reporting (eDaily, MBC News, Asia Today).**

## Signing Event

- **Signing date**: 2025-11-01
- **Korean signer**: 유재성 경찰청장 직무대행 (Yoo Jae-sung, Acting KNPA Commissioner)
- **Chinese signer**: 다이 빙 (Dai Bing), Chinese Ambassador to the Republic of Korea
- **Ceremony observers**: Both heads of state (Korean President + Chinese President during APEC Korea 2025)
- **Venue**: Korea (in connection with APEC Korea 2025)

## MOU Title

- **Korean**: 「보이스피싱·온라인 사기 범죄 대응 공조 양해각서」
- **English (working translation)**: Memorandum of Understanding on Cooperation in Response to Voice Phishing and Online Fraud Crime
- **Counterparties**: 대한민국 경찰청 (KNPA) ↔ 중국 공안부 (Chinese Ministry of Public Security, MPS)

## Scope of Cooperation (verbatim summary)

The MOU commits the Korean and Chinese police authorities, within the bounds of each country's domestic laws, to:

1. **Information sharing on scam-crime bases** — sharing intelligence about Southeast Asian scam compounds (especially Cambodia) and adjacent crime infrastructure.
2. **Evidence collection, exchange, and analysis** — joint evidence handling within legal limits.
3. **Joint operations and cooperative investigations** — to track and apprehend criminals.
4. **Victim rescue, protection, and repatriation** — supporting Korean and Chinese nationals victimised in Southeast Asia.
5. **Financial tracking and freezing of scam-crime proceeds** — asset tracing and freezing.

The MOU explicitly identifies the focus areas as voice phishing (보이스피싱), online scams (온라인 사기), employment fraud (취업 사기), and unlawful detention (불법 감금) occurring in Cambodia and Southeast Asia.

## Korea-China Joint Response Council (한·중 공동대응 협의체)

The MOU formally establishes a 「한·중 공동대응 협의체」 (Korea-China Joint Response Council) for scam-crime cooperation. Designated departments inside the KNPA and the Chinese Ministry of Public Security will operate the standing cooperation network.

## Key Quote

> "보이스피싱과 온라인 사기 범죄는 국경을 넘는 대표적 민생 침해 범죄"
> ("Voice phishing and online fraud are representative transnational crimes harming citizens' livelihoods")
