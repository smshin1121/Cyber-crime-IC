The U.S. Attorney's Office for the District of Arizona announced guilty pleas of Solomon Ekunke Okpe (Nigerian national) and Johnson Uke Obogo for BEC, work-from-home, check-cashing, romance, and credit card scams targeting U.S. victims. Okpe was arrested in Malaysia at U.S. request and detained for over two years contesting extradition. The case involved cooperation with Malaysian and Nigerian authorities.
