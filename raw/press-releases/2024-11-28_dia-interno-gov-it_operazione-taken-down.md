# OPERAZIONE TAKEN DOWN

The Italian Direzione Investigativa Antimafia page names the action as Operazione Taken Down and describes it as a Catania-directed enforcement action against transnational audiovisual piracy and illegal IPTV infrastructure.

The notice states that Italian State Police and Postal Police, with Eurojust and Europol coordination, carried out searches and seizures across Italy and several other jurisdictions. It records 89 searches in 15 Italian regions, 14 additional searches in the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, Sweden, Switzerland, Romania, Croatia, and China, and 102 persons under investigation.

The release describes illegal IPTV infrastructure serving more than 22 million users, the seizure of more than 2,500 illegal channels and servers, an alleged turnover above EUR 250 million per month, and international working tables that enabled Croatian police to execute 11 custodial orders.
