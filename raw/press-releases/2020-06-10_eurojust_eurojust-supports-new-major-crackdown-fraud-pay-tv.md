# Eurojust supports new major crackdown on fraud with pay TV

Eurojust reported a June 2020 action coordinated through Eurojust against a criminal organisation offering illegal access to streaming services, subscription television channels, and films. Authorities in Spain, Denmark, Sweden, and Germany arrested 11 suspects, while 50 servers were taken down in France, Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, Denmark, Sweden, the Czech Republic, Poland, and Spain.

The source reports that the network operated mainly from Spain, served an estimated 2 million subscribers worldwide, used websites and servers across the EU and third countries, and generated proceeds estimated at least EUR 15 million. Europol provided analytical support and joined Eurojust in organising a meeting to set up the action day.
