U.S. Secret Service press release on the extradition of Maksim Silnikau (a.k.a. Maksym Silnikov, "J.P. Morgan," "xxx," "lansky"), a 38-year-old Belarusian-Ukrainian dual national, from Poland to face indictments in the District of New Jersey and Eastern District of Virginia. USSS coordinated the cybercrime investigation of the Angler exploit kit malvertising scheme (October 2013–March 2022) and Ransom Cartel ransomware (2021–onward). Co-defendants Volodymyr Kadariya and Andrei Tarasov remain at large.
