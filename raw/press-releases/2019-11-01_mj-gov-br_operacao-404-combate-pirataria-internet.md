# Operacao 404 combate pirataria na internet

Brazil's Ministry of Justice and Public Security reported the launch of Operation 404 on 1 November 2019 under the coordination of Seopi/MJSP. The official balance described 136 sites and 100 illegal-content streaming applications suspended, 30 search-and-seizure warrants, and eight in-flagrante arrests.

The source states that the anti-piracy action counted on collaboration from ANCINE, CNCP, intellectual-property protection associations, the U.S. Embassy in Brazil through U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement's Brasilia attache, and the U.S. Department of Justice.
