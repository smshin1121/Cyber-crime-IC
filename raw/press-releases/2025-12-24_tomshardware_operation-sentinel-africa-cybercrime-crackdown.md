## Summary

Tom's Hardware summarized Operation Sentinel as a 19-country African cybercrime crackdown focused on BEC, ransomware and digital extortion. The article repeated the 574 arrests, USD 3 million recovered, 6,000 malicious links taken down and six ransomware variants decrypted, while giving extra narrative detail on the Senegal, Ghana and Benin cases.
