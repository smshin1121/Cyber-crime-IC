# Operation Cyber Guardian (사이버 가디언 작전) — Asia 6-Country CSAM Crackdown

## Summary

Six Asian countries/regions — South Korea, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, and Hong Kong — conducted a coordinated 5-week operation dubbed "Operation Cyber Guardian" (사이버 가디언 작전) targeting the production, distribution, and possession of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) through information communication networks. The operation ran from February 24 to March 28, 2025, and was initiated by the Singapore Police Force.

## Key Facts

- **Operation name:** Cyber Guardian (사이버 가디언)
- **Period:** February 24 – March 28, 2025 (5 weeks)
- **Initiated by:** Singapore Police Force
- **Lead investigating agency (Korea):** National Investigation Headquarters (국가수사본부, 국수본)
- **Participating countries/regions:** South Korea, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Hong Kong
