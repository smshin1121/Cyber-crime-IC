## Summary-Only Archive

The FBI described the Gameover Zeus botnet disruption as a multinational effort with international law-enforcement, private-sector, and U.S. government partners. The source explains the peer-to-peer botnet structure, losses over USD 100 million, related CryptoLocker command-and-control seizure, and use of CERTs and private partners for remediation.
