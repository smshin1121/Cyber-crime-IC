## Summary-Only Archive

The FBI announced Operation Card Shop as a two-year undercover carding investigation that used the Carder Profit forum to identify cybercriminals trafficking stolen payment-card data, identity information, counterfeit documents, malware, and fraud services.

The release states that the initial coordinated action involved 13 countries including the United States, produced 24 arrests, more than 30 searches, and more than 30 interviews, and helped prevent more than USD 205 million in potential losses.
