## Summary-Only Archive

The FBI story announced Operation Phish Phry as a major cyber-fraud takedown involving charges in the United States and Egypt. It described nearly 100 charged people, phishing of U.S. bank customers, and about USD 1.5 million in fraudulent transfers.
