## Summary

ESET's WeLiveSecurity summarized Operation Pleiades as a major offensive against DD4BC, stating that one main target was arrested, another detained, and an extensive amount of evidence seized. The article also noted that the operation was initiated by Austria, supported by Europol EC3 and J-CAT, and focused on a group that had expanded from online gambling extortion into financial services and entertainment.

## Key Data Points

- **Operation name:** Operation Pleiades
- **Initiating country:** Austria
- **Arrest output:** 1 arrest, 1 detention
- **Supporting structure:** Europol EC3 and J-CAT
- **Threat sectors:** online gambling, financial services, entertainment
