DOJ follow-up press release announcing that all eight extradited distributors of ANOM hardened encrypted devices have pleaded guilty to racketeering conspiracy in the Southern District of California, of the 17 foreign nationals indicted in 2021. Operation Trojan Shield was the underlying FBI-led international encrypted device sting.
