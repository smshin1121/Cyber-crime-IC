## Summary-Only Archive

INTERPOL announced Operation Eclipse, a February 2025 to January 2026 operation across nine countries in Central America, North America, and the Caribbean targeting production and distribution of child sexual abuse and exploitation material.

The release reports 60 arrests, 65 child victims identified and safeguarded, support from UNODC and Child Rescue Coalition, reassessment of 57 existing INTERPOL Notices, 12 individuals located and apprehended from that review, and country examples involving Panama, the Dominican Republic, and Costa Rica.
