## Summary-Only Archive

The FBI announced Operation Ghost Click as a two-year investigation that dismantled an international cyber ring using DNSChanger malware to infect millions of computers and manipulate the online advertising ecosystem. The release reports six Estonian nationals arrested and charged and credits the FBI, NASA OIG, Estonian Police and Border Guard Board, and Dutch National Police National High Tech Crime Unit.

The source also records the temporary clean DNS remediation approach used to prevent infected victims from losing Internet access before they could remove the malware or restore their DNS settings.
