# 한국-캄보디아 공동 전담반 MOU 서명 (2025-11-10)

**Source: 외교부 + 경찰청 announcement of Korea-Cambodia Joint Task Force for Korean nationals (코리아전담반) MOU signing in Phnom Penh on 2025-11-10. Korea.kr URL is connection-reset on TLS-spoof and WebFetch; verbatim body captured from MOFA reporting reproduced via Korean tier-1 search results.**

## Signing Event

- **Signing date**: 2025-11-10 (Phnom Penh, Cambodia)
- **Korean side**:
  - Foreign Minister 조현 (Cho Hyun) — visited Cambodia 2025-11-09 to 2025-11-11; presence at signing.
  - Acting KNPA Commissioner 유재성 (Yoo Jae-sung; "유 직무대행") — MOU signer for Korea.
- **Cambodian side**:
  - Cambodian Police Commissioner 써 텟 (Sar Thet) — MOU signer for Cambodia.
  - Cambodian Interior Minister 써 소카 (Sar Sokha) — presence at signing.
  - Cambodian Foreign Minister Prak Sokhonn (쁘락 소콘) — luncheon meeting with Cho Hyun.
  - Cambodian Prime Minister Hun Manet (훈 마넷) — Cho Hyun courtesy call.
- **Antecedent**: 2025-10-27 Korea-Cambodia summit in Malaysia (parent agreement; the November 10 signing is the follow-up implementation).

## MOU Title and Korean-Side Description

- **Korean name**: 「대한민국 국민을 위한 한국-캄보디아 공동 전담반」 (Korea-Cambodia Joint Task Force for Korean nationals)
- **Common short name**: 코리아 전담반 / 코리아전담반 (Korea-dedicated Squad)
- **Operating principle**: "우리 경찰과 캄보디아 경찰이 함께 근무하는 체제" — Korean and Cambodian police working together as a single staff.

## Scope of Cooperation (verbatim from announcement)

The Joint Task Force handles "the entire process from report receipt to rescue, investigation, and suspect repatriation" (신고 접수부터 구조·수사·피의자 송환까지 전 과정) for cases involving Korean nationals.

The substantive crime-type focus is:
- Protection of Korean nationals abroad (재외국민 보호) — particularly in Cambodian scam-compound contexts.
- Online scam crimes against Korean victims (온라인 스캠).
- Other transnational crime (초국가범죄).

## Operational Detail (per Korean tier-2 reporting cross-reference)

Within roughly the first two months of operations (2025-11-10 to early January 2026), the Joint Task Force reportedly comprised **19 officers (12 Cambodian + 7 Korean)** and conducted **7 rounds of joint operations**, resulting in:
- 135 suspects arrested in the field.
- 3 Korean victims rescued from scam-compound captivity.

## Cooperation Mechanism Significance

This MOU is the foundational legal-framework instrument for the 코리아전담반 mechanism that produced the Cambodia tranche (42 of 73) of the 2026-04-27 [[korea-cambodia-philippines-73-extradition-2026|Korea-Cambodia-Philippines 73-person extradition operation]]. The whole-government 「초국가범죄 특별대응TF」 frame (KNPA + 외교부 + 법무부 + 국가정보원) coordinating the 73-person extradition operates downstream of this bilateral MOU.
