# Eurojust supports Spanish action against illegal streaming of football matches

Eurojust reported a Spanish action against illegal football streaming through the Mobdro mobile application. The source states that more than 43 million users worldwide had downloaded the app and that the app used servers and platforms to tap illegally into regular audiovisual signals.

The action involved Spain, Portugal, the Czech Republic, and assistance to or from Andorra. Eurojust supported European Investigation Orders and MLA, while Europol provided analytical support. Spanish authorities arrested one suspect and questioned three other persons.
