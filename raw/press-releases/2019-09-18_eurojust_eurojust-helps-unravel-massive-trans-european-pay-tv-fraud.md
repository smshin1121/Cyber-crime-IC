# Eurojust helps unravel massive trans-European pay-TV fraud

Eurojust reported a 18 September 2019 action day against an international criminal network selling illegal pay-TV access. The public record describes investigations led by prosecutors in Naples and Rome with support from authorities in Bulgaria, Germany, Greece, France, the Netherlands, and Eurojust.

The action disrupted illegal IPTV signals across Europe, took more than 200 servers offline, blocked over 150 PayPal accounts, and identified 22 suspects. Eurojust describes the underlying activity as illegal rebroadcasting and sale of pay-per-view products and services through special servers that generated illegal IPTV signals.
