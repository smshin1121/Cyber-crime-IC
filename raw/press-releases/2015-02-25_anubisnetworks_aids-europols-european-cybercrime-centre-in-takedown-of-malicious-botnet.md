## Summary

AnubisNetworks reported that it worked with Europol, Microsoft and Symantec in the Ramnit takedown. The company described the botnet as infecting 3.2 million computers worldwide and emphasized the shutdown of command-and-control servers and the sinkholing of criminal infrastructure.
