US Secret Service press release announcing the nine-year federal sentence imposed on Sumit Garg, 33, of Seattle, for an extensive cyberstalking campaign against a former roommate, her family, boyfriend, and the police and prosecutors involved in the case. The Secret Service was a parallel federal agency credited in the investigation alongside the FBI, with U.S. District Judge John C. Coughenour calling Garg's conduct "unparalleled" in the district.
