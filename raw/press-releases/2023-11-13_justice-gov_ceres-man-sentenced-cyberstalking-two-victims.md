DOJ sentencing press release for Kevin James Strutz, the Ceres man previously charged and pleaded guilty to cyberstalking two female victims. Strutz was sentenced to three years and four months in prison followed by three years of supervised release. The case involved covert recording of an Airbnb guest and threats to coerce sexual content; a second victim received 15 handwritten letters with sexual threats over a multi-month period.
