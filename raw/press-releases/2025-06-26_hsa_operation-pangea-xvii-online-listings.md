## Summary-Only Archive

Singapore's Health Sciences Authority announced that it removed 1,288 illegal health product listings from local e-commerce and social media platforms during Operation Pangea XVII, held from 16 December 2024 to 16 May 2025.

The release describes Pangea XVII as a global Internet-based enforcement action coordinated by INTERPOL to combat pharmaceutical crime with participation from 90 countries. HSA warned 732 sellers, blocked 16 overseas websites selling unregistered contact lenses to Singapore consumers, and described extensive collaboration with INTERPOL and overseas regulatory authorities.
