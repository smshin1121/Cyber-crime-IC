## Summary

The Hacker News reported that Romanian police arrested five people suspected of infecting tens of thousands of computers across Europe and the United States by spreading CTB Locker and Cerber. The article described Operation Bakovia as a joint action involving Europol, the FBI, and Romanian, Dutch, and UK authorities, with six houses raided in eastern Romania. It also stressed that the suspects were distributors, not malware developers, and summarized the ransomware-as-a-service context for both malware families.

## Key Data Points

- **Operation name:** Operation Bakovia
- **Arrests:** 5
- **Houses raided:** 6
- **Countries referenced:** Romania, Netherlands, United Kingdom, United States
- **Operational characterization:** ransomware distributor takedown rather than developer arrest

## Relevance to IC
