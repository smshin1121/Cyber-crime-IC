## Summary-Only Archive

DOJ announced a multinational action against Gameover Zeus and CryptoLocker, including criminal charges against Evgeniy Bogachev, court-authorized substitute servers, CryptoLocker server seizures, and victim-remediation coordination through US-CERT, foreign partners, and private-sector companies.

The release identifies law-enforcement participants from Australia, the Netherlands, Europol EC3, Germany, France, Italy, Japan, Luxembourg, New Zealand, Canada, Ukraine, and the United Kingdom, in addition to the United States.
