# Detido em Lisboa lider de organizacao internacional de criptoativos

**Source: Policia Judiciaria (Portugal) press release, published 25-26 September 2025.**
**Operation date: 17 September 2025.**
**Location: Lisbon, Portugal.**

## Summary

The Portuguese Policia Judiciaria (PJ), through the Unidade Nacional de Combate a Corrupcao (UNCC) of the Direccao Nacional, conducted a joint action day on 17 September 2025 against an international criminal organisation engaged in cryptocurrency investment fraud and money laundering. The action was conducted jointly with judicial and law-enforcement authorities of Spain (Corpo Nacional de Policia), Bulgaria, and Lithuania, supported by Europol and Eurojust.

## Arrest and Charges

One leading suspect was arrested in Lisbon on the basis of a European Arrest Warrant issued by the requesting Spanish authorities. The suspect is presented to Lisbon Court of Appeal (Tribunal da Relacao de Lisboa) for surrender proceedings. The charges, qualified under Portuguese law in the requesting Spanish proceedings, are criminal association (associacao criminosa), money laundering (branqueamento), and qualified fraud (burla qualificada).

## Fraud Scheme (verbatim summary)

The perpetrators built the conviction in potential investors -- private citizens -- that they could obtain great profitability in virtual assets (cryptocurrency) through digital platforms. In reality, the funds were transferred to Lithuanian accounts controlled by third parties whose intent was theft of the deposited capital. Money flowed from Lithuania onwards to Portugal, Spain, the United Kingdom, and Bulgaria.

## Searches and Seizures in Portugal (verbatim)

- Nine searches conducted in Portugal.
- Properties seized: 6 (high-value real estate).
- High-end vehicles seized: 3.
- Motorcycle seized: 1.
- Watches seized: 6.
- Paintings seized: 15.
- Cryptocurrency seized: approximately EUR 20,000.

## Financial Impact

- Portuguese victims: losses exceeding EUR 1 million.
- Victims also identified in Spain, the United Kingdom, and Bulgaria.

## International Cooperation Partners (verbatim, as named in the PJ release)

- **Spain:** Corpo Nacional de Policia (Spanish National Police) -- requesting authority for the European Arrest Warrant.
- **Bulgaria:** national law-enforcement and judicial authorities participating in the joint action day.
- **Lithuania:** national law-enforcement and judicial authorities participating in the joint action day (Lithuanian bank accounts identified as the principal proceeds-laundering route).
- **Europol:** operational and analytical support.
- **Eurojust:** judicial coordination of the cross-border action.

## Case Status

Under investigation at DCIAP -- Departamento Central de Investigacao e Accao Penal (Central Department of Criminal Investigation and Criminal Prosecution), Lisbon.
