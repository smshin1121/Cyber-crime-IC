# Eurojust coordinates action in Italy and ten other countries, taking down over 5,550 computer servers

Eurojust reported a November 2020 action day in the continuing pay-TV and film-streaming piracy investigation associated with the Italian authorities. The action took down more than 5,550 servers used to transmit and store television broadcasts, live sports games, and films without authorization.

The source lists affected infrastructure or assistance in Italy, Bulgaria, France, Germany, Lithuania, Malta, Romania, Slovenia, Sweden, the Netherlands, and Greece. One alleged leader was arrested, equipment and payment instruments were seized, and Eurojust coordinated European Investigation Orders and searches.
