USAO-EDCA sentencing press release for Jason Keith Arnold, 46, of Chandler, Arizona. Sentenced to 7 years 3 months for conspiring to distribute heroin and methamphetamine via 'TheSickness' and 'SicknessVersion2' vendor accounts on Dream Market. Operation grossed over $350,000 in cryptocurrency across 3,000+ transactions.
