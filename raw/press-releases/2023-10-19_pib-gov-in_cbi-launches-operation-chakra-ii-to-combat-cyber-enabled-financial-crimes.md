# CBI launches Operation CHAKRA-II to combat and dismantle organized cyber enabled financial crimes

The Press Information Bureau release said CBI launched Operation Chakra-II against transnational organized cyber-enabled financial crime infrastructure in India. It reported 76 searches across multiple Indian states, five registered cases, nine call centers tied to international tech-support fraud, and seizures including phones, laptops or hard disks, two server images, SIM cards, pen drives, frozen bank accounts, and email-account data.

The release is the primary source for the broader cooperation metadata. It said CBI worked with national and international agencies and private-sector companies, and specifically named the FBI, INTERPOL Cyber Crime Directorate and IFCACC, the UK National Crime Agency, Singapore Police Force, and Germany's BKA as counterparts for further leads.
