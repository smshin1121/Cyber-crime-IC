FBI Pittsburgh Field Office press release announcing the deportation of Ryan Andrew Gustafson (aka Jack Farrel, aka Willy Clock) from the Republic of Uganda to face federal charges in the Western District of Pennsylvania for an international counterfeit currency operation. Documents over $1.4 million in counterfeit Federal Reserve Notes seized worldwide. Gustafson allegedly created Community-X dark web site dedicated to counterfeit currency manufacturing and distribution.
