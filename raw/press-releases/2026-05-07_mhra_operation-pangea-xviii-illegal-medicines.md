## Summary-Only Archive

The UK Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency reported the UK component of Operation Pangea XVIII. MHRA and UK Border Force seized more than 2 million doses of illicit medicines worth about GBP 4.6 million during the 10-23 March 2026 international operation.

The release says MHRA also disrupted online supply routes, targeting websites, social media accounts and posts, and listings on online marketplaces. It describes Pangea as an INTERPOL-coordinated global initiative involving police, customs, health regulators, and private-sector partners across approximately 90 countries and territories.
