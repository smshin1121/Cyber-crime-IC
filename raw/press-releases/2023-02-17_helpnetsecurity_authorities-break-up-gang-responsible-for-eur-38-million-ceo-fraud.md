## Summary

Help Net Security summarized the Europol-supported investigation into the Franco-Israeli CEO fraud gang, stating that five action days between January 2022 and January 2023 led to eight house searches, eight arrests, and about EUR 5.5 million in seizures. The article also broke down the recovered assets by Portugal, Hungary, Croatia, Spain, and cryptocurrency, and described the two core French victim episodes that linked the case together.

## Key Data Points

- **Arrests:** 8
- **House searches:** 8
- **Action days:** 5
- **Total seizures:** about EUR 5.5 million
- **Laundering route:** Europe -> China -> Israel
- **Victim profile:** France-based companies targeted through CEO impersonation and fake-lawyer pretexts
