ICE/HSI parallel agency press release announcing the seven-year federal sentence imposed on Matthew Witters of Seattle for selling thousands of doses of fentanyl on the AlphaBay and Dream Market darknet marketplaces between 2015 and 2017. HSI was the lead federal investigative agency. $1.1 million in cryptocurrency, cash, and other proceeds were seized.
