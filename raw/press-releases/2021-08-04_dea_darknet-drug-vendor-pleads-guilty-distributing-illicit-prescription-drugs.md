DEA parallel press release on Cullen Roberts (Duluth, GA), 23, who pleaded guilty August 3, 2021 to distributing prescription drugs through the dark web under the username "Pillpusher" on the Yellow Brick Market. Roberts faces up to 20 years; arrest occurred December 2, 2020 after FBI controlled buys.
