USAO N.D. Okla. extradition press release announcing that Sunday Daniel Ganyo, a Nigerian national, was extradited from South Africa to face federal cyber fraud charges in the Northern District of Oklahoma. The case involves an alleged BEC scheme targeting a Tulsa technology company in which more than $400,000 of computer equipment was fraudulently ordered and redirected toward South Africa.
