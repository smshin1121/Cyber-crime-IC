## Summary

In remarks at the INTERPOL Global Cybercrime Conference, Singapore’s Ministry of Home Affairs cited Operation Killer Bee as a recent example of regional cooperation against scam syndicates. The speech states that Singapore took part in the 11-country operation and that it led to the arrest of three malware-fraud suspects in May 2022.
