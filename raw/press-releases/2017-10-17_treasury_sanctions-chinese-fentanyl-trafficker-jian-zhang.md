Treasury press release announcing OFAC sanctions against Jian Zhang (Shanghai) and his organization on the same day as the DOJ indictment. Treasury identified Zhang as a Significant Foreign Narcotics Trafficker under the Foreign Narcotics Kingpin Designation Act, freezing U.S.-jurisdiction assets and prohibiting U.S. persons from transactions. The release confirms cooperation among Treasury, DOJ, DEA, and the State Department.
