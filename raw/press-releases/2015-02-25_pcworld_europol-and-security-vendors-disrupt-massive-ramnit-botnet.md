## Summary

PCWorld reported that European law-enforcement agencies and security vendors disrupted Ramnit’s command infrastructure and described the malware’s banking-trojan and credential-stealing capabilities. The article also underscored the variety of information Ramnit could steal, including banking credentials, FTP passwords, session cookies and personal files.
