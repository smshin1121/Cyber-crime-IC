# Operacao internacional contra pirataria tira do ar 675 sites e 14 aplicativos de streaming

Brazil's Ministry of Justice and Public Security reported the seventh phase of Operation 404 on 19 September 2024. The phase targeted internet intellectual-property crime and removed audiovisual content, blocked or suspended 675 sites and 14 illegal streaming applications, and produced 30 search and seizure warrants and nine arrests.

The source states that the Cyber Operations Laboratory within the Ministry's public-security structure coordinated the action with Brazilian state civil police and prosecutors. It also lists international law-enforcement and intellectual-property partners from Argentina, the United States, Paraguay, Peru, and the United Kingdom.
