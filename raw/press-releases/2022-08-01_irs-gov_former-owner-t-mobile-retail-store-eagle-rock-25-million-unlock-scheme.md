IRS Criminal Investigation case summary recording the August 1, 2022 federal jury conviction of Argishti Khudaverdyan, of Burbank, on 14 felonies tied to a $25 million scheme to illegally unlock and unblock cellphones from August 2014 to June 2019. The summary lists IRS-CI's Western Area Cyber Crime Unit, the U.S. Secret Service Cyber Fraud Task Force, the FBI, LAPD, the LA County DA's Office and the California Highway Patrol as investigating agencies, and confirms parallel federal-agency credit beyond the USAO press release.
