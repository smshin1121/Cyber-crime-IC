DEA press release announcing Melissa Scanlan's 13-year (160-month) federal prison sentence for "Drug Llama" dark web fentanyl trafficking and international money laundering. Confirms DEA lead-investigative role, USPS/HSI partnership, and conspiracy charges including fentanyl distribution resulting in death. Companion parallel-agency confirmation for the SDIL DOJ press release.
