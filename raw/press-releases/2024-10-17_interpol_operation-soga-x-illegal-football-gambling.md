## Summary-Only Archive

INTERPOL announced Operation SOGA X, a June to July 2024 operation across 28 countries and territories targeting illegal online football gambling during the UEFA 2024 European Football Championship.

The release reports more than 5,100 arrests, more than USD 59 million recovered, tens of thousands of illegal websites shut down, more than 650 human-trafficking victims rescued in the Philippines, an online gambling ring in Vietnam generating about USD 800,000 in daily transactions, Thai seizures worth more than USD 9 million, and a Greek illegal betting ring using VPNs, fake or mule accounts, and forged identity documents.

The release names the participating jurisdictions as Australia, Belgium, Brunei, Czech Republic, Greece, Guinea Bissau, Hong Kong (China), Hungary, India, Italy, Lebanon, Macao (China), Maldives, Malta, Namibia, Philippines, Portugal, Republic of Korea, San Marino, Singapore, South Africa, Spain, Sri Lanka, Switzerland, Thailand, Trinidad and Tobago, Türkiye, and Vietnam.
