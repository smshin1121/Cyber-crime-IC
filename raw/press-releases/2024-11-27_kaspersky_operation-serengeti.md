## Summary

Kaspersky's post on Operation Serengeti said the operation dismantled 134,089 malicious infrastructures and networks tied to ransomware, BEC, digital extortion and online scams, and identified more than 35,000 victims. It described the company's role as sharing threat-actor intelligence, ransomware and malware data, and indicators of compromise relevant to African investigations, including data on malware families such as Grandoreiro, LockBit, Rhysida and Medusa.
