U.S. Treasury (OFAC) announced sanctions on Russia-based Evil Corp members including Maksim Yakubets and Igor Turashev, in coordination with the United Kingdom and Australia. Treasury identified Evil Corp as having operated the Dridex/Bugat malware campaign that caused over $100M in losses across 40+ countries. Tri-lateral sanctions action exemplified U.S./UK/AU cooperation on cybercrime financial designations.
