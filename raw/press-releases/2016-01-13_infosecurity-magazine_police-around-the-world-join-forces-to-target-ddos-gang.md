## Summary

Infosecurity Magazine reported that a closely coordinated international operation led to the arrest of a key target linked to DD4BC. The article said the action took place on 15 and 16 December with authorities from Austria, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Germany, and the United Kingdom alongside Europol, and emphasized that the UK's Metropolitan Police Cyber Crime Unit identified key members of the group in Bosnia.

## Key Data Points

- **Operation name:** Operation Pleiades
- **Action dates:** 2015-12-15 to 2015-12-16
- **Arrest output:** one key arrest and one detained suspect
- **Operational participants:** Austria, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Germany, United Kingdom, Europol
- **Investigative trigger:** UK MPCCU identification of Bosnian suspects
