IRS-CI parallel-agency release confirming federal indictment of Amitoj Kapoor (29) and Siddharth Lillaney (29), both of Glastonbury, CT, on a 45-count indictment returned in New Haven on February 3, 2026 — covering wire fraud, aggravated identity theft, and money laundering. Defendants allegedly used PII of approximately 3,000 identity-theft victims (purchased on the darknet and via Telegram) to open FanDuel and other online-gambling accounts, exploiting promotional bonuses to net roughly $3 million since 2021.
