## Summary

Latvia is a small EU and NATO member state that ratified the Budapest Convention in 2007. Cybercrime cooperation is channeled primarily through EU mechanisms (European Investigation Order, Europol) and the Council of Europe framework. The State Police operates a dedicated cybercrime combating unit and serves as the 24/7 contact point under Article 35 of the Budapest Convention.
