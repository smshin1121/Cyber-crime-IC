DOJ OPA press release announcing the sentencing of two senior Infraud Organization administrators on March 19, 2021. Sergey Medvedev (Russia) was sentenced to 10 years and Marko Leopard (North Macedonia) to 5 years, both for racketeering conspiracy. Confirms updated total loss figure of $568 million, IRS-CI / HSI / FBI participation, and the District of Nevada venue. Direct sentencing follow-up to the 2018-02-07 indictment.
