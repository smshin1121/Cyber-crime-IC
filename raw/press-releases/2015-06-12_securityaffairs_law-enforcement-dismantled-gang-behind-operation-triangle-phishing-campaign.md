## Summary

Security Affairs reported that Operation Triangle led to 49 arrests, including country-level arrest numbers in Italy, Spain, Poland and Belgium. The article focused on the group’s phishing and email-account intrusion methods and noted that the operation targeted a criminal organization composed largely of Nigerians, Cameroonians and Spaniards.
