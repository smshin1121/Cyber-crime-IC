# Pressemitteilung 3/2026 vom 23.01.2026

## Nach Auslieferung aus Costa Rica: Zentralstelle Cybercrime Bayern erhebt Anklage gegen mutmasslichen Kopf eines internationalen Betrugsnetzwerks

(English working title: After Extradition from Costa Rica: Central Cybercrime Unit Bavaria Indicts Alleged Head of International Fraud Network)

## Key facts extracted

- **Issuing authority**: Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Bamberg — Zentralstelle Cybercrime Bayern (Central Cybercrime Unit Bavaria), reporting jointly with Kriminalpolizeiinspektion (KPI) Neu-Ulm.
- **Indictment filed**: October 2025 at the Cyber-Wirtschaftsstrafkammer des Landgerichts Bamberg (Cyber-Economic Criminal Chamber of the Bamberg Regional Court).
- **Defendant**: 42-year-old male, dual Israeli and Portuguese national; name not disclosed in the public release per German practice.
- **Charge**: Gewerbs- und bandenmäßiger Betrug (commercial and organized-gang fraud) — operating a transnational online-investment-fraud / cryptocurrency-scam call-centre network.
- **Damages**:
  - Germany: at least €4.5 million from German victims.
  - Worldwide: estimated at least €21 million across multiple jurisdictions.
- **Extradition source country**: Costa Rica.
- **Extradition date**: July 2025 — defendant surrendered to German custody by Costa Rican judicial authorities.
- **Cooperating LE / judicial agencies named in the release**:
  - Germany — Kriminalpolizeiinspektion Neu-Ulm (Bavarian State Police, Neu-Ulm Criminal Police Inspectorate)
  - Germany — Zentralstelle Cybercrime Bayern at Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Bamberg
  - Kosovo — kosovarische Ermittlungsbehörden (Kosovo investigative authorities; joint Action Day in Pristina, March 2021)
  - Costa Rica — costaricanische Justizbehörden (Costa Rican judicial authorities; executed extradition request)
  - Israel — israelische Behörden (Israeli authorities; provided assistance regarding the suspect, an Israeli national)
- **Verbatim cooperation quote (German, primary source)**:
  > "Die Ermittlungen der Kriminalpolizeiinspektion Neu-Ulm und der Zentralstelle Cybercrime führten bereits im März 2021 zu einem gemeinsamen Action Day mit den kosovarischen Ermittlungsbehörden in Pristina"
  (Translation: "The investigations by the Neu-Ulm Criminal Police Inspectorate and the Central Cybercrime Unit already led, in March 2021, to a joint Action Day with the Kosovar investigative authorities in Pristina.")

## Operational background

- The network operated cold-call investment-fraud / crypto-scam call centres targeting victims worldwide, with Bavaria identified as one of the principal injured jurisdictions.
- The 2021 joint Action Day in Pristina with Kosovo investigators was the operational foundation: simultaneous searches and arrests in Kosovo dismantled call-centre infrastructure.
- The 42-year-old defendant fled to Costa Rica after the 2021 action; Bamberg ZCB issued an international arrest warrant, INTERPOL Red Notice followed.
- Costa Rica granted extradition in 2025 after a lengthy bilateral extradition proceeding; defendant was transferred to Germany in July 2025.
- Indictment filed October 2025; trial expected at the Bamberg Regional Court's specialised Cyber-Economic Criminal Chamber.
- The release notes Israeli authorities provided assistance regarding the suspect's identification and asset tracing given his Israeli nationality.
