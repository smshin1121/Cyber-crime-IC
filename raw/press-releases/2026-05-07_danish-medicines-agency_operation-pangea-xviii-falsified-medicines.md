## Summary-Only Archive

The Danish Medicines Agency reported Denmark's participation in Operation Pangea XVIII. Danish authorities reported 6 websites to the police and detained 19 consignments containing illicit medicines, narcotics, and doping substances.

The release describes Pangea as an annual INTERPOL-coordinated enforcement initiative involving police, customs, health, and safety authorities around the world. It cites the INTERPOL global figures for the 90-country operation, including 6.42 million doses seized, USD 15.5 million in seizures, 269 arrests, and 66 criminal groups dismantled.
