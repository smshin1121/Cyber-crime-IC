DOJ Main Justice (Office of Public Affairs) press release announcing the federal jury conviction of Sumit Garg, 33, of Seattle, on conspiracy and cyberstalking counts in the Western District of Washington. Garg used his computer privacy consulting expertise to anonymize and amplify thousands of harassing messages targeting a former roommate, her family, her boyfriend, and law enforcement personnel.
