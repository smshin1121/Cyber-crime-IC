## Summary-Only Archive

FBI Los Angeles announced 53 U.S. defendants, 47 Egyptian suspects, and 33 U.S. arrests in Operation Phish Phry. The release describes a multinational investigation and states that the operation was the first joint cyber investigation between Egyptian law enforcement authorities and U.S. officials.
