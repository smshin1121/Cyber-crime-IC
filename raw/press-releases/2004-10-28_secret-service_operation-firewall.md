## Summary-Only Archive

The U.S. Secret Service announced that Operation Firewall, an international undercover investigation led by the Secret Service, produced 28 arrests across eight U.S. states and six foreign countries. The source identifies Shadowcrew, CarderPlanet, and Darkprofits as target groups involved in online trafficking of stolen payment-card data and identity documents.

The release credits international law-enforcement support from the United Kingdom's National Hi-Tech Crime Unit, Vancouver Police Department, Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Europol, Bulgaria, Belarus, Poland, Sweden, the Netherlands, Ukraine, and the U.S. Department of State.
