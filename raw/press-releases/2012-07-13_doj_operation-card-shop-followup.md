## Summary-Only Archive

DOJ reported three additional arrests in Operation Card Shop after the June 2012 takedown. The source identifies arrests by Indian authorities in Mumbai, Canadian authorities in Alberta, and the FBI in Colorado, raising the total arrest count to 27.

The source states that the United States would seek extradition of the Indian and Canadian arrestees for prosecution.
