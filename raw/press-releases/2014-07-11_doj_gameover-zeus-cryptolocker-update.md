## Summary-Only Archive

DOJ reported follow-up results from the Gameover Zeus and CryptoLocker disruption. The update states that the measures had been successful, all or nearly all active Gameover Zeus computers were communicating with the court-authorized substitute server, infections had declined by 31 percent since disruption began, and CryptoLocker had been neutralized.
