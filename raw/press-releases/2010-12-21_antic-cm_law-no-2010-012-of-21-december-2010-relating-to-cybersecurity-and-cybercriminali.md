## Summary

Cameroon deposited its instrument of accession to the [[budapest-convention|Budapest Convention on Cybercrime]] in **February 2024**, after being invited to accede on 8 February 2023 [1][2]. Cameroon's principal substantive and procedural cybercrime law is **Law No. 2010/012 of 21 December 2010 on Cybersecurity and Cybercriminality**, one of the more comprehensive cybercrime laws in Central Africa [3].
