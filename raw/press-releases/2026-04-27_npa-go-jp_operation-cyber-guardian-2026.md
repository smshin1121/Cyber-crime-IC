# Operation Cyber Guardian 2026 — Japan NPA Results Press Release

## Summary (translated from official Japanese PDF)

Operation Cyber Guardian (オペレーション・サイバー・ガーディアン) is an international joint operation conducted by Japan and six other Asian jurisdictions targeting online child sexual exploitation offenses, particularly child sexual abuse material (CSAM) distribution and possession. This is the second iteration of the operation, following the 2025 edition (six-country format).

## Operation Details

- **Period**: 2026-03-23 (Monday) through 2026-04-17 (Friday)
- **Participating jurisdictions (7)**: Japan, Singapore, Thailand, South Korea, Hong Kong, Brunei, Malaysia
- **Operation framing**: "国際協同オペレーション" (international joint operation) — mutual cooperation and information sharing across the 7 jurisdictions
- **Japan's positioning**: Designated the operation period as "Period of Strengthened International Cooperation for the Eradication of Child Pornography" (児童ポルノ撲滅に関する国際協力強化期間)

## Aggregate Multilateral Results (across all 7 jurisdictions)

- **Suspects investigated**: 445 persons
  - Youngest: 12 years old; oldest: 72 years old
  - 430 male, 15 female
- **Search/seizure locations**: 382
- **Electronic devices seized**:
  - PCs: 116
  - Mobile phones: 340
  - Tablets: 25
  - External storage media: 140
  - Routers: 16

## Japan-Specific Results (domestic)

- **Suspects arrested**: 99 persons
  - Youngest: 14, oldest: 72
  - 95 male, 4 female
- **Charges**: Public display, manufacture, possession, provision, storage of child pornography; child prostitution; non-consensual sexual intercourse
- **Search/seizure locations**: 131
- **Electronic devices seized**:
  - PCs: 45
  - Mobile phones, etc.: 101
  - Tablets: 16
  - External storage media: 49

## Notable Japanese arrest examples

- Unemployed man (60s): public display of child pornography to file-sharing software users via the internet
- Former childcare facility staff (40s): kidnapped a girl under 10 from his facility for indecent purposes, committed non-consensual indecent acts and recorded them
- Public high school teacher (20s): committed indecent acts on a teenage girl at his home and recorded them
- Unemployed man (20s): committed indecent acts on cohabiting teenage girls and filmed them — child welfare law violation, non-consensual indecent acts, sexual posture filming, child pornography manufacture

## Forward Outlook

NPA states intent to "further strengthen international cooperation and effectively suppress crimes of child sexual exploitation."

## Cross-validation (from regional partners)

- **Malaysia (PDRM / Royal Malaysia Police)**: Chief of Police Datuk Seri Mohd Khalid Ismail announced 69 Malaysian arrests, 204,934 CSAM files plus 293,756 adult pornography files seized, 62 suspects charged with 81 counts. Malaysian portion ran 6-8 April 2026.
  - Source: berita.rtm.gov.my (RTM, Malaysian state broadcaster) and malaysiagazette.com
- Quote from Malaysian announcement: "operasi mendapat kerjasama antarabangsa melibatkan tujuh negara, masing-masing Malaysia, Singapura, Thailand, Brunei, Hong Kong, Korea Selatan dan Jap[un]" — "the operation received international cooperation involving seven countries, namely Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Brunei, Hong Kong, South Korea and Japan"
