### Key Findings

- INTERPOL reported that Operation Serengeti 2.0 involved investigators from 18 African countries and the United Kingdom.
- Public results included 1,209 arrests, USD 97.4 million recovered, nearly 88,000 victims identified, and 11,432 malicious infrastructures dismantled.
- The release identified ransomware, online scams, BEC, crypto-investment fraud, and related infrastructure as target areas.
