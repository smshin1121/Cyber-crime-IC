## Summary-Only Archive

DOJ announced the Operation Card Shop takedown through the Southern District of New York. The release describes Carder Profit, an FBI undercover carding forum launched in June 2010 and taken offline in May 2012, and records 24 initial arrests from a 13-country coordinated action.

The release also reports notification of more than 411,000 compromised credit and debit cards, notification of 47 breached organizations, and more than USD 205 million in estimated potential losses prevented.
