# U.S. law enforcement assists Brazilian law enforcement takedown of numerous digital piracy sites and apps

The U.S. Department of Justice reported U.S. assistance to Brazil's Operation 404, a Brazilian-led takedown of digital piracy sites and applications. The source identifies the coordinated law-enforcement operation, domain seizures in the United States, and the illegal distribution of copyrighted television shows and movies.

The source names Brazil's Secretariat of Integrated Operations at the Ministry of Justice and Public Security, DOJ, Homeland Security Investigations, the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Eastern District of Virginia, and the City of London Police Intellectual Property Crime Unit as cooperation partners.
