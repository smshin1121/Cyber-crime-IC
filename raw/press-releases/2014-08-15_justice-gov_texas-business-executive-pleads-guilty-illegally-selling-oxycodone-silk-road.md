## Summary

DOJ-MDFL press release announcing Matthew Verran Jones's guilty plea to a federal felony for illegally selling oxycodone on the Silk Road darknet marketplace. Jones was Chief Technology Officer for a Dallas software consulting company and operated as "Caligirl" on Silk Road. He admitted shipping more than 400 oxycodone tablets and over 900 hydrocodone tablets from Texas to Central Florida between July 2013 and March 2014, placing him in the top 5% of Silk Road vendors. He was arrested in May 2014 returning from Colombia. Sentencing scheduled for late 2014.
