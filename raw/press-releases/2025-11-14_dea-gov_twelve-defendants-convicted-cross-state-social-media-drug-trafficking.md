DEA parallel agency press release announcing twelve guilty pleas in DNH for a Lowell/Lawrence MA-based drug trafficking organization that distributed fentanyl, methamphetamine, and cocaine to NH and Maine via social media-facilitated transactions. ~20 kg fentanyl, 1 kg meth, 200 g cocaine; firearms-for-drugs trades.
