### Key Findings
- URL-TOPIC MISMATCH: The stored NCA URL refers to Operation Dark HunTOR (October 2021), NOT the Darkode forum takedown (July 2015). Dark HunTOR targeted an unspecified criminal dark web marketplace (sometimes linked to the DarkMarket takedown follow-on), and is unrelated to Darkode
- Actual operation: Operation Dark HunTOR, announced 27 October 2021, was a coordinated international takedown following German authorities' earlier arrest of a dark web marketplace operator. The investigation exploited infrastructure accessed after the marketplace operator was detained
- UK results alone: 24 arrests in the UK, £220,000+ in criminal cash and bitcoin seized, 50+ kg of drugs recovered (cocaine, MDMA, cannabis, methamphetamine, ketamine)
- Global results: 150 alleged suspects arrested across participating nations, €26.7 million (~US$31 million) in cash and cryptocurrency seized, 234 kg of drugs recovered including 152 kg amphetamine, 27 kg opioids, 25,000+ ecstasy pills, and 45 firearms
- Participating countries: Australia, Bulgaria, France, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Switzerland and the United States — coordinated by Europol and Eurojust
- Dark HunTOR marked a major dark-web drug marketplace enforcement action and is distinct from the 2015 Darkode cybercrime forum takedown (Operation Shrouded Horizon), which focused on malware/botnet trading rather than narcotics
