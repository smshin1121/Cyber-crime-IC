## Summary

Team Cymru said it supported Operation Sentinel by sharing threat-intelligence capabilities with INTERPOL and AFRIPOL. The company repeated the operation's 574 arrests, USD 3 million recovered, 6,000 malicious links taken down and six ransomware variants decrypted, while emphasizing intelligence sharing, infrastructure mapping and partner support for African cybercrime investigations.
