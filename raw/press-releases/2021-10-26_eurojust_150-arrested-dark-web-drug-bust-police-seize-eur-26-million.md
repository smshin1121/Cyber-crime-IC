Eurojust press release announcing Operation Dark HunTor results: 150 arrests across nine countries (US 65, Germany 47, UK 24, Italy 4, Netherlands 4, France 3, Switzerland 2, Bulgaria 1), seizure of EUR 26.7 million (USD 31 million) in cash and virtual currencies, 234 kg of drugs, and 45 firearms. Operation followed the takedown of DarkMarket earlier in 2021. Coordinated by Europol and Eurojust. The Sacramento darknet vendor case (CaliPlugMike / Jonathan Patrick Turrentine) was part of the US contingent of 65 arrests credited to Operation Dark HunTor.
