## Summary

An Associated Press dispatch on Operation Jackal III reported that police arrested 300 people, seized USD 3 million and blocked 720 bank accounts across 21 countries. The article restated that the operation targeted online financial fraud by West African organized crime groups, especially Black Axe, and emphasized the five-continent scope of the enforcement effort.
