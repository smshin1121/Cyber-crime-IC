## Summary-Only Archive

Singapore's Health Sciences Authority announced that it took down 959 illegal health product listings from local e-commerce and social media platforms and issued warnings to 152 sellers as part of Operation Pangea XVIII.

HSA also seized 6,641 units of illegal health products intercepted at Singapore land, air, and sea checkpoints. The release identifies Operation Pangea XVIII as an INTERPOL-led global enforcement exercise across 90 countries from 10 to 23 March 2026.
