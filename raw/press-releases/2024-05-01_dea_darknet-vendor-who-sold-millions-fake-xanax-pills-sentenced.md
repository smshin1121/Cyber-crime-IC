DEA parallel press release announcing Brandon Adams's 24-month federal sentence for operating a counterfeit Xanax operation under the darknet alias "BenzoBoys" from October 2019 through May 2021. Adams ordered to forfeit nearly $1 million and pay $10,000 fine. Investigation involved DEA, FBI, USPIS, and the Eastern District of Missouri Strike Force.
