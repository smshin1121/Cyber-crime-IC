DEA parallel press release confirming Humberto Garcia, 42, of Monroe, Washington, sentenced to 10 years in prison for conspiracy to distribute controlled substances. Garcia served as the "right hand man" of a Puget Sound region drug ring distributing heroin, methamphetamine, and fentanyl. Investigation seized 16,000 fentanyl pills, 30 pounds of methamphetamine, and 6 pounds of heroin. Lead investigative agency was DEA Seattle.
