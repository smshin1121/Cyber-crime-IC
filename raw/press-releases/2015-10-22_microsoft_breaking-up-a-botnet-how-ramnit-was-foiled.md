## Summary

Microsoft described how its Digital Crimes Unit assisted the February 2015 Ramnit takedown alongside Europol and investigators from Germany, Italy, the Netherlands and the United Kingdom. The blog explains the sinkholing of roughly 300 domains and how Microsoft used telemetry and cloud analytics to map infections and support the disruption.
