DEA parallel press release announcing charges against Alan Bill, 33, of Bratislava, Slovakia, for running Kingdom Market darknet marketplace selling drugs, malware, stolen identities, counterfeit money, and fraudulent passports. Bill arrested at Newark Liberty International Airport on December 15, 2023. Investigation involved DEA, IRS-CI, FBI, USPIS, and HSI in the Eastern District of Missouri.
