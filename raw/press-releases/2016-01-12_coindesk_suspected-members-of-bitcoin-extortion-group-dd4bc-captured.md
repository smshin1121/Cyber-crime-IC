## Summary

CoinDesk reported that Europol announced one arrest and one detention under Operation Pleiades and identified the participating or supporting authorities across Bosnia and Herzegovina, Germany, France, Japan, Romania, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, and the United States. The article framed DD4BC as a Bitcoin-denominated DDoS extortion group targeting a range of online services and highlighted that the operation targeted members located in Bosnia and Herzegovina.

## Key Data Points

- **Operation name:** Operation Pleiades
- **Arrests:** 1 arrest, 1 detention
- **Countries highlighted:** Bosnia and Herzegovina, Germany, France, Japan, Romania, Switzerland, United Kingdom, United States
- **Threat model:** Bitcoin-backed DDoS extortion
