DEA parallel press release confirming Humberto Lopez Rodriguez, 31, Mexican citizen of Renton, Washington, sentenced to additional 5 years for second drug trafficking offense after absconding from prior federal sentence. Arrested December 2023 with 13 kilos of cocaine and a loaded firearm. Investigation tied to Mexico-Colombia supply chains.
