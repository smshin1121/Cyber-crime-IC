## Summary-Only Archive

FBI New York announced charges against 37 defendants in 21 federal cases for roles in global bank-fraud schemes using Zeus Trojan malware and false-name bank accounts to steal funds from compromised U.S. bank accounts.

The release describes malware-based credential theft, unauthorized online transfers, false-name receiving accounts, and proceeds moved overseas through a money-mule organization. It also records cooperation among U.S. federal, state, local, and foreign law-enforcement officials.
