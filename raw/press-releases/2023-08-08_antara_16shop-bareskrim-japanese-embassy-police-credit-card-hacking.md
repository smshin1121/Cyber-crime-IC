## Captured cooperation elements

The Antara Foto caption documents the **8 August 2023 Bareskrim Polri press conference** in Jakarta at which the 16shop phishing-platform takedown was publicly announced from the Indonesian side. The frame shows three officials on stage:

1. **Brigadier General Pol. Adi Vivid Agustiadi Bachtiar** — Director of the Directorate of Cyber Crimes (*Tindak Pidana Siber*, "Dirtipidsiber") at Bareskrim Polri. He is the same senior Indonesian National Police officer quoted on the record in the INTERPOL release published the same day.
2. **Brigadier General Pol. Ahmad Ramadhan** — Karo Penmas (Head of Public Information Bureau) of the Public Relations Division (Humas) of Polri.
3. **Mr. Miyagawa** — Police attaché of the Japanese Embassy for Indonesia (described in the caption as "Tope Police of the Japanese Embassy for Indonesia" — interpreted as Top Police Officer / police attaché).

The caption explicitly states the cooperation in operational terms:

- "**Ditipidsiber Bareskrim Polri together with the Japanese Police party managed to secure two suspects of WNI**" — Indonesian Bareskrim cyber-crime directorate cooperated with Japan's police (here represented through the Embassy police attaché) to detain two Indonesian-citizen suspects.
- Evidence seized at the Indonesian arrests: smart phones, laptops, hard disks.
- Reported loss: **Rp1.6 billion** (Indonesian Rupiah ~ USD 105,000 at August 2023 exchange) — this represents the Indonesian-Japanese sub-case loss figure, lower than the global PaaS victimisation footprint reported by INTERPOL the same day (>70,000 users compromised across 43 countries).
