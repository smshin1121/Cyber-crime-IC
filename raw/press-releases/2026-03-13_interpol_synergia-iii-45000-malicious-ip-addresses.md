### Key Findings

- INTERPOL reported a 72-country and territory operation targeting malicious cyber infrastructure.
- Public results included more than 45,000 malicious IP addresses and servers taken down, 94 arrests, 110 further suspects or individuals under investigation, and 212 electronic devices or servers seized.
- The operational window ran from July 2025 to January 2026.
