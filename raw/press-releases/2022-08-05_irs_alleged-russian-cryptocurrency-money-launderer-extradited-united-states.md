Parallel IRS-CI press release announcing the August 4, 2022 extradition of Russian national Alexander Vinnik (age 42) from Greece to the Northern District of California to face a 21-count superseding indictment for operating BTC-e, an unlicensed cryptocurrency exchange that allegedly laundered more than $4 billion. Confirms IRS-CI, FBI, HSI, and U.S. Secret Service collaborated on the investigation. FinCEN had previously imposed civil penalties of approximately $88.6 million against BTC-e and $12 million against Vinnik.
