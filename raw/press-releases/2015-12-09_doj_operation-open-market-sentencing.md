## Summary-Only Archive

The Department of Justice announced a sentencing in Operation Open Market and summarized the Carder.su case. DOJ reported that Operation Open Market targeted a Carder.su organization involved in trafficking compromised credit card data, counterfeit identification documents and credit cards, money laundering, narcotics trafficking, and computer crime.

The release states that 56 persons were charged in four indictments and that 33 had been convicted by December 2015. It also describes Carder.su forums as generally hosted within the former Soviet Union with senior members located there.
