# Treasury Takes Action as Part of a U.S. Government Response to Russia's Foreign Malign Influence Operations

**Date:** September 4, 2024
**Source:** U.S. Department of the Treasury, Office of Foreign Assets Control
**Press Release ID:** JY-2559
**URL:** https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/jy2559

**Subhead:** *The United States takes action to defend and protect U.S. election institutions and processes from Moscow's attempts to influence the 2024 Presidential Election*

## Headline Facts (verified — primary source body text)

- **OFAC designated 10 individuals and 2 entities** as part of a coordinated U.S. government response to Moscow's malign influence efforts targeting the 2024 U.S. presidential election.
- All designations issued under **Executive Order 14024** (Russia harmful foreign activities; IEEPA authority).
- The release explicitly states the action **complements law enforcement actions taken by the Department of Justice** and the **State Department's designation of Rossiya Segodnya media group and five subsidiaries (RIA Novosti, RT, TV-Novosti, Ruptly, Sputnik) as Foreign Missions**, plus visa restrictions and a **Rewards for Justice (RFJ) reward of up to $10 million** for information on foreign election interference.

## Designated Entities (2)

| # | Entity | Role |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Autonomous Non-Profit Organization (ANO) Dialog** | Russian nonprofit founded in 2019 by the Moscow city government; uses AI for online disinformation; **linked to Doppelgänger** influence network (identified 2022); operates "War on Fakes" website |

## Doppelgänger / RRN Operative Findings (per Treasury)

- **ANO Dialog is linked to Doppelgänger** — a Russia-linked influence operation network identified in 2022 — that used **deep fake content** to develop Russian disinformation campaigns.
- In **fall 2022**, Tabak attended a meeting with senior Russian government officials where the presumed success of Doppelgänger activity was discussed and feedback/guidance provided.
- In **July 2022**, ANO Dialog and Tabak provided services for the creation of fake online posts on **"Reliable Recent News" ("RRN")** and **"War on Fakes"**.
- An ANO Dialog advisor purchased RRN's new domain **"rrn.world"** to avoid using the prior domain "rrussianews.com" which would have maintained a Russia connection.
- In **late fall 2023**, ANO Dialog personnel identified **U.S., UK, and other public figures** as potential targets for deepfake projects. (Treasury notes this is the basis on which **the EU separately sanctioned ANO Dialog** for its role as owner/operator of "War on Fakes" — this is a *prior* EU action, not a coordinated same-day package.)
- In **May 2024**, Tabak and Dialog Regions officials coordinated with Russian government officials regarding the creation of bot accounts on social media messaging platforms for use in a misinformation campaign regarding voting locations in the U.S. 2024 election.

## Companion Federal Action Cited

> "Also today, the Federal Bureau of Investigation announced the seizure of domains associated with RRN/ANO Dialog and **Social Design Agency (SDA)** — which OFAC had previously designated pursuant to E.O. 14024 for its activities on behalf of the Russian government — that have been used in furtherance of the Russian Doppelgänger disinformation efforts targeting the United States and Europe."

This explicitly ties Treasury's JY-2559 designations to:
- **DOJ/FBI domain seizures** (32 domains; companion: [[us-doj-doppelganger-russian-influence-op-domain-seizures-2024]])
- **Social Design Agency (SDA)** — note: SDA was previously designated by OFAC, **not in this JY-2559 action**.

## RT Funding Operation Context (background to SDNY indictment)

The release describes RT's covert recruitment of unwitting American influencers beginning early 2024, using a front company to disguise involvement. Two of the same-day SDNY indictees (Kalashnikov and Afanasyeva) are also OFAC-designated here. This illustrates DOJ–Treasury parallelism: criminal indictment + asset freeze on the same defendants.

## Sanctions Implications (verbatim summary)

- All property and interests in property of the designated persons in the U.S. or in possession/control of U.S. persons are **blocked** and must be reported to OFAC.
- Entities owned 50%+ by blocked persons are also blocked.
- U.S. persons generally prohibited from any transactions involving blocked property.
- Financial institutions engaging in transactions with sanctioned persons may face enforcement.

## Legal Authority

- **Executive Order 14024** of April 15, 2021 — "Blocking Property With Respect To Specified Harmful Foreign Activities of the Government of the Russian Federation."
- Underlying statute: **International Emergency Economic Powers Act (IEEPA), 50 U.S.C. §§ 1701–1708**.

## International Cooperation Dimension (analyst note)

This release **does not credit any foreign government, foreign LE agency, or international organization as a cooperation partner**. It references:
- **DOJ + FBI** (US domestic) as same-day partners.
- **State Department** (US domestic) as same-day partner.
- **EU** as a *prior* (separate) sanction issuer of ANO Dialog — explicitly *not* a same-day coordinated package.

The IC scope of this operation is therefore primarily:
1. **Target side foreign:** all designated individuals are Russian nationals; both designated entities are Russian.
2. **Underlying conduct foreign:** Russian state-directed influence operations targeting the U.S. and Europe.
3. **Companion action foreign-targeted:** DOJ domain seizures crossed multiple TLDs including ccTLDs.
4. **No reciprocal foreign assistance documented:** Russia is uncooperative; no foreign LE channel contributed to the OFAC designation evidence per the public release.
