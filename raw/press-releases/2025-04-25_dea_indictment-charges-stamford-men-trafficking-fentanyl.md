DEA parallel-agency release confirming federal indictment of Tasean Lemar Brown (35) and Stanley Charles (44), both of Stamford, CT, for conspiracy to distribute and to possess with intent to distribute 400+ grams of fentanyl. The indictment was returned April 3, 2025; defendants arrested April 7, 2025. Investigators intercepted two parcels mailed by Brown from California and Arizona to Charles in Connecticut: ~1 kg fentanyl powder and ~3 kg counterfeit oxycodone pills containing fentanyl. Multi-agency case: DEA, U.S. Postal Inspection Service, Connecticut State Police, and Stamford/Greenwich/Bridgeport/Danbury/Stratford/Norwalk Police Departments.
