### Key Findings

- Three-country coordinated action day on 2024-12-17 in Tirol (Austria), Bukarest and Râmnicu Vâlcea (Romania), and the Regensburg area (Bayern, Germany).
- 13 locations searched; approximately 150 police officers deployed across the three countries.
- 5 male suspects (of 7 identified, ages 22-38) arrested under cross-jurisdictional warrants.
- At least 120 compromised merchant accounts; suspects induced customers to place orders totalling approximately EUR 110 million; 381 identified victims with confirmed losses of approximately EUR 192,000 for undelivered goods plus EUR 17,000 from at least 7 real-estate fraud victims; approximately EUR 70,000 cash seized.
- Joint Investigation Team (JIT) with Romanian prosecutorial authorities established under Eurojust participation; cross-border prosecution by Bavarian, Austrian, and Romanian investigators.
- Named cooperating agencies: Zentralstelle Cybercrime Bayern (ZCB) of the Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Bamberg, Polizeipräsidium Mittelfranken / Kriminalpolizei Nürnberg (Germany); Landeskriminalamt Tirol (Austria); D.I.I.C.O.T - Directoratul de Investigare a Infracțiunilor de Criminalitate Organizată și Terorism (Romania); Eurojust.
- Investigation initiated August 2023; action day 2024-12-17; press release 2025-01-15.
