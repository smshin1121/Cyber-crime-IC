# UEFA supports international anti-piracy operation

UEFA reported support for Operation Kratos, an international anti-piracy operation against illegal streaming platforms and illegal IPTV services. The source describes a Bulgaria-led effort with support from Italian and Croatian law-enforcement agencies, Europol, and Eurojust.

The source reports 112 searches and seizures, 102 suspects identified, 11 arrests, and a network reaching more than 22 million users worldwide. Because these figures and target characteristics overlap with the 2024 Taken Down record, this repository treats Kratos as an alias/source for that operation pending stronger deconfliction evidence.
