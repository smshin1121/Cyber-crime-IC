## Summary

Spain's Policía Nacional reported that a Europol-coordinated investigation dismantled a Franco-Israeli organization that defrauded more than EUR 38 million through CEO fraud. The release said eight people were arrested in France and Israel and that eight searches were conducted. Spanish investigators traced a French national using a false Belgian passport to Madrid, where she obtained an NIE, opened a company, and received nearly EUR 900,000 in fraud proceeds; the release also recorded EUR 400,000 frozen in Spanish bank accounts as part of the wider asset recovery effort.
