The U.S. Attorney's Office for the District of Massachusetts announced the civil forfeiture recovery of $5.3M tied to a 2023 BEC scheme that defrauded a Dorchester workers union of $6.4M. The funds were traced through bank accounts in Hong Kong, China, Singapore, and Nigeria before recovery.
