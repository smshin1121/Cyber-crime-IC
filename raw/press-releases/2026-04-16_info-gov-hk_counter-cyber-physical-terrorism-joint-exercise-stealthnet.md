# Press Release — Counter Cyber and Physical Terrorism Joint Exercise 2026 successfully concludes (with photos)

**Date:** 16 April 2026
**Source:** Information Services Department, Government of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region (HKSAR), on behalf of the Hong Kong Police Force
**URL:** https://www.info.gov.hk/gia/general/202604/16/P2026041600803.htm

## Verbatim text (key passages, as extracted from the official HKSAR Government Information Services Department wire)

> The Hong Kong Police Force concluded the Counter Cyber and Physical Terrorism Joint Exercise 2026, codenamed "STEALTHNET", today (April 16).

> The exercise was jointly organised by the Hong Kong Police Force's Crime Wing and Operations Wing, with the participation of the INTERPOL, the Macao Judiciary Police, the Singapore Police Force, the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore and the Korean National Police Agency.

> More than 380 participants from local law enforcement agencies, overseas counterparts, government departments, financial regulators and the banking sector took part in the drill.

> The drill simulated a terrorist financing scenario, followed by a cross-border terrorist cell conducting cyber and physical attacks against the financial, aviation and healthcare systems across Hong Kong, Macao, Singapore and Korea, including planting explosive devices and deploying drones at major event venues.

> The exercise reaffirmed that cross-border law enforcement cooperation and intelligence sharing are essential for safeguarding critical infrastructures.
