## Summary

An Associated Press dispatch reported that Operation 2BaGoldMule led to 20 arrests of alleged QQAAZZ members across multiple countries. The article restated the role of Portuguese investigators and Europol, described the network’s laundering of millions of euros stolen through malware schemes, and noted house searches in Latvia, Bulgaria, the United Kingdom, Spain and Italy.
