## Summary

PCWorld reported that Operation Pleiades led to the arrest of a main DD4BC target and the detention of another suspect after joint raids in Bosnia and Herzegovina. The article highlighted the UK Metropolitan Police Cyber Crime Unit's role in identifying key network members in Bosnia and said authorities from Austria, Germany, and the United Kingdom carried out the coordinated action with Europol support.

## Key Data Points

- **Operation name:** Operation Pleiades
- **Target:** DD4BC (DDoS for Bitcoin)
- **Arrests:** 1 arrest, 1 detention
- **Lead investigative clue:** UK MPCCU identification of network members in Bosnia and Herzegovina
- **Supporting countries:** Australia, France, Japan, Romania, Switzerland, United States
