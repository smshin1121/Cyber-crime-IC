DEA parallel press release announcing John Cruz of Rochester, NY's 54-month federal sentence for conspiracy to misbrand and sell counterfeit drugs on the dark web. Cruz pleaded guilty twice (2023 and 2024) after operating darknet sales from October 2019 through May 2021 and continuing while on bond. Investigation involved FBI, DEA, USPIS, and ICE in Eastern District of Missouri.
