## Summary

OCCRP reported on the takedown of the QQAAZZ money-laundering network and described it as a cross-border operation involving arrests, house searches and the opening of parallel prosecutions in the United States, Portugal, Spain and the United Kingdom. The article highlighted the network’s use of shell companies, personal and corporate bank accounts, and services advertised to major cybercriminal groups.
