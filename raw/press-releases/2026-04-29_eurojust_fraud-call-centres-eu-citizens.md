### Key Findings

- Austrian and Albanian authorities dismantled call centres operating from Tirana.
- Eurojust supported a JIT, coordination meetings, action-day funding, and interpretation services.
- Europol supported operational measures and action-day evidence exchange.
- The action led to 10 arrests, three call-centre searches, nine residence searches, and seizure of EUR 891,735 and hundreds of devices.
