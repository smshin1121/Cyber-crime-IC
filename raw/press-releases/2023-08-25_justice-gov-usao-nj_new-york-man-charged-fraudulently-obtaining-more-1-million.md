USAO-D.N.J. press release announcing the original charging of Terrell Fuller, 33, of Brooklyn, NY, with one count of wire fraud after a fraudulent EIDL application defrauded the SBA of $1.2 million in 2021. Fuller was the sole signatory on accounts that received approximately $400,000 of the disbursed funds.
