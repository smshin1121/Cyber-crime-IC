## Key Facts (verbatim from Eurojust release)

- **Action day:** 22 January 2024.
- **Participating countries:** Germany, Cyprus, Bulgaria, Sweden.
- **Authorities listed (verbatim):** "Germany: Public Prosecutor's Office of Göttingen; Centre for Cybercrime, Police Göttingen. Bulgaria: Sofia City Public Prosecutor's Office; General Directorate Bulgarian National Police. Cyprus: International Police Cooperation Sub-Directorate - Police Cooperation Bureau Cyprus Police. Sweden: Swedish Police Authority."
- **Modus operandi:** organised criminals operated fraudulent online trading platforms from call centres in Cyprus, impersonating investment advisors and persuading victims to deposit funds via false profit guarantees.
- **Arrests:** four suspects.
- **Damages:** EUR 3.3 million and EUR 164,000 attributed to two main platforms targeting German victims; total European damages reported to be substantially higher.
