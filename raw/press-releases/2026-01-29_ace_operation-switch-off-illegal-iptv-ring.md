## Summary-Only Archive

The Alliance for Creativity and Entertainment announced Operation Switch Off as a coordinated global law-enforcement action against industrial-scale illegal IPTV services.

ACE says the action was led by the District Prosecutor's Office of Catania, the Italian State Police Postal and Cybercrime Police, and C.O.S.C. Catania, with international coordination by Eurojust and support from Europol and Interpol.

ACE reports 31 suspects after 29 raids in 11 Italian cities and 14 countries worldwide, including Spain, Romania, the United Kingdom, Canada, India, South Korea, and the United Arab Emirates. Reported results include illegal IPTV infrastructure taken offline, about 250 Italian resellers dismantled, more than 125,000 Italian end users disconnected, major platforms including IPTVItalia, migliorIPTV, and DarkTV seized, Telegram-based sales channels removed, and six servers seized outside Italy.
