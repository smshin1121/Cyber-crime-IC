# Operation Perfect Storm: Crackdown on illegal use of pay-TV signals

Eurojust published an Operation Perfect Storm case summary describing a 2019 major operation led by the Italian desk at Eurojust against an organised criminal group involved in large-scale pay-TV piracy and fraud. The source states that more than 200 servers were taken down and that the network involved illegal online streaming through cheap set-top boxes.

The publication records continued investigations into 2020 and lists Bulgaria, France, Germany, Italy, Lithuania, Malta, the Netherlands, Romania, Slovenia, and Sweden as participating countries.
