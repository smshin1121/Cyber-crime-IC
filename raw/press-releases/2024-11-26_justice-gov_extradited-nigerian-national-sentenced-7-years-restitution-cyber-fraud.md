USAO N.D. Okla. press release announcing that Sunday Daniel Ganyo was sentenced to seven years in federal prison and ordered to pay $2.8 million in restitution for cyber fraud conspiracy. Ganyo had been arrested in South Africa in January 2021 and extradited to the United States in July 2023; he pled guilty in November 2023. The investigation determined Ganyo attempted to steal nearly $8 million worth of products and was successful in obtaining at least $2.33 million worth of goods, which were redirected to a warehouse he controlled in South Africa. Following his sentence Ganyo will be deported to Nigeria.
