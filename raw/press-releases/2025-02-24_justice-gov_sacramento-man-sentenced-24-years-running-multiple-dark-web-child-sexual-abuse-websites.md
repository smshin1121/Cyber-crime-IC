USAO-EDCA sentencing press release for Louis Donald Mendonsa, 62, of Sacramento, sentenced to 24 years 4 months in prison. Mendonsa managed four dark web websites dedicated to CSAM distribution from December 2021 until his November 2022 arrest. Confirms HSI lead role and CEOS High Technology Investigative Unit support.
