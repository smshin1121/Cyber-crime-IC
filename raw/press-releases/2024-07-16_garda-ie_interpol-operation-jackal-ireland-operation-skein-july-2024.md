## Summary

Garda's July 2024 release on Operation Jackal reported 63 people arrested in Ireland, 17 charged, six handled through the Juvenile Diversion Programme, 37 searches carried out, 11 phones seized, two suspected laundering-funded vehicles seized, EUR 49,000 in goods seized, cryptocurrency worth EUR 81,133 and USD 260,953 seized, and 17 bank accounts blocked for suspected false-name use. The release linked these actions directly to the April-July 2024 international Operation Jackal action period.
