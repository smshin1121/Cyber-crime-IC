## Summary-Only Archive

The U.S. Secret Service announced Operation Open Market as an investigation into a transnational organized-crime group using online forums to buy and sell stolen personal and financial information, counterfeit identification documents, and access devices. The release reported 19 initial arrests in March 2012 and described the case as the first Secret Service cybercrime case resulting in a RICO indictment.
