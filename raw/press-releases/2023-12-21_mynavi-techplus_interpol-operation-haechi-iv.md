## Summary

Japanese outlet TECH+ reported that HAECHI IV led to more than 3,500 arrests across 34 countries and noted that Japan was among the participating states. The article focused on the operation's anti-fraud scope, including voice phishing, investment fraud, BEC, online sextortion and e-commerce fraud, and reiterated the scale of bank-account blocking and asset seizures.
