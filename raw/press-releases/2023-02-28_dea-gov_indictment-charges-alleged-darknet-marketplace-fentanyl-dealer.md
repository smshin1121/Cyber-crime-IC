DEA press release announcing the dual-district indictments of Jacob Blair, 25, of Coraopolis, PA, on charges of running a darknet marketplace fentanyl trafficking operation under the profile YourVendorsSupplier (YVS). Indictments returned in both the Western District of Pennsylvania and the District of Columbia. Search warrants on Feb 24, 2023 yielded over 10,000 fentanyl pills, raw powder, a pill press, and 11 firearms including AK-47 and AR-15.
