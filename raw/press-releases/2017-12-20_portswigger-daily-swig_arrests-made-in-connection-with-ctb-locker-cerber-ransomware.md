## Summary

The Daily Swig reported that five individuals were apprehended during Operation Bakovia for spreading CTB-Locker and Cerber across Europe and the United States. The article said three suspects were first arrested for distributing CTB-Locker and that a parallel investigation in Bucharest led to the arrest of two more suspects tied to Cerber activity against U.S. systems. It also repeated Europol's description of evidence seized in Romania and the prosecution focus on unauthorized access, system interference, device misuse, and blackmail.

## Key Data Points

- **Operation name:** Operation Bakovia
- **Arrests:** 5
- **Evidence seized:** hard drives, laptops, external storage devices, cryptocurrency mining devices, documents
- **Affected geography:** Europe and the United States
- **Charges described:** unauthorized access, serious hindering of a computer system, misuse of devices, blackmail
