# Crackdown on illegal streaming network with 22 million users worldwide

Eurojust reported a 26 November 2024 joint operation against one of the world's largest illegal streaming services. Eurojust and Europol supported cooperation between authorities from Italy, Croatia, the Netherlands, Romania, Sweden, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom.

The source reports more than 100 searches against 102 suspects, 11 arrests in Croatia, server seizures and shutdowns, cryptocurrency and cash seizures, and a service that reached more than 22 million users worldwide through illegal streaming of films, series, sports channels, and other protected television content.

Eurojust describes coordination meetings in The Hague and Europol analytical and deployment support. The listed authorities include the Catania Public Prosecution Office, Croatian anti-corruption and organised-crime authorities, Dutch fiscal and prosecution authorities, Romanian organised-crime and cybercrime authorities, Swedish prosecution and police authorities, Swiss prosecution and federal police authorities, and City of London Police.
