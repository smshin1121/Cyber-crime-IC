USAO-EDMO follow-up press release announcing former Mississippi County, Missouri Sheriff Cory Hutcheson's guilty plea to wire fraud and aggravated identity theft, with agreement to resign. Hutcheson admitted to obtaining cellular phone location data of hundreds of individuals via fraudulent Securus LBS requests between April 2014 and March 2017.
