## Summary-Only Archive

ICE announced that Jordan Georgievski, a Macedonian national charged in Operation Open Market, was extradited from Albania to the United States. The source states that the extradition resulted from coordination among HSI attaché offices in Vienna and Athens, the State Department Diplomatic Security Service, DOJ Office of International Affairs, Austrian authorities, Albanian National Police, and Interpol Tirana.

The release also summarizes Operation Open Market as a long-term undercover investigation by HSI, the U.S. Secret Service, and the Southwestern Identity Theft and Fraud Task Force targeting Carder.su.
