# 해외 해킹 조직의 총책급 범죄인, 태국에서 범죄인인도

**Publisher:** 법무부 (Ministry of Justice of the Republic of Korea)
**Publication date:** 2026-05-13 (Wednesday)
**Source URL:** https://www.moj.go.kr/bbs/moj/182/606523/artclView.do
**Department:** 법무부 국제형사과 (International Criminal Affairs Division)
**Minister of Justice:** 정성호 (Jeong Seong-ho)

## Summary (English)

The Ministry of Justice of the Republic of Korea (Minister Jeong Seong-ho) announced that on the morning of Wednesday, 13 May 2026, in cooperation with the Korean National Police Agency (경찰청), it had extradited from Bangkok, Thailand to Incheon International Airport a senior-level (총책급) member of an overseas hacking criminal organisation. The 40-year-old Chinese-national defendant ("ㄱ○○") is suspected of hacking multiple websites including those of mobile-virtual-network-operator (알뜰폰 / MVNO) carriers, illegally collecting personal information, and using it to make unauthorised withdrawals of over KRW 38 billion (≈ USD 25.5 million) from the financial accounts and virtual-asset accounts of Korean victims between approximately August 2023 and April 2025.

Confirmed victims to date include 16 individuals, including a major-conglomerate chairman, the chief of a venture firm, and BTS member 정국 (Jungkook). HYBE share-transfer suspension (triggered by BigHit Music in January 2024) prevented an attempted KRW 8.4 billion theft against Jungkook from being completed.

This is the second senior member of the same hacking syndicate extradited from Thailand. A 36-year-old Chinese co-leader was extradited from Thailand in August 2025 and is on trial in Seoul.

## Crime alleged

- **Crime type (Korean penal code):** 정보통신망 침해, 개인정보부정사용, 사기 (unauthorised computer-network intrusion, illegal use of personal information, fraud)
- **Modus operandi:** Hacked the websites of MVNO (알뜰폰) operators and other companies; harvested customer personal information; used the harvested data to gain unauthorised access to victims' financial accounts and virtual-asset (crypto) accounts; withdrew large sums without authorisation.
- **Period of crime:** August 2023 – April 2025
- **Total criminal proceeds:** over KRW 38 billion (≈ USD 25.5 million)
- **Confirmed victims:** 16 (so far), including chairman of a major conglomerate, head of a venture firm, BTS member Jungkook, and other high-net-worth Koreans/celebrities

| 2026-05-13 | Present extradition: defendant transferred from Bangkok to Incheon by Korean MOJ + KNPA officers |
