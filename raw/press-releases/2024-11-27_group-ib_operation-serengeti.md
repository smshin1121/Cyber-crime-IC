## Summary

Group-IB said it supported Operation Serengeti as a private-sector partner of INTERPOL and AFRIPOL. The company reported that the operation ran from 2 September to 31 October 2024, resulted in 1,006 arrests, dismantled 134,089 malicious infrastructures and networks, identified more than 35,000 victims, and was linked to nearly USD 193 million in global losses. It also noted that its own investigative support covered investment scams, government-impersonation schemes, phishing, pig-butchering and online-casino activity.
