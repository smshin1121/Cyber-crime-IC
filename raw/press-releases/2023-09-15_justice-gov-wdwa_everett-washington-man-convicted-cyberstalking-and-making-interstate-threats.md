WDWA conviction press release on Christopher Scott Crawford. Following two-week trial, jury found Crawford guilty of cyberstalking, interstate threats, and contempt for violating restraining orders. Confirms three-year campaign against ex-wife including posting intimate images online and emailing colleagues in the Navy.
