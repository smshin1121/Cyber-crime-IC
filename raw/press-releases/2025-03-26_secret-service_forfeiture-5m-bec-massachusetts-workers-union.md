The U.S. Secret Service announced its role in the $5.3M civil forfeiture recovery for a Dorchester, Massachusetts workers union victimized by a $6.4M BEC scheme in January 2023. The fraudulently obtained funds were laundered through intermediary accounts and a cryptocurrency exchange to bank accounts in Hong Kong, China, Singapore, and Nigeria. Multi-jurisdictional asset tracing and international cooperation enabled the recovery.
