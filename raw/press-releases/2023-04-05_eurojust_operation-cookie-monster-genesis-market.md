## Summary-Only Archive

Eurojust reported that Genesis Market was taken down in a multi-country effort dubbed Operation Cookie Monster. The release describes an operation involving judicial and law enforcement authorities in the United States, nine European Union countries, Australia, Canada, and the United Kingdom.

The source states that Eurojust and Europol supported a coordinated action in which law enforcement in 13 countries conducted more than 100 arrests and more than 200 searches of Genesis Market customers who had purchased stolen credentials.
