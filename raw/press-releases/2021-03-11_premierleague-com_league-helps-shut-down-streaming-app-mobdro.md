# League helps shut down streaming app Mobdro

The Premier League reported that Mobdro, described as the world's largest infringing streaming app, ceased operations after a major investigation and criminal referral by the Premier League and the Alliance for Creativity and Entertainment led to Spanish police and Europol action.

The release corroborates that Mobdro provided unauthorised live TV and video content, including sports, to smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, and illicit streaming devices.
