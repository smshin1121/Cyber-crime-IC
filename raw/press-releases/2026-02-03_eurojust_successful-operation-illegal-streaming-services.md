## Summary-Only Archive

Eurojust reported a February 2026 action against illegal streaming services following analysis of data seized during the earlier Operation Taken Down.

The release states that authorities from Italy, Romania, Spain, the United Kingdom, Canada, Kosovo, and South Korea, with Eurojust and Europol, seized multiple illegal streaming services. Eurojust's structured participating-country tags list Italy, Romania, Spain, and the United Kingdom.

Eurojust reports that 31 suspected members were linked to unauthorized pay-TV distribution, illegal access to IT systems, computer fraud, and money laundering. An action day in nine countries shut down services for millions of users; three illegal IPTV platforms were seized in Italy and a major global IPTV provider using Romanian servers was taken down.
