DHS Homeland Security Investigations (HSI) press release announcing the 15-year (180-month) federal sentence for Michael Posey, 50, of Kent, Washington, who served as a moderator on a dark web CSAM site monitored by HSI-Portland (Maine). Posey approved CSAM posts submitted by others. Search warrant executed at his residence in April 2024.
