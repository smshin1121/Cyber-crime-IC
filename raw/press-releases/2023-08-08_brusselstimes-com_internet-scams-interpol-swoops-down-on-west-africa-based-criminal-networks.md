## Summary

The Brussels Times summarized the official announcement on Operation Jackal and highlighted the international scale of the crackdown, reporting 103 arrests, more than EUR 2 million seized or frozen, over 1,100 suspects identified, and more than 200 bank accounts blocked. The story also emphasized the Portuguese and Irish sub-results and republished the 21-country participant list.
