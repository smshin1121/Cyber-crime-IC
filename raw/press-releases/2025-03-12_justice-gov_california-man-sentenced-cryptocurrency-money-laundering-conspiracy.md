EDTX DOJ press release announcing John Khuu's 87-month federal prison sentence on March 12, 2025. Confirms $5.35M+ laundered through cryptocurrency for a darknet marketplace pharmaceutical/MDMA importation scheme using product imported from Germany. Operation Crypto Runner OCDETF investigation. USSS, USPIS, and HSI San Francisco named as investigative agencies.
