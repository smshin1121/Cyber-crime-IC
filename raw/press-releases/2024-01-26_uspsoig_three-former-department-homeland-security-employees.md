USPS OIG press release announcing parallel agency credit on the January 26, 2024 sentencing of three former DHS employees. Defendants conspired to steal proprietary software and sensitive law-enforcement databases containing PII of over 200,000 federal employees, sharing data with India-based software developers. Sentences: Venkata 4 months, Edwards 18 months, Patel probation/home incarceration.
