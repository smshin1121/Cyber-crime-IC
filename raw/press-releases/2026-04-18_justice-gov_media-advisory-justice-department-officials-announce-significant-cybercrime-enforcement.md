U.S. Attorney's Office, Western District of Pennsylvania

News Conference Set for Wednesday, May 8, at 2 p.m. ET at U.S. Courthouse in Pittsburgh

PITTSBURGH – Federal authorities will hold a news conference on May 8 at 2 p.m. ET in Pittsburgh to discuss a significant cybercrime enforcement action. This investigation was led by the United States Attorney’s Office in Western Pennsylvania and the Federal Bureau of Investigation – Pittsburgh Field Office, and also included the Criminal Division of the U.S. Department of Justice.

WHO:

U.S. Attorney Scott W. Brady, Western District of Pennsylvania

Deputy Assistant Attorney General Richard W. Downing, Justice Department’s Criminal Division
