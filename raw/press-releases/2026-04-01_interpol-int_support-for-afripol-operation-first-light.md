## Summary

INTERPOL's partnership page on support for AFRIPOL uses Operation First Light 2024 as an example of regional-policing cooperation. It states that the 61-country operation froze 6,745 bank accounts, arrested 3,950 suspects, and seized USD 257 million, while highlighting that AFRIPOL, ASEANAPOL, GCCPOL and Europol were all brought into the action through the ISPA programme.
