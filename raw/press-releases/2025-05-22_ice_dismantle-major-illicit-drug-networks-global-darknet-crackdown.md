ICE Homeland Security Investigations (HSI) announced its role in Operation RapTor, the global darknet drug trafficking takedown. The operation resulted in 270 arrests across the United States, Austria, Brazil, France, Germany, the Netherlands, South Korea, Spain, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom. ICE/HSI partnered with Europol, NCA, and other foreign agencies.
