DOJ Office of Public Affairs press release announcing the sentencing of UK national Nathan Wyatt to 60 months in federal prison and $1.46 million in restitution for his role in The Dark Overlord hacking group, which extorted U.S. companies including healthcare providers in the Eastern District of Missouri. Sentencing followed his December 2019 extradition from the United Kingdom and 2020 guilty plea to conspiracy and aggravated identity theft.
