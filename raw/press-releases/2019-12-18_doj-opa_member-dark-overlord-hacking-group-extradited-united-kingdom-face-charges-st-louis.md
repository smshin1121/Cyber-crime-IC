DOJ Office of Public Affairs parallel press release announcing the extradition of Nathan Wyatt from the United Kingdom to the Eastern District of Missouri to face charges related to The Dark Overlord hacking group. Wyatt was arraigned December 18, 2019, before U.S. Magistrate Judge Shirley Padmore Mensah in St. Louis on a six-count indictment including conspiracy, aggravated identity theft, and threatening damage to a protected computer.
