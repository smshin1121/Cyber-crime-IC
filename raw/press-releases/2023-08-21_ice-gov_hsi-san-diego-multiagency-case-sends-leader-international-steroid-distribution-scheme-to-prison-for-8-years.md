ICE/HSI parallel agency press release announcing the eight-year federal sentence imposed on James Charles Rivera, 32, of New York, who operated under the moniker "JuicePal" on the dark web. Rivera distributed steroids to nearly every U.S. state plus Canada, Germany, Australia, and countries in the United Kingdom and the European Union from 2018 to 2021. Used encrypted communications and cryptocurrency to evade detection, taking in millions in proceeds.
