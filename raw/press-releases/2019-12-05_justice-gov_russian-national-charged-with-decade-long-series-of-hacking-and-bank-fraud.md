### Source Page Summary

The Dridex/Evil Corp disruption and prosecution was a **multi-year international law enforcement operation** targeting the Dridex banking trojan (also known as Bugat and Cridex) and its operators within the Russia-based cybercriminal organization **Evil Corp**. The operation unfolded in two major phases: (1) the **October 2015 technical disruption and arrest** of botnet administrator Andrey Ghinkul, conducted jointly by the FBI and UK National Crime Agency (NCA); and (2) the **December 2019 indictment, sanctions, and reward** targeting Evil Corp leader **Maksim Yakubets** and deputy **Igor Turashev**, coordinated between the US DOJ, US Treasury (OFAC), and NCA.
