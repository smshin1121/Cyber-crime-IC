IRS-CI press release confirming extradition of Douver T. Braga from Switzerland to the U.S. to face 13-count indictment for wire fraud and conspiracy regarding the Trade Coin Club bitcoin investment scheme that defrauded over 100,000 investors of more than 82,000 bitcoins.
