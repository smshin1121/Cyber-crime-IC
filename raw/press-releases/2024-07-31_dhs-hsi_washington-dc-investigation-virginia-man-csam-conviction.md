DHS / HSI Washington D.C. press release confirming the EDVA conviction of Matthew Scott Rocco, 38, of Woodbridge, VA on June 22, 2024 for possession of child sexual abuse material. HSI agents executed an April 23, 2023 federal search warrant at Rocco's residence and seized a cellphone containing darknet links and a folder titled "Balalaika_1" with ten CSAM videos.
