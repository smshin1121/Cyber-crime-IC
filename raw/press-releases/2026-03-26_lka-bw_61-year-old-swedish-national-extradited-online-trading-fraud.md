# Press release notice (raw archive)

This file archives the ingestion-time reference to the official press release issued by the Cybercrime-Zentrum (CCZ) at the Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Karlsruhe in joint statement with Landeskriminalamt Baden-Württemberg (LKA-BW). The release was distributed via presseportal.de (the standard German federal/state government press-release distribution platform used by BKA and all 16 state LKAs), under the LKA-BW issuer account (pm/110980). Issuer signature is "Landeskriminalamt Baden-Württemberg" — tier-1 subnational German police agency.

## Key facts (extracted from release, 2026-03-26 10:15 CET)

- Headline (German): "LKA-BW: Cybercrime-Zentrum erwirkt Auslieferung einer 61-jährigen Tatverdächtigen aus Schweden wegen mutmaßlicher Beteiligung an organisiertem Online-Trading-Betrug"
- Issuing agencies: Cybercrime-Zentrum (CCZ) at Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Karlsruhe + Landeskriminalamt Baden-Württemberg (LKA-BW)
- Defendant: 61-year-old Swedish national (female), located in Helsingborg, Sweden
- Charge: aiding and abetting (Beihilfe zum Betrug) organized online-trading fraud targeting German victims
- Fraud amount: investor deposits totaling more than EUR 1.17 million
- Suspect compensation: approximately EUR 280,000 received over several years for her role
- Victim count: 24 victims across multiple German states (mehrere Bundesländer)
- Operative method: criminal group operating from at least 2018 ran professionally designed online "trading" platforms offering fake investment products in financial derivatives. The Swedish suspect allegedly set up corporate accounts and ledger structures used to receive and forward victim deposits.
- International cooperation: European Arrest Warrant (Europäischer Haftbefehl) issued by CCZ Karlsruhe; executed by Swedish authorities → extradition to Germany in March 2026; defendant in pretrial detention (Untersuchungshaft) since arrival
- Status: investigation ongoing (Ermittlungen dauern an); further perpetrators not yet identified in the release

## L24 verbatim quote (foreign-LE cooperation acknowledgement)

> "Die 61-jährige schwedische Staatsangehörige wurde im März 2026 aufgrund eines vom CCZ erwirkten Europäischen Haftbefehls aus Schweden nach Deutschland ausgeliefert und befindet sich seither in Untersuchungshaft."

(English: "The 61-year-old Swedish national was extradited from Sweden to Germany in March 2026 on the basis of a European Arrest Warrant obtained by the CCZ and has been in pretrial detention ever since.")

This sentence explicitly acknowledges the bilateral judicial cooperation channel: the German prosecutor (Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Karlsruhe / CCZ) issued the EAW; Swedish judicial authorities executed it and surrendered the defendant. EAW is a tier-1 formal IC mechanism under Framework Decision 2002/584/JHA. ≥2-country requirement satisfied: Germany (Baden-Württemberg LKA + CCZ Karlsruhe) and Sweden (extraditing judicial authority).
