DEA press release announcing federal indictment of seven defendants — Kelldon Hinton, Heshima Harris, Emanuel Payton, Marvin Ogman, Shawn Stephens, Arnaldo Echevarria, and Cheryle Tyson — for operating a large-scale counterfeit pill manufacturing operation out of an East Haven, CT garage. Investigators determined Hinton purchased protonitazene, dimethylpentylone, xylazine, and pill press parts from China and elsewhere, manufacturing counterfeit oxycodone, Xanax, and Adderall pills containing methamphetamine and synthetic opioids, then sold them on the darknet and shipped via U.S. Mail. Between Feb 2023 and Feb 2024, more than 1,300 packages were mailed nationwide; one seized pill press could produce 100,000 pills per hour. Five suspects were arrested Sept 5, 2024.
