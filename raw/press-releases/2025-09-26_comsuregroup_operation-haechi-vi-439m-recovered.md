## Summary

Comsure Group summarized HAECHI VI as a 40-country anti-financial-crime operation that recovered USD 439 million, blocked more than 68,000 bank accounts and froze around 400 crypto wallets. The article focused on the recovery trend across the HAECHI series and highlighted the South Korea-UAE, Thailand and Portugal case examples carried in the INTERPOL release.
