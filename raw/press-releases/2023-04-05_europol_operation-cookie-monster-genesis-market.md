## Summary-Only Archive

Europol reported that an unprecedented 17-country law enforcement operation took down Genesis Market, a marketplace selling stolen account credentials and digital identities. The action day on 4 April 2023 resulted in 119 arrests, 208 property searches, and 97 knock-and-talk measures.

The source states that the international sweep was led by the FBI and Dutch National Police, with a command post at Europol's headquarters in The Hague. Europol EC3 had supported the investigation since 2019 through data analysis, operational meetings, and information exchange. Eurojust supported the cross-border judicial-cooperation layer.
