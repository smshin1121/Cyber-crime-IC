## Summary

European Affairs Magazine described the June 2015 action as Operation Triangle and reported that the group targeted companies through man-in-the-middle and email-intrusion tactics to divert legitimate payments. The article also noted the EUR 6 million fraud total and identified suspects as mainly coming from Nigeria, Cameroon and Spain.
