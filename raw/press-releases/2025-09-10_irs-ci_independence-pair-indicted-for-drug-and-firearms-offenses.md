IRS-CI republishes the Independence, Mo. drug/firearms indictment of April Henderson and Donnell Timley. Confirms August 28, 2025 DEA search yielded 1.1 kg cocaine, 317 g powder fentanyl, 100 fentanyl pills, 10 lbs marijuana, 8 oz suspected liquid PCP, five firearms, and over $66,000 cash. Investigating agencies: DEA, IRS-CI, Kansas Highway Patrol, Independence Police Department.
