## Summary

CNN reported that the joint INTERPOL-EFCC action in Port Harcourt led to the arrest of a Nigerian suspect tied to a USD 60 million scam network and the arrest of a second suspect aged 38. The article repeated that victim businesses were located in Australia, Canada, India, Malaysia, Romania, South Africa, Thailand and the United States.
