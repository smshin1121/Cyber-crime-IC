### Key Findings

- INTERPOL reported a 14-country pan-African operation targeting romance scams and sextortion.
- Public results included 260 arrests, 1,235 electronic devices seized, 1,463 victims identified, and 81 infrastructures disrupted.
- The operation ran from 2025-07-28 to 2025-08-11.
