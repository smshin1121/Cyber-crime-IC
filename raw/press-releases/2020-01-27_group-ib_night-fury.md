## Summary

Group-IB described Operation Night Fury as a joint action with INTERPOL and Indonesian cyber police against the GetBilling JavaScript skimmer group. The post said the suspects infected hundreds of e-commerce websites and were arrested in Indonesia after intelligence sharing with INTERPOL’s ASEAN cyber team.
