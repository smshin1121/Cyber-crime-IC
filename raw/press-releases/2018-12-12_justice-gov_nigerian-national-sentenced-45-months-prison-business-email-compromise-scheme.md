Adeyemi Odufuye, 32, of Nigeria (aliases "Micky," "Micky Bricks," "Yemi," "GMB," "Bawz," "Jefe"), was sentenced on December 12, 2018 by U.S. District Judge Janet C. Hall in New Haven to 45 months of imprisonment plus restitution of $921,497.87 for his supervisory role in a business e-mail compromise scheme that targeted hundreds of CFOs and controllers at U.S. businesses, nonprofits, and schools. Odufuye was arrested in the UK in December 2016 while a student at Sheffield Hallam University and extradited to face the Connecticut indictment.
