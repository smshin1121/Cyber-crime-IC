DOJ Office of Public Affairs press release announcing federal charges against four defendants — including Ryan Andrew Gustafson — in connection with the international Uganda-based cyber counterfeiting scheme. Documents the cross-border (Uganda-Pittsburgh) distribution chain of counterfeit Federal Reserve Notes and dark-web sales via Community-X.
