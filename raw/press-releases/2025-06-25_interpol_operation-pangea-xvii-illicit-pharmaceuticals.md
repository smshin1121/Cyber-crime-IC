## Summary-Only Archive

INTERPOL announced Operation Pangea XVII, a December 2024 to May 2025 operation across approximately 90 countries targeting the online sale of illicit pharmaceuticals.

The release reports 769 arrests, 123 criminal groups dismantled, 50.4 million doses seized, an estimated USD 65 million in illicit pharmaceuticals seized, 1,728 investigations, 847 search warrants, and approximately 13,000 criminal-linked websites, social media pages, channels, and bots shut down.

INTERPOL also lists participating jurisdictions and support from national health regulatory agencies, Europol, the International Narcotics Control Board, the Pharmaceutical Security Institute, the Transnational Alliance to Combat Illicit Trade, UNODC, the Universal Postal Union, the World Customs Organization, and the World Health Organization.
