IRS-CI Boston Field Office press release on the Brazilian-national rideshare/delivery fraud ring. Although focused on co-defendants Edvaldo Rocha Cabral and Julio Vieira Braga, the release explicitly references the parent docket "U.S. v. Wemerson Dutra Aguiar and U.S. v. Priscila Barbosa, et al." and confirms IRS-CI participation as a parallel federal agency. 16 of 19 defendants arrested at time of release.
