FBI press release announcing charges against four individuals — including two officers of the Russian Federal Security Service (FSB) — for the 2014 hack of Yahoo. Defendants include FSB officers Dmitry Dokuchaev and Igor Sushchin, plus criminal hackers Alexsey Belan (Russian national) and Karim Baratov (Kazakhstan-born Canadian citizen). The FSB officers are charged with paying, directing, and protecting Belan and Baratov to steal information from over 500 million Yahoo accounts including journalists, U.S./Russian government personnel, and corporate employees. Largest computer hacking case ever charged by the U.S. government.
