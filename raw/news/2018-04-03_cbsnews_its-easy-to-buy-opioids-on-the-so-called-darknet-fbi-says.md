CBS News (Associated Press wire) reports the early-2018 J-CODE (Joint Criminal Opioid Darknet Enforcement) arrests including Antoin Austin (DARKKING22) of Euclid, Ohio. Federal agents purchased fentanyl from DARKKING22 via undercover bitcoin buys; packages were traced to Wickliffe Post Office and the supplier identified as a Chinese fentanyl distributor. The piece situates the case among 8 J-CODE arrests with weapons/computer seizures and 2,000+ lethal doses recovered, and 19 overdose deaths attributed to similar darknet supply chains.
