## Summary-Only Archive

Ars Technica reported that seven Estonian and Russian defendants were indicted in the DNSChanger malware and advertising-fraud scheme. The article describes more than 4 million infected computers worldwide, more than USD 14 million in revenue, and the DNSChanger mechanism that redirected victims' DNS traffic through criminal-controlled servers.
