Local LA TV station ABC7 (KABC) coverage of the August 1, 2022 federal jury verdict against Argishti Khudaverdyan, 44, of Burbank, on 14 federal counts tied to the $25M T-Mobile unlock scheme operated out of an Eagle Rock store. Article notes black market resale of unlocked phones and the use of stolen funds to buy properties in Burbank and Northridge; sentencing scheduled October 17, 2022 with potential 165-year maximum.
