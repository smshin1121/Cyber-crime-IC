## Summary-Only Archive

The Register reported that a U.S.-led undercover operation headed by Secret Service agents produced 28 arrests across seven countries and targeted identity-fraud groups trading data and documents through Shadowcrew, CarderPlanet, and Darkprofits. The article repeats the 1.7 million stolen credit-card number and USD 4.3 million loss figures.

The article also identifies cooperation with UK, Canadian, Europol, Bulgarian, Belarusian, Polish, Swedish, Ukrainian, and Dutch counterparts.
