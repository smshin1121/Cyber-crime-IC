## Summary

CBS Baltimore: 26-year-old Sheldon Kennedy of Lincoln, Nebraska was taken into custody in Miami and charged with conspiracy to traffic controlled substances and related offenses. Federal complaint filed January 31, 2014, unsealed February 20, 2014. Court documents allege undercover Baltimore agents purchased cocaine and a pistol from Kennedy through Silk Road; he also allegedly sold counterfeit currency to someone in Maryland. Silk Road context: $1+ billion in illicit transactions January 2011-September 2013, ~1 million registered users.
