Reports on the sentencing of Juan R. McCullum, a former staff member of Virgin Islands Del. Stacey Plaskett, to a year and a day in federal prison for cyberstalking after he distributed stolen private images of the congresswoman and her spouse. Co-defendant Dorene Browne-Louis pled guilty to a federal cyber-related charge and a D.C. obstruction-of-justice offense. Sentencing was in U.S. District Court for the District of Columbia.
