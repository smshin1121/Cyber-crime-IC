CBS News (with AP) initial-charges coverage of two former U.S. House employees charged with crimes related to distributing private nude images of a Congress member and her spouse. Juan McCullum faced two counts of cyberstalking after allegedly obtaining unauthorized access to a colleague's iPhone and sharing intimate photos via fake email and social media accounts. Coworker Dorene Browne-Louis was charged with obstruction of justice for false statements.
