# PJ envolvida em operacao que acabou com o 'Mobdro', maior plataforma de TV pirata na Internet

Correio da Manha reported that Portugal's Policia Judiciaria participated in the operation against Mobdro and that a Portugal-hosted server was deactivated, with stored data and hardware seized. The report described the action as involving Spain, Andorra, the Czech Republic, and Portugal.

This digest is retained as a Portugal-side media corroboration for the cross-border hosting and seizure component of the Mobdro operation.
