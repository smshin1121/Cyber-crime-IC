CyberScoop coverage of the DOJ unsealing of an indictment in Pittsburgh against three Chinese nationals — Wu Yingzhuo, Dong Hao, and Xia Lei — who allegedly worked for the China-based internet security firm Boyusec (Guangzhou Bo Yu Information Technology Co. Ltd). The three are accused of computer hacking, theft of trade secrets, conspiracy, and identity theft against Moody's Analytics, Siemens AG, and Trimble Inc. between 2011 and May 2017. Industry researchers (FireEye, Recorded Future) link Boyusec to the cyber-espionage group APT3.
