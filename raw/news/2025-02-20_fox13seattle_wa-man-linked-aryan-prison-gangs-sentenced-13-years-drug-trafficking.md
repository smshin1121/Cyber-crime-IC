FOX 13 Seattle reports on Shawn Ellis being sentenced to 13 years in federal prison after his role in a drug trafficking operation tied to Aryan prison gangs in Washington state. Ellis was a prolific redistributor with two prior felony drug convictions who was prohibited from possessing firearms; the case is part of the broader takedown that resulted in approximately two dozen federal arrests.
