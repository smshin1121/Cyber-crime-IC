## Summary

Ukrainian national "Denis K" arrested in Alicante, Spain on March 26, 2018, identified as alleged mastermind of the Carbanak/Cobalt criminal operation responsible for over EUR 1 billion in losses across 100+ financial institutions in 40+ countries. Joint investigation by Spanish National Police, Europol (EC3), and FBI. Seized assets included EUR 500,000 in computers/jewelry, two luxury vehicles, two houses worth ~EUR 1 million, and ~15,000 bitcoins. The gang used spear-phishing to deploy Carbanak malware against bank employees, then commanded ATMs to dispense cash and transferred funds via compromised internal networks. The arrest was the result of the multi-year cross-border task force involving European national police, Europol, FBI, and private-sector intelligence partners.
