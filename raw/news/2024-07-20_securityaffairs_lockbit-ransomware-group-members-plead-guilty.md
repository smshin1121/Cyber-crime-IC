Security Affairs reports the July 2024 guilty pleas in Newark federal court (D.N.J.) by Astamirov (alias "BETTERPAY") and Vasiliev (alias "Ghostrider"). Astamirov agreed to forfeit $350,000 seized cryptocurrency; Vasiliev caused $500,000+ damages including school targets. Article notes both face up to 25 and 45 years respectively.
