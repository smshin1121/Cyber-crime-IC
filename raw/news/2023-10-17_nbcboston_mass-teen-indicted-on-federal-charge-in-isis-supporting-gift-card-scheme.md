NBC Boston (Asher Klein, Mike Pescaro) reports the federal grand jury indictment of Mateo Ventura, 18, of Wakefield, MA, on one count of knowingly concealing the source of material support to a foreign terrorist organization (ISIS). Ventura allegedly used $705 in gift cards intended for resale on the dark web to fund ISIS activities. Independent local-press confirmation of the DOJ press release.
