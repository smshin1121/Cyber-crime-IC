Bloomberg reports that the U.S. unsealed charges against Rostislav Panev, dual Russian-Israeli national, in the District of New Jersey for serving as a developer of LockBit ransomware. The article frames the charging within Operation Cronos and ongoing pursuit of LockBit conspirators.
