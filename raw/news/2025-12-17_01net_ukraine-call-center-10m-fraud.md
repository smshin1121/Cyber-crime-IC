# Phone scams: criminal call center stole more than EUR 10 million from 400 people

01net reported that a network of fraudulent call centres in Ukraine defrauded more than 400 European victims of over EUR 10 million. The report described bank-adviser and police impersonation, urgency pressure, false supporting documents, remote-control software, and physical card-collection scenarios. It also summarized the 72 searches in Dnipro, Ivano-Frankivsk, and Kyiv, the 12 arrests, and seized evidence including vehicles, weapons, a polygraph, computers, cash, and forged police or bank employee IDs.
