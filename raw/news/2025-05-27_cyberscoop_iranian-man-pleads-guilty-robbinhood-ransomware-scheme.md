CyberScoop reports Iranian national Sina Gholinejad pleaded guilty for his role in the Robbinhood ransomware scheme that severely damaged Baltimore's municipal services in 2019. Co-conspirators targeted cities, corporations, and healthcare organizations. The plea came in the Eastern District of North Carolina; Gholinejad faces up to 30 years. The article also references additional municipal victims including the City of Gresham, Oregon, and the City of Yonkers, New York.
