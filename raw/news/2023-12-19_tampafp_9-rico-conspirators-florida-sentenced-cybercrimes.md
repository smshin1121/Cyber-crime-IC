Tampa Free Press covered the December 2023 sentencing of nine RICO conspirators in MDFL who used the dark web to purchase server credentials of Certified Public Accounting and tax preparation firms, then exfiltrated tax returns of thousands of clients. The enterprise claimed more than $45 million in false tax refunds.
