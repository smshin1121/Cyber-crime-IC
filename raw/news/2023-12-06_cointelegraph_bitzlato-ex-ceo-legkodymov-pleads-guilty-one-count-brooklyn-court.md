Cointelegraph coverage of Anatoly Legkodymov's guilty plea in EDNY (Brooklyn) before Judge Eric Vitaliano. Legkodymov, a Russian national who resided in Shenzhen, China, agreed to dissolve Bitzlato and release any claim over approximately $23 million in seized assets.
