Cincinnati Enquirer (via Yahoo News, Cameron Knight) reports the sentencing of PillCosby/Slanggang ringleaders Khlari Sirotkin (16 years) and Sean Deaver (18 years). Operating on dark web marketplaces including Silk Road, Empire, Dream, Sleep, and Nightmare and accepting Bitcoin/cryptocurrency, they sold an estimated 33.9 kg of fentanyl in five months in 2019. Three other indicted co-defendants were also covered.
