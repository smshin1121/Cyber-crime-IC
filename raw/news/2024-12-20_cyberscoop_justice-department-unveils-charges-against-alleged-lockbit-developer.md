CyberScoop reports on the December 20, 2024 unsealing of charges in the District of New Jersey against Rostislav Panev, dual Russian-Israeli national, for his role developing LockBit ransomware. The article notes Panev was arrested in Israel pursuant to a U.S. provisional arrest request and awaits extradition. Quotes U.S. Attorney Philip R. Sellinger and references Operation Cronos disruption from February 2024.
