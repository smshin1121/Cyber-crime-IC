CyberScoop reports that Deniss Zolotarjovs, a 33-year-old Latvian national living in Russia, was arrested in Georgia in December 2023 and extradited to the United States in August 2024 to face charges including money laundering conspiracy, wire fraud conspiracy, and Hobbs Act extortion. Zolotarjovs appeared in federal court in Cincinnati on August 27, 2024. Court documents authored by FBI Special Agent Connor Lentz allege he operated under the alias "Sforza_cesarini" within the Karakurt extortion group, conducting "cold case" extortion negotiations against victims. The investigation linked him via cryptocurrency wallet activity and dark-web chat communications obtained through a confidential source.
