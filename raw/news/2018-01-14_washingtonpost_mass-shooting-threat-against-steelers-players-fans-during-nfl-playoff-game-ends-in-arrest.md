Washington Post coverage of the arrest of Yuttana Choochongkol, 41, of San Antonio, Texas, on charges of making a terroristic threat after sending threatening messages to KDKA-Pittsburgh and Heinzfield.com saying he would conduct a mass shooting at the AFC playoff game between the Steelers and Jaguars at Heinz Field on January 14, 2018. The IP address was traced to Worldwide Clinical Trials, a San Antonio company. The game went on as planned after authorities apprehended Choochongkol.
