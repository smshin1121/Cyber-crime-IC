Same-day SecurityWeek coverage of the FBI/DOJ seizure of WeLeakInfo.com. Confirms the international coalition (FBI, UK NCA, Dutch National Police Corps, German BKA, PSNI) and the same-day arrests in Northern Ireland and the Netherlands. Notes 12 billion records from 10,000+ breaches and subscription pricing as low as $2/day.
