# CBI busts two illegal call centres cheating Japan citizens; 6 held

Hindustan Times reported that CBI arrested six individuals and unearthed two fake call centers under Operation Chakra V after raids at 19 locations across Delhi, Haryana, and Uttar Pradesh. The report said the accused posed as Microsoft and Apple support representatives and allegedly defrauded Japanese victims of more than INR 1.2 crore, approximately 20.3 million Japanese yen.

The report corroborates the Japan-victim scope, call-center pattern, and loss estimate for the Chakra-V operation record.
