## Summary-Only Archive

Wired reported the Operation Card Shop global carding-ring bust, describing the FBI's Carder Profit undercover forum, 11 U.S. arrests, 13 foreign arrests, more than 30 search warrants, and the notification of more than 411,000 compromised payment cards.
