HuffPost coverage following the January 24, 2017 federal jury conviction of Howard Webber for the prison-identity tax fraud scheme. Reports that Webber and co-conspirator Clifford Bercovich filed more than 700 false tax returns using fellow inmates' identities, obtaining over $600,000. Webber was sentenced May 24, 2017 to seven years.
