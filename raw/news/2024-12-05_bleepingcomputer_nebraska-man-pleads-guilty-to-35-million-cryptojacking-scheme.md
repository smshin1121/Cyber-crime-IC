BleepingComputer report on Charles O. Parks III ("CP3O") pleading guilty to wire fraud in EDNY for a multi-million-dollar cryptojacking scheme that defrauded two cloud computing providers out of more than $3.5 million in computing resources, mining cryptocurrency including Ether, Litecoin and Monero with proceeds of nearly $1 million. Article notes Parks used aliases such as MultiMillionaire LLC and CP3O LLC and faces forfeiture including a Mercedes-Benz luxury vehicle.
