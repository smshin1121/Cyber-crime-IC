The Hacker News reports John Telusma, the 14th member of the Infraud Organization to be sentenced, received a 4-year prison term for his role as a prolific buyer/user of stolen credit card data. Article details the Infraud Organization's seven-year activity period, $568M+ in losses, 4M+ stolen card numbers, and 16-country joint operation that arrested 13 members in February 2018.
