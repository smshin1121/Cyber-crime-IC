# 11 arrests during actions against fraudulent call centre

Border Security Report summarized the Eurojust-coordinated Latvia-Lithuania-Ukraine action against a fraudulent call centre in Dnipro. The report repeated the core action-day figures: 11 arrests, 32 searches, EUR 400,000 cash seized, two cryptocurrency wallets, eight luxury cars, and seizure of electronic evidence. It also described the fake cryptocurrency investment scheme and use of remote-access software to move victim funds.
