## Summary

Eveline Cismaru pleaded guilty to federal charges in DC for the January 2017 ransomware attack that disabled approximately two-thirds of MPD's outdoor surveillance cameras (126 of 187 DVRs) just before the 2017 Presidential Inauguration. Co-conspirator Mihai Alexandru Isvanca; ransom demand $60,800 in Bitcoin (not paid). The conspiracy used the compromised infrastructure to attempt mass distribution of stolen credentials and ransomware against up to 170,000 other computers worldwide. Wire-fraud conspiracy: max 20 years; computer-fraud conspiracy: max 5 years. Sentencing scheduled December 3, 2018.
