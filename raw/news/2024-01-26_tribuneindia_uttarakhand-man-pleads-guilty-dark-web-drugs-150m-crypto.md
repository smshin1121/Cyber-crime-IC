The Tribune (India) reports the Banmeet Singh dark web narcotics guilty plea in U.S. federal court. The article emphasizes the $150M cryptocurrency/cash seizure described by UK National Crime Agency as the largest single DEA seizure. Singh, originally from Uttarakhand, India, was arrested in London in 2019 and extradited to the United States in 2023.
