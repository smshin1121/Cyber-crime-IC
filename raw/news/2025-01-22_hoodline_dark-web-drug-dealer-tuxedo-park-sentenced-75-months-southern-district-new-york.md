Hoodline reports that Kyle Weiland, 36, of Tuxedo Park, New York, was sentenced to a total of 75 months in prison (63 months for the new charges plus 12 months consecutive for supervised release violation) by U.S. District Judge Cathy Seibel in Manhattan federal court on January 22, 2025. Weiland pleaded guilty in September 2024 to distributing methamphetamine and selling misbranded drugs sold via the dark web. As part of the plea, he agreed to forfeit $2,093,887.72 including cash, three luxury vehicles (two Maserati GranTurismos and a McLaren 570S), and various cryptocurrencies. DEA investigated.
