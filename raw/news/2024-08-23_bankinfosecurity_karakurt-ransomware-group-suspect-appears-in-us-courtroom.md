BankInfoSecurity (Mathew J. Schwartz) reports on Deniss Zolotarjovs, the 33-year-old Latvian national arrested in Georgia in December 2023 and extradited to the United States in August 2024 on a four-count indictment in the Southern District of Ohio (conspiracy to commit money laundering, wire fraud, Hobbs Act conspiracy, and extortion). Operating under "Sforza_cesarini," prosecutors allege he served as Karakurt's extortion specialist, attempted to recruit journalists to pressure victims, and participated in rebranding efforts (TommyLeaks, SchoolBoys). A federal judge ordered detention pending trial citing flight risk.
