Fortune (with Bloomberg) coverage of August 3, 2023 plea by Lichtenstein and Morgan. Lichtenstein pleaded guilty to orchestrating the 2016 Bitfinex hack of 119,756 BTC; couple forfeited nearly $73M, with Lichtenstein facing up to 20 years and Morgan up to 5 years.
