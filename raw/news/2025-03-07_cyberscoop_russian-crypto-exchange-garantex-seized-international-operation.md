CyberScoop trade-press coverage of the international takedown of Russian cryptocurrency exchange Garantex on March 7, 2025. The operation involved the U.S. Secret Service, DOJ, sanctions bodies, and international partners. The unsealed EDVA indictment charged Aleksej Besciokov and Aleksandr Mira Serda for processing approximately $96 billion in transactions linked to ransomware, terrorism financing, and sanctions evasion.
