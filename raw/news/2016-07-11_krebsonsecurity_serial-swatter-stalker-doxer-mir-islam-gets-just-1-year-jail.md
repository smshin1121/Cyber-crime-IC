KrebsOnSecurity firsthand reporting on Mir Islam's July 11, 2016 sentencing. Brian Krebs, himself a victim of Islam's swatting in 2013, attended the sentencing. Sentence: 2 years; with credit for time served, ~1 year remaining. Sentencing guidelines were 37-46 months; judge imposed less.
