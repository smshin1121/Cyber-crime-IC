## Summary-Only Archive

Krebs on Security reported on the FBI briefing for Operation Trident Breach. The article identifies five SBU detentions, Zeus/Jabber Zeus malware, at least 390 U.S. company victims, more than 3,500 money mules, eight SBU search warrants, 39 U.S. arrests, 92 U.S. charges, and a U.K. money-mule charging component.

The article also records the Netherlands Police Agency high-tech crime role in monitoring Dutch infrastructure used by the miscreants and helping investigators connect the dots.
