BleepingComputer coverage of John Telusma's four-year prison sentence for his role in the Infraud Organization. The piece details the international scope of the case (defendants apprehended across the US, Australia, UK, France, Italy, Kosovo, and Serbia) and Telusma's role as one of the most prolific dump-vendors during the 2011-2017 period.

## Extracted Summary

John Telusma (37, NY) sentenced to four years in prison for selling/using stolen and compromised credit cards on the Infraud carding portal. Infraud Organization operated October 2010 to February 2018 with approximately 10,901 registered members by March 2017. Over four million stolen credit and debit cards traded; victim losses exceeded $568 million. Of 13 defendants apprehended across the US and six other nations (Australia, UK, France, Italy, Kosovo, Serbia), multiple received sentences of 7-10 years.
