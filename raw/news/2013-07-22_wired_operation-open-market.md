## Summary-Only Archive

Wired reported the undercover mechanics of Operation Open Market, including the Secret Service's use of the Celtic online identity and a government-run fake-ID storefront to build evidence against Carder.su participants. The article reports four indictments against 55 defendants in 10 countries and explains the relationship between the undercover ID operation, Carder.su trust structures, and follow-on cybercrime prosecutions.
