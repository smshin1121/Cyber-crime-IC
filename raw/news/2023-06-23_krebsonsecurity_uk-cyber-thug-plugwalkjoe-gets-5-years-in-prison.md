KrebsOnSecurity coverage of Joseph James O'Connor (PlugwalkJoe) sentencing. The 24-year-old U.K. national was extradited from Spain in April 2023 and pleaded guilty to participating in the July 2020 Twitter hack and a separate years-long SIM-swapping campaign that included cryptocurrency theft and cyberstalking. O'Connor was sentenced to five years in prison and ordered to forfeit $794,012.64.
