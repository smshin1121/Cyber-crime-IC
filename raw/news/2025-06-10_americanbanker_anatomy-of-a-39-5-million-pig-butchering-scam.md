## Summary

American Banker investigative anatomy of the multi-defendant pig-butchering scheme that netted over $36.9 million. Five defendants pleaded guilty including Shengsheng He (39, La Puente, CA), Joseph Wong, Yicheng Zhang, Jose Somarriba, and Jingliang Su. Scheme operated from scam centers primarily in Cambodia. Axis Digital Limited (Bahamian entity, November 2021) received funds from U.S. shell company accounts and converted them to Tether (USDT) via Deltec Bank and Trust Limited (Bahamas) between June 2022 and July 2023. Victims were initially contacted via social media, telephone, text messages, and online dating services. He agreed to $7,560,014 restitution.
