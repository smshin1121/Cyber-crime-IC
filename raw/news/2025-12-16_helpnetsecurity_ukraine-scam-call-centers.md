# European police busts Ukraine scam call centers

Help Net Security reported that law enforcement authorities from several European countries arrested 12 people in a Eurojust-supported action against scam call centres operating from Ukraine. The report described bank and police impersonation, remote-access installation, banking-detail capture, 72 searches in three Ukrainian cities, seizure of devices and other evidence, 45 identified suspects, more than 400 known victims, and over EUR 10 million in losses.
