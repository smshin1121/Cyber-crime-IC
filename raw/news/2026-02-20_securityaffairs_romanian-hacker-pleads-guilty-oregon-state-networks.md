Security Affairs reports the Catalin Dragomir guilty plea in U.S. federal court for selling access to Oregon state government networks. Dragomir was arrested in Romania in November 2024 following an indictment returned by a federal grand jury in Portland on May 21, 2024. Extradition to the U.S. occurred in January 2025.
