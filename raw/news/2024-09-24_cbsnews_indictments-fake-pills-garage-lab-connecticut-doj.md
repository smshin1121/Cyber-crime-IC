CBS News national coverage of the federal indictment unsealed in District of Connecticut charging Kelldon Hinton and six others with operating a counterfeit pill manufacturing scheme out of an East Haven garage. Reports the use of dark web markets, U.S. Mail distribution, and Chinese precursor chemicals. Highlights the scale of the seizures: hundreds of thousands of counterfeit pills and one of the largest pill-press systems seen in New England.
