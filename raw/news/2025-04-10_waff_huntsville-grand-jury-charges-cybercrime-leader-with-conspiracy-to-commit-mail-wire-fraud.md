Huntsville television station WAFF reports that a federal grand jury in Huntsville charged Kareem Mustafa Ahmad, 21, of Alexandria, Egypt, with conspiracy to commit mail and wire fraud on April 10, 2025. Ahmad allegedly used the Telegram persona "Noir" to operate "Noir's Luxury Refunds," a refund-fraud-as-a-service channel with over 5,900 followers that ran from July 2020 to July 2022. Investigators say the group also developed malware to circumvent retailer fraud-prevention controls. The article identifies cooperating private-sector entities (Target, Amazon, Walmart, Dell, and Google's CyberCrime Investigation Group) and notes a maximum penalty of 20 years' imprisonment.
