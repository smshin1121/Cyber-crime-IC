Local DC ABC affiliate WJLA covers former House staffer Juan McCullum's sentencing to one year and one day in prison for cyberstalking Delegate Stacey Plaskett (Virgin Islands). McCullum stole nude images and videos from the congresswoman's iPhone in 2016 while posing as someone repairing it, then distributed the material via email and social media. Co-defendant Dorene Browne-Louis faced separate charges for false statements to law enforcement.
