BleepingComputer reports that Feras Khalil Ahmad Albashiti (also known as "r1z," "Feras Bashiti," and "Firas Bashiti"), a 40-year-old Jordanian national, pleaded guilty in the District of New Jersey to fraud involving access credentials. He sold unauthorized access to at least 50 corporate networks for cryptocurrency to an undercover U.S. law enforcement officer on May 19, 2023, and operated under the moniker "r1z" on the XSS.is forum. He was extradited from Georgia in July 2024 via the DOJ Office of International Affairs. Sentencing is scheduled for May 11, 2026 before Judge Michael A. Shipp.
