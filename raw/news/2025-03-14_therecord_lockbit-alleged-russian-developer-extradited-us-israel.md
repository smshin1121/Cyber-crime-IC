The Record reports on Rostislav Panev's March 13, 2025 court appearance before U.S. Magistrate Judge André Espinosa in Newark, following his extradition from Israel to the District of New Jersey. The 51-year-old dual Russian-Israeli national faces 40 charges related to LockBit ransomware development and faces up to 185 years in prison if convicted on all counts.
