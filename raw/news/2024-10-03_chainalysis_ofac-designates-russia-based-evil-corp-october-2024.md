## Summary

October 1, 2024 trilateral US-UK-Australia OFAC/FCDO/DFAT designations targeting Evil Corp members. Maksim Yakubets (founding leader) and numerous affiliates designated. Investigations revealed overlap between Evil Corp and LockBit — evidence Evil Corp used LockBit as a rebranding mechanism to evade sanctions. Yakubets had previously worked for Russia's FSB. Family network involved: father Viktor Yakubets and father-in-law Eduard Benderskiy (former FSB officer who coordinated activities between Evil Corp and Russian intelligence).
