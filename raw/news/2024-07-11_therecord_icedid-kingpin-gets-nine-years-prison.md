The Register reports the July 2024 sentencing of Vyacheslav Penchukov to nine years in federal prison, three years of supervised release, and $73 million in restitution following his February 2024 guilty plea on racketeering and wire-fraud conspiracy. Sentencing occurred in the District of Nebraska after extradition from Switzerland.
