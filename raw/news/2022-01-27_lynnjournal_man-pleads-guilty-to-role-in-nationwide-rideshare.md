Lynn Journal local Massachusetts press coverage of Wemerson Dutra Aguiar's January 2022 guilty plea in Boston federal court for the nationwide rideshare/delivery account fraud conspiracy. Confirms 29-year-old Brazilian national, formerly residing in Lynn and Woburn, MA. Plea was on conspiracy to commit wire fraud and aggravated identity theft. Between June 2019 and January 2021 Aguiar received over $375,000 from selling and renting fraudulent driver accounts.
