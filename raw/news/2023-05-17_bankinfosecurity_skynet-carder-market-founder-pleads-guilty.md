BankInfoSecurity (Rashmi Ramesh) reports the May 2023 guilty plea of Michael Mihalo (Illinois) to eight counts related to operating the Skynet Market darknet carding site. The article specifically notes: "A third member of the conspiracy, British national Simon Kaura, faces an extradition request by the United States." This contemporaneous reference establishes the U.S. extradition request that culminated in Kaura's 2024 transfer.
