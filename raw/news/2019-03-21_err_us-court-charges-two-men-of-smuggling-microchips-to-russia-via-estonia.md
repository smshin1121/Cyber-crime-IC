Estonian Public Broadcasting (ERR) English-language coverage via BNS reporting Valeri Kosmatšov's arrest in Tallinn on September 12, 2018, and his extradition to the United States on March 14, 2019. Reports the detention hearing in California federal court on March 20, 2019. Confirms charges against Kosmatšov and Sergey Vetrov for using Estonia-based companies (Adimir OU, Eastline Technology OU) as fronts to misrepresent end-users to procure US microelectronics destined for Russia.
