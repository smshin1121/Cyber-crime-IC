## Extracted Summary

Yuttana Choochongkol (San Antonio TX area) arrested January 14, 2018 for threats against AFC Divisional playoff game (Jaguars vs Steelers) at Heinz Field. Written message sent to Heinz Field security director and Pittsburgh TV station KDKA. Threat content: planned to "kill Steelers football players and fans" before suicide. Terroristic threat (3rd-degree felony); up to 10 years prison. FBI Pittsburgh + Pittsburgh Police coordinated to apprehend before game. Mayor Peduto publicly thanked law enforcement.
