## Summary-Only Archive

Wired reported that more than 100 alleged phishers were charged in the United States and Egypt in a two-and-a-half-year investigation called Operation Phish Phry. The article describes 53 U.S. defendants, 47 Egyptian alleged co-conspirators, phishing of bank credentials, and money movement through U.S. accounts.
