## Summary-Only Archive

Diario Libre reported Dominican Republic-specific details from Operation Eclipse, including two arrests in the Dominican Republic and INTERPOL's wider 60-arrest, 65-victim operation across nine countries.

The report also described Panama-Dominican coordination that located two suspects wanted by Panamanian authorities in Dominican territory and triggered extradition proceedings.
