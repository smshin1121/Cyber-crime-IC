Fox61 (Hartford-area broadcast) news report covering the federal indictment of Amitoj Kapoor and Siddharth Lillaney, both 29 of Glastonbury, on charges of using approximately 3,000 stolen identities to game promotional bonus systems at FanDuel and other online gambling platforms, generating around $3 million since 2021. Highlights the use of darknet markets and the encrypted Telegram messenger to acquire victim PII, plus background-search subscriptions used to bypass identity verification.
