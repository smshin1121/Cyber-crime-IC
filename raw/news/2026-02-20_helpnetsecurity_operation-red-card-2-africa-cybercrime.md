### Key Findings

- Help Net Security reports Operation Red Card 2.0 as an INTERPOL-supported cybercrime sweep involving law-enforcement agencies from 16 African countries.
- It confirms the 651 arrests, USD 4.3 million recovery, USD 45 million loss exposure, 1,247 identified victims, 2,341 seized devices, and 1,442 infrastructure takedowns.
