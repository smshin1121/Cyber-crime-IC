NPR coverage of the FBI's January 15-16, 2020 seizure of WeLeakInfo.com, a clearnet site that aggregated more than 12 billion records from over 10,000 data breaches and sold subscription access for as little as $2/day. The article confirms international cooperation with the UK National Crime Agency (NCA), the Netherlands National Police Corps, the German Bundeskriminalamt, and the Police Service of Northern Ireland, and notes that two 22-year-old suspects were arrested the same day in Northern Ireland and the Netherlands.
