Infosecurity Magazine reports Banmeet Singh, an Indian national arrested in London in April 2019 and extradited to the U.S. in 2023, pleaded guilty to dark web narcotics and money laundering conspiracy. The article details the European Court of Human Rights extradition appeal that delayed his transfer until 2023 and the $150M forfeiture (largest in DEA history per UK NCA).
