## Summary

Operation Endgame Phase 2 (May 19-22, 2025): coordinated US-European law enforcement action that dismantled ransomware infrastructure (300 servers, 650 domains taken offline), seized approximately $3.5 million, and resulted in arrest warrants for nearly two dozen individuals. The U.S. Justice Department charged 16 alleged members of the DanaBot malware operation, which infected more than 300,000 computers and facilitated $50 million in ransomware/fraud damage. Notable named defendants include Aleksandr Stepanov and Artem Aleksandrovich Kalinkin, both of Novosibirsk, Russia. The operation also targeted Bumblebee, Latrodectus, Qakbot, Hijackloader, TrickBot, and WarmCookie loader/dropper families. CrowdStrike, Google, and Amazon assisted.
