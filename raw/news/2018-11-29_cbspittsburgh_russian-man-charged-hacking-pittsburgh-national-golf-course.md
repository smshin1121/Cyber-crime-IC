CBS Pittsburgh covers the November 29, 2018 indictment of Ilya Kulkov of Barnaul, Russia. Confirms the cross-border hacking pattern: hundreds of fraudulent eBay accounts created in 2016; January-February 2017 hacking of Pittsburgh National Golf Course PC to bypass eBay suspensions; merchandise routing to Russia. Federal arrest warrant issued. International cooperation case (Russian fugitive).
