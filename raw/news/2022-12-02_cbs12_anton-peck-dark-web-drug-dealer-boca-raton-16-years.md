CBS12 (Kennedy McKinney) reports the December 1, 2022 sentencing of Anton Peck, 29, of Boca Raton, FL to 16 years in federal prison for running a dark web drug distribution operation under the vendor name "Syntropy." Co-conspirator Kevin Fusco (34) received 11 years; Vincent Banner (31) was scheduled for sentencing February 2023. Peck maintained over 6,000 customers nationwide. Evidence was seized in West Palm Beach, Boca Raton, and New York City.
