Brooklyn Eagle, the local newspaper for the EDNY courthouse district, reports that Charles O. Parks III ("CP3O") was sentenced on August 15, 2025 to one year and one day in prison for his cryptojacking scheme. Article notes the forfeiture of $500,000 and a Mercedes-Benz purchased with proceeds, with restitution to be determined.
