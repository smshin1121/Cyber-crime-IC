Local10 (WPLG Miami) reports the February 2026 arrest of Michael Ramsden, 23, of Coquitlam, British Columbia, Canada, on conspiracy to commit wire fraud and mail fraud charges connected to the Noir's Luxury Refunds Telegram scheme. Ramsden, who used the handle "Reign", was booked into Broward County jail. The article confirms the leader Kareem Mustafa Ahmad ("Noir") is based in Alexandria, Egypt, and that the group operated as a refund-fraud "concierge" service from July 2020 to July 2022.
