BleepingComputer reports Poland arrested a suspect in the international Phobos ransomware operation as part of a coordinated multi-country takedown. Two Russian nationals, Roman Berezhnoy and Egor Glebov, were arrested as part of the disruption; the operation also included server seizures in Thailand. Phobos victimized over 1,000 entities globally and collected over $16M in ransoms.
