# A group that provided 2 million people with a cheap way to connect to multiple paid streaming services such as Netflix is arrested

GIGAZINE reported on the June 2020 European pay-TV and streaming piracy action, citing Eurojust and Bloomberg. It corroborated 11 arrests, more than 50 servers, more than two million users, services such as Netflix, Amazon Prime Video and HBO, Spain-centered operation, and payment through PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency.

This digest is retained as English-language media corroboration for the operation-level record while Eurojust remains the controlling source for judicial-cooperation details.
