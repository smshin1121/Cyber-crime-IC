ABC 6 (Matthew Thomas) reports the September 2020 indictment of five defendants including Khlari Sirotkin (Colorado), Sean Deaver (Nevada), Kelly Stephens, Abby Jones, and Sasha Sirotkin for operating the PillCosby/Slanggang/Slanggangcentral dark-web drug trafficking conspiracy. Federal agents recovered 2.5 kg of fentanyl, ~6,000 lbs of marijuana, 10 firearms, and $80,191 in cash. The action was part of a broader international enforcement effort yielding 179 arrests and $6.5 million seized.
