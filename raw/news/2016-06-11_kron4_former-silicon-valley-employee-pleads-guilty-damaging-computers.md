KRON4 (Bay Area television news) coverage of Kenneth Kezeor's guilty plea. Kezeor, 47, of Felton, California, admitted to attacking the corporate computer application of his former Silicon Valley employer (Agilent Technologies) for about four months after his October 2012 termination, using his administrator accounts to cause damage. Faces statutory maximum of 10 years and $250,000 fine.
