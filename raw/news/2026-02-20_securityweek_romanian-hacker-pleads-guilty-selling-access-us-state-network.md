SecurityWeek reports Catalin Dragomir, 45, of Constanta, Romania, pleaded guilty in the District of Oregon to obtaining information from a protected computer and aggravated identity theft for selling access to an Oregon state government office network and other U.S. victims. Dragomir operated under the handle "inthematrix1" as an initial access broker. He was arrested in Romania in November 2024 and extradited to the U.S. in January 2025. Sentencing scheduled for May 26, 2026.
