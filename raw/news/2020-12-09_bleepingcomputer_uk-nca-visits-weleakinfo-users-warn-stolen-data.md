Follow-up reporting on the UK National Crime Agency's December 2020 follow-on operation against UK customers of WeLeakInfo: 21 arrests of UK customers and 69 cease-and-desist visits, executed roughly 11 months after the initial January 2020 domain seizure. Demonstrates a sustained joint US-UK enforcement cycle that began with the WeLeakInfo.com seizure.
