The Record reports the May 2022 DOJ/FBI seizure of weleakinfo.to, ipstress.in, and ovh-booter.com. Confirms partner agencies: Netherlands National Police Corps and Belgian Federal Police, with arrest of a main subject, multiple search warrants, and seizure of server infrastructure. Independent press coverage of the international footprint of the operation.
