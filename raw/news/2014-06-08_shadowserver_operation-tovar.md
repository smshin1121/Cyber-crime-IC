## Summary-Only Archive

Shadowserver described Operation Tovar as a joint effort by international law-enforcement agencies including the FBI, UK National Crime Agency, Europol EC3, and private partners. Shadowserver stated that it provided operational infrastructure and infection data to support victim notification and remediation.

The source also explains Gameover Zeus's peer-to-peer communications, fallback domain-generation algorithm, and remediation-reporting categories relevant to the takedown.
