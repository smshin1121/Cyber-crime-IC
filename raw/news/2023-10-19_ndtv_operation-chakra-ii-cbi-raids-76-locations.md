# CBI Launches Operation Chakra-2 Against Cyber Crime, Raids 76 Locations

NDTV, carrying a Press Trust of India report, described CBI searches at 76 locations under Operation Chakra-2 after five cyber-enabled financial fraud cases were registered. The report said two cases arose from Amazon and Microsoft complaints involving call centers posing as technical support for foreign nationals, that nine call centers were searched, and that CBI acted on inputs from FIU, FBI, INTERPOL, and other international agencies.
