## Summary-Only Archive

La Nacion reported Costa Rica-specific details from Operation Eclipse, including 18 arrests in Costa Rica and the broader INTERPOL-reported totals of 60 arrests across the Americas during the February 2025 to January 2026 operation.

The source describes the operation as a joint INTERPOL-police effort against offences involving child sexual abuse and exploitation material, with investigations spanning nine countries.
