## Summary-Only Archive

DefenceWeb reported that Operation Catalyst was coordinated by INTERPOL and AFRIPOL from July to September 2025 in Angola, Cameroon, Kenya, Namibia, Nigeria, and South Sudan. It corroborates the 83 arrests, including 16 linked to cyber-enabled scams and 18 linked to illicit virtual-asset use, and notes strategic cyber intelligence and private-sector data from Binance, Moody's, and Uppsala Security.
