Seattle local news coverage of Shawn Ellis, the Renton man sentenced in U.S. District Court in Tacoma to 13 years in prison for his role in a drug trafficking ring connected to Aryan prison gangs. Ellis ordered up to 30 pounds of methamphetamine at a time and was found with buckets of fentanyl pills, kilos of methamphetamine, cocaine, fake Xanax pills, four guns including a rifle hidden in a violin case, body armor, and a homemade silencer.
