Daily Chronicle reports the September 2023 arrest of Waylan R. Graves, 52, after JNET (Joint Narcotics Enforcement Team), Thurston County Narcotics Task Force (TNT), and DEA found 4,700-5,000 suspected fentanyl pills in his car at a Chehalis hotel. Recovered: 28g meth, 26g heroin, 27g fentanyl powder, $2,800 cash, and drug paraphernalia. $1M bail.
