Audacy/KNSS Radio (Wichita) reports that David L. Mongeau, 54, who operated Mongeau Enterprises in Holcomb, Kansas, pleaded guilty to one count of false statements related to crop insurance and one count of bank fraud. The 2020 USDA crop insurance claim concealed sale of over 33,000 bushels of corn, causing $241,645 in FCIP losses. Bank fraud caused over $300,000 in losses to First National Bank of Syracuse. Sentencing set for July 1, 2026.
