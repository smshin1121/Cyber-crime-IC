BleepingComputer trade-press coverage of Igor Dekhtyarchuk's indictment in the Eastern District of Texas and addition to the FBI Cyber Most Wanted list. Reports the operation of "Marketplace A" carding shop (48,000+ compromised email accounts, 39,000+ online accounts), 13 covert FBI purchases between March-July 2021, and charges of wire fraud, access device fraud, and aggravated identity theft.
