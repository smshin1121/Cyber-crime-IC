Patch (New Haven) local news coverage of the federal indictment of seven Connecticut defendants for operating a large-scale counterfeit pill manufacturing scheme out of East Haven. Reports DEA, FBI, and U.S. Postal Inspection Service involvement; describes the seized pill presses, several hundred thousand counterfeit pills, and the dark-web/U.S. Mail distribution model.
