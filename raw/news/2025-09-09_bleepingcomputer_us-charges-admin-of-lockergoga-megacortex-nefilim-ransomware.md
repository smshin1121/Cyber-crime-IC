BleepingComputer reports on the EDNY indictment of Volodymyr Viktorovich Tymoshchuk (aka deadforz, Boba, msfv, farnetwork), a Ukrainian national charged as administrator for LockerGoga, MegaCortex, and Nefilim ransomware operations. Article details the $10M State Department reward for information leading to his arrest, and notes 250+ victim networks compromised.
