CNN reporting on the FBI's announcement that they had thwarted an alleged mass-shooting plot at Heinz Field during the AFC Divisional playoff game between the Pittsburgh Steelers and Jacksonville Jaguars. Yuttana Choochongkol of San Antonio was arrested after threats were sent to local Pittsburgh broadcaster KDKA's website and Heinz Field's website.
