Boston.com (Shannon Murphy) reports that Mateo Ventura, 21, of Wakefield, MA was sentenced to 50 months in federal prison plus 7 years supervised release for concealing the source of material support to ISIS. Ventura had pleaded guilty in October 2024. Coverage adds details on $1,670 total donations across multiple periods and the defendant's stated intent to fund a "war on kuffar."
