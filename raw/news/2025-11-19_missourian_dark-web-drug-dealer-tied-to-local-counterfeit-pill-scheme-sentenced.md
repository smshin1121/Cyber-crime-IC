## Summary

John Cruz (31, NY) sentenced to 54 months in federal prison for purchasing counterfeit Xanax from a Sullivan, Missouri-based pill-press operation led by Brandon Adams. Cruz bought at least $66,000 worth of counterfeit pills shipped to Rochester, NY. Original guilty plea November 2022; he then continued operating darknet marketplace "The Anonymous Shop" while under indictment. New July 2025 charges added for resumed dark-web sales using different cryptocurrencies and alias "mrmastR." The conspiracy used chemical precursors imported from China and a pill press capable of >16,000 pills/hour.
