Industry trade publication FrankOnFraud profiles the "Noir's Luxury Refunds" Telegram operation as part of FBI's broader "Operation Chargeback" investigation. The article names additional indicted members (Aiman Akram Kaif "@Dior", Damion Wayne Scarlett "@Dash", Dhruv Gargi "@Panda", and six others) and details five fraud tactics including malware development to bypass retailer fraud controls. Stolen goods were resold on eBay and to wholesalers; estimated millions in fraudulent refund claims processed.
