CoinDesk reports on the District of New Jersey's $54 million civil forfeiture of cryptocurrency tied to Christopher Castelluzzo's narcotics distribution scheme (2010-2015) on darknet marketplaces. Castelluzzo had used Bitcoin earnings to buy 30,000 ETH in Ethereum's 2014 ICO; the holding has appreciated from ~$9,000 to over $54 million. Castelluzzo is currently serving 20-year concurrent federal/state sentences.
