## Summary-Only Archive

Peters & Peters summarized INTERPOL's Operation Eclipse as a nine-country Americas operation resulting in 60 arrests and 65 child victims identified and safeguarded.

The source highlights the operation's reassessment of 57 existing INTERPOL Notices, including Red and Blue Notices, and notes that 12 individuals had been located and arrested with further tracing and extradition efforts ongoing.
