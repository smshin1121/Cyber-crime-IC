## Summary

Nathaniel Dunlap (28, South Carolina) charged in federal court in Pittsburgh with cyberstalking and aggravated identity theft for harassing a Pennsylvania woman by sending fake hostage messages to Pittsburgh pizzerias (December 2016-January 2017). In February, Dunlap sent a message claiming to be Dakota James, a missing Duquesne University graduate student whose body was later found in the Ohio River. Prosecutors did not disclose motive. Initial appearance scheduled before federal magistrate in Pittsburgh.
