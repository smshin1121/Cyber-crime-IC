Las Vegas Sun republication of Associated Press story on Kristy Lynn Felkins's 5-year federal sentencing on 21 July 2023 for murder-for-hire. In 2016 she paid $5,000 in bitcoin (and offered $4,000 more to expedite) on a dark web hitman website to kill her ex-husband in Chico, California. The website turned out to be a scam.
