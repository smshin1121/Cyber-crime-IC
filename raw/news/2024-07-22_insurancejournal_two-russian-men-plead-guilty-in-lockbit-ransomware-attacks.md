Insurance Journal (republishing Bloomberg's David Voreacos) reports the July 18, 2024 guilty pleas by Astamirov (21, Chechen Republic) and Vasiliev (34, Bradford, Ontario, dual Canadian-Russian) in Newark federal court. Article details Vasiliev's UK and Switzerland educational targets and Astamirov's victims in France, Florida, Tokyo, Virginia, Kenya. Sentencing scheduled for January 8.
