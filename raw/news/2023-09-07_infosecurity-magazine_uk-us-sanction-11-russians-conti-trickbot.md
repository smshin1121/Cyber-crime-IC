Infosecurity Magazine coverage of the September 7, 2023 coordinated US-UK sanctions package against 11 Russians tied to Conti/TrickBot. Documents joint OFAC + FCDO action with FBI + NCA investigations.

## Extracted Summary

11 Russians sanctioned: Zhuykov, Galochkin, Rudenskiy, Tsarev, Putilin, Khaliullin, Loguntsov, Valiakhmetov, Kurov, Chernov, Mozhaev. All 18 (incl. 7 from February 2023 wave) face travel bans, asset freezes. US Treasury OFAC + UK FCDO joint announcement; FBI + UK NCA investigations. DOJ concurrently unsealed indictments against nine individuals. UK Foreign Secretary James Cleverly: "exposing their identities... dismantling their business models."
