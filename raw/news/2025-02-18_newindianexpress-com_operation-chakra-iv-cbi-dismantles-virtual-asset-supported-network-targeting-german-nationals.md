# Operation Chakra IV: CBI dismantles virtual asset-supported cybercrime network targeting German nationals

The New Indian Express reported that CBI, in close coordination with German authorities, dismantled a virtual-asset-supported cybercrime network targeting German nationals under Operation Chakra-IV. The report described the 12 February 2025 case registration, searches at six locations from 14 to 17 February, one arrest, an illegal call center in West Bengal, and recovered digital evidence.

The report also described the remote-support fraud pattern: accused persons allegedly gained unauthorized access to German victims' computers and bank accounts, falsely claimed accounts were compromised, and induced transfers to cryptocurrency wallets.
