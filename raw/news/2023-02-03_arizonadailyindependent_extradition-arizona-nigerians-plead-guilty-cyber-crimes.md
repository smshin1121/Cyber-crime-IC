Arizona Daily Independent (local press) reports the guilty pleas in District of Arizona by Solomon Okpe and Johnson Obogo following extradition. The article details the BEC, work-from-home, check-cashing, romance, and credit card scams executed from Nigeria and Malaysia, and the years-long extradition contest from Malaysia.
