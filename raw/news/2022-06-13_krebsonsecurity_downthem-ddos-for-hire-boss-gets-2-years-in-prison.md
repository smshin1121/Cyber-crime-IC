Brian Krebs' security trade-press write-up of the June 13, 2022 sentencing of Matthew Gatrel (DownThem.org / AmpNode.com) to two years in federal prison after a nine-day Central District of California trial. Article situates the case within the broader 2018 federal crackdown on 15 booter domains and notes a co-defendant (Juan Martinez) who pleaded out separately.
