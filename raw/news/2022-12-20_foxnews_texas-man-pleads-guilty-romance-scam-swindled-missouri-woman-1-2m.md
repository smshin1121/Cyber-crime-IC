## Summary

Rotimi Oladimeji (37, Texas) pleaded guilty in EDMO to two counts mail fraud, two counts wire fraud, one count conspiracy to commit mail/wire fraud after defrauding a St. Louis woman of $1.2 million in an online romance scam. Fraudsters impersonated a Belgian national supposedly stranded in the UAE on a business deal. Victim sent or attempted to send ~$931,000 directly to scammers; additional $314,000 paid to two Texas-based money mules. Oladimeji arrested in Atlanta February 2021 by HSI agents with incriminating text messages on his phone. Co-defendant Olumide Akinrinmade (Richardson, TX) pleaded not guilty; Adewale Adesanya sentenced to 4 years in related case. Sentencing for Oladimeji scheduled March 23, 2023.
