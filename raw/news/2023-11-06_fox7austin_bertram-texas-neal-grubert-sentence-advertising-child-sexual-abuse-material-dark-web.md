FOX 7 Austin local Texas coverage confirming Neal Grubert (35, of Bertram, Texas) sentencing to 15 years in U.S. federal court (D. Mass., Boston) for advertising CSAM on dark-web sites. Confirms the Swedish-origin investigation: Swedish authorities began the case in May 2020 after identifying a Swedish citizen producing CSAM, leading to identification of Grubert as a U.S.-based site administrator.
