Help Net Security covers the District of New Jersey guilty plea by Feras Khalil Ahmad Albashiti, 40, to fraud and unauthorized network access. The article reports that the FBI identified Albashiti after he demonstrated an EDR bypass exploit on an FBI-monitored server, and traces his IP address to a ransomware attack causing $50+ million in damages to a U.S. manufacturing company. Albashiti was extradited from Georgia in July 2024 and sentenced May 11, 2026 to up to 10 years.
