Healthcare Fraud Group LLC reports the February 12, 2021 federal complaint charging Kevin Ombisi (31) and Eric Russell Jr. (35) of Katy, Texas, in the Western District of Tennessee for darknet/Wickr-based distribution of counterfeit Adderall pills containing methamphetamine. Cooperating agencies named: DOJ Criminal Division, WDTN USAO, DEA Louisville, ICE/HSI El Paso and Houston, and FDA Kansas City. Over $5 million in assets seized.
