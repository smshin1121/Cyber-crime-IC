Hoodline Pittsburgh local news coverage of Tai Jauna Jones's guilty plea to credit card fraud and aggravated identity theft. Jones acquired credit card numbers and personal information from the dark web and worked with a complicit manager at a Monroeville rental car outlet to enable rentals of over 140 unauthorized vehicles, causing more than $500,000 in financial damages. Investigation by U.S. Secret Service and Pittsburgh Bureau of Police.
