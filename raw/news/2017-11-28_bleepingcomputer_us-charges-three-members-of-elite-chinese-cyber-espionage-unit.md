BleepingComputer trade press coverage of the U.S. indictment of three Chinese nationals — Wu Yingzhuo, Dong Hao, Xia Lei — for cyber-espionage on behalf of Boyusec. Reports the link to the APT3 advanced persistent threat group, which has been associated with the Chinese government's Ministry of State Security. Indictment unsealed in U.S. District Court for the Western District of Pennsylvania.
