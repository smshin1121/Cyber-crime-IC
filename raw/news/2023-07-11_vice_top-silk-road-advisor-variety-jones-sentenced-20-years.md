## Extracted Summary

Roger Thomas Clark (alias "Variety Jones") sentenced July 11, 2023 to 20 years. Arrested Thailand December 2015, pleaded guilty January 30, 2020. US Attorney Damian Williams: "central figure in helping to lead Silk Road and in advocating violence, even murder, to protect this digital drug empire." Motherboard and security researcher La Moustache identified Clark's real identity in September 2015 through leaked emails.
