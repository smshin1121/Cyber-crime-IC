WALA Fox10 (Mobile) reports the December 2024 sentencing of Sean Donnell White (30, Theodore, AL) to 32 months for bank fraud conspiracy, aggravated identity theft, and wire fraud. White used a USPS "arrow" key (purchased for $2,500 from postal employee Kristen Arieale Williams) to steal mail at Bel Air Mall, Mobile. Separately, White bought stolen identities on dark web marketplaces and used them to open Coinbase + bank accounts for cryptocurrency overdraft fraud.
