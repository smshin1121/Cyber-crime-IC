## Extracted Summary

George Sotiris Vlastos (38, Pittsburgh) pleaded guilty November 5, 2024 to making false statements on ATF Form 4473 (Firearms Transaction Record) in attempt to purchase firearm as convicted felon. At least two false written statements. Sentencing scheduled April 2, 2025 (later moved to May 8, 2025). Up to 10 years prison max, $250,000 fine. AUSA Kelly M. Locher prosecuting; investigated by ATF; Judge Cathy Bissoon presiding.
