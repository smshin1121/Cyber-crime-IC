BleepingComputer reports U.S. federal indictment of Russian nationals Maksim Yakubets and Igor Turashev for their roles in the Evil Corp cybercriminal organization operating Dridex/Bugat malware. The article details Treasury's tri-lateral sanctions with UK and Australia and FBI/NCA cooperation in the investigation.
