## Summary

DOJ charged Tal Prihar (37) and Michael Phan (34), both Israeli citizens, with one count of conspiracy to commit money laundering for operating DeepDotWeb (DDW). Prihar arrested by French police in Paris; Phan arrested simultaneously by Israeli police. DDW operated as a darknet news/reviews site funneling users to marketplaces with 4% referral commissions; received approximately 8,155 bitcoins (peak value $15.4 million), nearly half from AlphaBay. DDW referred over 198,000 users to Hansa marketplace (~half its user base) and was linked to >23% of AlphaBay transactions. FBI seized the DDW website following arrests.
