## Summary-Only Archive

Homeland Security Today reported on Operation Catalyst as an INTERPOL-AFRIPOL six-country operation announced in October 2025. The article corroborates the 83 arrests figure, 160 persons of interest, cyber-enabled scams, illicit virtual-asset use, approximately USD 260 million identified, and approximately USD 600,000 seized.
