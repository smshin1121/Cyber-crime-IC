Organized Crime and Corruption Reporting Project (OCCRP) covered the August 2023 guilty pleas of Adetunji Adejumo and Ibrahim Jinadu in the MDFL Tampa transnational tax fraud case. The article notes the parallel UK extradition proceedings against Olufemi Odedeyi and Ibrahim Davies.
