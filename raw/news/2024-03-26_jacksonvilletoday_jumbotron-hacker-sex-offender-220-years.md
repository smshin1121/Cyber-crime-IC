Local Jacksonville press coverage of Samuel Arthur Thompson's 220-year federal sentence. Thompson was convicted of producing and possessing child sex abuse material and hacking the Jaguars stadium jumbotron after being terminated as a contractor in 2018. Article details the connection between the hacking and the discovery of the child exploitation material during the FBI investigation.
