Sahara Reporters (Nigerian press) reports the U.S. sentencing of Solomon Ekunke Okpe to four years and one month in prison for BEC and other cyber-enabled fraud targeting U.S. victims. Co-defendant Johnson Uke Obogo received one year and one day. The article emphasizes Okpe's prior detention in Malaysia and the bilateral extradition cooperation that brought him to U.S. custody.
