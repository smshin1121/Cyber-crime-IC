CBS News (with AP) reports the October 9, 2018 sentencing of Gal Vallerius, a 36-year-old French national from Brittany, to 20 years in prison after pleading guilty to drug conspiracy and money laundering tied to the Dream Market darknet marketplace. Vallerius was arrested in August 2017 at Atlanta's Hartsfield-Jackson Airport while traveling from Paris for the World Beard and Mustache Championships. The DEA traced him through writing-pattern analysis (use of "cheers," double exclamation marks). U.S. District Judge Robert Scola imposed half the maximum penalty.
