The Columbian (Vancouver, WA local newspaper) reports the federal sentencing of Joseph Anthony Cannarozzi, 41, to 10 years in prison. Identified in May 2021 as the source of potentially deadly fentanyl, Cannarozzi was selling fentanyl pills and anabolic steroids through the mail. October 2021 search warrant resulted in seizure of 10,000 fentanyl pills, suspected steroids, and six firearms.
