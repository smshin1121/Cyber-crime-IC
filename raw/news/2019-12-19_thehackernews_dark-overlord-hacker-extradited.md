Cybersecurity trade press coverage of Nathan Wyatt's extradition from the UK to the U.S. on charges related to The Dark Overlord hacking group. Article details Wyatt's history with the group dating to 2016, the group's blackmail of U.S. healthcare and accounting firms, ransom demands in bitcoin between $75,000 and $350,000, and the group's prior leak of unreleased Netflix episodes.
