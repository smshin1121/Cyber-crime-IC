## Summary

Larry Dean Harmon, operator of dark web cryptocurrency service Helix, entered a guilty plea to money laundering charges on August 18, 2021. Helix moved over 350,000 bitcoin (USD 300+ million at the time) between 2014 and 2017, partnering with AlphaBay, Evolution, and Cloud 9 marketplaces. Harmon also ran Grams, a dark web search engine. Plea agreement: forfeit 4,400+ bitcoin (over USD 200 million); maximum 20 years in prison, $500,000 fine. FinCEN previously imposed $60 million civil penalty (October 2020) for Bank Secrecy Act violations.
