Darknet-focused trade publication DarknetLive reports the SDAL sentencing of Laderian Odom (28, Monroeville, AL) to 24 months for one count of possession of fifteen or more unauthorized access devices. The piece situates Odom's April 2023 arrest within Operation Cookie Monster, the internationally coordinated takedown of Genesis Market that involved search warrants at 208 properties worldwide and 119 arrests.
