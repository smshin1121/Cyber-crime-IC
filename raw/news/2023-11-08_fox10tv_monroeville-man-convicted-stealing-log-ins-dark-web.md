Mobile, Alabama Fox affiliate WALA-TV reports Laderian Odom's two-year federal sentence in the SDAL Genesis Market case. The article notes the FBI shut down Genesis Market in April 2023 — a marketplace operating since 2018 with "over 80 million account access credentials" — and confirms Judge Moorer's denial of a sentence reduction after Odom lied on a handgun-purchase form.
