WTNH News 8 broadcast-news coverage of the April 2025 federal indictment of two Stamford men, Tasean Lemar Brown and Stanley Charles, for fentanyl-trafficking conspiracy. Reports the postal service-based bulk shipments from California and Arizona, the seizure of nearly 4 kg of fentanyl-laced narcotics, and the multi-agency investigative team that included the DEA, USPIS, and Connecticut State Police.
