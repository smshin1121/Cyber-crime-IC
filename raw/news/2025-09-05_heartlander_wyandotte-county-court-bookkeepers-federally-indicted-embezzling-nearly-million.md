The Heartlander reports the August 27, 2025 federal indictment of Julia Roberts and Vicki Robinson, both former Wyandotte County District Court bookkeepers, on wire fraud conspiracy, wire fraud, and aggravated identity theft charges. Scheme spanned January 2018-February 2023 with 400+ unauthorized checks ($189-$10,000) totaling $900,000+.
