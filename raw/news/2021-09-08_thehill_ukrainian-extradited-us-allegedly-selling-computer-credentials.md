The Hill reports the September 2021 extradition of Glib Oleksandr Ivanov-Tolpintsev from Poland to the US Middle District of Florida on charges of conspiracy, trafficking in unauthorized access devices, and trafficking in computer passwords. The article emphasizes the DOJ allegation that he ran a botnet capable of decrypting credentials for 2,000 computers per week.
