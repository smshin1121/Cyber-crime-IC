## Summary-Only Archive

Cybernews reported on Operation SOGA X as an INTERPOL-coordinated illegal online gambling crackdown spanning 28 countries and territories between June and July 2024.

The article corroborates more than 5,100 arrests, more than USD 59 million recovered, tens of thousands of illicit football betting websites shut down, and examples involving scam-centre victims in the Philippines, an online gambling ring in Vietnam, asset seizures in Thailand, and fake or mule betting accounts in Greece.
