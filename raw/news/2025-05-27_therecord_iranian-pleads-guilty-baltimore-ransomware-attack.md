The Record reports Iranian national Sina Gholinejad pleaded guilty in U.S. federal court to charges connected to the 2019 Robbinhood ransomware attack on Baltimore that disrupted property tax processing, water bills, and parking citations for months and caused over $19M in damages. The plea was entered in the Eastern District of North Carolina. He faces up to 30 years in prison.
