Crypto.News covers the D.N.J. civil forfeiture of $54M ETH from Castelluzzo's darknet drug ring. Article documents 2014 ICO purchase of 30,000 ETH for ~$9,000, growth to ~$54M, and additional Cardano/Solana/Avalanche/Algorand/BNB/Uniswap/Polkadot tokens. Asset transfers to government wallets occurred in July 2022 and August 2023.
