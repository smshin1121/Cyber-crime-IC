Hoodline Boston (Sam Cavanaugh) coverage of Neal Staton Grubert's 15-year sentence for advertising CSAM. Important: confirms Sweden-United States international cooperation. Investigation began in Sweden where authorities arrested a Swedish citizen producing CSAM showing the abuse of his three-year-old niece. Phone forensics from the Swedish defendant identified Grubert as a U.S.-based co-administrator of the dark-web sites. Swedish defendant received 7 years in Sweden; Grubert received 15 years in U.S. federal court in Boston.
