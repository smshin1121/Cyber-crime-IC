# What Is Operation Chakra V? Inside CBI's Crackdown On Global Cyber Scam Targeting Japanese Citizens

ABP Live reported that Operation Chakra V targeted a tech-support scam aimed at Japanese citizens. The report corroborated six arrests, coordinated searches at 19 locations, two illegal call centers dismantled, and the public-private cooperation pattern involving CBI, Japan's National Police Agency, and Microsoft.

This digest is retained as an independent media corroboration for the Chakra-V operation-level record.
