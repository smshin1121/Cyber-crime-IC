SecurityWeek (Eduard Kovacs) covers a trend piece on the surge in U.S. prison sentences for Nigerian cybercriminals in late 2024, identifying Simon Kaura as one of the cases — extradited from the United Kingdom and sentenced in September 2024 to five years for selling stolen financial information on the dark web.
