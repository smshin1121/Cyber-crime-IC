Small Business Trends (Joshua Sophy) reports the Gary James Harmon sentencing (4 years 3 months) for stealing 712 BTC (~$4.8 million) from IRS-seized wallets using credentials from his brother Larry Dean Harmon, operator of the Helix darknet bitcoin mixer (which laundered 350,000+ BTC valued at $300 million+). Gary consented to forfeiture of cryptocurrency and assets now valued over $20 million.
