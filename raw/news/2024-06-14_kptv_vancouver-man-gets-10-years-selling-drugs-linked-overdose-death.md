KPTV (Portland-Vancouver Fox affiliate) reports the federal sentencing of Joseph Cannarozzi, 41, of Vancouver, WA, to 10 years in prison. Confirms link to a 2021 Clark County overdose death and the federal investigation involving Clark County Sheriff's Office, USPS, and federal investigators.
