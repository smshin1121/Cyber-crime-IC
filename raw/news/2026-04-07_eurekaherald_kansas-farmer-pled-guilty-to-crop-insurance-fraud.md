Eureka Herald reports the guilty plea of David L. Mongeau, 54, of Holcomb, Kansas, to false statements related to crop insurance and bank fraud/attempted bank fraud. Article details the 2019 federal crop insurance policy, the false January 2020 claim of hail/wind damage in Rooks County while 33,000+ bushels of corn had been sold to commodity traders ($241,645 FCIP loss), and the bank-fraud collateral diversion ($300,000+ loss to First National Bank of Syracuse). Sentencing scheduled for July 1, 2026.
