## Summary-Only Archive

The FBI story "Cyber Bust" summarizes the October 2010 Zeus banking-fraud disruption. It says the FBI announced arrests and search warrants in multiple countries with partners in the United States, United Kingdom, Ukraine, and Netherlands.

The story records USD 70 million stolen, attempted theft of about USD 220 million, Zeus malware credential theft, money-mule laundering, and 10 New York arrests in the U.S. enforcement layer.
