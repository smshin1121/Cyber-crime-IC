Cleveland 19 News (Maddi Hebebrand) reports the August 25, 2022 sentencing of former Woodmere mayor Charles E. Smith, 52, to 33 months prison plus $10,621.23 restitution. Smith (a former MLB pitcher for the Florida Marlins) pleaded guilty May 19, 2022 to one count of access device fraud and one count of aggravated identity theft. He purchased over 600 stolen credit/debit cards from the dark web (affecting victims in 25 states), used them to buy gasoline at Northern Ohio stations, and used the gas to fuel his Amazon delivery vehicles.
