KRON4 (San Francisco Bay Area NBC affiliate) reports on Tony Tan's sentencing to 18 months for operating "Adderall123" dark-web vendor account, which distributed counterfeit Adderall pills laced with methamphetamine via USPS Priority Mail flat rate envelopes. Tan, 28, of San Francisco, forfeited $17,744 in USD plus seized cryptocurrency (ETH, BTC, LTC).
