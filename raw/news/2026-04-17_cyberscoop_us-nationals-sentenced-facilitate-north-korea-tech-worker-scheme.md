CyberScoop reports U.S. nationals Kejia Wang (108 months) and Zhenxing Wang (92 months) sentenced in the District of Massachusetts for facilitating a DPRK remote IT worker scheme that placed North Korean operators at 100+ U.S. companies using stolen identities of 80+ U.S. persons. The scheme generated $5M+ in illicit revenue for the DPRK government. Multi-agency investigation involved DOJ National Security Division, FBI, and U.S. Treasury sanctions enforcement.
