KSAT-TV (San Antonio ABC affiliate) coverage of San Antonio resident Yuttana Choochongkol's arrest after federal authorities identified him as the source of online mass-shooting threats targeting the Pittsburgh Steelers vs. Jacksonville Jaguars NFL playoff game at Heinz Field. Coverage details how the IP address used to send threats traced back to Worldwide Clinical Trials in San Antonio.
