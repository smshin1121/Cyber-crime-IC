Infosecurity Magazine (UK-based cyber trade press) coverage of the federal indictment of two Glastonbury, Connecticut residents — Amitoj Kapoor and Siddharth Lillaney — for using ~3,000 stolen identities purchased on the darknet and Telegram to defraud FanDuel and other online gambling operators of approximately $3 million. Frames the case within the broader trend of darknet identity-data marketplaces and bonus-abuse fraud against sportsbook platforms.
