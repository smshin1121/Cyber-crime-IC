Al Jazeera reports U.S. federal indictment of Maksim Yakubets and Igor Turashev for operating the Russia-based Evil Corp cybercriminal organization that deployed the Dridex/Bugat malware to harvest banking credentials globally. The Western District of Pennsylvania returned a 10-count indictment. State Department offered a $5M reward for information leading to Yakubets' arrest, the largest cybercrime reward at that time.
