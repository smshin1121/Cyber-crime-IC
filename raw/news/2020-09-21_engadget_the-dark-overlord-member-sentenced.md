Tech press coverage of Nathan Wyatt's sentencing to five years in federal prison plus $1.46 million in restitution for his role in The Dark Overlord hacking group, following extradition from the UK and guilty plea. Article connects the case to broader Dark Overlord activity including extortion of healthcare providers, accounting firms, and the leak of Netflix's "Orange Is the New Black" Season 5.
