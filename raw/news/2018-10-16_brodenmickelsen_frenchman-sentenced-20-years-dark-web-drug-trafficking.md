Dallas criminal defense firm Broden & Mickelsen recap the Vallerius sentencing, noting Dream Market contained "more than 47,000 illegal drug listings at the time of the Frenchman's arrest." Vallerius sold heroin, cocaine, oxycodone, fentanyl, and methamphetamine. The DEA used undercover Dream Market purchases plus social-media writing-style analysis to identify him. Forfeiture totaled approximately $700,000 (100 BTC + 121 BCH).
