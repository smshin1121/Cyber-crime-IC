Wikipedia's DeepDotWeb entry, last revised March 21, 2026, summarizes the May 2019 multi-jurisdictional seizure (FBI + Europol affiliates), the March 2021 guilty plea, and the January 2022 97-month sentence of Tal Prihar. It records the cited DOJ figure that approximately 23.6% of AlphaBay transactions involved DeepDotWeb referral links during operation.
