Patch (Mayfield-Hillcrest, OH; reporter Chris Mosby) covers Antoin Austin's March 2018 federal charge for conspiracy to possess/distribute controlled substances. Austin used the dark web moniker "DARKKING22" to order fentanyl and analogues from China. Surveillance documented him mailing packages with false return addresses from the Wickliffe Post Office. Investigation jointly led by FBI and U.S. Postal Inspection Service.
