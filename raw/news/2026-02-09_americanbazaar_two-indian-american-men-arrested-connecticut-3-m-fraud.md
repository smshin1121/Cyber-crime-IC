American Bazaar (Indian-American community newsmagazine) report on the federal indictment of Amitoj Kapoor and Siddharth Lillaney, two Indian American men residing in Glastonbury, CT, on charges of identity theft, wire fraud, and money laundering totaling approximately $3 million in alleged FanDuel and online-gambling fraud. Reports both defendants released on $300,000 bond after appearing in New Haven federal court.
