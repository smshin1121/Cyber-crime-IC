Local Hampton Roads ABC/NBC affiliate WAVY-TV reported on the sentencing of Damier Dorsey, 29, of Newport News, who received nine years and 11 months in prison for leading a credit card fraud and identity theft scheme that caused at least $1.5 million in loss. Dorsey and co-conspirators purchased account information for victims from the BriansClub darknet marketplace and re-encoded the data onto cards for in-person fraud, including buying lottery tickets to launder proceeds. WAVY's coverage independently confirmed the EDVA prosecution announced on March 8, 2024.
