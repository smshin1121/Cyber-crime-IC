Local Western Pennsylvania newspaper coverage of Kaleb Levicky's federal sentencing in Pittsburgh to 37 months in prison for cyberstalking and threats to injure. Levicky, 23, formerly of Carmichaels, Greene County, sent threatening messages via Instagram and phone numbers to victims from November-December 2020, including a December 14, 2020 text threatening to kill hundreds of innocent people. Sentenced by U.S. District Judge J. Nicholas Ranjan.
