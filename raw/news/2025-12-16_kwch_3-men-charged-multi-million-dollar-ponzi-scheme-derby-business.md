KWCH reports the indictment of Steve Parish (54, formerly Derby), Richard Dean (66, Plano TX), and Joshua Owen (39, Derby). Parish was CEO of Premier Global Corp; Dean owned DDI Advisory Group LLC. Counts include conspiracy, securities fraud, bank fraud, money laundering, and wire fraud. Investigators: FBI, Kansas Department of Insurance Securities Division, Oklahoma Department of Securities.
