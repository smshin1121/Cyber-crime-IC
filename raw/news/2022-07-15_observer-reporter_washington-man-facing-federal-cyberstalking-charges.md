Observer-Reporter (Washington, PA local newspaper) coverage of the federal indictment of Eric Scholl, 54, of Washington, PA on cyberstalking charges. Reports the GPS tracker placed on his estranged wife's car, harassing emails/texts/calls disguised through web services, lewd internet posts, and impersonation in messages — all during the period of an active Pennsylvania Protection from Abuse Order.
