Tech Startups (Nickie Louise) profiles Alla "Max" Witte, noting she previously resided in Paramaribo, Suriname; was self-employed as a website designer; and hosted TrickBot development malware on her personal vanity website (allawitte[.]nl). She wrote code for victim-data web panels, ransomware control, payment collection, and victim status tracking using color-coded indicators.
