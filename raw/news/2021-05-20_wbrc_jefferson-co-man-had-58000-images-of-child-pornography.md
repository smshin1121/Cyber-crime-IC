WBRC (Birmingham CBS affiliate) reports the May 20, 2021 sentencing of James Curtis Brasher, 49, of Warrior, Alabama, to 78 months for possession of child pornography. The article confirms Brasher accessed CSAM through the Tor application on the Darknet, and identifies the FBI Birmingham Child Exploitation Human Trafficking Task Force, Alabama Law Enforcement Agency, and Warrior Police Department as cooperating investigators. $69,000 in restitution ordered.
