KIVI-TV (Boise ABC affiliate) reports the December 2025 federal grand jury indictment of Charles Thomas Abrahamson on wire fraud and three counts of tax fraud. Abrahamson allegedly stole over $1.45 million from Idaho American Legion 2017-2024. IRS-Criminal Investigations led the investigation.
