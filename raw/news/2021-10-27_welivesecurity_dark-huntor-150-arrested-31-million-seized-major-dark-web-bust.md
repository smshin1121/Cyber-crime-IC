WeLiveSecurity news article by Amer Owaida reporting on Operation Dark HunTor coordinated by Europol/Eurojust with arrests in nine countries including 65 in the United States. EUR 26.7 million / USD 31 million seized along with 234 kg of drugs and 45 firearms. Notes the operation built on the DarkMarket takedown earlier in 2021. Provides specialized cybersecurity-press coverage of the international darknet vendor enforcement sweep that included the Sacramento Empire Marketplace vendor (CaliPlugMike / Turrentine).
