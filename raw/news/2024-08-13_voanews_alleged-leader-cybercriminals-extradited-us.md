Voice of America reports on the extradition of Maksim Silnikau from Poland to the United States to face indictments for the Angler exploit kit malvertising scheme (D.N.J.) and Ransom Cartel ransomware (E.D. Virginia). Silnikau is described as a Belarusian-Ukrainian dual national.
