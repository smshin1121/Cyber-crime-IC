Washington Examiner reports the February 2021 arrest of Kevin Ombisi and Eric Russell Jr. for operating a darknet vendor account that sold counterfeit Adderall containing methamphetamine. DEA undercover agents purchased samples with Bitcoin; lab analysis confirmed methamphetamine. Ombisi's wallet showed approximately 113.55 BTC from 174 transactions between October 2019 and April 2020, valued at roughly $5.3 million.
