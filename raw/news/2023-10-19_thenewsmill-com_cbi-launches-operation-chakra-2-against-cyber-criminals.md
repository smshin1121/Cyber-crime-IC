# CBI launches 'Operation Chakra 2' against cyber criminals, searches over 76 locations

The News Mill carried an ANI report on Operation Chakra-II. It corroborated the nationwide CBI search action, five registered cases, 76 search locations, seizure of mobile phones, laptops, hard disks and pen drives, and the international-tech-support-fraud subset involving foreign victims and illegal call centers.

This digest is retained as an independent news-wire corroboration alongside the official PIB release and the NDTV / Press Trust of India report already present in the corpus.
