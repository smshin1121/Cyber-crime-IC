Wisconsin Law Journal (Steve Schuster) covers the September 7, 2023 indictments of nine Russian nationals across three federal jurisdictions for TrickBot malware and Conti ransomware conspiracies. The piece details the role of the FBI Cleveland Field Office leading the TrickBot investigation, lists the Tennessee local government / EMS targets, and references the SDCA Scripps Health hospital attack.
