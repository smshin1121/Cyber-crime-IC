WAFF reports the November 14, 2019 guilty plea of Ryan Thomas Carver (29, Huntsville, AL) on possession of CSAM. The case is part of the international takedown of "Welcome To Video" — described as the largest CSAM Darknet marketplace by content volume — which arrested operator Jong Woo Son in South Korea in March 2018 and produced 337 arrests across 38 countries with 23 minor victim rescues. Article credits HSI, USAO Northern District of Alabama, and USAO District of Columbia.
