CBS Pittsburgh (KDKA) coverage of Khaled Miah's sentencing to 72 months in federal prison for threatening FBI agents on Twitter and obstructing the FBI investigation. Miah, a former University of Pittsburgh student, posted threatening tweets in late December 2020 directed at FBI Pittsburgh Special Agents and the FBI generally, then deleted the posts after learning of the federal investigation. Sentence imposed by U.S. District Judge W. Scott Hardy.
