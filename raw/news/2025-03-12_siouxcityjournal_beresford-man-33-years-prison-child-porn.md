Sioux City Journal local coverage of the sentencing of Theodore John Jungbauer, 61, of Beresford, S.D., to 33 years and 4 months for receipt and distribution of child pornography on the dark web. Critically, the article confirms that Jungbauer was identified after Australian authorities discovered him distributing CSAM and contacted Homeland Security Investigations (HSI). This Australia-to-US referral is the IC element that makes this otherwise domestic prosecution an international cooperation case.
