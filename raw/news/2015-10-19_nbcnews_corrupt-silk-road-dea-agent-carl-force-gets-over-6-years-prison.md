NBC News reports that Carl Mark Force IV, a former DEA agent assigned to the Baltimore Silk Road Task Force, was sentenced to 78 months (over 6 years) for stealing bitcoin during the federal probe of the Silk Road dark-web marketplace. U.S. District Judge Richard Seeborg said the scope of his betrayal was "breathtaking." Force is one of two federal agents on the task force prosecuted; Secret Service agent Shaun Bridges was prosecuted separately.
