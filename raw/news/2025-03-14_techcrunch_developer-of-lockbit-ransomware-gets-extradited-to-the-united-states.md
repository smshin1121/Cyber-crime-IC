TechCrunch covers the March 14, 2025 extradition of Rostislav Panev from Israel to the District of New Jersey, quoting U.S. Attorney John Giordano: "If you are a member of the LockBit ransomware conspiracy, the United States will find you and bring you to justice." Notes Panev is the third person arrested for LockBit involvement.
