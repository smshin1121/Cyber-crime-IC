# Operation Chakra-V: CBI busts transnational scam that posed as tech support to cheat Japanese citizens

The New Indian Express reported that CBI arrested six people under Operation Chakra-V after searches at 19 locations in Delhi, Haryana, and Uttar Pradesh. The report said the operation dismantled two illegal call centers involved in a transnational tech-support scam targeting Japanese citizens, and that CBI worked with the National Police Agency of Japan and Microsoft Corporation to identify the perpetrators and trace the syndicate's operating structure.
