## Extracted Summary

Mihai Alexandru Isvanca and Eveline Cismaru (28, Romanian) charged in December 2017. Attack January 12, 2017 disabled 123/187 MPD surveillance cameras. Larger scheme: USA.txt file with 179,616 email addresses; ~3,800 stolen credit card accounts; fraudulent Amazon storefront "Lake L." for laundering. Ransomware variants: cerber + dharma. Ransom demand ~$60K BTC; systems restored Jan 15 without payment. Cismaru arrested in London March 23, 2018; Isvanca in Romanian custody at article date.
