Hays Post reports the September 2024 sentencing of Simon Kaura, a Nigerian citizen extradited from the United Kingdom, to five years in federal prison without parole. U.S. District Judge Greg Kays imposed the sentence; the court found intended loss of $6,338,500. Kaura pleaded guilty May 22, 2024 to conspiracy to commit access device fraud and access device fraud. He operated under aliases "apples," "applepiecards," "cartman," and "dpharoah" and helped administer the Skynet darknet market (April 2016 to October 2019). Victims spanned all 50 U.S. states, D.C., Australia, Canada, and the United Kingdom.
