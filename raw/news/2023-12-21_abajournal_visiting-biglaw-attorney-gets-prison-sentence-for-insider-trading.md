ABA Journal coverage of December 2023 sentencing of Romero Cabral Da Costa Neto, 33, Brazilian visiting attorney at Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher in DC, to two months for insider trading. Used firm's internal file system to access confidential pharmaceutical-deal information. Prosecutors had sought eight months.
