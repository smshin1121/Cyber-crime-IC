## Summary

Liridon Masurica (33, Gjilan, Kosovo, alias "@blackdb"), administrator of BlackDB.cc cybercrime marketplace, was arrested by Kosovo Police's Cybercrime Investigation Directorate on December 14, 2024 and extradited to the United States on May 9, 2025 pursuant to the U.S.-Kosovo extradition treaty. He made his initial appearance in federal court in Tampa, Middle District of Florida on May 12, 2025. Charges include one count of conspiracy to commit access device fraud and five substantive counts of fraudulent use of 15+ unauthorized access devices; maximum penalty 55 years. BlackDB.cc has operated since 2018, offering compromised credentials, stolen credit card data, and PII primarily targeting US victims. The FBI partnered with Kosovo Police on the investigation. (Note: this is the BlackDB extradition; the same DOJ press release headline pattern applies to a separate Rydox case from December 2024.)
