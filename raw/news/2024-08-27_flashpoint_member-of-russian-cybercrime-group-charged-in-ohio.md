Flashpoint analyzes the Zolotarjovs indictment, noting he was "the first alleged group member [of Karakurt] to be arrested and extradited to the United States." The post documents his Moscow residence, the Georgia arrest in December 2023, August 2024 extradition, and federal charges of conspiracy to commit money laundering, wire fraud, and Hobbs Act extortion. Karakurt's modus operandi (data theft, extortion, leaks-and-auction website) is described in context of the broader cybercrime ecosystem.
