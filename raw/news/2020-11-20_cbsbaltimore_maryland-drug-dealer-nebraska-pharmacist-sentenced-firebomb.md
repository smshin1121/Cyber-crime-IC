CBS Baltimore reported on the November 20, 2020 sentencing of William Burgamy IV (14 years) and Hyrum T. Wilson (9 years) by the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia in Alexandria. Burgamy ran the NeverPressedRx darknet vendor account selling prescription drugs; Wilson was a Nebraska pharmacist who illegally mailed over 19,000 dosage units to Burgamy. The two plotted "Operation Firewood" to firebomb a competing Auburn, Nebraska pharmacy.
