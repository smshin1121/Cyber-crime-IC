KMJ Now (Fresno-area news talk radio) report on the 27 November 2023 federal sentencing of Kevin James Strutz, 52, of Ceres, California to 3 years 4 months in prison plus 3 years of supervised release for cyberstalking two female victims. Covers the February 2020 Airbnb shower-recording incident and the 2018 letter-and-Facebook campaign against a second victim.
