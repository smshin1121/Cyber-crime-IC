Tulsa World coverage of the unsealed federal grand jury indictment in N.D. Okla. charging four foreign nationals — three Russian and one Kazakhstani — with conspiracy and damage to protected computers tied to the Anyproxy and 5Socks botnet residential proxy services. Documents the FBI Oklahoma City Cyber Task Force lead, joint cooperation with the Eastern District of Virginia, the Dutch National Police – Amsterdam Region, the Netherlands Public Prosecution Service, and the Royal Thai Police.
