# European police bust Albanian investment and call centre scam

Reuters, republished by MarketScreener, reported that Europol said Albanian and Austrian authorities dismantled an Albania-based investment-fraud call-centre network. The report confirmed 10 arrests, nearly EUR 900,000 in cash seized, Tirana call centres offering telephone investment advice through fake brokers, and operator teams organized by language for German, English, Italian, Greek, and Spanish markets.
