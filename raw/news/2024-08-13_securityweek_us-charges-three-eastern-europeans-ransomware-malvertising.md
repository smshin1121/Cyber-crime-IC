SecurityWeek reports on the unsealed indictments against Maksim Silnikau, Volodymyr Kadariya, and Andrei Tarasov for malvertising operations distributing the Angler exploit kit (October 2013–March 2022) and the Ransom Cartel ransomware (May 2021 onward). Silnikau was extradited from Poland to the District of New Jersey and faces parallel charges in Virginia. Maximum sentences: 27 years for wire fraud conspiracy.
