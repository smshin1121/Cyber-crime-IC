Pittsburgh Post-Gazette coverage of the June 2020 arrest in Detroit of Justin Sean Johnson, the suspect in the 2014 UPMC hack that stole personal information of 65,000 hospital employees. Charges include conspiracy and identity theft. The case was handled in the Western District of Pennsylvania, with parallel federal investigations. Stolen data was used to file fraudulent tax returns and convert refunds to Amazon gift cards shipped to Venezuela.
