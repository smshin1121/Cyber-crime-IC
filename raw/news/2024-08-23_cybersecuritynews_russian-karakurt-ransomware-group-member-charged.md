Cyber Security News (Balaji N) reports the federal indictment of Deniss Zolotarjovs, 33, of Moscow, on conspiracy to commit money laundering, wire fraud, and Hobbs Act extortion. Detained by Georgian law enforcement in December 2023 and extradited to the United States, he appeared in U.S. District Court in Cincinnati. The article identifies Assistant U.S. Attorney Megan Gaffney Painter as prosecutor and details Karakurt's data-theft-plus-extortion model.
