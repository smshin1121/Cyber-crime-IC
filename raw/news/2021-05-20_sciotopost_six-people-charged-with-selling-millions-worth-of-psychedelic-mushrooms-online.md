Scioto Post (Jeremy Newman) reports the May 2021 federal indictment of six defendants — James Verl Barlow, Matthew Taylor Barlow, Ronald Royal Edward Brust, Jennifer Helen Campbell, Monet L. Carriere, and Tony Du Phan — for operating "TRIPWITHSCIENCE" dark-web sales of liquid psychedelic mushrooms (November 2013 - April 2021). Investigation led by South-Central Ohio High Intensity Drug Trafficking Area Cyber Task Force; prosecution by Southern District of Ohio.
