National Law Review legal-press coverage of the May 31, 2017 Second Circuit affirmance of Ulbricht's conviction and life sentence. Notes $183M Silk Road transaction volume estimate and 144,000 Bitcoins ($28.5M) FBI seizure.

## Extracted Summary

Second Circuit unanimously affirmed Ulbricht's conviction and life imprisonment May 31, 2017. Silk Road = largest online black market at 2013 shutdown; first known darknet market; ~$183M in illegal drugs traded. FBI seized 144,000 BTC ($28.5M at seizure); Ulbricht possessed over $18M BTC on laptop. Court rejected Fourth Amendment (pen register/IP), fair-trial, and procedural-reasonableness challenges. Also rejected challenges related to government agents who stole Bitcoins during investigation.
