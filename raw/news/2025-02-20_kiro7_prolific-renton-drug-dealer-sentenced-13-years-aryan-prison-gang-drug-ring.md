KIRO 7 reports on the sentencing of Shawn Ellis, 32, of Renton, Washington, to 13 years in federal prison for his role in an Aryan prison gang-linked drug trafficking ring. Coverage details the March 2023 coordinated takedown that involved 10 SWAT teams and over 350 law enforcement officers, resulting in the seizure of 177 firearms, more than 10 kilos of methamphetamine, 11 kilos of fentanyl pills, 3 kilos of heroin, and over $330,000 in cash.
