BleepingComputer (Lawrence Abrams) summarizes the week's ransomware news including the September 7, 2023 unsealing of three TrickBot/Conti indictments by the U.S. Department of Justice in three federal jurisdictions, and the joint U.S./UK sanctions on eleven Russian nationals tied to TrickBot and Conti operations. The piece notes the defendants' roles spanned operation management, malware development, crypting, and infrastructure management, with reach to over 900 global victims.
