## Summary-Only Archive

Wired reported that Ukrainian authorities arrested five key suspects in a multinational Zeus banking-fraud ring and connected the action to Operation Trident Breach, the FBI's May 2009 ACH-fraud investigation.

The article corroborates the U.S., United Kingdom, Netherlands, and Ukraine cooperation set; USD 220 million attempted theft; USD 70 million stolen; 39 U.S. arrests; 20 U.K. arrests; 92 U.S. suspects charged; and more than 3,500 money mules.
