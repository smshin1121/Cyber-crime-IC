Stamford Patch local news coverage of the April 2025 federal indictment against Tasean Lemar Brown and Stanley Charles. Reports the U.S. Postal Inspection Service intercepted parcels containing approximately 1 kg of fentanyl powder and 3 kg of counterfeit oxycodone pills mailed from California and Arizona to Stamford. Defendants face mandatory-minimum 10-year sentences.
