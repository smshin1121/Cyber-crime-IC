CyberScoop reported on the September 2021 extradition of Glib Oleksandr Ivanov-Tolpintsev from Poland to the United States to face charges in the Middle District of Florida for trafficking in unauthorized access devices and computer passwords. The article cites the DOJ press release and details the alleged operation of a botnet capable of brute-forcing about 2,000 computer credentials per week, with the credentials sold on a dark web marketplace.
