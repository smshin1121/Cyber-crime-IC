KTTN reports Simon Kaura's five-year federal sentence in the Western District of Missouri for the Skynet darknet conspiracy. Co-defendant Taylor Ross Staats received 18 months for verifying stolen card validity. Prosecution led by U.S. Attorney's Office, Western District of Missouri, with FBI investigation support. Tens of thousands of illegal transactions occurred between February 2016 and October 2019.
