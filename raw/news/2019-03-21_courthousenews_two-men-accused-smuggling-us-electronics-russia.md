Courthouse News Service covers the federal indictment unsealed in San Francisco against Dmitry Kosmachov and Sergey Vetrov for conspiring to procure controlled U.S.-origin microelectronics in violation of the International Emergency Economic Powers Act (IEEPA). Kosmachov, a Russian-born naturalized Estonian citizen, was extradited from Estonia on March 14, 2019. The indictment includes 12 substantive IEEPA counts, 19 smuggling counts, and 17 international money laundering counts.
