KWCH (Wichita CBS affiliate) reports the federal grand jury indictment of David L. Mongeau, 54, of Holcomb, Kansas, on two counts of making false statements and 17 counts of bank fraud/attempted bank fraud. The investigation is led by USDA, with AUSA Jason Hart prosecuting. Mongeau allegedly claimed a crop loss while failing to disclose sales of thousands of bushels of wheat reported as ruined.
