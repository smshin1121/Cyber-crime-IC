## Summary

ABC15 Phoenix coverage of Jason Arnold's arrest at Damaged Ink Tattoo & Body Piercing in Chandler, Arizona on federal charges of distribution and conspiracy to distribute controlled substances. FBI Sacramento investigators identified darknet vendor "SICKNESSVERSION2" selling heroin (4.81/5 star rating); four undercover heroin purchases arrived concealed in Haribo gummy bear packages. Postal tracking led investigators to Arnold's Chandler tattoo shop near Chandler Boulevard and Alma School Road. Two co-defendants also arrested.
