The Record reports that LockBit affiliates Ruslan Magomedovich Astamirov (Russian, 21) and Mikhail Vasiliev (Canadian-Russian, 34) pleaded guilty to conspiracy charges in Newark federal court on July 18, 2024. Astamirov targeted at least 12 victims (extorting $1.9M); Vasiliev targeted educational institutions in UK and Switzerland and businesses in NJ and Michigan, causing $500K+ in damages. Vasiliev had previously received 4 years in Canadian court (March 2024).
