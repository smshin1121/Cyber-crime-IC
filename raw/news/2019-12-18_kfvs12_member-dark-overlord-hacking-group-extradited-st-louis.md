Local CBS affiliate coverage of Nathan Wyatt's extradition from the UK to St. Louis to face charges related to The Dark Overlord hacking group. Reports detail Wyatt's arraignment in U.S. District Court in the Eastern District of Missouri and the indictment's allegations that Wyatt and co-conspirators stole sensitive data from U.S. healthcare providers and accounting firms and demanded ransoms in bitcoin.
