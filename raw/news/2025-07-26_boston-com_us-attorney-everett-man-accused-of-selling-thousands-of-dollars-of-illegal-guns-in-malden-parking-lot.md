Boston.com (Morgan Rousseau) coverage of Joao Victor Da Silva Soares (21, Everett MA, Brazilian national) federal grand jury indictment for dealing firearms without a license and conspiracy. Confirms August 26, 2024 sale of two AR-15-style rifles for $6,000 in Malden parking lot, and September 11, 2024 sale of five firearms in Milford. Part of larger investigation involving 18 Brazilian nationals and ~110 firearms seized; some defendants linked to PCC (Primeiro Comando da Capital) Brazilian transnational criminal organization.
