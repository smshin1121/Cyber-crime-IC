Krebs on Security investigative coverage of the Hared/Ditman SIM swap indictments February 6, 2019. Documents alias linkage to ogusers[.]com forum, ~40 BTC theft, lifestyle indicators (BMW i8 purchase).

## Extracted Summary

Northern California prosecutors charged Ahmad Wagaafe Hared (Tucson, AZ) and Matthew Gene Ditman (Las Vegas) with SIM swap-based crypto theft and extortion (Oct 2016 - May 2018). Hared used hacker alias "winblo," tied to ogusers[.]com forum. Allegedly stole ~40 BTC (~$136K at time, more during 2017 peak). Defendant allegedly bought BMW i8 (~$150K) with proceeds. Tricked/bribed wireless reps to transfer phone numbers. Context: part of broader SIM swap reckoning incl. Joel Ortiz, Nicholas Truglia.
