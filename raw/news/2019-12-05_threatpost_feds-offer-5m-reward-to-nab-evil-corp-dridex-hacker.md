## Summary

US State Department announced $5 million reward for Maksim V. Yakubets (32, Russian, alias "aqua"), leader of Evil Corp — the largest cybercriminal reward to date at the time. Yakubets allegedly oversaw Dridex banking trojan and Zeus malware (~$220M attempted theft across 21 municipalities/banks/orgs). Co-defendant: Igor Turashev (38). US Treasury also imposed sanctions on Evil Corp the same day. Reward announcement included photos of Yakubets's lavish spending (customized Lamborghini, $330K+ wedding).
