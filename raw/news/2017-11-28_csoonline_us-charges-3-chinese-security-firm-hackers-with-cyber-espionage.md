CSO Online enterprise security press coverage of the U.S. DOJ indictment in Pittsburgh against three Chinese hackers working at Boyusec for corporate cyber-espionage targeting Moody's Analytics, Siemens AG, and Trimble Inc. Article details the APT3 connection and the indictment's role in the broader U.S. response to alleged Chinese state-sponsored economic espionage.
