KTLA reports that Arbi Setaghaian Sangbarani, 41, of Glendale was sentenced to 120 months in federal prison for his role in the "NoLove" darknet drug trafficking organization that distributed methamphetamine and MDMA domestically and internationally. The article details the December 2023 trial verdict, the seizure of nearly 50 pounds of methamphetamine and 6,700 MDMA pills from a shed, and the bitcoin-laundering element of the scheme.
