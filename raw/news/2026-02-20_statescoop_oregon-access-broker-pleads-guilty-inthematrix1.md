StateScoop reports Catalin Dragomir, the prominent initial access broker known online as "inthematrix1," pleaded guilty in U.S. federal court (District of Oregon) to obtaining information from a protected computer related to an Oregon state government office. The article details that Dragomir's activities caused at least $250,000 in losses to multiple U.S. victim networks and that his arrest required Romanian-U.S. cooperation.
