Krebs on Security blog post documenting the arrest in Uganda of Ryan Andrew Gustafson (aka Willy Clock), the dark-web counterfeit currency dealer who operated Community-X. Investigative reporting predates the deportation. Krebs links Gustafson to a known darknet-economy money-mule and counterfeit-distribution operation that surfaced fake U.S. notes in Pittsburgh and Uganda.
