WIFR Rockford CBS affiliate (Bridgette Fox) coverage of Demario Sorrells's February 2, 2024 guilty plea in Boston federal court for conspiracy to commit wire fraud. The conspiracy used dark-web-purchased payment card data to defraud businesses; Sorrells was responsible for $106,000 in victim losses. Co-defendants include rapper Herbert Wright (G Herbo) and Antonio Strong.
