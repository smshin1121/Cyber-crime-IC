KWCH (Wichita CBS affiliate) reports the guilty plea of Alexander James Rosell, 34, of Wichita, to one count of possession of child pornography. The activity was discovered through an international investigation into a dark web website plus IP-based reports tied to a residence Rosell could access. Investigation: FBI, Wichita Police, Kansas ICAC Task Force. Sentencing scheduled April 4, 2025.
