## Summary

INTERPOL's Operation First Light 2024 (61 countries): 3,950 suspects arrested, 14,643 additional suspects identified, $257 million seized ($135M fiat, $2M crypto, $120M real estate/luxury items). 6,745 bank accounts frozen. Crime types: phishing, investment fraud, fake online shopping, romance/impersonation scams. Tools: INTERPOL Global Rapid Intervention of Payments (I-GRIP). Regional notes: Hong Kong arrests, Brazil jewelry/electronics seizures, Namibia network dismantled (88 youths rescued, 163 computers + 350 phones seized). Funded by China's Ministry of Public Security; concluding meeting in Tianjin. First Light operations have run since 2014.
