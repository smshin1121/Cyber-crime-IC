# CBI Dismantles Global Cybercrime Network Targeting Germans Since 2021 In Operation Chakra-IV

Free Press Journal reported that CBI, as part of Operation Chakra-IV, dismantled a virtual-asset-supported cybercrime network targeting German nationals since 2021. The report said the action was executed in close coordination with German authorities, that CBI registered a case on 2025-02-12, searched six locations from 2025-02-14 to 2025-02-17, arrested Rahul Shaw, dismantled an illegal call center in West Bengal, and recovered digital evidence including hard disks, phones, a laptop, and documents.
