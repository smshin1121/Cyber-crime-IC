KOIN (Portland local TV news) reports Catalin Dragomir, 45, of Constanta, Romania, pleaded guilty to selling access to an Oregon state government office computer network. Dragomir's activities caused approximately $250,000 in losses across multiple U.S. victims. He pleaded guilty in U.S. District Court in Portland; sentencing is set for May 26, 2026. Local press provides Oregon perspective on the international extradition case.
