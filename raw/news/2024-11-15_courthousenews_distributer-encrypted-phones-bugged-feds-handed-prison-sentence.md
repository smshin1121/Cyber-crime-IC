Courthouse News coverage of Osemah Elhassen, a Lebanese-Australian citizen arrested in Colombia in June 2021, extradited to the United States, and sentenced in November 2024 in SDCA to 63 months in prison for his role as a distributor of ANOM encrypted devices in the FBI's Operation Trojan Shield. He pleaded guilty to a racketeering conspiracy.
