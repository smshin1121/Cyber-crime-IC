15 minutos (Spanish-language news) reports the January 11, 2022 sentencing of Jose Rodolfo Barraza Flores, 26, of Arizona, to 160 months in federal prison. Operating from 2018-2020, Barraza Flores sold methamphetamine, cocaine, and other narcotics on darknet markets using Bitcoin, concealing drugs inside wax candles he melted and molded in an Arizona trailer. U.S. District Judge Aileen Cannon imposed the sentence. He pleaded guilty in October 2021.
