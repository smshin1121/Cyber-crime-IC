BleepingComputer trade-press reporting on the 7-year (84 months) sentence handed down to Justin Sean Johnson (aka TheDearthStar / Dearthy Star) for the 2014 hack of UPMC HR databases that stole personal information of 65,000 healthcare workers. Reports that stolen data was sold on dark web forums and used by conspirators to file fraudulent tax returns claiming hundreds of thousands in false refunds, converted to Amazon gift cards and shipped to Venezuela. Investigation involved IRS-CI and FBI Pittsburgh.
