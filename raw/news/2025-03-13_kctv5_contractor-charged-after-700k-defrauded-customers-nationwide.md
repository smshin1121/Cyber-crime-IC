KCTV5 reports the federal indictment of Bobby Bates Sr., 42, of Grand Island, Nebraska, owner of Built Rite Construction, on two counts of wire fraud. Defrauded customers in 10 states (Kansas, Missouri, Iowa, Oklahoma, Nebraska, South Dakota, Illinois, Georgia, Alabama, Texas) of $700,000+ between Feb-Oct 2023. USPIS and FBI investigated.
