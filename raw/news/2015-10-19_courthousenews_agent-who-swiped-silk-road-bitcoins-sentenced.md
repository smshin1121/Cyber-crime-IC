Courthouse News Service covers the federal sentencing of Carl Mark Force IV at the U.S. District Court in San Francisco. Force was assigned to the Baltimore Silk Road Task Force (DEA-led, multi-agency). The sentence — 78 months — was at the upper end of the plea agreement range. Force's bitcoin theft and extortion of Ross Ulbricht as "Nob" / "French Maid" personas were detailed.
