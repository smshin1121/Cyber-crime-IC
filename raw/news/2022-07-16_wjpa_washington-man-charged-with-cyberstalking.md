## Summary

Eric Scholl (53, Washington PA) federally indicted on cyberstalking charges following a PFA order filed by his ex-wife. Conduct: placed GPS tracking device under ex-wife's vehicle, hacked her phone (changed linked email to his own), posted explicit Reddit content impersonating the victim, accessed her PayPal without authorization, sent threatening emails, broke into her residence to remove items. Crimes committed while residing in a Pittsburgh halfway house. Harassment began March 2021. Detained federally at time of publication.
