CyberScoop trade-press coverage of the DOJ's Infraud Organization takedown announced on February 7, 2018. Reports the indictment of 36 defendants in the District of Nevada, names Svyatoslav Bondarenko (Ukraine) and Sergey Medvedev (Russia) as co-founders/administrators, and details the carding-forum slogan "In Fraud We Trust." Confirms losses of approximately $530 million and intended losses exceeding $2.2 billion across more than 10,000 members.
