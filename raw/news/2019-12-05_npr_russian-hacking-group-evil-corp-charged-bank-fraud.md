NPR reports federal indictment of Russian nationals Maksim Yakubets and Igor Turashev for operating the Evil Corp cybercriminal group responsible for the Dridex/Bugat banking malware. The article notes Treasury's allegation that Yakubets has worked with Russian FSB intelligence since 2017, complicating extradition prospects.
