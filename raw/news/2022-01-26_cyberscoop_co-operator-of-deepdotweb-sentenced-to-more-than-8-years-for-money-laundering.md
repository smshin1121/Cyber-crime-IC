CyberScoop's January 26, 2022 follow-up article by Joe Warminsky reports the 97-month (8 years 1 month) federal prison sentence imposed on Tal Prihar by Senior U.S. District Judge Donetta Ambrose in W.D. Pa. The article notes Prihar's $8,414,173 forfeiture and the still-pending extradition of co-defendant Michael Phan from Israel. Sentencing was below the 11-year guideline recommendation due to acceptance of responsibility and waiver of extradition fight.
