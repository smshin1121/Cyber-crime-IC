## Summary-Only Archive

Computerworld reported that 24 people from 13 countries were arrested in Operation Card Shop, described as the largest coordinated international law-enforcement action against online carding operations at the time.

The article independently summarizes the stolen-card and financial-data trafficking focus and names several foreign arrest countries.
