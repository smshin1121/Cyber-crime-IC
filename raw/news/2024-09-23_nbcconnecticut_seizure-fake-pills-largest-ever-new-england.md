NBC Connecticut local news coverage describing the federal seizure of counterfeit pills in East Haven as one of the largest such busts ever in New England. Quotes federal officials announcing the indictment of seven Connecticut residents — Kelldon Hinton, Heshima Harris, Emanuel Payton, Marvin Ogman, Shawn Stephens, Arnaldo Echevarria, and Cheryle Tyson — and emphasizes the danger posed by fentanyl-laced counterfeit oxycodone and Adderall.
