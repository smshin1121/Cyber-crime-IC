Daily Voice (a local New York / Hudson Valley outlet) reports on Kyle Weiland's 75-month sentence for operating a dark-web pill manufacturing and distribution business out of Tuxedo Park. The article notes Weiland's prior 2019 conviction for online pill sales and that he committed the new offence while on supervised release. Forfeiture of luxury vehicles and cryptocurrency described.
