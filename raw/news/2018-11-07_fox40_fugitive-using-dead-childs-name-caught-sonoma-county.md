FOX40 news coverage of Ronald Victor Solakian's case. Solakian, 70, who had been on the run since 1995 after bailing out of jail in Southern California while facing marijuana trafficking charges, was discovered after 23 years living under the identity of a 5-year-old child who died in 1953. The U.S. State Department's Diplomatic Security Service flagged his September 2017 passport application, leading to his February 2018 arrest. He was sentenced November 2 to 30 months.
