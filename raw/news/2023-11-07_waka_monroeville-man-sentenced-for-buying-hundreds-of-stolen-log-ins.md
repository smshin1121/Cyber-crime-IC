WAKA-TV (Montgomery, Alabama) reports the November 7, 2023 sentencing of Laderian Odom, 28, of Monroeville, Alabama, to 24 months in federal prison for purchasing approximately 950 sets of stolen login credentials from Genesis Market on the dark web. The article credits FBI-Milwaukee and the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Eastern District of Wisconsin alongside the SDAL prosecution, and notes that Genesis Market was taken down in "Operation Cookie Monster — a joint international cyber operation." Judge Terry F. Moorer denied a sentencing reduction because Odom had lied about being under indictment when attempting to purchase a handgun in August 2023.
