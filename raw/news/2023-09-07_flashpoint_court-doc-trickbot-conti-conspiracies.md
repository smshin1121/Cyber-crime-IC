Flashpoint's analyst note enumerates the nine Russian nationals charged in the Northern District of Ohio Trickbot indictment (Maksim Galochkin/Bentley, Maksim Rudenskiy/Buza, Mikhail Tsarev/Mango, Andrey Zhuykov/Defender, Dmitry Putilin/Grad-Staff, Sergey Loguntsov/Begemot-Zulas, Max Mikhaylov/Baget, Valentin Karyagin/Globus, Maksim Khaliullin/Maxfax-Maxhax-Kagas). It also lays out the parallel Middle District of Tennessee Conti indictment (Galochkin, Rudenskiy, Tsarev, Zhuykov) and the Southern District of California indictment of Galochkin for the Scripps Health attack (May 1, 2021). Conti reportedly attacked over 900 victims across 47 U.S. states and 31 foreign countries.
