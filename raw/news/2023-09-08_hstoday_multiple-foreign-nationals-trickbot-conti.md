Homeland Security Today summarizes the three TrickBot/Conti indictments unsealed September 7, 2023 (NDOH, MDTN, SDCA) and notes prior convictions in the same prosecution thread: Latvian national Alla Witte received 32 months, while Russian Vladimir Dunaev was in custody pending trial. The article cites the FBI's 2021 finding that Conti was used to attack more critical infrastructure victims than any other ransomware variant.
