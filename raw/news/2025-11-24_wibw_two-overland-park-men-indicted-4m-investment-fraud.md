WIBW reports the indictment of Baha Ibrahim, 21, and Jawad Albadawi, 21, both of Overland Park, KS, on wire-fraud conspiracy, wire fraud, money-laundering conspiracy, money laundering, and transacting-in-criminal-proceeds counts. ~$4M scheme operated March-August 2024; funds diverted to luxury vehicles, cryptocurrency, and trips to Dubai, Greece, and Las Vegas. FBI investigated; AUSA Ryan J. Huschka prosecuting.
