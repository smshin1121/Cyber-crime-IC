ThaiCERT publishes a CERT-level advisory summarizing the U.S. charges in the District of New Jersey against Rostislav Panev, dual Russian-Israeli national, for LockBit ransomware development. The advisory frames the case as part of broader international cooperation against LockBit and provides translation/dissemination of the U.S. action to the ASEAN cybersecurity community.
