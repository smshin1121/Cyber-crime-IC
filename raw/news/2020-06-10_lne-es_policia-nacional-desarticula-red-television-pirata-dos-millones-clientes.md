# La Policia Nacional desarticula una red de television pirata con dos millones de clientes

La Nueva Espana, carrying EFE reporting, described the Spanish National Police dismantlement of an illegal pay-TV network with about two million clients. It reported cooperation with Europol and Eurojust, 11 arrests, 15 searches, 50 servers disconnected, roughly 1,000 websites, more than 40,000 channels or platform feeds, and proceeds estimated at EUR 15 million.

The report also described the criminal network's international footprint and asset seizures including luxury vehicles, a property, watches, cash, cryptocurrency, and frozen bank accounts.
