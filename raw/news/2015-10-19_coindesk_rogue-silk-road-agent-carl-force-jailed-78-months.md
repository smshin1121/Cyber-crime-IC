CoinDesk reports on the sentencing of former DEA agent Carl Force IV to 78 months in prison for stealing bitcoin during the Silk Road investigation. Force pleaded guilty in July 2015 to extortion, money laundering, and obstruction. He had used multiple personas to communicate with Ross Ulbricht while assigned to the Baltimore Silk Road Task Force, soliciting payments in bitcoin in exchange for inside information about the investigation.
