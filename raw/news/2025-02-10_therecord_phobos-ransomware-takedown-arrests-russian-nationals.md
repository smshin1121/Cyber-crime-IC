The Record reports two Russian nationals, Roman Berezhnoy (33) and Egor Glebov (39), were arrested as part of an international coordinated disruption of the Phobos ransomware operation. The Justice Department unsealed charges in the District of Maryland. The operation also extradited Evgenii Ptitsyn from South Korea in November 2024. International partners included Europol, Eurojust, and law enforcement from 14 countries.
