DataBreaches.net reports the international Phobos ransomware takedown, including arrest of Russian nationals Roman Berezhnoy and Egor Glebov, technical disruption of group infrastructure, and coordination among U.S., European, Polish, Thai, and South Korean authorities. The article details the federal indictment in the District of Maryland.
