CBS San Francisco news coverage of the federal grand jury indictment of Maxito Pean, 51, of Port-au-Prince, Haiti, on charges of conspiracy to commit wire fraud and four substantive wire fraud counts. Pean allegedly sent a phishing email to a Saratoga victim to capture his Deutsche Bank credentials, then caused $233,200 to be transferred from the victim's account.
