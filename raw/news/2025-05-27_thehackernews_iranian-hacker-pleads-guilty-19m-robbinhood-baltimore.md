The Hacker News reports the guilty plea of Iranian national Sina Gholinejad, 37, in U.S. federal court (EDNC) to one count of computer fraud and abuse and one count of conspiracy to commit wire fraud for his role in the Robbinhood ransomware scheme. The article details Robbinhood's operational pattern of compromising city, healthcare, and corporate networks and demanding ransom; Baltimore alone lost over $19M.
