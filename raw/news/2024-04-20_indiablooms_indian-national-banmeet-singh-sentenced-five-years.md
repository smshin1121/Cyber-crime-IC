Indiablooms (Indian press) reports the U.S. sentencing of Banmeet Singh of Haldwani, Uttarakhand, to five years in prison for selling controlled substances on dark web markets. Singh was arrested in London in 2019 and extradited via UK proceedings to the U.S. in 2023. The article provides Indian-origin coverage of the international cooperation case.
