Local 12 (WKRC Cincinnati) reports that Khlari Sirotkin (Colorado, 16 years) and Sean Deaver (Nevada, 18 years) were sentenced as leaders of the "Pill Cosby" dark web drug trafficking and money-laundering conspiracy. They manufactured/distributed more than one million fentanyl-laced counterfeit pills and laundered approximately $2.8 million between 2013 and 2020. U.S. Attorney Kenneth Parker (Southern District of Ohio) announced; the FBI's Cincinnati Field Office coordinated the investigation.
