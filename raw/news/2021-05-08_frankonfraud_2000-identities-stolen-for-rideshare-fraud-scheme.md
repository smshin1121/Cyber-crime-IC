Frank on Fraud (specialist fraud-industry publication) coverage of the May 2021 indictment of 19 Brazilian nationals including Wemerson Dutra Aguiar (then 25 years old, of Massachusetts) for the nationwide rideshare-account fraud scheme. Confirms 2,000+ stolen identities, GPS-spoofing/bot manipulation of referral programs, and the Massachusetts-based WhatsApp distribution network.
