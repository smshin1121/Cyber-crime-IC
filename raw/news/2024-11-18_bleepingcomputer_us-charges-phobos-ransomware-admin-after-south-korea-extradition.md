Trade press coverage of Evgenii Ptitsyn's extradition from South Korea to face U.S. cybercrime charges in the District of Maryland. Ptitsyn, a Russian national, was indicted on 13 counts (wire fraud, computer fraud conspiracy, extortion) tied to administering the Phobos ransomware operation that extorted >$16M from 1,000+ victims worldwide. Reports the "derxan" / "zimmermanx" online monikers used.
