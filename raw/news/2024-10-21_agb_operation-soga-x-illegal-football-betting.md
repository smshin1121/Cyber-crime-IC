## Summary-Only Archive

Asia Gaming Brief reported on Operation SOGA X as an INTERPOL-led global sting against illegal football betting, conducted from June to July 2024 with law-enforcement agencies from 28 countries and territories.

The article corroborates more than 5,100 arrests, more than USD 59 million recovered, tens of thousands of unauthorized gambling websites shut down, rescue of more than 650 trafficking victims in the Philippines, and online betting or financial-crime examples in Vietnam, Thailand, and Greece.
