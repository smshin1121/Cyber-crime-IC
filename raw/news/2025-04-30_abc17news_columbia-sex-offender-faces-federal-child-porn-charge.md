ABC 17 News (Ryan Shiner) reports the federal complaint against Andrew Charles Nicholls, 38, a registered sex offender in Columbia, Missouri, charged with sending and receiving child pornography. Boone County Sheriff's Office Cyber Crimes Task Force received NCMEC tip on January 5 about uploads to Nicholls's Google account; Google detected uploads on September 5. Nicholls had a 2004 second-degree child molestation conviction.
