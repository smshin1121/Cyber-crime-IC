Newsweek (Cammy Pedroja) reports the August 2, 2021 sentencing of William Kaetz, 56, of Paramus, NJ, to 16 months federal prison for doxxing a federal judge on social media. Confirms guilty plea to making restricted information publicly available, the October 18, 2020 posting of the judge's home address, and details prior threatening communications including calling the judge a "traitor."
