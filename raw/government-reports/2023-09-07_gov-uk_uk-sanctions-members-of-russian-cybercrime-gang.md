## Summary

UK sanctions, in coordination with US Treasury, on 11 members of the TrickBot/Conti ransomware gang (September 7, 2023). Sanctioned: Maksim Galochkin (Bentley/Volhvb/Max17), Andrey Zhuykov (Defender/Dif/Adam), Maksim Rudenskiy (Buza/Silver/Binman), Mikhail Tsarev (Mango/Fr*ances/Khano), Dmitry Putilin, Maksim Khaliullin, Sergey Loguntsov, Alexander Mozhaev, Vadym Valiakhmetov, Artem Kurov, Mikhail Chernov. NCA assessed the group extorted at least $180 million globally and approximately £27 million from 149 UK victims. Foreign Secretary Cleverly framed action as exposing identities and disrupting business models. Group targeted hospitals and critical infrastructure including during COVID-19.
