## Summary-Only Archive

Eurojust's Operation BlackShades evaluation describes a worldwide investigation targeting creators, sellers, and users of Blackshades malware in 16 states. The investigation used Eurojust coordination meetings and a coordination centre supported by Europol EC3.

The report states that the two action days produced 359 house searches, 97 arrests, more than 1,100 data-storage devices seized, and seizure of the Blackshades website domain.
