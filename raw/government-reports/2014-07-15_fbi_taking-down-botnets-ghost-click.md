## Summary-Only Archive

FBI Cyber Division testimony before the Senate Judiciary Committee discussed botnet takedowns and cited Operation Ghost Click as a two-year FBI investigation that dismantled an international cyber ring. The testimony reports that three Estonian nationals were extradited to the United States in November 2013 to face charges related to the crimes.
