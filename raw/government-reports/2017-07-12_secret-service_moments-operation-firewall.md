## Summary-Only Archive

The U.S. Secret Service retrospective describes Operation Firewall as a 2004 undercover investigation that arrested more than 28 people across eight U.S. states and six foreign countries. The retrospective repeats the core metrics from the original operation, including more than 1.7 million stolen credit card numbers and more than USD 4 million in actual losses.
