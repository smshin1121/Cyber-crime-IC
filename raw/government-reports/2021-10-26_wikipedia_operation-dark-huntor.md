Wikipedia article on Operation Dark HunTor, summarizing the 10-month international darknet vendor sweep concluded in October 2021. Lists US participating agencies under JCODE: FBI, DEA, HSI, USPIS, IRS-CI, ATF, NCIS, CBP. International coordination by Europol EC3, Eurojust, Australian Federal Police, and national police forces. Total 151 arrests with US 65, Germany 47, UK 24, Netherlands 4, Italy 4, France 3, Switzerland 2, Bulgaria 1. Targeted markets include Dream, WallStreet, White House, DeepSea.
