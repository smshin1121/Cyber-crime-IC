## Summary

FinCEN advisory FIN-2024-A002 urges financial institutions to identify and report suspicious activity tied to precursor chemicals, pill presses, die molds, and manufacturing equipment used to synthesize illicit fentanyl and other synthetic opioids. The advisory asks institutions to reference the key term `FENTANYL FIN-2024-A002` in relevant SAR filings.
