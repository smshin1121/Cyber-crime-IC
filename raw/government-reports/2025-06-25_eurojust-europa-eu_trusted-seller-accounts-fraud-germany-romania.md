## Summary

Eurojust reported that authorities from Germany and Romania worked through a Eurojust-supported joint investigation team to disrupt a fraud group that hijacked trusted online seller accounts. The group took over 405 seller accounts; customers initially placed orders worth more than EUR 106 million, and 556 completed purchases caused more than EUR 400,000 in damage.

## Key Details

- Publication date: 2025-06-25
- Participating countries named by Eurojust: Germany and Romania
- Mechanisms: joint investigation team, European Arrest Warrants, coordinated action days, house searches, and seizure of IT systems
- Reported enforcement: December 2024 searches/arrests in Germany and Romania, followed by three additional detentions in Romania on 2025-06-24
