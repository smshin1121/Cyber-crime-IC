## United States v. Victor Hernandez

On 26 June 2024, DOJ announced that Victor Hernandez was sentenced to 130 months in prison for leading a conspiracy to sell cocaine and counterfeit pills over the dark web and launder the proceeds through cryptocurrency.

DOJ also highlighted firearm possession in furtherance of the conspiracy.
