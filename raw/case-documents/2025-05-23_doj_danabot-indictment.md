## United States v. Stepanov et al. — DanaBot Indictment (Operation Endgame Phase 2)

As part of Operation Endgame Phase 2 (announced 23 May 2025), the US Department of Justice unsealed a 16-count indictment charging 16 Russian nationals for operating the DanaBot malware network.

### Key Named Defendants

- **Aleksandr Stepanov** (aka "JimmBee"), 39, of Novosibirsk, Russia
- **Artem Kalinkin** (aka "Onix"), 34, of Novosibirsk, Russia
- 14 additional defendants (names pending official confirmation from DOJ court filings)

### DanaBot Facts

- DanaBot infected 300,000+ computers worldwide
- Estimated USD 50 million+ in financial damages
- DanaBot served dual purposes: cybercrime (credential theft, fraud) and espionage (targeting government and military entities)
- The malware evolved from a banking trojan to a multi-purpose botnet platform

### Operation Endgame Phase 2 Context

The DanaBot indictment was announced alongside the broader Operation Endgame Phase 2 action, which resulted in 300 servers taken down, 650 domains neutralized, EUR 3.5 million in cryptocurrency seized, 20 international arrest warrants issued, and 18 suspects added to the EU Most Wanted list.
