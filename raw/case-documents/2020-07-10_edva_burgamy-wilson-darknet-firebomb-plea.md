## United States v. William Anderson Burgamy IV and Hyrum T. Wilson

On 10 July 2020, DOJ announced that darknet vendor William Anderson Burgamy IV and pharmacist Hyrum T. Wilson pleaded guilty in a case involving prescription-drug trafficking, laundering, and a firebomb plot against a competitor pharmacy.
