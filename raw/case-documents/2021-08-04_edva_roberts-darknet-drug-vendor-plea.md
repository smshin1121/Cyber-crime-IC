## United States v. Cullen Roberts

On 4 August 2021, DOJ announced that Cullen Roberts pleaded guilty to distributing illicit prescription drugs over the darknet using the moniker `Pillpusher`.

The case is a straightforward example of undercover-purchase-based darknet drug enforcement.
