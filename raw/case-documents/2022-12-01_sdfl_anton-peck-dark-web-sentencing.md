### Source Page Summary

On 1 December 2022, DOJ announced that Anton Peck was sentenced to 16 years in prison in the Southern District of Florida for conspiring to distribute fentanyl, methamphetamine, and heroin through dark web markets using the vendor profile "Syntropy." DOJ described a multi-location drug-distribution scheme involving cryptocurrency payments and shipments around the United States. The case is notable for the severe sentence and substantial drug quantities seized.
