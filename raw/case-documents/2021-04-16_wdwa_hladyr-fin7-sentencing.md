## Summary

The U.S. Attorney's Office for the Western District of Washington announced on April 16, 2021 that Fedir Hladyr was sentenced to 10 years in prison for his role as a systems administrator for the FIN7 cybercrime organization. The release states that FIN7 targeted restaurant, gaming, and hospitality companies to steal payment card data from more than 100 U.S. victims.

## Key Details

- Defendant: Fedir Hladyr
- Court: U.S. District Court for the Western District of Washington
- Docket: CR18-152-RSL
- Disposition: Sentenced to 10 years' imprisonment
- Conduct: Conspiracy to commit wire fraud and access-device fraud tied to FIN7 intrusions
