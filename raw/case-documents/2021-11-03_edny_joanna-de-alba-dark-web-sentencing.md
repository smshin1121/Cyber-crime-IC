## United States v. Joanna De Alba

On 3 November 2021, DOJ announced that Joanna De Alba was sentenced to 96 months in prison for distributing fentanyl, heroin, methamphetamine, cocaine, oxycodone, methadone, and MDMA over the dark web.

DOJ said De Alba accepted bitcoin and other cryptocurrency in exchange for narcotics and shipped drugs nationwide.
