## United States v. Bryan Connor Herrell

On 1 September 2020, DOJ announced that Bryan Connor Herrell was sentenced to 11 years in prison for serving as a moderator on AlphaBay.

DOJ said Herrell moderated disputes between buyers and sellers and helped maintain the functioning of what was then the world's largest online drug marketplace.
