### Source Page Summary

On 11 January 2022, DOJ announced that Jose Rodolfo Barraza Flores was sentenced to 160 months in prison for selling methamphetamine, cocaine, fentanyl-laced pills, and other narcotics on dark web markets such as White House Market, Empire Market, and Yellowbrick Road. DOJ said Barraza Flores concealed narcotics inside wax candles and shipped them around the country after buyers paid in bitcoin.
