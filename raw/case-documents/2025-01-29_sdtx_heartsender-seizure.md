## Summary

The U.S. Attorney's Office for the Southern District of Texas announced that 39 domains and associated servers were seized in an international disruption of a Pakistan-based HeartSender/Saim Raza marketplace network selling phishing kits, scam pages, email extractors, and other fraud-enabling tools. The seizure was coordinated with Dutch National Police and supported the disruption of business email compromise and credential-theft tooling.

## Key Details

- Matter: HeartSender / Saim Raza seizure action
- Public announcement: 2025-01-30
- Seizure date reported by DOJ: 2025-01-29
- Result: 39 domains and associated servers seized
- Partners: FBI Houston Field Office, DOJ, and Dutch National Police cooperation
- Victim impact cited by DOJ: more than USD 3 million in U.S. victim losses
