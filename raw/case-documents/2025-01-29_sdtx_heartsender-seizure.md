## Summary

The U.S. Attorney's Office for the Southern District of Texas announced on January 29, 2025 that U.S. and Dutch authorities seized 39 domains and associated servers linked to the HeartSender / Saim Raza cybercrime service. The release attributes the action to court-authorized seizure measures supporting disruption of phishing and business email compromise tooling.

## Key Details

- Matter: HeartSender / Saim Raza seizure action
- Court linkage: Court-authorized U.S. seizure process referenced in DOJ release
- Jurisdiction: Southern District of Texas
- Conduct: Sale of phishing kits, scam pages, email extractors, and related fraud tooling
- Enforcement result: 39 domains seized with U.S.-Dutch coordination

## Relevance

Although the public release does not provide a docket number, it is the official DOJ record of the court-backed seizure action underlying Operation Heart Blocker.
