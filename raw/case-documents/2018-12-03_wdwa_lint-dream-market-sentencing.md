## United States v. Allen D. Lint

On 3 December 2018, DOJ announced that Allen D. Lint was sentenced to five years in prison for selling LSD through the Dream Market darknet marketplace.

When agents searched Lint's residence, they found thousands of doses of LSD and processing materials used for darknet orders.
