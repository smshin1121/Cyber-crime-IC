## United States v. Brian McDonald

On 21 October 2024, DOJ announced that Brian McDonald was sentenced to 248 months in prison for trafficking fentanyl-laced pills and cocaine through darknet marketplaces and for possessing firearms in furtherance of drug trafficking.

DOJ said McDonald admitted that one sale caused a fatal fentanyl overdose.
