## United States v. Holly Adams

On 3 June 2025, DOJ announced that Holly Adams was sentenced to 12 years in prison for conspiring to distribute fentanyl and launder the resulting proceeds through dark web sales.

DOJ said Adams and a coconspirator sold counterfeit oxycodone pills pressed with fentanyl on the dark web and resumed activity even after an earlier state search and arrest.
