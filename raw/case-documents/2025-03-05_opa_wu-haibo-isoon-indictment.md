## Summary

On March 5, 2025, DOJ announced charges against Wu Haibo and other i-Soon employees, Chinese law-enforcement officers, and APT27-linked operators for global computer intrusion campaigns. The release ties the prosecution to a federal indictment in the District of Columbia.

## Key Details

- Defendants: Wu Haibo and multiple co-defendants tied to i-Soon and Chinese state security organs
- Court: U.S. District Court for the District of Columbia
- Docket: 1:25-cr-00072
- Charges: Computer intrusion, wire fraud, aggravated identity theft, and related conspiracy counts
- Additional measures: Treasury sanctions and Rewards for Justice offer announced in parallel

## Relevance

This is the principal formal charging document announcement for the i-Soon / APT27 operation and supports a dedicated case entry.
