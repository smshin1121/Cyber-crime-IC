### Source Page Summary

On 22 February 2023, the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Middle District of Florida announced the arrest and extradition of Russian national Dariy Pankov from the Republic of Georgia. DOJ said Pankov was charged with conspiracy, access-device fraud, and computer fraud in connection with his role in the xDedic Marketplace ecosystem. According to DOJ, Pankov developed a malware tool called NLBrute, which decrypted login credentials and enabled access to tens of thousands of compromised computers worldwide. He then sold those credentials, including through the xDedic Marketplace, and allegedly generated more than USD 358,000 in criminal proceeds. The case is important because it links the xDedic operation not only to administrators but also to a major supplier-side actor. DOJ later reported that Pankov pleaded guilty and was sentenced to 60 months' imprisonment. This source was generated.
