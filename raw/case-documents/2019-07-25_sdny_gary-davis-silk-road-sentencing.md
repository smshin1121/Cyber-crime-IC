## United States v. Gary Davis

On 25 July 2019, DOJ announced that Gary Davis, an Irish national known as "Libertas," was sentenced to 78 months in prison for serving as a forum moderator and later site administrator for Silk Road.

DOJ said Davis was extradited from Ireland and helped resolve disputes, respond to support requests, and enforce marketplace rules on Silk Road.
