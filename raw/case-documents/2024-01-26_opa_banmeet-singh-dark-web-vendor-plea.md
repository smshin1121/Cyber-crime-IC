### Source Page Summary

On 26 January 2024, DOJ announced that Banmeet Singh, an Indian national, pleaded guilty in the Southern District of Florida to selling controlled substances on dark web marketplaces and agreed to forfeit USD 150 million. DOJ said Singh used vendor marketing sites on Silk Road, AlphaBay, Hansa, and other dark web markets to distribute fentanyl, LSD, ecstasy, Xanax, ketamine, tramadol, and other drugs from Europe to the United States. The case stands out for its extraordinary forfeiture amount and the transnational shipping model behind the vendor operation.
