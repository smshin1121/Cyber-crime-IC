## United States v. Pavlo Kharmanskyi

In DOJ's 4 January 2024 xDedic investigation roundup and on the associated victim-services page, Pavlo Kharmanskyi is identified as one of the marketplace administrators. DOJ said Kharmanskyi lived in Kyiv, advertised for xDedic, paid administrators, and provided customer support to buyers.

The same official materials state that Kharmanskyi was arrested at Miami International Airport in 2019 as he attempted to enter the United States. He later pleaded guilty and was sentenced in 2021. The victim-services page lists his case number as `8:18-CR-546-TPB-AEP`.

This case is useful because it shows that xDedic generated not only overseas extraditions but also opportunistic U.S. arrests when administrators traveled through reachable jurisdictions.
