## United States v. Sean Shaughnessy

On 12 February 2024, DOJ announced that Sean Shaughnessy, who used the moniker `Fent4U`, was sentenced to 293 months in prison for darknet fentanyl trafficking and possession of child pornography.

DOJ said he sold fentanyl, carfentanil, pentedrone, and analogues over the dark web and was convicted at trial in 2023.
