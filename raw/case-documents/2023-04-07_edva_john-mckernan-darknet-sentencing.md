## United States v. John McKernan

On 7 April 2023, DOJ announced that John McKernan was sentenced to 78 months in prison for distributing fentanyl on the darknet under the moniker "KingofKeys."

DOJ said undercover purchases and bitcoin payments were central to the investigation.
