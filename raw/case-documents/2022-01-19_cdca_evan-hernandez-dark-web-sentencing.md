## United States v. Evan Jaime Hernandez

On 19 January 2022, DOJ announced that Evan Jaime Hernandez was sentenced to 97 months in prison for selling methamphetamine and laundering bitcoin proceeds for the dark-web operations of drug traffickers.

The case is useful because it focuses on laundering support for other dark-web sellers, not only direct vending.
