## Legal Basis
Joint investigation teams are generally established through treaty-based or regional legal frameworks that allow designated authorities to conduct coordinated investigative activity under an agreed mandate.

## How It Works
Participating states designate members, define scope and duration, and then share evidence and operational planning through a combined team instead of relying only on sequential MLA requests.
