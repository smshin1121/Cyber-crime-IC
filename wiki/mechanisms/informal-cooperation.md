## Legal Basis
Informal cooperation typically relies on agency relationships, liaison channels, and voluntary disclosure rules, but it remains bounded by domestic law and cannot substitute for compulsory process where one is required.

## How It Works
Investigators use trusted contacts to exchange leads, deconflict activity, or preserve time-sensitive intelligence before a formal MLAT, production order, or extradition package is completed.
