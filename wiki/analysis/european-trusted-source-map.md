## Rules For Page Enrichment

1. Arrests, seizures, charges and participating states should default to official law-enforcement or court sources.
2. Malware mechanics, botnet structure and intrusion tradecraft can be supplemented with vendor technical reports and academic papers.
3. If a national-agency claim cannot be anchored to an official site, it should be labeled as an inference or omitted.
4. Placeholder or source-derived case pages should not invent named defendants where sources do not provide them.
