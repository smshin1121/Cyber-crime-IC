## Summary

The Korea–Cambodia Joint Task Force for Korean Nationals MOU (Korean: 「대한민국 국민을 위한 한국-캄보디아 공동 전담반」 양해각서; commonly 코리아전담반 MOU) is a bilateral memorandum of understanding between the Korean National Police Agency (KNPA) and the Cambodian National Police, signed in Phnom Penh on 2025-11-10. The MOU establishes a Korean-Cambodian shared-staff police unit operating from a Phnom Penh-based shared office and responsible for "the entire process from report receipt to rescue, investigation, and suspect repatriation" for cases involving Korean nationals. The MOU is the implementation of decisions reached at the 2025-10-27 Korea-Cambodia summit in Malaysia.

## Key Provisions for IC

The 2025-11-10 MOU establishes a single bilateral cooperation instrument with three operative axes:

1. **Joint operational staffing** — a single Joint Task Force office in Phnom Penh staffed by both Korean and Cambodian officers, operating as a shared police unit rather than parallel cooperation.
2. **End-to-end case handling** — the Joint Task Force is responsible for "the entire process from report receipt to rescue, investigation, and suspect repatriation" (verbatim) for cases involving Korean nationals.
3. **Substantive scope** — Korean-national protection plus online-scam transnational crime, with explicit project-relevant focus on the Cambodian scam-compound problem.

## Implementation in Practice

The MOU's joint task force began operations immediately after the 2025-11-10 signing. Per Korean tier-2 reporting, by early January 2026 the task force had completed 7 rounds of joint operations resulting in 135 suspects arrested in the field and 3 Korean victims rescued from scam-compound captivity.

The bilateral MOU is the foundational legal-framework instrument for the 코리아전담반 mechanism, which produced the Cambodia tranche (42 of 73 Korean nationals) of the 2026-04-27 [[korea-cambodia-philippines-73-extradition-2026|Korea-Cambodia-Philippines 73-person extradition operation]]. The whole-government 「초국가범죄 특별대응TF」 frame (KNPA + 외교부 + 법무부 + 국가정보원) coordinating the 73-person extradition operates downstream of this bilateral MOU.

## Relationship to Other Frameworks

The 2025-11-10 Korea-Cambodia MOU is structurally paralleled by the 2026-03-03 [[korea-philippines-police-mou-2026-revision|Korea–Philippines police cooperation MOU revision]], which expands an analogous Korean Desk model in the Philippine National Capital Region Police Office. Together these two bilateral instruments form the backbone of Korea's 2025–2026 Southeast Asian scam-compound cooperation infrastructure.

## Korean Perspective (한국 관점)

The 2025-11-10 MOU is, on its face, a primarily-bilateral arrangement, but it is the foundational instrument for an operationally novel Korea-side construct — a joint Korean-Cambodian field unit physically embedded in Cambodian Police HQ. This is a discrete IC mechanism class that warrants tracking as it operationalises Korea-as-requesting-state cooperation in a way that the prior Korea-Cambodia 2024-2025 cooperation cycle did not. The MOU is part of the Korean government's policy response to the 2025 Cambodia kidnapping cases and the broader scam-compound captivity problem.
