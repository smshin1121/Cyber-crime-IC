## International-Cooperation Relevance

Wire fraud is useful in the corpus because online fraud almost always uses interstate or foreign communications. The provision therefore appears in BEC, marketplace, identity-theft, credential, and platform-fraud records where foreign victims, suspects, payment flows, or service providers create an international-evidence problem.
