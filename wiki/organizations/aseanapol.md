## Summary

**ASEANAPOL** is the regional police cooperation body for Southeast Asian police agencies. In the cybercrime corpus, its clearest source-backed role is as a regional support layer in INTERPOL's online-scam operations, especially [[operation-first-light-2024|Operation First Light 2024]].

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

The Operation First Light 2024 source set describes a 61-country INTERPOL-coordinated crackdown on online scams, with 3,950 arrests, 14,643 additional suspects identified, 6,745 bank accounts frozen, and USD 257 million in assets seized. The source explicitly identifies ASEANAPOL as one of the regional policing bodies that bolstered the operation, alongside AFRIPOL, GCCPOL, and Europol.

That role matters because Southeast Asian cyber-enabled fraud often crosses multiple ASEAN jurisdictions before funds or victims appear elsewhere. ASEANAPOL should therefore be understood as a regional coordination and liaison layer rather than as a single national investigative agency.
