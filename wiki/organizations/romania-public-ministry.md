## Summary

The **Romania Public Ministry** is the parent prosecution institution for [[romania-diicot|DIICOT]], Romania's specialized prosecutorial directorate for organized crime, terrorism, and cybercrime. This page exists to preserve parent-institution context, while DIICOT remains the operational cybercrime prosecution page.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

The Council of Europe DIICOT source identifies DIICOT as a specialized directorate operating under the Romanian Public Ministry and attached to the High Court of Cassation and Justice. That makes the Public Ministry relevant whenever cybercrime cooperation requires prosecutorial authority, mutual legal assistance, or Eurojust-facing judicial coordination.

## IC Relevance

Use this page for Romanian prosecution-system hierarchy. Use [[romania-diicot]] for cybercrime prosecutorial capacity and [[romania-police]] for police-level investigation.
