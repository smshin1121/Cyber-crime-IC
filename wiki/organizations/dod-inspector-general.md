## Summary

The **DoD Office of Inspector General** is the parent oversight organization for [[us-dcis|Defense Criminal Investigative Service]]. In cybercrime IC, this page is mainly useful for organizational hierarchy; operational cybercrime detail belongs on [[us-dcis]].

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

DCIS appears in cyber-enabled fraud and infrastructure-disruption matters where Department of Defense equities, procurement, fraud, or defense networks intersect with criminal activity. The 2026 SocksEscort proxy-service takedown is the clearest current operation anchor for DCIS and therefore for the DoD OIG hierarchy.
