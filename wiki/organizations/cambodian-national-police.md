## Summary

The Cambodian National Police is the national police force of the Kingdom of Cambodia, under the Ministry of Interior. In the public IC corpus the agency is documented as the Cambodian counterpart for the bilateral 한국-캄보디아 공동 전담반 (Korea–Cambodia Joint Task Force for Korean nationals, "코리아전담반") MOU signed in Phnom Penh on 2025-11-10, and as the operational counterpart for the joint Korean-Cambodian field unit that produced the 42-person Cambodian tranche of the 2026-04-27 Korea-Cambodia-Philippines extradition cycle.
