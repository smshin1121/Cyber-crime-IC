## Summary

**Group-IB** is a private-sector cybercrime intelligence and investigation partner. In this corpus it appears as a recurring source of technical intelligence for INTERPOL-led and national operations, especially BEC, carding, and malicious infrastructure cases.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

Group-IB sources document support for [[operation-falcon]], [[operation-delilah]], [[operation-lyrebird]], and [[operation-synergia-ii]]. The common pattern is private telemetry and investigative lead generation, followed by INTERPOL enrichment and national police action.
