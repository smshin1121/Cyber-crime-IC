## Summary

The **INTERPOL ICSE Database** is the corpus entry for INTERPOL's International Child Sexual Exploitation database. It is best understood as an investigative mechanism, not a conventional organization: it supports victim identification, duplicate-material deconfliction, and cross-border routing of child sexual exploitation leads.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

Child sexual exploitation cases often involve material circulating across platforms and jurisdictions long after the original abuse. A shared international database helps investigators determine whether a victim is already known, whether a series is linked to another jurisdiction, and whether new imagery may reveal a still-unidentified child or location. The database therefore sits at the evidence-analysis layer of international cooperation.
