## Summary

**ICANN** appears in this wiki as an infrastructure-governance actor relevant to domain disruption, sinkholing, and abuse handling. It is not a police agency, but domain governance can matter when malware infrastructure relies on large-scale domain generation or abused registration channels.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

Operation Avalanche is the key local reference. The takedown relied on coordinated action against a fast-changing botnet and malware infrastructure ecosystem where domain handling, sinkholing, and registrar/registry coordination were operationally significant.

## IC Relevance

Use this page for domain-governance and infrastructure-coordination roles. Keep arrest, seizure, and prosecution authority on law-enforcement and judicial pages.
