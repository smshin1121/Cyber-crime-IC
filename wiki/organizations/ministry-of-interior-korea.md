## Summary

The **Korea Ministry of the Interior and Safety** is the ministry context for Korea's national police structure in this repository. Cybercrime investigation detail belongs on [[knpa]] and [[knpa-cyber-bureau]], but this page is useful for parent-ministry and reform context.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

The KNPA source identifies the Korea National Police Agency as South Korea's primary law-enforcement authority, with cybercrime investigation handled through national investigation structures. The prosecution-source material in the corpus also flags reform discussions and future institutional division of investigation and prosecution functions, including cybercrime as a serious-crime category.
