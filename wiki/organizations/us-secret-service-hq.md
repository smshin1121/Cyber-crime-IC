## Summary

**US Secret Service Headquarters** is the parent organization entry for Secret Service cyber-financial investigations. The operational cybercrime details are maintained on [[us-secret-service|US Secret Service - Cyber Fraud Task Forces]], while this page preserves the headquarters and parent-agency context.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

The Secret Service source set describes the 2020 consolidation of Electronic Crimes Task Forces and Financial Crimes Task Forces into Cyber Fraud Task Forces, reflecting the convergence of cybercrime and traditional financial crime. The cyber investigations source reinforces that the agency handles technology-enabled financial crime through that task-force model.

The [[bidencash-takedown|BidenCash takedown]] is the strongest operation-level example currently tied to Secret Service sources. The 2025 Secret Service release attributes the marketplace-domain seizure to the Secret Service Frankfurt Resident Office and Cyber Investigative Section, working with the FBI and DOJ and with international partners.

## IC Relevance

Use this page for headquarters, parent-agency, or Secret Service-wide context. Use [[us-secret-service]] for Cyber Fraud Task Forces, field offices, and specific cyber-financial investigative capacity.
