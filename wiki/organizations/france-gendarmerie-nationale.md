## Summary

The **Gendarmerie nationale** is one of France's two major national law-enforcement institutions. In the cybercrime corpus it is the parent institution for [[france-gendarmerie|C3N]] and the broader Gendarmerie cyber architecture, including COMCyberGEND and the regional investigative network.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

Official Gendarmerie source material describes a cyber structure that centralizes and coordinates the institution's cyber capabilities while keeping specialist investigators close to field cases. C3N handles high-end judicial cybercrime work, including dark-web, cryptocurrency-enabled, and attacks-on-systems investigations, while the wider Gendarmerie network supports territorial and regional case development.

The Gendarmerie appears in major multinational operations through that cyber-investigative function. The repository tracks it in ransomware, malware infrastructure, encrypted communications, and cyber-enabled fraud cases, often alongside [[france-national-police]], [[france-junalco]], [[europol-ec3]], and partner national agencies.
