## Summary

The Security Service of Ukraine (SBU) is recorded here because the FBI's public Operation Trident Breach release explicitly names SBU participation in the 2010 Zeus/Jabber Zeus banking-theft operation. The SBU detained five individuals on 30 September 2010 and executed eight search warrants with approximately 50 officers.

## IC Relevance

Use this page when a source specifically names the SBU. Use [[ukraine-police]] for later National Police/Cyber Police references where the source identifies that institution instead. Eurojust's Dnipro call-centre release also names the SBU as a Ukrainian action-day partner.
