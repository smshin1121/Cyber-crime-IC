> [!info] Status — Active (parent org structure noted)
> The **Seoul Metropolitan Police Agency (서울특별시경찰청, SMPA)** is active and not directly affected by the 2023 KNPA national-level reorganization that dissolved the standalone 경찰청 사이버수사국. SMPA's parent is the [[knpa|KNPA (Korea National Police Agency)]], not [[knpa-cyber-bureau]] directly — the prior `parent_org` wikilink has been corrected. SMPA underwent a **structural reorganization on 2023-10-30** into a 3-차장 체제 (Public Safety / Investigation / Everyday Life), 7 divisions, 26 departments, and 31 precincts [1][2].
>
> **Terminology note:** The academic and policy literature often refers to city/provincial "사이버수사대". The correct current terminology for the Korean national/metropolitan police is **사이버수사과 (Cyber Investigation Division)** under the metropolitan/provincial police 수사부, with precinct-level **사이버수사팀 (Cyber Investigation Team)** working beneath it — there is no formally-named "사이버수사대" in the current KNPA organization [3].

**Name:** 서울특별시경찰청 (Seoul Metropolitan Police Agency, SMPA) — unchanged
**Parent:** [[knpa|경찰청 (Korea National Police Agency)]]

**Structure (post 2023-10-30):** 3 차장 (Deputy Commissioner for Public Safety / Investigation / Everyday Life), 7 divisions, 1 단, 2 담당관, 26 과, 15 직할대, 31 precincts [2]
**Cybercrime unit:** **수사부 사이버수사과 (Cyber Investigation Division under Investigation Bureau)**; cybercrime teams also exist at precinct level as **사이버수사팀** [3]
**Status:** **Active**

Cybercrime investigation at SMPA is coordinated nationally through the [[knpa-cyber-bureau|경찰청 수사국 사이버수사심의관]] (deputy-director-level unit within KNPA's National Investigation Headquarters since December 2023), not through SMPA directly. SMPA conducts operational Seoul-area investigations and feeds into the national cybercrime line.

## Summary

The Seoul Metropolitan Police Agency (서울특별시경찰청, SMPA) is the metropolitan police agency responsible for law enforcement in the Seoul Special City. It is a subordinate regional command of the [[knpa|Korea National Police Agency (경찰청)]] and operates cybercrime investigation functions through its 수사부 사이버수사과 (Cyber Investigation Division) and precinct-level 사이버수사팀.

SMPA has conducted bilateral cooperation with Chinese police on voice phishing cases, most notably the [[korea-china-voice-phishing-qingdao|Qingdao joint operation]] (August 2023), which was the first significant Korea-China voice phishing roundup under bilateral cooperation arrangements. Seoul-area voice phishing cases frequently involve SMPA leadership in cooperation with Chinese or Southeast Asian counterparts, coordinated through the [[knpa-cyber-bureau|national cybercrime enforcement line]].
