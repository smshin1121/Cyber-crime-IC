## Summary

The **Netherlands Police parent organization** entry exists to separate the broader Dutch police institution from the specialist cyber unit page [[netherlands-politie]]. The Dutch source corpus repeatedly centers Team High Tech Crime as one of Europe's most active cybercrime units; this page preserves the parent-organization context.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

Dutch police cybercrime work combines national technical investigators, prosecutorial partners, Europol and Eurojust channels, and international operational teams. The Dutch police Team High Tech Crime source describes high-complexity cybercrime work and highlights the importance of international cooperation because cybercrime crosses borders. The Council of Europe country-profile source gives the broader legal-cooperation background.

## IC Relevance

Use this parent page when the source speaks broadly about Dutch police or the national police organization. Use [[netherlands-politie]] when the source specifically names Team High Tech Crime, NHTCU/THTC, or the Dutch cyber unit in an operation.
