## Summary

**FIRST** is the global Forum of Incident Response and Security Teams. In this repository it is used as context for CERT/CSIRT cooperation, not as a police or prosecutor organization.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

FIRST-style cooperation supports the technical side of cybercrime response: incident notification, trusted-team relationships, vulnerability and malware handling, and operational trust between national and sectoral response teams. That technical coordination can preserve evidence, contain infrastructure, and support later law-enforcement requests, but FIRST itself should not be described as an arresting or prosecuting body.

## IC Relevance

Use this page where a national cyber-security agency profile discusses global incident-response community membership. Use the national CERT, police, or prosecutor page for incident-specific or enforcement-specific action.
