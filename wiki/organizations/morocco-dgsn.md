## Summary

**DGSN** is Morocco's National Security Directorate. In this corpus, it provides police-side context for Moroccan cybercrime investigation and arrests, while [[maroc-cert]] covers the technical incident-response role and [[morocco-dgst]] covers intelligence/security-service context.

## Cybercrime Cooperation Role

Operation Lyrebird is the main operation anchor: INTERPOL reported a Moroccan arrest tied to phishing, carding, and online fraud. The source set does not always cleanly separate DGSN and DGST roles, so attribution should remain source-bound.
