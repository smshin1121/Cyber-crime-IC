## Source Summary

DOJ District of Connecticut press release announcing Adeyemi Odufuye's December 12, 2018 sentencing to 45 months in federal prison and $921,497.87 in restitution. The press release confirms his UK arrest, extradition, and prior January 2018 guilty plea on conspiracy and aggravated identity-theft counts.
