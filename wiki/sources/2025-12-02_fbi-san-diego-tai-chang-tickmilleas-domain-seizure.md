# FBI San Diego — Tai Chang tickmilleas.com domain seizure (2025-12-02)

## Summary

On 2025-12-02 the FBI San Diego Field Office and the U.S. Attorney's Office for the District of Columbia announced the Scam Center Strike Force's seizure of `tickmilleas.com`, a third fake-cryptocurrency-investment domain operated from the Tai Chang scam compound (a.k.a. Casino Kosai) in Kyaukhat, Burma. The investigation rests on FBI San Diego casework, but the press release also formalises an embedded FBI presence inside the Royal Thai Police War Room Task Force in Bangkok — a working US-Thai cybercrime-investigation channel against Southeast Asian "pig butchering" compounds.
