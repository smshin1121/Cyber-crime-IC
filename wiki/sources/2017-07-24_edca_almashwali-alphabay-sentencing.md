## Source Summary

On 24 July 2017, DOJ announced that Abudullah Almashwali was sentenced to six and a half years in prison for distributing heroin and cocaine on AlphaBay under the vendor names `Area51` and `DarkApollo`. DOJ said the case involved bitcoin payments and undercover heroin purchases delivered to the Eastern District of California.
