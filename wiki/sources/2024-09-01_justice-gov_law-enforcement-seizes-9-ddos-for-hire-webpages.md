## Source Summary

DOJ CDCA announced the 7 May 2025 PowerOFF phase, including seizure of 9 DDoS-for-hire domains and simultaneous Polish arrests of 4 administrators assisted by U.S. authorities. The source also identifies recurring partners including Europol, DOJ CCIPS, FBI, HSI, DCIS, BKA, UK NCA, Netherlands Police, Poland, Brazil, Japan, and France.
