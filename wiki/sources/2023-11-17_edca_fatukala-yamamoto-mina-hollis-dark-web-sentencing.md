## Source Summary

On 17 November 2023, DOJ announced the sentencing of four defendants in a dark web cocaine distribution scheme. Viliami Mosese Fatukala received five years in prison, Quynhmy Quoc Yamamoto 15 months, John Phillip Hollis II one year, and Iris June Micu Mina time served. DOJ said the conspirators sold approximately 13.7 kilograms of cocaine over the dark web beginning in 2019.
