## Summary

Press release 3/2026 issued by the Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Bamberg on 23 January 2026 announces that the Zentralstelle Cybercrime Bayern (ZCB; Central Cybercrime Unit Bavaria) has filed indictment in October 2025 against a 42-year-old Israeli-Portuguese dual national identified as the alleged ringleader of an international online-investment-fraud and cryptocurrency-scam call-centre network. The defendant was extradited from Costa Rica to Germany in July 2025; trial is pending at the specialised Cyber-Economic Criminal Chamber of the Bamberg Regional Court.

## International cooperation footprint

The release explicitly credits three foreign jurisdictions' authorities as cooperating with German law enforcement:

| Jurisdiction | Counterpart agency | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Kosovo | kosovarische Ermittlungsbehörden (Kosovo investigative authorities) | Joint Action Day in Pristina (March 2021) dismantled call-centre infrastructure; evidence-gathering basis for the German indictment |
| Costa Rica | costaricanische Justizbehörden (Costa Rican judicial authorities) | Executed extradition request; surrendered defendant to German custody July 2025 |
| Israel | israelische Behörden (Israeli authorities) | Assistance regarding the suspect (Israeli national) — identification and asset tracing |
