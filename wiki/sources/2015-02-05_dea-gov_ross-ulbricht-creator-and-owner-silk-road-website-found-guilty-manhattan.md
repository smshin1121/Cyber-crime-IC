## Source Summary

DEA New York Field Division press release marking the day-after announcement of Ross Ulbricht's conviction on all seven federal counts in the Silk Road prosecution. The release reproduces the SDNY U.S. Attorney's account of the trial and provides DEA's institutional confirmation of the joint-agency investigation that produced the verdict.
