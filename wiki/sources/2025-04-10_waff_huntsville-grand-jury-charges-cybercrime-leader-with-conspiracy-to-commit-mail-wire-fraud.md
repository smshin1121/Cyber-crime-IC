## Source Summary

WAFF, the Huntsville NBC affiliate, reports the April 10, 2025 federal indictment of Kareem Mustafa Ahmad ("Noir") for conspiracy to commit mail and wire fraud. The article details the Telegram-based "Noir's Luxury Refunds" scheme that defrauded online retailers between July 2020 and July 2022 and identifies Google's CyberCrime Investigation Group plus major retailers as cooperating private partners.

## Relevance to IC

This source provides independent local press coverage of a transnational cybercrime indictment (defendant in Egypt, victims across the U.S.), corroborating the U.S. Attorney's Office Northern District of Alabama press release and naming private-sector cooperation partners not detailed in the DOJ release.
