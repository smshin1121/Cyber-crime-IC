## Source Summary

FinCEN's supplemental advisory FIN-2024-A002 covers suspicious-activity indicators tied to the procurement of precursor chemicals, pill presses, die molds, and manufacturing equipment used to synthesize illicit fentanyl and other synthetic opioids. The previous source note incorrectly linked the advisory to the Justin Udvardi sentencing; that case-specific claim is not supported by the advisory text.
