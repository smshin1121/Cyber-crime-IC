## Source Summary

FBI Los Angeles announced that 100 people linked to an international computer hacking ring had been charged by U.S. and Egyptian authorities in Operation Phish Phry. It describes a multinational investigation, 53 U.S. defendants, 47 Egyptian suspects, and 33 U.S. arrests on 7 October 2009.

## Relevance to IC

This is the strongest source for the international-cooperation classification. It explicitly states that Operation Phish Phry was the first joint cyber investigation between Egyptian law enforcement authorities and U.S. officials.
