## Source Summary

Eurojust's evaluation describes Operation BlackShades as a 16-state worldwide cybercrime investigation coordinated through Eurojust and supported by Europol EC3. The report provides the principal action-day figures: 359 searches, 97 arrests, more than 1,100 devices seized, and domain seizure.
