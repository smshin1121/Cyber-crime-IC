## Summary

Press release of **Poliția Republicii Moldova** (the General Police Inspectorate of the Republic of Moldova), dated **12 May 2026**, jointly authored by its **Centrul pentru combaterea crimelor cibernetice** (Centre for Combating Cybercrime, within the INI — National Investigation Inspectorate) and the **PCCOCS** (the Prosecutor's Office for Combating Organized Crime and Special Cases). It is the Moldovan tier-1 own-domain counterpart to the BKA/ZIT release of 8 May 2026 and explicitly documents Moldovan participation in the international Crimenetwork takedown.
