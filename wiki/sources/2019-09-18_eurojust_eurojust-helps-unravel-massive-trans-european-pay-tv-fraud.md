## Source Summary

Eurojust reports the September 2019 action day against an international pay-TV fraud network that generated illegal IPTV signals and sold unauthorized pay-per-view access. The record supplies the initial action-day results, the Italian-led investigative frame, and the source-backed country set for the Operation Perfect Storm record.
