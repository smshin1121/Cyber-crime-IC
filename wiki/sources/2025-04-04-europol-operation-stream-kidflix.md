# Europol-led Operation Stream Takes Down Kidflix CSAM Platform

## Source Overview

Security Affairs and TRM Labs reported on the Europol-coordinated takedown of Kidflix, one of the largest known child sexual abuse material (CSAM) streaming platforms. The operation, led by the Bavarian State Criminal Police and ZCB, involved 35+ countries and resulted in 79 arrests and the identification of 1,393 suspects.

## Key Data Points

- Platform operational since 2021; seized March 11, 2025
- 1.8 million registered users; 91,000 unique videos (6,288 hours)
- 79 arrests across 35+ countries
- 3,000+ devices seized; 39 children identified and protected
- Cryptocurrency-based payment system traced through exchanges and DeFi platforms
