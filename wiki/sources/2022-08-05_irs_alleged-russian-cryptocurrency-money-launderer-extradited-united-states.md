## Source Summary

Parallel IRS Criminal Investigation press release confirming Alexander Vinnik's August 4, 2022 extradition from Greece to the Northern District of California. The article details the 21-count superseding indictment charging Vinnik and BTC-e with operation of an unlicensed money service business, conspiracy to commit money laundering, and 17 substantive money-laundering counts.

## Relevance to IC

This source provides parallel federal agency confirmation (IRS-CI) for [[operation-alleged-russian-cryptocurrency-money-launderer-extradited-to-united-states]] and documents the multi-agency US task-force composition (IRS-CI/FBI/HSI/USSS) supporting the cooperation with Greece.
