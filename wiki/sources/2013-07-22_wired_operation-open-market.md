## Source Summary

Wired reported the undercover mechanics of Operation Open Market, including the Secret Service's use of the Celtic online identity to sell counterfeit identification documents while building evidence against Carder.su members. The article describes four indictments against 55 defendants in 10 countries and provides technical context on how investigators penetrated the carding forum ecosystem.

## Relevance to IC

This source supplies independent investigative reporting for [[operation-open-market|Operation Open Market]], especially the multi-country defendant scope and undercover methodology not fully detailed in the official March 2012 announcement.
