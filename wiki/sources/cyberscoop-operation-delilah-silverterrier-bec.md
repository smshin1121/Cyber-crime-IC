## Summary

CyberScoop's coverage confirms the **March 2022 arrest of an alleged SilverTerrier ringleader** via INTERPOL's Operation Delilah. Key points:

1. A 37-year-old Nigerian national was arrested upon re-entry to Nigeria after evading a 2021 sting operation
2. Private-sector threat-intelligence contributions were central: Palo Alto Networks (Unit 42), Group-IB, and Trend Micro provided domain attribution and malware telemetry
3. ~50,000 victims attributed to the SilverTerrier cluster since 2014
4. BEC is framed within the US$2.4 billion 2021 IC3 loss figure, positioning Delilah as high-priority enforcement
5. Operation Delilah is continuous with the earlier Falcon I (2020) and Falcon II (2021) suppressions — a pattern of *sustained* rather than *episodic* enforcement
