## Source Summary

Computerworld reported that law-enforcement agencies in 16 countries arrested 97 people in the Blackshades action and conducted 359 house searches, citing Eurojust reporting and the Dutch Public Prosecution Service's statement that the action was coordinated from Eurojust in The Hague.
