## Source Summary

FBI Seattle press release announcing Brian Richard Farrell, aka 'DoctorClu,' sentenced to eight years for his role as senior administrator of Silk Road 2.0 darknet marketplace. Farrell helped approve vendors and organized denial-of-service attacks against competitors.
