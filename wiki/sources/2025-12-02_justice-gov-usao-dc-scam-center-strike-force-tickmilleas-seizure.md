# USAO-DC — Scam Center Strike Force Tai Chang tickmilleas.com seizure (2025-12-02)

## Summary

On 2025-12-02 the U.S. Attorney's Office for the District of Columbia, spearheading the Scam Center Strike Force, announced the seizure of `tickmilleas.com`, a fake-cryptocurrency-investment domain operated from the Tai Chang scam compound (a.k.a. Casino Kosai) in Kyaukhat, Burma. The release is a tier-1 own-domain U.S. federal-prosecutor statement distinct from the parallel FBI San Diego Field Office release of the same date, and reports the same Strike Force action with additional institutional detail (Strike Force composition, Team Burma assignment, AUSA leadership, USAO-RI collaboration).

The release explicitly states the US-Thailand LE cooperation channel: "the Scam Center Strike Force has deployed FBI agents to Bangkok, who are working alongside the Royal Thai Police in their War Room Task Force, to investigate and combat scam compounds, including Tai Chang and other compounds in Burma."
