## Source Summary

DOJ District of Connecticut press release covering the May 2019 guilty plea of Stanley Hugochukwu Nwoke, the third Nigerian national to admit a role in the same BEC scheme charged in December 2016. It documents the rare Mauritius-to-U.S. extradition (first in 15+ years) and the loss figures attributed to Nwoke.
