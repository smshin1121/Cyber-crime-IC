## Source Summary

DOJ provided a July 2014 status update on the Gameover Zeus and CryptoLocker disruption. The update reports successful technical and legal measures, substantial victim-remediation progress, a 31 percent reduction in Gameover Zeus infections, and neutralization of CryptoLocker command-and-control communications.

## Relevance to IC

This source supplies follow-up results for [[operation-tovar|Operation Tovar]] and verifies that the disruption produced measurable remediation after the initial June 2014 enforcement announcement.
