This source documents the Greek limb of the November 2025 phase of Operation Endgame — specifically the arrest in Attica on 2025-11-03 of the alleged principal developer of VenomRAT, executed by Hellenic Police pursuant to a European Arrest Warrant issued by French judicial authorities and with FBI representatives participating on-site at the residential search.
