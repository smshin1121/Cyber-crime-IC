## Source Summary

Singapore Police Force's Commercial Affairs Department used its 2024 annual report to describe national participation in [[operation-first-light-2024]]. The report adds a participating-state perspective and connects the broader INTERPOL operation to a bilateral Singapore-Malaysia investigation against a money-laundering cell handling scam proceeds.

## Relevance to IC

This source broadens [[operation-first-light-2024]] beyond the INTERPOL headquarters narrative. It gives direct evidence of Singapore's operational role and of cross-border investigative work with Malaysia during the First Light 2024 window.
