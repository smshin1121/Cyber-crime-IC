# Major international operation against the Ragnar Locker ransomware group with Eurojust support

**Publisher**: [[eurojust|Eurojust]] (EU agency, tier-1 own-domain)
**URL**: https://www.eurojust.europa.eu/news/major-international-operation-against-ragnar-locker-ransomware-group-eurojust-support
**Date**: 2023-10-20

## Summary

Eurojust's own-domain press release announcing the conclusion of the 16-20 October 2023 international action week against the Ragnar Locker ransomware group. The release confirms the Europol-published participant roster and adds numeric precision and judicial-counterpart detail that the Europol version omits: 168 victim companies attacked worldwide since 2020, 9 servers taken down (5 NL + 2 DE + 2 SE), ransom demands ranging from USD 5 million to USD 70 million per victim, and 6 suspects heard across four jurisdictions (Czech Republic, Spain, Latvia, France).

The Eurojust release also documents a previously unrecorded second wave in the multi-year case: an October 2022 arrest of an additional suspect in Canada via French-Canadian-US joint action — extending the Europol-named first wave (September 2021 arrests in Ukraine) to a documented three-wave sequence culminating in the European action week.

Eurojust's own role is laid out: case opened in May 2021 at French request, five pre-action coordination meetings hosted, action-week coordination centre established to enable rapid judicial-authority cooperation. Named prosecutorial counterparts include JUNALCO Paris, Metropolitan PPO Prague, Staatsanwaltschaft Leipzig, Milan PPO, East Netherlands PPO, Swedish PPO, PPO Alicante + Investigative Court Torrevieja, and Kyiv City PPO.
