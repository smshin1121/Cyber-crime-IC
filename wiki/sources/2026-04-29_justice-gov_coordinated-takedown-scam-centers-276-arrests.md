## Source Summary

DOJ Office of Public Affairs press release (Number 26-411, dated 2026-04-29) announcing the unsealing in the Southern District of California of charges against six defendants tied to "pig-butchering" cryptocurrency investment fraud schemes operated from scam compounds. The release reports an action "last week" (April 22-26 2026) led by Dubai Police under the UAE Ministry of Interior, with at least 276 individuals arrested and at least nine scam centers dismantled. FBI, Royal Thai Police, and the Chinese Ministry of Public Security are named as cooperating partners, alongside private-sector support from Meta Platforms, Inc.

## Relevance to IC

This is the principal tier-1 source for the rare US–UAE–China–Thailand cooperation pattern on cryptocurrency-investment fraud. It supplies the names and roles of cooperating agencies, defendant nationalities (Burmese, Indonesian), the lead-apprehending authority (Dubai Police), the secondary-apprehension authority (Royal Thai Police), the prosecutorial line (USAO–SDCA, CCIPS, OIA), and the private-sector partner (Meta). It also independently corroborates Operation Level Up (FBI San Diego–Phoenix joint initiative, 2024) and references FBI San Diego's Tai Chang Scam Enterprise investigation in Burma's Karen State.
