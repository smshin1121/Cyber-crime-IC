## Source Summary

UK NCA press release (2026-02-20) describing the 2026-01-13 arrest of seven men in Agbor, Nigeria, following NCA intelligence sharing. Seizures: 26 mobile phones, 42 SIM cards, 1 laptop. The cohort allegedly operated online investment scams, cryptocurrency fraud, and phishing attacks targeting UK residents and US persons resident in the UK. The NCA describes the operation as a partnership between the NCA, Meta, and the Nigerian Police, with the Nigerian Police National Cybercrime Centre named as the operational counterpart in the parallel Meta blog post co-published on the same date.
