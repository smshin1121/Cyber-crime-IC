## Source Summary

Europol newsroom press release dated 2025-11-26 (publication date estimate based on action-day timing; the Europol newsroom JSON does not include a date field). The release supersedes and extends the Eurojust 2025-11-04 release on Phase 1 alone, by adding (a) Phase 2 details (2025-11-25 to 2025-11-26 affiliate-marketing-infrastructure action days), (b) the cumulative network-laundering scale of EUR 700 million+, and (c) three additional participating countries (Bulgaria, Israel, Malta) beyond the 5-country Phase 1 roster.

## IC Pattern

This release establishes a new IC mechanism class: **two-phase EU + non-EU partner takedown of crypto-fraud + affiliate-marketing infrastructure**. Notable features:

- **Phase 1 = laundering / call-centre infrastructure** action targeted at the OCG members.
- **Phase 2 = affiliate marketing infrastructure** action targeted at the deepfake-celebrity ad operators that drive victim-targeting upstream of the call centres.
- **Israel as Phase 2 participant** — the wiki's first record of Israeli National Cybercrime Unit (Intelligence Division) operational involvement in a Europol-coordinated cryptocurrency-fraud takedown.
- **Malta as Phase 1 participant + Bulgaria as Phase 2 participant** — adds two further EU member-state partners to the Phase 1-only Eurojust release.
- **Deepfake-celebrity-impersonation advertising** as the upstream marketing channel for crypto-investment-fraud — first wiki record of this specific scam-marketing methodology recorded in primary IC documentation.
- **Europol cryptocurrency-specialist deployment** (similar pattern as in [[eurojust-100m-crypto-investment-fraud-takedown-2025|the September 2025 €100M Spain-Lithuania JIT crypto-fraud takedown]]).
