## Summary

This Cyber Express article reports the **June 2025 US takedown of BidenCash**, a stolen-card marketplace that operated from March 2022 serving 117,000+ users and trafficking 15M+ payment card records for ~US$17M in revenue. Law enforcement seized **~145 domains** and obtained cryptocurrency-wallet seizure warrants; the operation is framed as part of **Operation Endgame** (US + Netherlands + Finland + Germany + France + Denmark).

Notable TTP: between October 2022 and February 2023, BidenCash released **3.3 million stolen cards as free promotional giveaways** — a viral-marketing tactic distinctive among criminal marketplaces.

The BidenCash case is *not* the Marketplace A / Dekhtyarchuk 2022 EDTX indictment that the wiki's operation page documents.
