## Source Summary

Tier-1 U.S. Attorney's Office (SDNY) press release, issued jointly with FBI New York, announcing the unsealing of a four-count criminal Indictment and Complaint against Kai West (a/k/a "IntelBroker," a/k/a "Kyle Northern"), 25, a British national, for a years-long hacking and stolen-data resale scheme operated through the "IntelBroker" online identity on a hacker marketplace identified as "Forum-1" (BreachForums). West was arrested in France in February 2025 and the United States is seeking his extradition.

## Relevance to IC

- Tri-national-plus cooperation: arrest by French authorities (February 2025) and acknowledged assistance from French, Spanish, British, and Dutch authorities, coordinated by the DOJ Office of International Affairs and FBI New York. Establishes a clean US–FR–UK extradition track plus ES/NL evidentiary cooperation.
- Cybercrime-forum nexus: West is identified as the "owner" of Forum-1 (BreachForums) from August 2024 to January 2025, anchoring the case to the BreachForums-as-marketplace storyline parallel to the LeakBase takedown ([[leakbase-takedown-2026]]).
