> [!info] Source Summary
> Tier-1 official US Treasury press release (SB-0230) announcing OFAC sanctions on a four-actor cross-border DPRK IT worker fraud network. Demonstrates the Russia-based DPRK consular official + Russian national facilitator + Chinese front company + DPRK trading company quadrilateral, with cryptocurrency-to-USD conversions feeding the DPRK Ministry of People's Armed Forces General Political Bureau via Chinyong.

## Key Quotes

- "The North Korean regime continues to target American businesses through fraud schemes involving its overseas IT workers, who steal data and demand ransom" — Under Secretary John K. Hurley.
- "Today's action expands on the designation of Chinyong Information Technology Cooperation Company, targeting additional entities in its network and combatting its use of cryptocurrency for sanctions evasion."
