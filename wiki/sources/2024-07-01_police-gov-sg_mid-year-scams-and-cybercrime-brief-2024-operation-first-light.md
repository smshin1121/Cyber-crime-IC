## Source Summary

The Mid-Year Scams and Cybercrime Brief 2024 provides a Singapore-side official accounting for [[operation-first-light-2024]]. It gives national metrics that complement INTERPOL's global totals and shows how the operation translated into local account-freezing, asset-blocking and investigative activity.

## Relevance to IC

This source strengthens [[operation-first-light-2024]] by adding a concrete participating-country view rather than relying only on INTERPOL's headquarters release. It is especially useful for Singapore's role in anti-scam fund tracing and account-freezing.
