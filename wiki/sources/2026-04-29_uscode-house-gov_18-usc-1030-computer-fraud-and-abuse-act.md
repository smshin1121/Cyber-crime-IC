## Source Summary

The current U.S. Code page for 18 U.S.C. 1030 sets out the federal computer-intrusion offences commonly known as the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act. It is the principal U.S. charging reference for unauthorized access, exceeding authorized access, computer damage, credential abuse, and related fraud conduct.
