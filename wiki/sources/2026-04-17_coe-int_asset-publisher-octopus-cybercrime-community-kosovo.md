## Source Summary

Kosovo is **not a party** to the [[budapest-convention|Budapest Convention]]. As a state not universally recognised and not a member of the Council of Europe, Kosovo cannot accede to the Convention through the standard procedure. However, Kosovo participates in Council of Europe **Octopus Project** and **GLACY+** capacity-building activities as a de facto participant under the "Kosovo*" designation.
