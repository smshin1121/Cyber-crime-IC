## Source Summary

DEA press release dated February 11, 2020 (one day before the SDIL DOJ press release) announcing Melissa Scanlan's 13-year sentence for trafficking fentanyl pills under the "Drug Llama" moniker on Dream Market. Confirms DEA lead-agency role, USPS Inspection Service / HSI participation, and the international money-laundering conspiracy element.

Parallel federal-agency (DEA) confirmation for [[operation-us-v-scanlan-dark-web]] and the related [[operation-us-v-brandon-arias-dark-web]]. The international-money-laundering charge supplies the cross-border IC element for both operations.
