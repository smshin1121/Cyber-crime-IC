## Source Summary

Tier-1 Korean-language primary press release issued by the **Seoul Eastern District Prosecutors' Office** ([[supreme-prosecutors-office-korea|대검찰청]] subordinate office) on 2026-04-30 (10:00 embargo), announcing the arrest, forced repatriation, and indictment-in-custody of a Korean-national voice phishing call-centre cell that had been operating in real time from a Clark (Philippines) office during 2025-06–07. The case was worked end-to-end by the **보이스피싱범죄 합동수사부 (Voice-Phishing Joint Investigation Unit, "합수부")** in cooperation with **Philippines-stationed Korean prosecutorial investigators (파견 검찰수사관)** and the **Philippine Bureau of Immigration Fugitive Search Unit (BI FSU / 수배자 추적대)**. Custody was secured through INTERPOL Red Notices and Korean passport invalidation; forced repatriation was coordinated with [[ministry-of-justice-korea|MOJ]] International Criminal Affairs and the Korean Embassy Manila police attaché.

## Relevance to IC

This source documents a Korea-as-requesting-state cooperation pattern that is **structurally distinct** from the [[korea-cambodia-philippines-73-extradition-2026|73-person KNPA extradition (2026-04-27)]] already in the wiki:

1. **Prosecution-led, not police-led.** The Korean lead actor is the [[supreme-prosecutors-office-korea|SPO]] (대검찰청 → 서울동부지검 합수부), with prosecutorial investigators physically deployed in-country, rather than the [[knpa|KNPA]]-led 코리아전담반 model documented for Cambodia.
2. **In-country prosecutorial investigator + foreign immigration authority chain.** The named partner agency is the **Philippine Bureau of Immigration Fugitive Search Unit (FSU)**, exercising domestic immigration-violation detention at the Bicutan facility, which is then converted into a repatriation channel via INTERPOL Red Notice. This is a different cooperation channel from PNP-to-KNPA police-to-police cooperation and warrants its own mechanism tracking.
3. **Four-agency Korean-side composition.** The 합수부 header lists Prosecution / Police / Customs / Tax Service together — i.e., the 합수부 is a four-pillar fusion structure for voice-phishing transnational fraud, not a single-agency unit.
4. **Verifiable, dated timeline.** The press release supplies a fully dated 2025-07-24 → 2026-04-28 cooperation timeline with named legal mechanisms (INTERPOL Red Notice, passport invalidation, MOJ International Criminal Affairs coordination, Manila Embassy police attaché) — a level of mechanism specificity rarely available in tier-1 primary sources for Korean transnational cybercrime cases.
5. **New modus-operandi attestation.** The 'review-task' (리뷰달기) two-stage mission scam — small-reward trust-building mission → high-extraction 'purchase-authentication team mission' with debt-coercion script — is presented as a new typology (신종 수법) in the primary text and is useful for the wiki's [[voice-phishing-ic|voice-phishing IC]] crime-type page.
