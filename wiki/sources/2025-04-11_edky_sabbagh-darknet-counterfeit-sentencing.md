## Source Summary

On 11 April 2025, DOJ announced that Alessandro Sabbagh was sentenced to 78 months in prison for conspiring to sell counterfeit alprazolam pills over darknet marketplaces. DOJ said Sabbagh and coconspirators manufactured counterfeit pills and received payment in cryptocurrencies.
