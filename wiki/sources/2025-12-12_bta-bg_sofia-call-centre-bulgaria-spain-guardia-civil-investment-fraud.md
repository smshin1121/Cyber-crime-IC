## Summary

This source is a 2025-12-12 BTA news article (Bulgarian News Agency, Bulgaria's national wire service) reporting an operational announcement by the Sofia City Prosecutor's Office. It describes a 2025-12-11 multi-agency raid in Sofia against an organized crime group running fraudulent online investment platforms via call centres. International cooperation was bilateral with Spain's Guardia Civil, channelled through EU police-cooperation mechanisms under Bulgarian authority direction.

Reliability is rated high (BTA is the official Bulgarian state news agency, and the article is a near-verbatim relay of the Sofia City Prosecutor's Office announcement). Credibility is rated probably-true rather than confirmed because the announcement is summarized through BTA's report rather than reproduced as the original SCPO release; specific numbers (suspect counts, victim counts, total scheme value) are not stated and would need a corroborating Eurojust / Europol / Spanish-side source to upgrade.

The action does NOT appear to be the same event as the Eurojust 700M cryptocurrency-fraud network takedown (Phase 1 2025-10-27, Phase 2 2025-11-25 to 2025-11-26): the announcement does not name Eurojust, Europol, France, Belgium, Cyprus, Germany, or Israel, and the timing (2025-12-11) post-dates Phase 2 by approximately two weeks. It is treated here as a distinct bilateral Bulgaria-Spain operation pending further corroboration.
