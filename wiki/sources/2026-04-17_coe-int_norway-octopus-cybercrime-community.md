## Source Summary

Norway is a **non-EU but deeply integrated** European partner on cybercrime cooperation. **Kripos** (Den nasjonale enhet for bekjempelse av organisert og annen alvorlig kriminalitet) is the National Criminal Investigation Service and hosts the **National Cyber Crime Centre (NC3)**. Norway ratified the Budapest Convention in 2006 and cooperates closely with EU mechanisms via the European Economic Area (EEA) agreement and Schengen participation.
