## Source Summary

BSI's Avalanche background page supplies the German technical and victim-remediation perspective on the takedown. It confirms the 30 November 2016 action, explains the supporting role played by BSI, and documents that the disruption did not itself clean infected machines, requiring prolonged follow-up notification and remediation.

## Relevance to IC

This source is a primary German-government reference for how public authorities handled victim notification, sinkholing and post-takedown cleanup. It also helps connect [[operation-avalanche]] and [[andromeda-botnet-takedown]] because BSI explicitly lists Andromeda/Gamarue among the malware families hosted on the infrastructure.
