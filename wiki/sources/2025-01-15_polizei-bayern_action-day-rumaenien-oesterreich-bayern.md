## Source Summary

Tier-1 joint press release of the Bayerische Polizei (Polizeipräsidium Mittelfranken / Kriminalpolizei Nürnberg) and the Zentralstelle Cybercrime Bayern (ZCB) of the Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Bamberg, dated **15 January 2025**, reporting on a **tri-country coordinated action day on 17 December 2024** against an internationally operating internet-fraud organised-crime group. The release explicitly enumerates the cooperating LE/prosecutor authorities (Germany: ZCB Bayern + Polizeipräsidium Mittelfranken; Austria: Landeskriminalamt Tirol; Romania: D.I.I.C.O.T; coordinating EU body: [[eurojust|Eurojust]]) and the cooperation modality (Joint Investigation Team with Romanian prosecutorial authorities under Eurojust participation; cross-border prosecution by Bavarian, Austrian, and Romanian investigators).
