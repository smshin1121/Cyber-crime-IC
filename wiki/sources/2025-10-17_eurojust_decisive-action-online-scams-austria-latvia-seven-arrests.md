## Source Summary

Official Eurojust newsroom press release on the October 2025 Austria-Latvia-Estonia online scams JIT enforcement action. Tier-1 intergovernmental judicial cooperation agency primary source.

## Relevance to IC

This is the controlling source for the four participating states (Austria, Latvia, Estonia, Finland), the Eurojust-hosted JIT structure, the named prosecutors' offices and police authorities in each state, the action-day coordination, the arrest count, the seizure inventory (SIM boxes, SIM cards, servers, vehicles), Europol's analytical support and on-site Riga deployment, and the Shadowserver Foundation private-sector linkage. Verbatim cooperation quote: "To tackle the criminal network, Eurojust supported and financed a joint investigation team (JIT) between the Austrian, Estonian and Latvian authorities. Further assistance was provided by the Finnish authorities."

## L24 Compliance Note

The L24 ≥2-country IC requirement is satisfied: the tier-1 source explicitly names cooperating LE/prosecutorial authorities in three EU Member States (Austria, Latvia, Estonia) with additional support from a fourth (Finland). All four are operating jurisdictions with formal LE/judicial cooperation, not adversary/origin/destination states.
