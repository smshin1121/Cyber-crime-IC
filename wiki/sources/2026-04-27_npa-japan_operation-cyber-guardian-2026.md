## Summary

Official Japanese-language press release from the [[japan-npa|Japan National Police Agency]] reporting the results of the second iteration of the international joint Operation Cyber Guardian, conducted 23 March – 17 April 2026 across seven Asian jurisdictions ([[japan]], [[singapore]], [[thailand]], [[south-korea]], [[hong-kong]], [[brunei]], [[malaysia]]).

Tier-1 primary source per CLAUDE.md (national police agency). Cross-validated by Malaysia PDRM Chief statement reproduced on RTM Berita (Malaysian state broadcaster).
