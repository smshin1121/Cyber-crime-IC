## Source Summary

The FBI New York field-office release documents the U.S. charging layer of the Zeus bank-fraud action. It confirms 37 federal defendants, malware-enabled account compromise, false-name receiving accounts, and proceeds transferred overseas through a money-mule organization.

## Relevance to IC

The source supports [[operation-trident-breach|Operation Trident Breach]] by showing how the international Zeus theft scheme was converted into U.S. charges and by preserving the cross-border money-mule and foreign-law-enforcement cooperation context.
