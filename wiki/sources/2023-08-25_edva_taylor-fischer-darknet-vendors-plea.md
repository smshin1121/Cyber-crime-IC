## Source Summary

On 25 August 2023, DOJ announced that Cheerish Noel Taylor and Robert James Fischer pleaded guilty to conspiring to distribute heroin, methamphetamine, para-fluorofentanyl, and other drugs over the darknet. DOJ said the couple ran vendor monikers such as `SafeServe` and `Sky_HIGH` across multiple darknet markets and made more than 1,100 sales.
