## Source Profile

This is the **second tier-1 own-domain primary source** for *Operación Aliados por la Infancia VI* (the first being the [[2026-04-29_mpf-caba_aliados-por-la-infancia-vi-megaoperativo-internacional|MPF CABA Argentina release]]). The Panama release was issued by the **Procuraduría General de la Nación de Panamá (PGN)** — Panama's national prosecutor's office — on the same calendar day as the action (2026-04-28), one day before the Argentine prosecutor's roll-up release.

The release is short (5 paragraphs) and tightly scoped to the Panama-jurisdiction contribution, but it explicitly:
1. Names the personal lead (procurador Luis Carlos Gómez)
2. Names the Panama auxiliary law-enforcement bodies (DNIP, DIJ — both Policía Nacional)
3. Names the US cooperation partner (Homeland Security Investigations — i.e., US DHS-HSI)
4. Provides Panama-jurisdiction-specific arrest count (7) and search-location count (province of Panamá + Chiriquí)
5. Confirms the international scope and dates (15 countries, simultaneous)
6. Lists 5 named partner countries (Argentina, Brasil, Costa Rica, España, Francia)
