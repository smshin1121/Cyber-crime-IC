## Source Summary

The current U.S. Code page for 18 U.S.C. 1343 defines the federal wire-fraud offence for schemes using interstate or foreign wire, radio, or television communications. In cybercrime records it often appears as the charging bridge for online fraud, BEC, marketplace, credential, and platform-abuse cases.
