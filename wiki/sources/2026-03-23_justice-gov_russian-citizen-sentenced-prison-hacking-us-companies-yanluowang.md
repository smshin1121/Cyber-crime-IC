## Source Summary

DOJ Office of Public Affairs press release (Number 26-280, dated 2026-03-23) announcing the 81-month federal prison sentence in the Southern District of Indiana of Aleksei Volkov, 26, of St. Petersburg, Russia, for his role as an initial-access broker enabling Yanluowang and other cybercrime groups to conduct ransomware attacks against US companies. The release records (a) USD 9 167 198.19+ in actual victim losses (full restitution ordered) and over USD 24 million in intended losses, (b) 4 SDIN counts (unlawful transfer of a means of identification; trafficking in access information; access device fraud; aggravated identity theft) and 2 EDPA counts (conspiracy to commit computer fraud; conspiracy to commit money laundering) consolidated in SDIN, (c) plea date 2025-11-25, and (d) the bilateral US-Italy extradition pathway, with arrest in Rome and extradition coordinated through DOJ Office of International Affairs working with the Government of Italy.
