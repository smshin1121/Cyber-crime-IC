## Source Summary

Official press release from Portugal's Policia Judiciaria reporting the September 2025 execution, by Spanish authorities, of a European Arrest Warrant issued by the DIAP da Amadora against a Portuguese-Angolan dual national wanted for computer fraud (burla informatica), unauthorized computer access, and money laundering tied to an online gaming-platform laundering structure under investigation since 2024.

## Relevance to IC

Tier-1 primary source for a four-country international cooperation event (Portugal, Spain, United Kingdom, Angola) using the European Arrest Warrant as the legal vehicle. Notable because it documents PJ-led cyber fraud / money-laundering enforcement against a dual-national suspect operating across EU and non-EU jurisdictions, without recourse to Eurojust or Europol (showing routine bilateral EAW execution).
