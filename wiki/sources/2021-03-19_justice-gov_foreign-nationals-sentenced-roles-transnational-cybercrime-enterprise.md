## Source Summary

DOJ Office of Public Affairs press release dated March 19, 2021, announcing the sentencing of Sergey Medvedev (Russia, 10 years) and Marko Leopard (North Macedonia, 5 years) for their administrator roles in the Infraud Organization. Reaffirms the District of Nevada venue, the $568 million total loss figure, and the joint IRS-CI / HSI / FBI investigation that produced the underlying 2018 indictment.

## Relevance to IC

Sentencing follow-up press release for the original [[operation-thirty-six-defendants-indicted-for-alleged-roles-in-transnational-criminal-organization-responsible-for-more-t]] and [[operation-us-v-bondarenko-infraud]] indictments. Demonstrates concrete cross-border prosecutorial outcomes following extraditions from Thailand and apprehensions in Eastern Europe.
