## Source Summary

IRS-CI press release announcing the February 2026 federal indictment of two Glastonbury men in a $3 million online-gambling fraud scheme using stolen PII purchased via the darknet and Telegram. Confirms the IRS-CI investigative role alongside the U.S. Attorney's Office for the District of Connecticut.
