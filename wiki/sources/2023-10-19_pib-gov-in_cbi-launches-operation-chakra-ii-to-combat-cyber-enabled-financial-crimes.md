## Source Summary

The PIB / Ministry of Home Affairs release is the primary public record for Operation Chakra-II. It describes the operation as a CBI-led action against transnational cyber-enabled financial crime infrastructure, with 76 searches and seizures of phones, laptops or hard disks, server images, SIM cards, pen drives, bank-account material, and email-account data.

## Relevance to IC

The release directly supports international-cooperation classification because it names the FBI, INTERPOL Cyber Crime Directorate and IFCACC, the UK National Crime Agency, Singapore Police Force, and Germany's BKA as counterparts receiving or developing further leads.
