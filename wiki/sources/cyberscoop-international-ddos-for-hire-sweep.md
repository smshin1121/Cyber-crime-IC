## Summary

CyberScoop's coverage of the **December 2016 international DDoS-for-hire sweep** describes a 5-day (5-9 December 2016) operation targeting *users* (not operators) of stresser and booter services. Key points:

1. **Targeting users, not operators** — the operation specifically targeted individuals who *paid for* DDoS-for-hire services, an enforcement strategy focused on shrinking the demand side of the market
2. **Demographics** — CyberScoop highlights that the majority of arrestees were male teenagers, aligning with the parallel awareness campaign's diversion framing
3. **Scale** — 34 arrests and ~101 suspects interviewed/cautioned across 13 countries (12 European nations + Australia, coordinated by Europol EC3 and FBI via the International Cyber Crime Coordination Cell (IC4))
4. **Continuity** — explicit reference to earlier January 2016 Europol enforcement as part of a multi-year targeting campaign
