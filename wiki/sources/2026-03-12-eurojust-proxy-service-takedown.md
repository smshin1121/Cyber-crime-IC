## Summary

Official Eurojust press release announcing the takedown of an unnamed IP proxy service offering anonymisation infrastructure to cybercriminals. Victims of the underlying malware (used to gain access to IP addresses for the proxy service) were identified in **102 countries**. Authorities from **8 countries** participated; only **four are named**: [[france|France]], [[austria|Austria]], [[netherlands|Netherlands]], and the [[united-states|United States]]. Coordination was provided by [[eurojust|Eurojust]] and [[europol-ec3|Europol]]. The operators are also suspected of running a payment platform supporting the proxy service.
