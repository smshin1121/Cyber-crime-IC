## Source Summary

On 22 March 2023, DOJ announced sentencing for three defendants convicted in a darknet fentanyl trafficking conspiracy involving AlphaBay, Dream, Wall Street, and Empire. DOJ reported that Alex Ogando received 12 years, Olatunji Dawodu 12 years, and Luis Spencer 12 and a half years after the conspiracy distributed kilograms of fentanyl-pressed pills.
