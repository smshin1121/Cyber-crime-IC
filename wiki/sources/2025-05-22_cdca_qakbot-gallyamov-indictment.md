## Source Summary

On 22 May 2025, the Department of Justice unsealed an indictment charging Rustam Rafailevich Gallyamov, 48, of Moscow, Russia, with leading the QakBot malware conspiracy since 2008. The indictment was unsealed in conjunction with Operation Endgame, the international law enforcement campaign targeting ransomware-enabling infrastructure. ### Charges Gallyamov was charged with conspiracy, wire fraud, and related offenses. He allegedly developed and deployed QakBot to build a botnet that provided access to co-conspirators who deployed ransomware variants including ProLock, DopplePaymer, Egregor, REvil, Conti, Name Locker, Black Basta, and Cactus.
