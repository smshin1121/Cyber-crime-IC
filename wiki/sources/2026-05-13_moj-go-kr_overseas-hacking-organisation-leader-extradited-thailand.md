## Korean side (lead + co-lead)

- **[[ministry-of-justice-korea|법무부 (MOJ Korea)]]** — central authority (공조 중앙기관); led extradition request, dispatched prosecutors and investigators to Thailand, executed transfer
- **[[knpa|경찰청 (KNPA)]]** — co-lead; accompanied defendant from Bangkok to Incheon
- (Per related Korean media: cooperation channels run through MOJ International Criminal Affairs Division — 국제형사과)

## Foreign LE / prosecutor counterparts (verbatim from MOJ release)

- **태국 대검찰청 (Royal Thai Office of the Attorney General — OAG Thailand)** — counterpart central authority; Jul 2025 in-person meetings; Oct–Dec 2025 video conferences
- **태국 경찰청 (Royal Thai Police)** — operational counterpart; Jul 2025 in-person meetings
- **[[interpol|INTERPOL]]** — joint-operation channel (인터폴 합동작전) through which the May 2025 on-site arrest of the co-leader and the present defendant's apprehension at the same scene were carried out

## Defendant nationality / venue

- Defendant ("ㄱ○○"): 40-year-old male, **Chinese national**
- Crime base: organised "in Thailand and other overseas locations" (태국 등 해외)
- Forensic indictment: planned in Seoul (Korean territorial-personality jurisdiction over Korean victims and use-of-Korean-financial-system theory of harm)
