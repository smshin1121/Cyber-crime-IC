## Summary

The Belgian Federal Judicial Police East Flanders (Federale Gerechtelijke Politie Oost-Vlaanderen, FGP Oost-Vlaanderen) announced on 22 April 2026 the arrest of a 20-year-old suspect accused of supplying phishing panels (a key piece of Crime-as-a-Service infrastructure) to fraud gangs. The investigation was led by the Public Prosecutor's Office of East Flanders (parket van Oost-Vlaanderen) under the Dendermonde investigating judge, with explicit cooperation from [[europol-ec3|Europol]]. The suspect was arrested in the Netherlands on 26 January 2026, his residence searched, and he was surrendered to Belgium on 16 April 2026 where he was remanded in custody.
