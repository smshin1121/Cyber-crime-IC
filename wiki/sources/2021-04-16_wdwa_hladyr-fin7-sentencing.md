## Source Summary

The U.S. Attorney's Office for the Western District of Washington announced on April 16, 2021 that Fedir Hladyr was sentenced to 10 years in prison for his role as a systems administrator for the FIN7 cybercrime organization. The release states that FIN7 targeted restaurant, gaming, and hospitality companies to steal payment card data from more than 100 U.S. victims. - Defendant: Fedir Hladyr - Court: U.S. District Court for the Western District of Washington
