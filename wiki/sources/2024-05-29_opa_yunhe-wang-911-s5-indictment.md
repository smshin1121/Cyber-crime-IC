## Source Summary

The Department of Justice announced on May 29, 2024 that a federal grand jury in the Southern District of Texas had indicted YunHe Wang for creating and operating the 911 S5 residential proxy botnet. DOJ described 911 S5 as the world's largest botnet, with more than 19 million IP addresses compromised in nearly 200 countries. According to the release, Wang allegedly sold access to infected devices through the 911 S5 service, enabling fraud, bomb threats, child exploitation offenses, financial theft, and export-control evasion. The operation involved coordinated investigative and seizure activity with partners in Germany, Singapore, Thailand, and the United States. - Cross-border evidence and enforcement support from Germany, Singapore, and Thailand
