## Source Summary

On 14 July 2023, DOJ announced sentencing outcomes for three darknet fentanyl vendors operating under monikers including `TrustedTraphouse`, `GoldenTrails`, and `PopcornPlug`. DOJ said the defendants made over 1,300 darknet sales and distributed fentanyl-laced counterfeit pills, methamphetamine, heroin, and cocaine. Veronica Dittman received 60 months; Schiffner and Langer had previously received sentences exceeding 20 years combined.
