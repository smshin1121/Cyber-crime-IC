## Source Summary

SPF press release dated 2025-06-17 announcing Operation Cryptoscam — a Singapore-Brazil cooperation case originated by SPF based on a January 2024 USD 1.4 million crypto-wallet compromise report, traced to Brazil and alerted to the Brazilian Federal Police (FPB) via INTERPOL channels. FPB executed the action in May 2025 across two simultaneous operations (one of which was Operation Cryptoscam) producing 26 search-and-seizure warrants + 11 arrest warrants.

## IC Pattern

This release establishes a Singapore → INTERPOL → Brazil intelligence-pipelined cooperation pattern that the wiki has not previously documented. Notable features:

- **SPF-as-originating-IC-partner** — the operation began as a domestic Singapore investigation (CCC under CID) following a USD 1.4 million crypto-wallet-compromise report.
- **INTERPOL international cooperation channels** as the cross-jurisdictional alerting mechanism.
- **Federal Police of Brazil (FPB)** — Repression Electronic Bank Fraud Coordination + FPB Regional Office Itajaí, Santa Catarina — as the Brazil-side action authorities.
- **Two-operation simultaneous May 2025 FPB action-week** with 26 search/seizure + 11 arrest warrants — Operation Cryptoscam being the SPF-originated track and one other (unnamed) parallel track being a Brazil-domestic financial-crime investigation that ran concurrently.
