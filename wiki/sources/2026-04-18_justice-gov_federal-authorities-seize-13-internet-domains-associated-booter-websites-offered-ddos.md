## Source Summary

DOJ CDCA announced the May 2023 Operation PowerOFF follow-up sweep, including seizure of 13 DDoS-for-hire domains. Ten were described as reincarnations of services seized in the December 2022 wave. The release also records four guilty pleas by defendants linked to RoyalStresser.com, SecurityTeam.io, Astrostress.com, and Booter.sx.

## Relevance to IC

This official DOJ source supports [[operation-power-off]] by documenting the reconstitution problem and the campaign's conversion of earlier infrastructure disruption into guilty pleas.
