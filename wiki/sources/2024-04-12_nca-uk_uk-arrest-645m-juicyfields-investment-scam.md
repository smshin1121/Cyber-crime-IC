## Summary

Official UK National Crime Agency press release (12 April 2024) announcing the UK arrest in the Eurojust-coordinated international takedown of the JuicyFields cannabis-investment Ponzi scheme, with €645 million in confirmed investments and 9 arrests across 10 European countries on 11 April 2024.
