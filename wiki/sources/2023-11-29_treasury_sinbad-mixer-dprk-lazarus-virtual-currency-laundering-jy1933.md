## Source Summary

This OFAC press release (JY-1933, 2023-11-29) announces the designation of Sinbad.io, a Bitcoin-based virtual-currency mixer, as a "key money-laundering tool" of the previously-designated [[north-korea|DPRK]] Lazarus Group. The action is highly likely the most operationally significant 2023 mixer designation by OFAC because it directly ties a single laundering vehicle to three specific DPRK cryptocurrency heists with a combined ~$820 million in stolen funds: the $620M Axie Infinity heist (March 2022), the $100M Horizon Bridge heist (June 2022), and the $100M Atomic Wallet heist (June 3, 2023).

The release establishes a clear OFAC enforcement pattern against virtual-currency mixers used by Lazarus Group: Blender.io (2022-05-06, first-ever mixer designation), Tornado Cash (2022-11-08 redesignation), two OTC traders (2023-04-24), and now Sinbad.io (2023-11-29). Industry experts believe Sinbad is highly likely a successor to Blender.io, suggesting infrastructure resilience among DPRK laundering networks despite repeated designations.

The legal authorities cited are confirmed: **E.O. 13694** as amended by **E.O. 13757** (cyber-enabled activity authority) and **E.O. 13722** (DPRK government / Workers' Party authority — used because the Lazarus Group itself is a designated DPRK government instrumentality). The release does not invoke E.O. 13687 or the DPRK-specific E.O. 13810/13551 used in later 2025 DPRK financial-network designations, indicating that the cyber-enabled and DPRK-government authorities were the prosecutor's chosen frame for a mixer infrastructure target.

The release also documents a secondary use case: Sinbad is described as serving non-state cybercriminals for "sanctions evasion, drug trafficking, the purchase of child sexual abuse materials, and additional illicit sales on darknet marketplaces." This is consistent with the multi-purpose laundering profile common to Bitcoin mixers and supports reading Sinbad as both a state-sponsored-theft laundering pathway and a general criminal-utility mixer.
