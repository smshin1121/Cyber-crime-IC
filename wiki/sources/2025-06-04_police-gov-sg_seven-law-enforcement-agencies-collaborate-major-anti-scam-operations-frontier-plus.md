## Summary

Official **Singapore Police Force own-domain press release** (police.gov.sg/media-hub/news, 2025-06-04) issued one day after the Hong Kong release on the same operation. This is the **Singapore-side counterpart** of HKSAR Government Information Services Press Release P2025060300475 (2025-06-03), reporting the results of the **first joint enforcement operation of the FRONTIER+ Cross-border Anti-Scam Collaboration Platform** conducted 2025-04-28 to 2025-05-28.

The release enumerates all seven participating law enforcement agencies with their full operational unit designations, gives identical aggregate results to the HK release, supplies a Singapore-specific breakdown that matches the HK Annex per-jurisdiction figures, and documents a concrete cross-border-recovery case study (Chinese Services Impersonation Scam, S$19K+ recovered from Malaysia via Malaysia's National Scam Response Centre).
