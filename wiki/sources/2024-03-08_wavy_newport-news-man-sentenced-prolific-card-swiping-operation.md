## Source Summary

Local Hampton Roads NBC affiliate WAVY-TV reported on the sentencing of Damier Dorsey, 29, of Newport News, who received nine years and 11 months in prison for leading a credit card fraud and identity theft scheme that caused at least $1.5 million in loss. Dorsey purchased stolen account data from the BriansClub darknet marketplace and re-encoded the data onto cards.
