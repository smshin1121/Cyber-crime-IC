DOJ Eastern District of Texas press release dated March 12, 2025, announcing John Khuu's 87-month sentence for the Operation Crypto Runner cryptocurrency money laundering conspiracy. Khuu imported MDMA and counterfeit pharmaceuticals from Germany and distributed via dark web markets, laundering over $5.35 million in proceeds.
