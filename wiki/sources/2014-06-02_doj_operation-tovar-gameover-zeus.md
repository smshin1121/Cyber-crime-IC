## Source Summary

DOJ announced a multinational legal and technical action against the Gameover Zeus botnet and CryptoLocker ransomware infrastructure. The release identifies participating foreign law-enforcement agencies, the criminal charges against Evgeniy Bogachev, the use of court-authorized substitute servers, and the CERT/private-sector remediation model.

## Relevance to IC
