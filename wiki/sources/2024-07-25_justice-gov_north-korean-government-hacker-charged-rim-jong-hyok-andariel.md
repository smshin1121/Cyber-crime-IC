## Source Summary

DOJ Office of Public Affairs press release dated **2024-07-25** announcing the indictment of North Korean national **Rim Jong Hyok** (림종혁) for conspiracy to commit ransomware attacks against US hospitals and healthcare companies and laundering the proceeds to fund subsequent intrusions into US Air Force bases, NASA-OIG, and defense, technology, and energy targets across the US, Republic of Korea, Taiwan, and PRC. The indictment (Case 2:24-cr-20061-HLT-ADM, returned 2024-07-24, US District Court, District of Kansas, Kansas City Docket) charges Rim with two counts: 18 U.S.C. § 371 conspiracy and 18 U.S.C. § 1956(h) money-laundering conspiracy.

The release attributes Rim to North Korea's **Reconnaissance General Bureau (RGB)** and identifies the unit publicly as **Andariel / Onyx Sleet / APT45 / Silent Chollima**. Co-announced same day: a joint Cybersecurity Advisory **AA24-207A** ("North Korea Cyber Group Conducts Global Espionage Campaign to Advance Regime's Military and Nuclear Programs") co-authored by **FBI, CISA, NSA, U.S. Cyber Command's Cyber National Mission Force (CNMF), DoD Cyber Crime Center (DC3)** on the US side, and explicitly with **Republic of Korea National Intelligence Service (NIS / 국가정보원)**, **Republic of Korea National Police Agency (NPA / KNPA / 경찰청)**, and **United Kingdom National Cyber Security Centre (NCSC)** as tier-1 international cooperation partners.

Financial actions announced: approximately **$114,000 in virtual currency** interdicted (DOJ + FBI), with approximately **$500,000** in prior FBI virtual-currency seizures referenced. The **U.S. Department of State Rewards for Justice** program offered up to **$10 million** for information leading to Rim's location or identification. Rim is reported by the U.S. government as last known to be in North Korea.

## Relevance to IC

This is the **principal tier-1 source** for the wiki on the 2024 US indictment of an RGB / Andariel actor for ransomware against US healthcare critical infrastructure. It is unusually high-IC-density: (a) the indictment itself is bilateral-cooperation-relevant because the victim roster spans the US, ROK, Taiwan, and PRC; (b) the same-day joint Cybersecurity Advisory AA24-207A is a tier-1 multilateral IC artifact co-signed by ROK NIS, ROK NPA, and UK NCSC alongside US authoring agencies; and (c) it is directly relevant to the wiki's standing Korean-cooperation corpus — South Korea is named both as a co-authoring authority on the joint advisory and as a victim jurisdiction (1 manufacturing-company ransomware victim + 2 defense-contractor exfiltration victims, per indictment paragraphs 9, 19, 20). The case is a landmark public-record indictment against an explicitly state-sponsored ransomware actor, and the $10M Rewards-for-Justice posting is one of the most concrete IC-incentive instruments the State Department has deployed against an RGB / Andariel individual.
