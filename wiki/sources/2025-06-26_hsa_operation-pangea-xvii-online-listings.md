## Source Summary

The HSA release provides national official confirmation of Singapore's digital enforcement under [[operation-pangea-xvii|Operation Pangea XVII]]. It confirms the operation's Internet-based character, INTERPOL coordination, 90-country participation, local e-commerce and social-media listing removals, seller warnings, and website blocking.

## Relevance to IC

The source is strong evidence for the operation's cyber-enabled and international-cooperation character because it links online platform enforcement to INTERPOL coordination and overseas regulatory partnerships.
