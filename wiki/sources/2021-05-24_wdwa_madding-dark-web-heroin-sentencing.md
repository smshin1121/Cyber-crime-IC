## Source Summary

On 24 May 2021, DOJ announced that Zachary Madding was sentenced to five years in prison for completing more than 1,600 heroin sales on AlphaBay, Dream Market, and Wall Street Market. The release emphasizes the nationwide scale of his heroin mail-order trafficking and the sentencing court's concern about the harm caused to users and families.
