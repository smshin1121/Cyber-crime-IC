## Source Summary

Brazil's Ministry of Justice and Public Security reports the seventh phase of Operation 404, including blocking or suspension of 675 sites and 14 illegal streaming applications, 30 searches, and nine arrests. The source also records prior phases from 2019 through 2023.

## Relevance to IC

This source supports treating Operation 404 as a multi-phase international digital-piracy and illegal-streaming operation with a clear 2019-2024 timeframe.
