## Source Summary

ICE announced the extradition of Jordan Georgievski, a Macedonian national charged in the Carder.su case, from Albania to the United States. The release connects the extradition to Operation Open Market and identifies the international coordination chain involving Albanian, Austrian, Interpol, DOJ, State Department, and HSI attaché channels.

## Relevance to IC

This source is the strongest international-cooperation evidence for [[operation-open-market|Operation Open Market]]. It documents extradition and multi-jurisdiction coordination rather than only the domestic arrest wave.
