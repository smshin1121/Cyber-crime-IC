## Source Summary

Eurojust reported that Genesis Market was taken down in a multi-country effort called Operation Cookie Monster. The release frames the action as involving the United States, nine EU countries, Australia, Canada, and the United Kingdom, and states that Eurojust and Europol supported coordinated arrests and searches across 13 countries.

## Relevance to IC

This source confirms the European judicial-coordination dimension of [[operation-cookie-monster|Operation Cookie Monster]]. It also supplies the public country list and confirms that the action involved both law enforcement and judicial authorities rather than a single-country seizure.
