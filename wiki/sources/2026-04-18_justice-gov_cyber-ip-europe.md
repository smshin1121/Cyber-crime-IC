## Source Summary

The DOJ Cyber & IP Europe page summarizes the International Computer Hacking and Intellectual Property program's European capacity-building and case-support work. For this corpus, its key value is a January 2026 entry naming [[operation-switch-off-illegal-iptv-2026|Operation Switch Off]] as a Romanian-Italian joint case involving illegal streaming of copyright-protected movies and television shows.

The source reports that ICHIP-mentored Romanian economic police officers conducted searches and issued a subpoena for interrogation in three Romanian cities, while Italian authorities seized three illegal IPTV platforms. It also records rightsholder-reported damages and a Romanian target connected to servers operated from Romania and an African country.
