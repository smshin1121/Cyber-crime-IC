## Summary

Tier-1 Dutch national police press release documenting a Netherlands-Spain bilateral cybercrime cooperation: Politie Limburg located a fugitive bank-helpdesk fraud suspect operating from Spain; on a Dutch request, the Spanish Policia Nacional UDYCO Greco Costa del Sol unit arrested him in Estepona on the same day Dutch officers searched two co-suspect addresses in Borne and Deurningen.

## Why it matters for the wiki

Concrete bilateral NL-ES arrest cooperation in a bank-helpdesk fraud case — useful for the wiki's documentation of (a) Dutch outbound cooperation patterns in cyber-enabled fraud, (b) Spain as an executing jurisdiction for Dutch cybercrime warrants on the Costa del Sol, (c) the UDYCO Greco specialised-unit role in transnational cyber-fraud arrests.
