## Summary

Reuters' wire story covers **Europol's 2024-09-18 announcement** that an international coalition had dismantled the encrypted-comms platform **Ghost**, which had been popular with transnational organised crime for drug trafficking and money laundering. Headline facts:

1. **51 arrests**: 38 in Australia, 11 in Ireland, 1 in Canada, 1 in Italy (Sacra Corona Unita)
2. **More arrests expected** (ongoing investigation)
3. **Operational outcomes**: Prevented 'several threats to life', dismantled a drug lab in Australia, seized weapons, drugs, and ~EUR 1 million in cash globally
4. **Participating jurisdictions**: Australia, Canada, France, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Netherlands, Sweden, USA — **Europol coordinated**
5. **Technical pattern**: Ghost is another in the series of bespoke criminal-comms platforms targeted by Western LE — following EncroChat (2020), Sky ECC (2021), ANOM/Trojan Shield (2021)
6. **Australian lead**: Australian Federal Police publicly used the codename **Operation Kraken** for its domestic component
