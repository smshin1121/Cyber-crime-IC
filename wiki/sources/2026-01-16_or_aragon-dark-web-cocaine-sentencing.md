## Source Summary

On 16 January 2026, DOJ announced that Dominick Jeffrey Aragon was sentenced to 60 months in prison for attempting to possess with intent to distribute cocaine purchased from the dark web. DOJ said postal inspectors and task-force officers intercepted multiple parcels containing cocaine and other controlled substances bound for Aragon in 2025.
