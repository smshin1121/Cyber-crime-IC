## Source Summary

The Department of Justice reported a sentencing in Operation Open Market and summarized the broader Carder.su investigation. The release identifies Carder.su's forum model, estimated membership, former Soviet Union hosting context, four indictments, 56 charged persons, and 33 convictions by December 2015.

## Relevance to IC

This source corroborates the scale, charging posture, and international nature of [[operation-open-market|Operation Open Market]], including the foreign infrastructure and unresolved fugitive dimension.
