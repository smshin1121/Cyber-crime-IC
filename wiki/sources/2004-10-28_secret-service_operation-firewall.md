## Source Summary

The U.S. Secret Service announced Operation Firewall as a global undercover cyber-financial crime investigation that produced 28 arrests and disrupted Shadowcrew, CarderPlanet, and Darkprofits. The source identifies the operation's cross-border scope, the stolen-card and identity-theft conduct, and the international law-enforcement partners supporting the case.
