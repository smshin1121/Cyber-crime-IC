## Summary

Official Europol press release announcing the disruption of the LockBit ransomware operation through **Operation Cronos Phase 1** on 20 February 2024. This was a multi-country enforcement action involving 10 countries and 12 agencies, led by the [[uk-nca|UK National Crime Agency]] and coordinated by [[europol-ec3|Europol]] and [[eurojust|Eurojust]].

The source provides detailed operational metrics including arrests, server seizures, indictments, cryptocurrency account freezes, and the release of free decryption tools. It describes the coordination mechanisms used (SIENA messaging, operational meetings, technical sprints) and lists all participating agencies.
