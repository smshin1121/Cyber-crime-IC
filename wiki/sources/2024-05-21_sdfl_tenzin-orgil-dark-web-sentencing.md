## Source Summary

On 21 May 2024, DOJ announced that Tenzin Orgil was sentenced to 168 months in prison for a drug-trafficking enterprise that included dark web sales of methamphetamine and fentanyl as well as clandestine manufacture of MDMA and methamphetamine. The case is notable for combining dark web distribution with physical lab manufacturing.
