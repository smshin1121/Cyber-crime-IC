## Source Summary

DOJ's 4 January 2024 xDedic roundup identifies Joshua Spencer of New York as a charged xDedic defendant convicted of conspiracy to commit access-device fraud and sentenced to 28 months in prison. The official summary does not provide a full narrative of Spencer's conduct, but it places him among the set of marketplace participants prosecuted after the xDedic takedown. The case is therefore useful as a sparse but official indicator that xDedic generated multiple non-administrator prosecutions.
