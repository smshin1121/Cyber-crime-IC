## Source Summary

DEA Seattle Field Division parallel press release on the October 2024 sentencing of Humberto Garcia, a Monroe, Washington resident, to 10 years in federal prison for his role as the "right hand man" in a Puget Sound drug distribution ring trafficking heroin, methamphetamine, and fentanyl. Confirms DOJ press release facts and adds DEA's role as lead investigative agency.
