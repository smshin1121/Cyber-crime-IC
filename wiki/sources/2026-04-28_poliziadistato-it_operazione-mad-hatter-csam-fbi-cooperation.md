## Source Profile

Official press release issued by the Italian State Police (Polizia di Stato) via the Questura di Milano press portal, announcing the conclusion of "Operazione Mad Hatter" — a transnational undercover investigation against online CSAM distribution conducted by the C.N.C.P.O. (Centro Nazionale per il Contrasto alla Pedopornografia Online), the national CSAM-investigation centre of [[italy-polizia-postale|Polizia Postale e per la sicurezza cibernetica]]. The investigation was coordinated by the Procura della Repubblica di Roma and conducted in cooperation with the U.S. [[fbi|Federal Bureau of Investigation]].

The release is a tier-1 primary source: it is published on the official `questure.poliziadistato.it` Polizia di Stato domain, signed institutionally, and provides operational figures (arrests, search warrants, deployed personnel, regional COSC participation) directly from the responsible Italian agency.

## Reliability and Credibility

- **Reliability: high** — official agency publication on a `.poliziadistato.it` Polizia di Stato sub-domain.
- **Credibility: confirmed** — first-party announcement by the lead investigative agency, with the foreign cooperation partner (FBI) explicitly named.
- **Sensitivity: public** — the press release is intended for general distribution; defendants' identities are protected (only ages and provinces are stated, consistent with the Italian presumption-of-innocence formula at the close of the text).

## Italian-Language Legal Terms Preserved

| Italian | English working translation |
|---|---|
| pedopornografia online | online child sexual abuse material (CSAM) |
| arresto in flagranza di reato | in-flagrante (caught-in-the-act) arrest |
| denuncia in stato di libertà | charge / criminal complaint while suspect remains at liberty |
| perquisizione domiciliare, personale e informatica | domiciliary, personal and computer search |
| Procura della Repubblica | Public Prosecutor's Office |
| Centro Nazionale per il Contrasto alla Pedopornografia Online (C.N.C.P.O.) | National Centre for Combating Online Child Pornography |

> [!note] Translation note
> "Denuncia in stato di libertà" has no exact English equivalent; it denotes that a formal criminal report has been forwarded to the prosecutor while the suspect is not in custody — distinct from both U.S. "indictment" and "summons" practice.
