## Source Summary

On 13 January 2025, DOJ announced that Adan Ruiz was sentenced to 215 months and Omar Navia to 180 months for supplying fentanyl-laced pills sold to more than 1,000 customers nationwide via the dark web. The sentencing court described the conspiracy as exceptionally sophisticated.
