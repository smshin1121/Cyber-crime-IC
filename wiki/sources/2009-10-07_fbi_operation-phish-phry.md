## Source Summary

The FBI story announced Operation Phish Phry as a major cyber-fraud takedown involving charges in both the United States and Egypt. It identifies phishing of U.S. bank customers, about USD 1.5 million in fraudulent transfers, and roughly 100 charged people split between U.S. and Egyptian defendants.
