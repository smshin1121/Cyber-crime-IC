## Source Summary

DOJ sentencing press release for Kevin James Strutz of Ceres, California. Strutz was sentenced in November 2023 to 3 years 4 months in prison, followed by three years of supervised release, for two counts of cyberstalking. The release confirms the Airbnb covert-recording-and-extortion scheme against the first victim and the multi-month handwritten letter threat campaign against a second victim.
