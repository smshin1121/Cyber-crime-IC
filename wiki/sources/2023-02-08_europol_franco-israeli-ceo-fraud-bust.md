## Source Summary

Europol supported the dismantling of a Franco-Israeli criminal network that employed business email compromise (BEC) and CEO fraud attacks to divert corporate payments to accounts under the threat actors' control. In one case, the fraudsters stole approximately EUR 38 million from a single company within days. The investigation, led by France, involved five action days between January 2022 and January 2023 across France and Israel. Eight suspects were arrested (six in France, two in Israel), including the group leader based in Israel. The criminal proceeds were laundered through a pre-existing money laundering scheme involving multiple bank accounts in the EU, China, and Israel. Authorities seized approximately EUR 5.5 million in total across multiple countries. - **Operation name:** (Unnamed; Franco-Israeli CEO Fraud investigation) - **Date:** Action days between January 2022 and January 2023
