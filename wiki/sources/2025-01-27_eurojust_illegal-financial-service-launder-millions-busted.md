## Source Summary

Eurojust described an international investigation into a laundering service used by other criminal organisations, detailing the JIT structure, cross-border actions and the seizure and freezing results.

## Relevance to IC

This source is directly relevant because it shows how Eurojust, Europol and multiple national prosecutors and police authorities coordinated a financial-crime and crypto-laundering takedown across Spain, Cyprus, Germany and France.
