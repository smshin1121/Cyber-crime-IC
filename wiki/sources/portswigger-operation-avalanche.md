## Summary

The Daily Swig article covers the **post-takedown fugitive-recapture phase** of Operation Avalanche. It reports that Ukrainian authorities detained alleged Avalanche organiser Genadiy Kapkanov in Kiev after he had previously escaped custody following the 2016 takedown.

The article also restates core Avalanche context: the original four-year investigation involved police from 25 countries, blocked 800,000 domains, and seized several hundred servers. It says Kapkanov also faced charges in France and Germany and could face up to 10 years in prison under Ukrainian law.
