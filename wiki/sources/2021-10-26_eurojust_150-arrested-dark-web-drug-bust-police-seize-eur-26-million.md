## Source Summary

Eurojust press release announcing the results of Operation Dark HunTor on 26 October 2021. The operation produced 150 arrests across nine countries including 65 in the United States, with seizures totaling EUR 26.7 million in cash and cryptocurrency, 234 kg of drugs, and 45 firearms. Eurojust and Europol coordinated the multi-jurisdictional action, which built on intelligence from the earlier DarkMarket takedown.

## Relevance to IC

This source provides parallel coordinating-body confirmation for the Sacramento darknet vendor prosecution of Jonathan Patrick Turrentine, which is part of the US contingent of arrests under [[operation-us-v-jonathan-patrick-turrentine]]. It places the otherwise local Sacramento case within the international Operation Dark HunTor framework.
