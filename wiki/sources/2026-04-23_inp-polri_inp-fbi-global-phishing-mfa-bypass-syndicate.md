## Source Profile

This is an English-language press release on the official newsroom of the **Indonesian National Police** (Kepolisian Negara Republik Indonesia, "Polri"), at `inp.polri.go.id`, dated **23 April 2026**. The byline `(mg/inp/pr/rs)` identifies it as a Polri Public Relations Division (Divisi Humas Polri) product. The release explicitly attributes its quoted official statement to Indonesia's state news agency Antara (`antaranews.com`), but the release itself is the institutional anchor.

The release announces a single coordinated arrest action conducted in Jakarta on **Wednesday, 22 April 2026**, against a couple — designated by initials **GWL** and **FYT** — for operating a global phishing-as-a-service scheme that bypassed Multi-Factor Authentication and enabled Business Email Compromise (BEC) at scale.
