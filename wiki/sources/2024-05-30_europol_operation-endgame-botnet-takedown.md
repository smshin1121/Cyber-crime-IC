## Source Summary

Between 27 and 29 May 2024, **Operation Endgame** — the largest-ever international action against botnets — targeted dropper malware families including IcedID, SystemBC, Pikabot, Smokeloader, Bumblebee, and Trickbot. Coordinated from Europol's headquarters with a command post of 20+ officers from Denmark, France, Germany, and the US, the operation resulted in 4 arrests, over 100 servers taken down across 10 countries, and over 2,000 domains seized. These malware droppers serve as initial-access vectors linked to at least 15 ransomware groups, including BlackBasta, REvil, and Conti. One key suspect accumulated at least EUR 69 million in cryptocurrency from renting criminal infrastructure for ransomware deployment. - **Operation name:** Operation Endgame - **Dates:** 27-29 May 2024
