## Source Summary

Joint BKA + Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Frankfurt am Main (ZIT) press release on the same day as the Europol newsroom announcement (2025-06-16) for the takedown of Archetyp Market under international Operation "Deep Sentinel". The release confirms the German-lead architecture from the German side, enumerates each named international LE partner with the agency's local name (Politie / IGPR / Polisen / Policia Nacional / USDOJ + HSI + IRS-CI), and adds operational detail that the Europol release does not enumerate (Bucharest search, 9-Polizeibehoerde German deployment, 47 smartphones + 45 computers further seized, EUR-7.8M asset total).

## IC Pattern (BKA-side framing)

- **German lead-prosecution + lead-police architecture** stated from the German side: ZIT (Frankfurt am Main) coordinates the prosecutorial side; BKA coordinates the police side.
- **Sequenced international action**: Spanish National Police special unit makes the admin arrest in Barcelona on 2025-06-11; Dutch National Police seizes and shuts down server infrastructure in NL data centre; followed by internationally coordinated additional searches in DE and SE.
- **Europol + Eurojust supporting framing** preserved verbatim: "von Europol und Eurojust unterstuetzt".
- **L24 verbatim IC quote**: "Dieser erneute Ermittlungserfolg von BKA, ZIT und internationalen Partnerdienststellen zeigt, dass die Strafverfolgungsbehoerden in immer schnellerer Abfolge illegale IT-Infrastruktur wie Darknet-Marktplaetze abschalten und mutmassliche Verantwortliche festnehmen koennen." — explicit acknowledgment of cooperation with foreign-LE counterparts.
