## Source Summary

Local Nevada-region newspaper coverage of the February 7, 2018 Infraud Organization indictment by the District of Nevada grand jury. Lists the international defendant footprint (Ukraine, Russia, France, UK, Italy, Australia, Bangladesh, Egypt, Pakistan, Serbia and others) and confirms RICO conspiracy charges, the $530 million actual-loss figure, and 10,901 registered members.
