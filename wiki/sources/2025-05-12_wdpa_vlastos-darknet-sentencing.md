## Source Summary

On 12 May 2025, DOJ announced that George Sotiris Vlastos was sentenced to 70 months in prison for distributing LSD, MDMA, ketamine, and cocaine acquired from an international MDMA/Ecstasy darknet vendor, along with firearms offenses. The case is notable because the defendant was not the vendor himself but a domestic redistributor supplied through the darknet.
