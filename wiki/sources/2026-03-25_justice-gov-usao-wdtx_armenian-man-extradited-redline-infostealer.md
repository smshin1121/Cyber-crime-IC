# Source — USAO-WDTX press release on Hambardzum Minasyan extradition

This source page summarises the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Western District of Texas (USAO-WDTX, justice.gov own-domain) press release dated 2026-03-25 announcing the extradition of Armenian national Hambardzum Minasyan from the Netherlands to the United States to face federal charges for his alleged role in the RedLine infostealing malware scheme.

## Why this source matters

This is a tier-1 own-domain DOJ press release that adds a fresh 2026 chapter to the multi-year U.S.–Netherlands–Belgium–Eurojust RedLine disruption pipeline. It documents:

1. A successful trans-Atlantic extradition under U.S.–Netherlands MLA / extradition arrangements;
2. The U.S. multi-agency military/civilian investigative coalition for an infostealer-as-a-service case (FBI cyber task force + NCIS + IRS-CI + DCIS + Army CID);
3. Continued international coordination follow-through from Operation Magnus (Oct 2024) into individual-defendant prosecutions in 2026.
