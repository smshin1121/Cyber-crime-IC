## Source Summary

Official Singapore Police Force press release describing the 2026 edition of the SPF-led cross-border crackdown on online child sexual exploitation (OCSE), conducted between 23 March and 17 April 2026 with police services from Hong Kong, Japan, South Korea, Malaysia, Thailand, and Brunei. The release reports 326 arrests, 119 subjects under investigation, 382 locations raided, and itemised seizure of digital devices.

## Relevance to IC

This is a tier-1 primary source from Singapore Police Force documenting a seven-country Asian police-to-police cooperation against OCSE. It is the basis for a new operation page in the wiki ([[cross-border-online-cse-operation-spf-2026]]) and is the first SPF-led OCSE cross-border release in the wiki to include the Royal Brunei Police Force as a participating agency. The 2026 release sits alongside (but is operationally distinct from) the 2025 six-jurisdiction [[operation-cyber-guardian]].
