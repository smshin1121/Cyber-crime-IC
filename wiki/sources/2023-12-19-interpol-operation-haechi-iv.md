## Summary

Operation HAECHI IV, the fourth iteration of Korea-initiated HAECHI series, ran July to December 2023 across 34 countries. ~3,500 arrests, USD 300 million seized ($199M fiat, $101M virtual assets), 82,112 bank accounts blocked. Targeted voice phishing, romance scams, investment fraud, BEC, e-commerce fraud and more. 260% arrest increase over HAECHI III.

## Key Data

- **Operation:** Operation HAECHI IV
- **Timeframe:** July – December 2023
- **Countries:** 34
- **Arrests:** ~3,500
- **Assets seized:** USD 300 million (USD 199M fiat + USD 101M virtual)
- **Bank accounts blocked:** 82,112
- **Crime types:** Voice phishing, romance scams, online sextortion, investment fraud, BEC, e-commerce fraud, money laundering
- **Emerging threats:** AI-generated voice cloning, fraudulent NFTs

## Relevance

Fourth HAECHI iteration showing massive scale increase. Cross-validated against HAECHI V data: consistent series growth (34 countries/3,500 arrests → 40 countries/5,500+ arrests).
