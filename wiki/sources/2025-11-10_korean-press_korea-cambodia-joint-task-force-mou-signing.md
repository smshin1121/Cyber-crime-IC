## Source Summary

Joint MOFA + KNPA announcement (Korea.kr 정책브리핑 newsId 156727324; MOFA overseas.mofa.go.kr seq 376617, both dated 2025-11-10) of the signing in Phnom Penh of the 「대한민국 국민을 위한 한국-캄보디아 공동 전담반」 (Korea–Cambodia Joint Task Force for Korean nationals; commonly 코리아전담반) MOU. Acting KNPA Commissioner Yoo Jae-sung 유재성 and Cambodian Police Commissioner Sar Thet 써 텟 signed the MOU in the presence of Korean Foreign Minister Cho Hyun 조현 and Cambodian Interior Minister Sar Sokha 써 소카. The signing was the implementation phase of the Korea–Cambodia summit decisions reached in Malaysia on 2025-10-27. The Joint Task Force operates as a Korean-Cambodian shared-staff police unit responsible for "the entire process from report receipt to rescue, investigation, and suspect repatriation" for cases involving Korean nationals, with operational focus on Korean-national protection and online-scam transnational crime.
