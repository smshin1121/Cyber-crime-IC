## Source Summary

Official HKSAR Government Information Services Department wire publishing the Hong Kong Police Force (HKPF) closing announcement of **STEALTHNET**, the Counter Cyber and Physical Terrorism Joint Exercise 2026. The release names five foreign and intergovernmental LE / cybersecurity partners — INTERPOL, the Macao Judiciary Police, the Singapore Police Force, the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore and the Korean National Police Agency — and frames the exercise as a multilateral capacity-building drill on cross-border counter-terror response covering financial, aviation and healthcare critical infrastructures across Hong Kong, Macao, Singapore and Korea.
