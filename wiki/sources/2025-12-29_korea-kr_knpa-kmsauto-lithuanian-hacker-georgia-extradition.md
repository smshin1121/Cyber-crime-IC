## Source Summary

KNPA Korea.kr 정책브리핑 press release (newsId=156737083) dated 2025-12-29 (online embargo 2025-12-28 09:00) announcing the extradition into Korea of a Lithuanian national accused of operating a memory-hacking clipper malware (`KMSAuto`-bundled) campaign. Verbatim Korean text was extracted from the KNPA-issued PDF attachment fetched via `curl_cffi` chrome120 against `korea.kr/common/download.do?fileId=198312514&tblKey=GMN` (HTTP 200, 313,624 bytes).

The release identifies the cooperation chain as: KNPA → 법무부 → 검찰청 → Lithuanian Ministry of Justice + Lithuanian Police (joint search 2024-12) → INTERPOL Red Notice → Georgian Police (arrest April 2025) → Korean extradition request to Georgia → custody in Korea late 2025. The release names KNPA Cyber Investigation Senior Officer 박우현 as spokesperson and the responsible unit as 국가수사본부 수사국 사이버테러대응과.
