## Source Summary

DOJ press release announcing coordinated international disruption actions against the BlackSuit (Royal) ransomware group under Operation Checkmate. The operation involved 5 US agencies and international partners from 7 countries, seizing 4 servers and 9 domains used for data leaking and ransom negotiations. BlackSuit had compromised 450+ US victims since 2022.

## Relevance to IC

This source demonstrates US-led multi-agency coordination (DOJ, HSI, USSS, IRS-CI, FBI) combined with international partnership for ransomware infrastructure takedown. The involvement of Ukraine and Lithuania alongside traditional Five Eyes + EU partners shows expanding geographic cooperation in ransomware enforcement.
