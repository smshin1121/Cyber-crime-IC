## Source Summary

This Garda release provides early official context for the Jackal series in Ireland. It explains how Operation Skein targeted Black Axe-linked fraud and laundering networks and records searches, arrests and charging activity from the 2022 phase that fed into later Jackal operations.

## Relevance to IC

This source documents the Irish investigative lineage behind Operation Jackal and helps explain why later Jackal operations repeatedly highlight Ireland as a key Black Axe laundering jurisdiction.
