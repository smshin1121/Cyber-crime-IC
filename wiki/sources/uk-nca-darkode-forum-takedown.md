## Summary

The NCA press release announces the **UK component of Operation Dark HunTOR**, a coordinated international dark-web drug marketplace takedown announced on 26-27 October 2021. Key points:

1. **UK results**: 24 arrests, £220,000+ in cash/bitcoin seized, 50+ kg of drugs recovered
2. **Global results**: 150 arrests, €26.7M (~$31M) in cash/crypto seized, 234 kg drugs, 45 firearms
3. **Participating countries**: 8 nations — Australia, Bulgaria, France, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, Switzerland, US
4. **Coordination**: Europol + Eurojust
5. **Investigation origin**: Began after German authorities arrested the alleged operator of a dark web marketplace and accessed its infrastructure — likely a follow-on from the January 2021 DarkMarket takedown
6. **Quote**: NCA emphasized that "those who try to use the dark web to anonymously commit crimes can be identified and will be tracked down"
