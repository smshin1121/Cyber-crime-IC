## Source Summary

Europol newsroom press release announcing Operation Deep Sentinel — the 11-13 June 2025 takedown of Archetyp Market, the longest-running darknet drug marketplace, with German lead authorities (Frankfurt am Main ZIT + BKA) and 5-EU-state plus US cooperation. Administrator arrested in Barcelona; 1 moderator + 6 top vendors targeted in Germany/Sweden; EUR 7.8M assets seized; 600,000+ users / EUR 250M+ transactions / 17,000+ listings disrupted.

## IC Pattern

This release establishes a fully-articulated 6-jurisdiction darknet-marketplace takedown pattern with explicit Eurojust EIO + MLA coordination during the action days:

- **Germany**: lead jurisdiction (Frankfurt am Main ZIT + BKA).
- **Netherlands**: infrastructure-hosting jurisdiction (platform infrastructure in NL taken offline).
- **Spain**: arrest jurisdiction for the German-national administrator (Barcelona).
- **Sweden + Germany**: action jurisdictions for 1 moderator + 6 highest vendors.
- **Romania**: action partner (specifics not enumerated).
- **United States**: HSI + IRS-CI + USDOJ analytical and operational partners.
- **Europol**: coordination meetings + analytical support.
- **Eurojust**: MLA + European Investigation Order coordination during action days and preliminary investigation.
