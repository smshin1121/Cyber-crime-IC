## Source Summary

Brazil's Ministry of Justice and Public Security reports the 1 November 2019 launch of Operation 404. The source records the ministry's coordination role, the first-phase disruption figures, and the collaboration of Brazilian public bodies, the U.S. Embassy in Brazil through ICE, and the U.S. Department of Justice.

## Relevance to IC

This official source anchors Operation 404's 2019 start date and supports the international-cooperation classification by naming U.S. law-enforcement and diplomatic support to the Brazilian-led action.
