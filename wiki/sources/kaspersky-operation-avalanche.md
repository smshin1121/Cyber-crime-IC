## Summary

Kaspersky reported its participation in INTERPOL's original **Operation Synergia**, which ran from September to November 2023 across more than 50 INTERPOL member states. The operation focused on malicious infrastructure involved in phishing, malware, and ransomware attacks.

The release says INTERPOL coordinated cyber-intelligence exchanges that produced more than 60 Cyber Activity Reports, identified around 1,300 suspicious servers, enabled action on 70 percent of those servers, and produced arrests or suspect identifications across multiple states.
