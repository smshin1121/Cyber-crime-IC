## Summary

Joint press release of the German Federal Criminal Police Office (Bundeskriminalamt, BKA) and the Frankfurt am Main General Public Prosecutor's Office Central Office for Combating Internet Crime (ZIT), dated 8 May 2026, announcing the international takedown of the relaunched "Crimenetwork" darknet trading platform. The release names Spain (Policía Nacional), Moldova (Inspectoratul Național de Investigații – Centrul pentru Combaterea Crimelor Cibernetice), and Eurojust as the principal foreign cooperation partners and is the canonical primary source for the operation.
