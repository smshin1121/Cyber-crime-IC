## Source Summary

BleepingComputer reports on the UK NCA's late-2020 follow-on operation against UK customers of WeLeakInfo. The NCA arrested 21 customers and conducted 69 cease-and-desist visits, an enforcement cycle that built directly on the joint domain-seizure foundation laid in January 2020.
