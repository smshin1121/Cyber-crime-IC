## Source Summary

Eurojust reports Spanish action against illegal football streaming through the Mobdro mobile application. The source identifies the 43-million-download scale, Spain-led action, Eurojust support for EIO and MLA channels, Europol analytical support, and the Spain/Portugal/Czechia hosting footprint.

This is an official source for a named illegal streaming app takedown with cross-border judicial and police cooperation.
