## Source Summary

On 10 April 2025, DOJ announced that James Bookman was sentenced to 15 years and 8 months in prison for distributing fentanyl and methamphetamine through a dark web marketplace. The sentencing release states that one sale led to a fentanyl overdose death in Montana, making the case one of the more serious downstream-harm dark web drug prosecutions in the current dataset.
