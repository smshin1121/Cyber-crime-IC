## Source Summary

21 U.S.C. 841 is a core Controlled Substances Act offence provision covering manufacture, distribution, dispensing, and possession with intent to distribute controlled or counterfeit substances. It is relevant to darknet-market and online-pharmacy records where cyber infrastructure enables drug trafficking.
