## Source Summary

INTERPOL coordinated Operation Jackal III from 10 April to 3 July 2024 across 21 countries on five continents, targeting online financial fraud and the West African organized crime syndicates behind it, particularly Black Axe. The operation resulted in approximately 300 arrests, the identification of over 400 additional suspects, the blocking of more than 720 bank accounts, and the seizure of assets worth USD 3 million. Multiple criminal networks were dismantled worldwide. Black Axe and similar groups are considered responsible for the majority of the world's cyber-enabled financial fraud, with business email compromise (BEC) as a primary crime type. - **Operation name:** Operation Jackal III - **Date:** 10 April - 3 July 2024
