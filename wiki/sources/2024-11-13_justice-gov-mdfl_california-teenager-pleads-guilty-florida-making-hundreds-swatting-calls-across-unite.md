## Source Summary

USAO MDFL parallel press release announcing Alan W. Filion, 18, of Lancaster, California, pleaded guilty to four counts of interstate threats. From August 2022 to January 2024, Filion made more than 375 swatting and threat calls targeting religious institutions, schools, and individuals nationwide.
