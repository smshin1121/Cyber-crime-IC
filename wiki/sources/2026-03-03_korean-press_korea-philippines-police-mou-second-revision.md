## Source Summary

KNPA announcement (Korea.kr 정책브리핑 newsId 156747062, dated 2026-03-04) of the second revision of the Korea–Philippines police bilateral cooperation MOU. Acting KNPA Commissioner Yoo Jae-sung 유재성 and Philippine National Police Chief Jose Melencio Nartatez Jr. signed the revision in Manila on 2026-03-03 (local time). This is the second revision since the original 2007 MOU. The revised agreement strengthens cooperation against "drugs, online scams, and sophisticated transnational crimes" (마약·온라인 스캠 등 지능화된 초국가범죄) and expands the bilateral Korean Desk (originally established 2012 at the Philippine National Capital Region Police Office) by deploying one additional KNPA liaison officer in the first half of 2026.

The announcement also names three related multilateral mechanisms in which Korea-Philippines cooperation participates: IICA (International Cooperation Consultative Body, KNPA-led), ANCRA (Asia Narcotic Crime Response Cooperation), and ICON (International Drug Investigation Conference, scheduled for Seoul 2026). The Acting Commissioner additionally held discussions on extradition procedures for Korean fugitives with Philippine Immigration Commissioner Joel Antonio Viado and Philippine organised-crime response committee chair Benjamin Acorda Jr.
