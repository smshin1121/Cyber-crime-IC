## Summary

The Hacker News reports on **Operation HAECHI-III**, INTERPOL's third annual cyber-financial crime operation in the HAECHI series. Key points:

1. **Timeframe**: 28 June – 23 November 2022 (5 months)
2. **Scale**: ~975 arrests, 1,600+ cases closed, ~US$130 million in virtual assets seized
3. **Crime types**: Voice phishing, romance scams, sextortion, investment fraud, illegal online gambling money laundering
4. **Coordination**: INTERPOL IFCACC (Financial Crime and Anti-Corruption Centre) supporting 30 countries
5. **Technical innovation**: Launch of ARRP (Anti-Money Laundering Rapid Response Protocol) as a new global stop-payment mechanism
6. **Notable case**: India-based call center scam impersonating INTERPOL/Europol officers to defraud Austrian victims

## Why this source matters

For the IC analysis on the HAECHI series, this article is the primary Tier 3 secondary source for HAECHI-III's outcomes. Its analytical value includes:
