## Source Summary

Spanish National Police (Policía Nacional) own-domain press release dated **9 January 2026** announcing the dismantling in Spain of a Black Axe-linked cyber-fraud network. Total of 34 arrests across Sevilla (28), Madrid (3), Málaga (2), and Barcelona (1). Cooperation is explicitly with the Bavarian police of Germany (*"la policía alemana del Estado de Baviera"*) and Europol support. The investigation began in September 2023 and continues; provisional imprisonment ordered for four principal leaders. Investigating judge: Juzgado de Instrucción nº 18 de Sevilla.

This release is the **2nd tier-1 own-domain source** for [[black-axe-spain-takedown-2026]], complementing the Europol Newsroom release of 12 January 2026 ([[2026-01-12_europol_34-arrests-spain-black-axe-criminal-organisation]]). The Policía Nacional release pre-dates the Europol release by three days and provides the Spanish-LE-side primary attribution, the judicial-authority detail (Juzgado de Instrucción nº 18 de Sevilla), the operation start date (September 2023), the provisional-imprisonment status of four principal suspects, and the granular predicate-fraud typology (MITM / BEC against corporate email).

The two sources are mutually corroborative on every numerical claim (34 arrests with identical geographic breakdown; EUR 5,937,589.70 / EUR 5.93 million damage; EUR 119,352 frozen; EUR 66,403 cash seized) and on every named partner (Spain + Bavaria + Europol). No contradictions between the two primaries.
