## Source Summary

DOJ announced the dismantling of Darkode as part of Operation Shrouded Horizon, a 20-country coordinated law-enforcement action. The release documents 12 U.S. defendants, FBI infiltration of the forum, seizure of the forum infrastructure, and actions against about 70 Darkode members and associates through charges, arrests, searches or seizures.

## Relevance to IC

This is the main DOJ source for [[operation-shrouded-horizon]]. It supports the operation-level target, 20-country scope, U.S. defendant structure, FBI infiltration finding, and the distinction between arrests and the broader set of charges/searches/seizures.
