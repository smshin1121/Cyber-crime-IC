## Source Summary

Official report by the Bulgarian News Agency (BTA, Bulgaria's national state news agency) on the 2026-05-12 cross-border action against an online fake-medicines and supplements network. The article centres on the Bulgarian component, identifies the leading Bulgarian prosecution office and the named Bulgarian LE units (including the GDCOC Cybercrime Directorate), and confirms the joint investigation team with Romania, Poland, Hungary and Moldova as well as the Eurojust/Europol coordination roles.
