## Source Summary

Europol newsroom press release (dated 2025-10-17) describing a Latvian-led action day in October 2025 dismantling a cybercrime-as-a-service (CaaS) SIM-box network that offered telephone numbers from over 80 countries for use in smishing, phishing, fraud, extortion, migrant smuggling, child sexual abuse material distribution, daughter-son scams, investment fraud, fake shops, fake bank websites, and fake-police-officer schemes. Action-day results: 26 searches, 5 arrests, ~1,200 SIM-box devices seized (operating 40,000 SIM cards), hundreds of thousands of further SIM cards seized, 5 servers seized, 2 websites taken down (gogetsms.com, apisim.com), EUR 431,000 + USD 333,000 in assets frozen, 4 luxury vehicles seized. More than **49 million online accounts** were created via the illegal service. Joint Investigation Team partners Austria, Estonia, Latvia, plus Finland support. Europol + Eurojust coordination; Shadowserver Foundation private-sector partner.
