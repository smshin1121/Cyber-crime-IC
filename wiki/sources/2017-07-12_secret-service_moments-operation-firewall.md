## Source Summary

The Secret Service retrospective revisits Operation Firewall as an early cyber-financial investigation that arrested more than 28 people, targeted suspects across eight U.S. states and six foreign countries, and involved stolen payment-card trafficking at large scale.

## Relevance to IC

This source provides official historical confirmation for [[operation-firewall|Operation Firewall]] and supports its classification as an early international cyber-financial crime operation.
