## Summary

Authoritative tier-1 primary source: 2024-08-08 USAO-MDTN press release announcing the indictment and arrest of **Matthew Isaac Knoot** (Nashville, Tennessee) for hosting a "laptop farm" enabling DPRK remote IT workers (operating from China) to fraudulently obtain employment with U.S. and U.K. companies under a stolen U.S. identity ("Andrew M.").

The press release establishes a public-record legal anchor for the DOJ-wide **DPRK RevGen: Domestic Enabler Initiative** (launched March 2024 by NSD + FBI Cyber + FBI Counterintelligence), and references the **October 2023 joint U.S.-Republic of Korea guidance** and the **May 2022 tri-seal advisory** (FBI + Treasury + State) as the multi-agency cooperation backbone against DPRK IT-worker fraud.
