## Source Summary

USAO-OR press release dated 2025-07-16 announcing the 2025-06-18 extradition of Karen Serobovich Vardanyan from Ukraine to the US for his role in Ryuk ransomware attacks (March 2019 – September 2020), with co-defendants in France (Avetisyan, extradition pending) and at large (Lyulyava and Prykhodchenko, both Ukrainian nationals). The Ryuk gang received ~1,610 bitcoins valued at USD 15M+ in ransom payments.

## IC Pattern

This release establishes a US-Ukraine-France three-country cooperation pattern for the Ryuk ransomware prosecution thread:

- **United States**: lead jurisdiction (FBI investigation; USAO-Oregon prosecution; DOJ OIA international-cooperation channel).
- **Ukraine**: extradition source for Vardanyan (June 2025); explicit credit to Ukrainian authorities in the press release.
- **France**: custody jurisdiction for Avetisyan; pending US extradition request.
- **Armenia**: defendant nationality (Vardanyan, Avetisyan) but not a cooperation participant.
