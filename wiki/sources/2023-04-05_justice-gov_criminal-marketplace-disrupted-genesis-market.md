## Source Summary

The DOJ Office of Public Affairs announced the coordinated international disruption of Genesis Market. The release identifies the operation as Operation Cookie Monster, reports the seizure of 11 domains under an Eastern District of Wisconsin warrant, and records assistance from many foreign law enforcement and judicial partners.

## Relevance to IC

This is the anchor U.S. government source for [[operation-cookie-monster|Operation Cookie Monster]]. It establishes the U.S. legal seizure action, the FBI/DOJ lead role, the global partner list, the use of mutual legal assistance from Bulgaria and Latvia, and the scale of Genesis Market's stolen-credential inventory.
