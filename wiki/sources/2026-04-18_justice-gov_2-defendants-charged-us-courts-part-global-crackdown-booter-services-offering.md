## Source Summary

DOJ CDCA announced the December 2024 PowerOFF wave, including 27 DDoS-for-hire domain seizures and criminal charges against two defendants. The source names assistance from Germany's BKA, the UK NCA, Netherlands Police, Poland's Central Cybercrime Bureau, Brazil's Federal Police, Europol, and Canadian police, and describes a coordinated prevention campaign involving HSI, the UK NCA, and Netherlands Police.
