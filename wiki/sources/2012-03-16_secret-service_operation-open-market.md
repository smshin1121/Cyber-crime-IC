## Source Summary

The U.S. Secret Service announced Operation Open Market as a cybercrime investigation into a transnational organized-crime group using online forums to trade stolen personal and financial data, counterfeit identification documents, and access devices. The release records the initial 19 arrests and three indictments unsealed in March 2012.
