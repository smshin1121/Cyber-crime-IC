## Source Summary

The Department of Justice announced the Shadowcrew indictment on 28 October 2004. The release details the 19-defendant indictment, describes the carding marketplace model, and confirms that foreign arrests were coordinated with domestic enforcement.

## Relevance to IC

This source corroborates the prosecutorial core of [[operation-firewall|Operation Firewall]], including the indictment count, Shadowcrew's size, the carding and identity-theft conduct, and the cross-border arrest coordination.
