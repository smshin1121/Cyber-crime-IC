## Source Summary

Infosecurity Magazine reports on Andrew Shenkosky's January 2025 indictment in the District of Minnesota for wire fraud, aggravated identity theft, and possession of unauthorized access devices. Shenkosky purchased 2,468 stolen credentials from Genesis Market between February-November 2020.
