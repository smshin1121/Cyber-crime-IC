## Source Summary

On 17 August 2023, DOJ announced that Justin Udvardi was sentenced to 42 months in prison for distributing fentanyl-laced pills and crystal meth on the darknet using the moniker "TrapGod." DOJ said Udvardi also trafficked in stolen credit card information and materials useful for fraudulent identification.
