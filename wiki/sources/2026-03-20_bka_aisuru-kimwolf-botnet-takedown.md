## Summary

Press release jointly issued by the German Federal Criminal Police Office (Bundeskriminalamt, BKA) and the Central and Contact Point Cybercrime North Rhine-Westphalia (Zentral- und Ansprechstelle Cybercrime Nordrhein-Westfalen, ZAC NRW), dated 20 March 2026, announcing a Germany-Canada-United States internationally coordinated 19 March 2026 action that disrupted the technical infrastructure of the Aisuru and Kimwolf IoT botnets and identified two administrators with searches in Germany and Canada. This is the canonical tier-1 primary source for the German leg of the operation; the corroborating US Department of Justice / FBI announcement covers the same operational date and adds the JackSkid and Mossad botnets to the disrupted set.
