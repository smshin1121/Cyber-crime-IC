## Source Summary

BleepingComputer covers the EDTX indictment of Russian national Igor Dekhtyarchuk for operating "Marketplace A," a Russian-language carding shop that sold compromised credentials. Reports the FBI Cyber Most Wanted listing, the marketplace metrics (48,000+ compromised email accounts, 5,000 daily visitors), and the FBI undercover purchase methodology that generated the indictment.
