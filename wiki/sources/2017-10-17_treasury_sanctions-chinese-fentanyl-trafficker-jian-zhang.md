## Source Summary

Treasury Department announces OFAC Kingpin Act sanctions against Jian Zhang and his organization, coordinated with the DOJ indictment of the same day. Demonstrates a whole-of-government approach to a Chinese fentanyl trafficker reaching U.S. victims.

## Relevance to IC

This source documents a parallel federal sanctions action that supplements the criminal indictment in [[operation-us-v-jian-zhang]] and provides an additional mechanism (financial sanctions) of international response.
