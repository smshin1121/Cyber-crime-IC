# Operation Cyber Guardian (사이버 가디언 작전) — Asia 6-Country CSAM Crackdown Six Asian countries/regions — South Korea, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, and Hong Kong — conducted a coordinated 5-week operation dubbed "Operation Cyber Guardian" (사이버 가디언 작전) targeting the production, distribution, and possession of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) through information communication networks. The operation ran from February 24 to March 28, 2025, and was initiated by the Singapore Police Force. - **Operation name:** Cyber Guardian (사이버 가디언)
