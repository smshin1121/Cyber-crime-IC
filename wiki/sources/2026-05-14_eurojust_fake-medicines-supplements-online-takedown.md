## Source Summary

Official Eurojust press release describing the 2026-05-12 cross-border action against a long-running online criminal group that sold fake medicines and supplements through hundreds of fraudulent websites and social-media pages to seriously ill EU citizens. The release identifies five lead cooperating jurisdictions (Romania, Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland and Moldova) and ten supporting jurisdictions.
