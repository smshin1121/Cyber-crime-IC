## Source Summary

Europol newsroom press release dated 2024-12-03 announcing the 2024-12-03 takedown of MATRIX — an invitation-only encrypted messaging service made by criminals for criminals, technically more complex than Sky ECC and EncroChat. Joint Investigation Team between Netherlands and France set up at Eurojust; Europol Operational Task Force (June 2024 onwards) with 5 countries (NL/FR/LT/IT/ES); Germany supported with technical expertise. Action day produced 3 arrests (1 FR, 2 ES under EAW from NL), 13 house searches (1 FR + 6 ES + 6 LT), and main-server takedowns in France and Germany. 3-month live interception captured 2.3M+ messages in 33 languages.

## IC Pattern

This release establishes an encrypted-comms-platform takedown pattern that complements the wiki's prior records of Sky ECC, EncroChat, Exclu, and Ghost takedowns. Notable IC mechanism classes:

- **Netherlands-France JIT at Eurojust** as the founding bilateral investigative instrument.
- **Europol Operational Task Force (OTF)** established June 2024 with 5 countries — first wiki record of an "Operational Task Force" as a discrete IC mechanism class distinct from JIT, J-CAT, and EAR.
- **3-month live interception phase** as the operational core, structurally similar to the EncroChat and Sky ECC live-monitoring phases.
- **Splash-page warning post-takedown** deployed to alert MATRIX users that authorities had intercepted the platform — a discrete deception/awareness-raising mechanism class.
- **European Arrest Warrant cross-border arrest pattern** (Netherlands → Spain for 2 of 3 action-day arrests).
- **Central server takedowns in France + Germany** — the multi-country server-physical-location dimension of an encrypted-comms platform, requiring action-day coordination across multiple jurisdictions.
