## Source Summary

DOJ District of Connecticut press release announcing Adeyemi Odufuye's January 3, 2018 guilty plea to wire-fraud conspiracy and aggravated identity theft for orchestrating a BEC scheme targeting U.S. CFOs/controllers. The release confirms his Sheffield Hallam University arrest in the UK on December 19, 2016 and subsequent extradition, plus FBI evidence of >$1.6M in fraudulent wires.
