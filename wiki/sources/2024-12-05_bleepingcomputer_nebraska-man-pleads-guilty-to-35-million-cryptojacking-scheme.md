## Source Summary

BleepingComputer reports that Charles O. Parks III pleaded guilty in EDNY (Brooklyn) to wire fraud for cryptojacking that defrauded two major cloud providers of $3.5M in computing resources. Parks used shell entities (MultiMillionaire LLC, CP3O LLC) to register accounts and mine Ether, Litecoin, and Monero worth nearly $1 million.
