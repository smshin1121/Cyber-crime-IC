# Source Summary — Operation Chargeback (Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Koblenz, 5 Nov 2025)

## Publisher and provenance

The Generalstaatsanwaltschaft Koblenz is the regional state-prosecutor authority for the German state of Rhineland-Palatinate. Its **Landeszentralstelle Cybercrime (LZC)** is the prosecutorial centre of competence for transnational cybercrime cases originating in or affecting Rhineland-Palatinate (and supports the BKA on federal cases). The Koblenz press release at `gstko.justiz.rlp.de/presse-aktuelles/...` is the own-domain prosecutor release that names the suspects' procedural posture, the German action-day footprint and the cooperating foreign jurisdictions verbatim.

Tier-1 mirrors (same facts, different framings) published on 4–5 November 2025:
