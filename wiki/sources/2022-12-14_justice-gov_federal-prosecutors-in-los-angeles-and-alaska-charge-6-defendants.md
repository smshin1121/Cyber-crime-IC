## Source Summary

DOJ CDCA announced the December 2022 PowerOFF wave, including the court-authorized seizure of 48 DDoS-for-hire domains and charges against six defendants. The release frames the action as part of international efforts against booter services and identifies parallel deterrence advertising by the FBI, UK NCA, and Netherlands Police.
