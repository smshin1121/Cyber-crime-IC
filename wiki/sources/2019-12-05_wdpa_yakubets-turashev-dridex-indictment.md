## Source Summary

On 5 December 2019, the United States and United Kingdom jointly announced criminal charges against Maksim V. Yakubets (aka "aqua"), 32, of Moscow, Russia, related to two separate international computer hacking and bank fraud schemes spanning from May 2009 to the present. ### Charges A federal grand jury in Pittsburgh returned a 10-count indictment charging Yakubets and Igor Turashev with conspiracy, computer hacking, wire fraud, and bank fraud in connection with the distribution of "Bugat" (later known as "Cridex" and "Dridex"), a multifunction malware package designed to automate the theft of banking credentials.
