## Source Summary

DOJ Office of Public Affairs press release of **2024-12-12** unsealing a **14-defendant indictment** (returned 2024-12-11) in the **U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Missouri, Eastern Division (St. Louis)**, charging fourteen Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) nationals with a multi-year fraudulent IT-worker conspiracy that generated **at least USD 88 million** in illicit revenue for the DPRK regime over approximately six years (April 2017 – March 2023). The 14 defendants operated under two DPRK-controlled front companies — **Yanbian Silverstar Network Technology Co., Ltd.** (Yanji, Jilin Province, PRC) and **Volasys Silver Star** (Vladivostok, Russian Federation) — using stolen U.S. identities, AI-generated credentials, and front companies to obtain remote IT employment with U.S. and other businesses worldwide. Some defendants additionally extorted their U.S. employers after termination. Concurrent actions: **USD 2.26 million** in bank-account seizures plus **USD 764,800** in supplemental seizures, **29 internet domains** seized across October 2023 and May 2024 actions, and a U.S. State Department **Rewards for Justice** reward of up to **USD 5 million** administered by the **Diplomatic Security Service**.

## IC Pattern

- **DPRK-controlled front companies in third-country jurisdictions** — Yanbian Silverstar (PRC) and Volasys Silver Star (Russia) — illustrate that the IT-worker scheme is hosted outside DPRK on the territory of two non-cooperating-on-cybercrime jurisdictions, raising the practical question of evidence-gathering and arrest unavailability.
- **AAG NSD + USAO E.D. Mo. + FBI St. Louis joint architecture** — predates the formal "DPRK RevGen: Domestic Enabler Initiative" announced as a discrete framework class in 2025-06-30 (see [[us-doj-coordinated-nationwide-actions-dprk-it-workers-2025]]) and provides a precursor enforcement template.
- **State Department Rewards for Justice / Diplomatic Security Service** — concurrent USD 5M reward demonstrates the DOJ + State multi-agency US-side architecture for DPRK economic-sanctions enforcement when defendants are physically out of reach.
- **Multi-wave domain seizure** (October 2023 + May 2024) — demonstrates an iterative infrastructure-disruption pattern preceding the indictment unsealing.
- **Extortion add-on** — post-termination data-leak extortion is an explicit charged element, distinguishing this scheme from a pure economic-sanctions / wage-fraud pattern.
