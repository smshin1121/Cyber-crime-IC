# Source — IRS-CI press release on Hambardzum Minasyan RedLine extradition

This source page summarises the Internal Revenue Service — Criminal Investigation (IRS-CI, irs.gov own-domain) press release dated 2026-03-25 announcing the extradition of Armenian national Hambardzum Minasyan from the Netherlands to the United States to face federal charges in W.D. Tex. for his alleged role in the RedLine infostealing malware scheme.

## Why this source matters

This is the **second tier-1 own-domain U.S. federal-LE source** for the Minasyan / RedLine matter, complementing the USAO-WDTX justice.gov release ([[2026-03-25_justice-gov-usao-wdtx_armenian-man-extradited-redline-infostealer]]). Critically:

1. **IRS-CI is itself a named member of the FBI Austin Cyber Task Force** that built this case — so the IRS-CI release is a participating-agency own-domain disclosure, not a redistributive wire.
2. **It independently confirms the international-cooperation paragraph** naming the Netherlands, Belgium, and Eurojust as October 2024 Operation Magnus partners, and the Eurojust-embedded ICHIP attorney advisor as the assistance conduit for the 2026 extradition.
3. **It is on an irs.gov path** (`/compliance/criminal-investigation/...`) — tier-1 own-domain under L25 strict.
