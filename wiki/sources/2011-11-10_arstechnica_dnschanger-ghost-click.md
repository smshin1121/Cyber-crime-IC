Ars Technica reported the DNSChanger/Operation Ghost Click indictment and explained how the malware redirected DNS queries to criminal-controlled servers, enabling fraudulent advertising revenue. The article corroborates the 4 million-plus infection scale and USD 14 million-plus revenue figure.
