## Source Summary

Europol Newsroom press release dated **12 January 2026** announcing the arrest of 34 alleged Black Axe members in Spain (28 Seville, 3 Madrid, 2 Málaga, 1 Barcelona), conducted by the Spanish National Police in coordination with the Bavarian State Criminal Police Office (Germany) and supported by Europol. Documented damages exceed EUR 5.93 million; EUR 119,352 frozen in bank accounts and EUR 66,403 seized in cash. Europol contributions included information analysis, a Madrid-held data sprint, and on-the-spot support; two German BLKA officers were deployed on-site during the action day.

The release establishes the **Bavaria-Andalusia bilateral track** as a recurring Europol-supported cooperation pattern against Black Axe and complements the wiki's existing Black Axe coverage ([[black-axe-bec-2021]] EU-South Africa-US 2021, [[operation-jackal]] / [[operation-jackal-iii]] INTERPOL, [[black-axe-switzerland-takedown-2026]] CH-IT-DE-AT-NL April 2026). The new Spain-Germany action precedes the Switzerland action by roughly three months.
