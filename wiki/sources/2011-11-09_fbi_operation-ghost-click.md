## Source Summary

The FBI announced Operation Ghost Click as the dismantling of an international cyber ring that infected millions of computers with DNSChanger malware and manipulated Internet advertising. The release records the arrest of six Estonian nationals and credits the Estonian Police and Border Guard Board, NASA OIG, and the Dutch National Police National High Tech Crime Unit.
