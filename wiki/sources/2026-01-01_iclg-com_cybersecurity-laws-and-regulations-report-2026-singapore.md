## Source Summary

Singapore is *highly likely* the leading cybercrime cooperation hub in Southeast Asia, with advanced forensics, 24/7 operational availability, and a prominent role in ASEAN cybercrime coordination. Its primary cybercrime statute is the **Computer Misuse Act 1993**, updated multiple times — notably by the 2017 amendments that expanded extraterritorial reach and criminalised dealing in stolen personal data.
