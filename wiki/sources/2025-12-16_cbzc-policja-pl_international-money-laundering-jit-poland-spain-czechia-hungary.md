## Source Summary

Polish-language official CBZC press release (16 December 2025) announcing the dismantlement of an international money-laundering network operated alongside cyber-enabled investment/online-fraud monetization. The investigation runs as a Eurojust-hosted Joint Investigation Team between CBZC's Kraków Branch and law enforcement from Spain, the Czech Republic, and Hungary, with action days carried out in Poland, the Czech Republic, and Hungary.

## Relevance to IC

This is the controlling source for:

- The four-country JIT roster (Poland, Spain, Czech Republic, Hungary) operating "under Eurojust auspices."
- Five additional source-named cash-out countries (Greece, Italy, Spain, Belgium, the Netherlands) for fund withdrawals.
- Suspect nationalities (Romanian, Polish, Czech, Hungarian).
- Numerical results: 12 charged, 10 in pretrial detention, ~EUR 2.23M + PLN 717,000 laundered, >40 forged IDs seized, 1 BMW X5 seized.
- Action-day chronology (initial Romanian arrests, Czech and Hungarian arrests with direct CBZC Kraków participation, 2025-12-05 Śląskie arrests, 2025-12-07 detention orders).
- The investigation's "developmental" status — further action days planned domestically and abroad.
