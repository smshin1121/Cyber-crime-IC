## Source Summary

CyberScoop's March 31, 2021 article by Tim Starks reports the guilty plea of Tal Prihar to conspiracy to commit money laundering for operating DeepDotWeb. The article identifies the French arrest at Charles de Gaulle, the Israeli arrest of co-defendant Michael Phan, and the cryptocurrency money-laundering mechanics underlying the kickback scheme.
