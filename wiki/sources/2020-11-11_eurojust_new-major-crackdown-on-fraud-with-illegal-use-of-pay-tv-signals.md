## Source Summary

Eurojust reports the November 2020 continuation of the Italian pay-TV and film-streaming piracy investigation, including more than 5,550 servers taken down and cross-border execution of European Investigation Orders. The source confirms the same investigative line continued after the September 2019 action.

## Relevance to IC

This source expands Operation Perfect Storm beyond the initial 2019 action day and supports the operation's multinational infrastructure and legal-coordination edges.
