## Summary

Official Hong Kong SAR Government Information Services press release (P2025060300475) of 2025-06-03 reporting the results of the **first joint operation conducted under the Cross-border Anti-Scam Collaboration Platform "FRONTIER+"**, executed 2025-04-28 to 2025-05-28. The release names six partner jurisdictions (Macao SAR, Malaysia, the Maldives, Singapore, South Korea, Thailand) plus Hong Kong, and provides aggregate enforcement totals along with a per-jurisdiction Annex.

This is a **tier-1 primary source**: official Hong Kong government press dissemination service issuing on behalf of the Hong Kong Police Force. The accompanying PDF Annex (also on info.gov.hk infrastructure) provides per-jurisdiction breakdowns of subjects interviewed, persons arrested, scam cases detected, financial loss, accounts frozen, and amounts recovered.
