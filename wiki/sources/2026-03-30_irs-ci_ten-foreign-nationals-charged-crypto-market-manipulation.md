## Source Summary

IRS-CI press release dated 2026-03-30 announcing the simultaneous initial appearance in federal court at Oakland of three defendants extradited from Singapore — Gleb Gora (Vortex CEO), Manu Singh (Contrarian CEO), and Vasu Sharma (Contrarian BDA) — and consolidating prior indictments and pleas across three indictments (Gotbit, Vortex, Contrarian/Antier) into a unified "Operation Token Mirrors" public-announcement event. The operation is a joint FBI + IRS-CI undercover investigation in which the FBI itself created cryptocurrency tokens to bait wash-trading market manipulation by illicit market makers operating across Taiwan, Russia, Serbia, India, and other jurisdictions, with extradition cooperation by Singapore.

## IC Pattern

This release establishes a previously-undocumented Singapore-US extradition cooperation pattern in the wiki for cryptocurrency-fraud cases — Singapore is recorded for the first time as an extradition source in a US-led cryptocurrency-fraud prosecution. The FBI Law Enforcement Attaché Singapore + DOJ OIA + Singapore Police Force + Singapore Attorney General's Chambers cooperation chain is the operational mechanism. The FBI undercover-token creation methodology (FBI agents creating cryptocurrency tokens to bait wash trading) is also a novel mechanism class for the corpus.
