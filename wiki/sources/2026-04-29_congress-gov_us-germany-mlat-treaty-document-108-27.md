Senate Treaty Document 108-27 transmits the U.S.-Germany Mutual Legal Assistance Treaty, signed on 14 October 2003. The document frames the treaty as a modern criminal-assistance channel for investigations and proceedings, including document production, testimony, evidence, and related assistance.
