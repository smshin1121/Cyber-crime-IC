## Source Summary

The Guardian reported that the FBI and Estonian police disabled the Ghost Click botnet, which affected more than 4 million PCs. It summarized the seven-defendant charging posture and the DNSChanger redirection scheme.

This source independently corroborates the U.S.-Estonia cooperation angle and the public enforcement scale of [[operation-ghost-click|Operation Ghost Click]].
