## Source Summary

On 30 July 2021, DOJ announced that Chukwuemeka Okparaeke, known as `Fentmaster`, was sentenced to 15 years in prison for importing and trafficking fentanyl analogues and synthetic opioids through a darknet marketplace. DOJ said Okparaeke admitted that one sale caused the overdose death of an 18-year-old victim and that he imported drugs from Hong Kong.
