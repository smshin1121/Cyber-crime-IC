## Source Summary

Eurojust described a 2025 action day that produced arrests, searches and asset-freezing measures across Spain, Portugal, Italy, Romania and Bulgaria in a long-running cryptocurrency investment-fraud investigation.

## Relevance to IC

This is a strong primary source for EU judicial cooperation because it explicitly records the JIT, the use of European Arrest Warrants and European Investigation Orders, and the cross-border freezing measures used to dismantle the network.
