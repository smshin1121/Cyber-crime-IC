## Source Summary

This is the official Royal Canadian Mounted Police press release from RCMP Central Region (Ontario) dated 2025-09-10, summarizing Canada's role in the international investigation, arrest, extradition and U.S. prosecution of Cameron Albert Redman, a Canadian national who exploited unauthorized access to Twitter's Partner Support Portal in May 2022 to facilitate the theft of NFTs and cryptocurrency from over 200 mostly U.S.-based victims, profiting approximately USD 794,000.

The release pairs with the U.S. Department of Justice press release issued by the U.S. Attorney's Office for the Eastern District of Virginia on 2025-07-30 (already ingested elsewhere in the wiki). Together they document a three-jurisdiction cooperation chain (Canada → Portugal → United States) and the formal use of the DOJ Office of International Affairs to secure the March 2025 extradition.
