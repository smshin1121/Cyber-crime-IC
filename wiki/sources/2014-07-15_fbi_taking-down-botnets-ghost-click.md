## Source Summary

The FBI Cyber Division testimony before the Senate Judiciary Committee discussed botnet disruption strategy and listed Operation Ghost Click as a recent success. It confirms that the operation dismantled an international cyber ring and records later extradition of three Estonian nationals to the United States.

## Relevance to IC

This source strengthens the extradition and long-tail prosecution dimension of [[operation-ghost-click|Operation Ghost Click]], showing that the operation continued beyond the 2011 disruption announcement.
