## Source Summary

DOJ OPA announced Operation DisrupTor on 22 September 2020 as a JCODE and Europol-coordinated international effort against darknet opioid trafficking. The release reported 179 arrests, over USD 6.5 million in cash and virtual currencies, approximately 500 kilograms of drugs worldwide, 63 firearms, and named U.S., European, Canadian, Australian, and other partner authorities. It tied the operation to intelligence developed after the 2019 Wall Street Market takedown.
