## Source Summary

DOJ-OPA Press Release **26-251** dated **19 March 2026** announcing **court-authorized seizure of 4 MOIS-affiliated domains** (Justicehomeland.org, Handala-Hack.to, Karmabelow80.org, Handala-Redwanted.to) used by **Iran's Ministry of Intelligence and Security (MOIS)** for cyber-enabled psychological operations. The Handala Hack persona was used to claim credit for a **March 2026 destructive malware attack against a US-based multinational medical technologies firm**, leak PII of ~190 IDF/Israeli government personnel, leak 851 GB of data from the Sanzer Hasidic Jewish community, and send death threats with **USD 250,000 bounties** invoking **Jalisco New Generation Cartel (CJNG)** "partners" in America and Canada.

The release establishes:

1. **State-sponsored cyber-enabled transnational repression** as a discrete IC-relevant pattern: Iranian MOIS used domain infrastructure for psychological operations against US persons, Israeli persons, and Iranian dissidents.
2. **Cyber-physical-violence convergence**: hack-and-leak operations were paired with **CJNG cartel-as-proxy** death threats, demonstrating how state-sponsored cyber operations can be operationalised into kinetic-violence threats via criminal organisations.
3. **DOJ National Security Cyber Section + FBI Baltimore Field Office cooperation track** for state-sponsored cyber operations targeting US critical infrastructure (medical technologies firm = critical infrastructure under DHS framework).
4. **Albania-Iran nexus** documented from the 2022 Justicehomeland.org claims (MOIS-attributed thefts from Albanian government in retaliation for Albanian support of MEK Iranian dissident group).

The cited release is heavy on US national-security framing rather than multi-country LE cooperation; the foreign cooperation surface is implicit (Albanian government as 2022 victim; Israeli persons as 2026 victims).
