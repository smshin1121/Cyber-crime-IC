## Source Summary

Europol newsroom press release (2026-04-28) describing a Swiss-led, German-supported, Europol-coordinated action day against the Nigerian-origin Black Axe criminal organisation. Action day produced 10 arrests including the "Regional Head" for Southern Europe. Predicate offences are romance scams, other cyber-enabled fraud, and money laundering, causing millions of Swiss francs in damages.

The release explicitly enumerates the participating countries (Germany + Switzerland) under "Participating countries", which is the tier-1 IC-scope basis for the operation page. The Italian @ON Network / DIA contribution is operational support and is documented in mechanisms but not in `participating_countries`.
