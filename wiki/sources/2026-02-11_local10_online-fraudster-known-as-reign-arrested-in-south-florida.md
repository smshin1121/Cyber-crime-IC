## Source Summary

WPLG Miami's Local10 reports the February 11, 2026 arrest of Michael Ramsden (handle "Reign"), a Canadian member of the Noir's Luxury Refunds conspiracy, in South Florida. The piece reaffirms that the scheme leader Kareem Mustafa Ahmad operates from Alexandria, Egypt, and the operation defrauded online retailers from July 2020 to July 2022.

## Relevance to IC

Strong international-cooperation evidence: a Canadian national arrested in U.S. federal jurisdiction for charges connected to an Egypt-led fraud ring. This is exactly the cross-border element that distinguishes the case from a domestic-only action and supports moving the operation away from "absorbed/follow-on" status in future review.
