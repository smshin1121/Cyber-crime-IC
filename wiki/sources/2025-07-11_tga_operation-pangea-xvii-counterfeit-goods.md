## Source Summary

The TGA release provides national official confirmation of Australia's role in [[operation-pangea-xvii|Operation Pangea XVII]]. It documents import screening, large-scale seizures, and international intelligence sharing.

## Relevance to IC

The source supports the cross-border supply-chain dimension and the online-consumer risk dimension. TGA explicitly warns that buying medicines online from overseas can result in counterfeit or substandard products and states that TGA works with international partners to share intelligence and resources.
