## Source Summary

NPR's national-news coverage of the WeLeakInfo.com domain seizure on January 15-16, 2020. The article describes the site's billion-record stolen data search-engine business model and identifies the international partners alongside the FBI: UK National Crime Agency, Netherlands National Police Corps, Germany's Bundeskriminalamt, and Police Service of Northern Ireland. It also reports the same-day arrest of two 22-year-old suspects in Northern Ireland and the Netherlands.
