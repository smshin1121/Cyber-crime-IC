## Summary

The FBI Seattle Field Office story describes how a multi-year investigation led to the arrest and indictment of three senior members of the prolific **FIN7** cybercrime group (also tracked as Carbanak/Navigator Group). Key facts as reported by FBI Seattle and the parallel DOJ unsealing:

1. **Three Ukrainian nationals charged**: Dmytro Fedorov (arrested Poland, Jan 2018), Fedir Hladyr (arrested Dresden/Germany, Jan 2018), Andrii Kopakov (arrested Spain, Jun 2018); all three subsequently extradited to the US
2. **100+ US companies** breached since at least 2015
3. **>15 million** customer payment-card records stolen
4. **>6,500 POS terminals** compromised at **>3,600 business locations** across **47 states + DC**
5. **Named victims** at time of publication: Chipotle, Chili's, Arby's, Jason's Deli
6. **Methodology**: Spear-phishing with malicious Word attachments, often reinforced by follow-up phone calls to targeted employees (social-engineering layered on technical exploit)
