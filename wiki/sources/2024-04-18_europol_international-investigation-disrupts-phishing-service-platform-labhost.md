## Source Summary

Europol newsroom press release dated 2024-04-18 announcing the disruption of the LabHost phishing-as-a-service platform during 14-17 April 2024 international action days. The release establishes a 19-country joint cooperation pattern led by the UK Met Police and supported by Europol EC3 + J-CAT, with action-phase Europol specialist deployment to the Dutch National Police. Names every participating jurisdiction and its lead authority.

## IC Pattern

This release establishes the most fully-articulated 19-country action-day enumeration in the wiki for a phishing-as-a-service takedown — covering EU member states, EFTA partners, Anglosphere Five Eyes, and the Pacific. Notable features:

- **UK-led + Europol EC3 + J-CAT-hosted** investigation with action-phase specialist deployment to the Netherlands.
- **Five Eyes participation outside the EU** (US, UK, Canada, Australia, NZ) co-acting with EU member states under Europol J-CAT umbrella.
- **PhaaS commoditisation** of phishing — $249/month subscription, 170+ fake-website templates, LabRat 2FA-capture campaign-management tool.
- **September 2023 → April 2024** investigation timeline (~8 months).
