## Source Summary

The HSA release provides national official confirmation of Singapore's digital and border enforcement under [[operation-pangea-xviii|Operation Pangea XVIII]]. It confirms the operation's INTERPOL-led 90-country scope, local e-commerce and social-media listing removals, seller warnings, and checkpoint seizures.

## Relevance to IC

This source adds participating-country evidence for the same global operation and confirms the digital-marketplace enforcement component.
