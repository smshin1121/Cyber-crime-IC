## Source Summary

The MHRA release provides national official confirmation of the UK component of [[operation-pangea-xvii|Operation Pangea XVII]]. It confirms the INTERPOL coordination model, approximate 90-country scope, UK seizures, online takedowns, arrest operations, and proceeds-of-crime activity.

## Relevance to IC

The source supports the cyber-enabled dimension by explicitly describing illegal online sale and distribution of medicines, website and social-media account removals, and exposure to fraud and identity-theft risks when consumers buy from illegal online sellers.
