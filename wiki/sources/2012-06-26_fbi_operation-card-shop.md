## Source Summary

The FBI announced Operation Card Shop as the largest coordinated international law-enforcement action directed at carding crimes at the time. The release describes the Carder Profit undercover forum, 24 initial arrests, foreign arrests across seven countries, more than 30 searches, more than 30 interviews, and large-scale victim notification.
