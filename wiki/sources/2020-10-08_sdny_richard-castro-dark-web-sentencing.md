## Source Summary

On 8 October 2020, DOJ announced that Richard Castro, known through several monikers including `Chemsusa`, was sentenced to 210 months in prison for distributing carfentanil and fentanyl over the dark web and laundering the proceeds. DOJ said Castro also forfeited more than USD 4 million in criminal proceeds.
