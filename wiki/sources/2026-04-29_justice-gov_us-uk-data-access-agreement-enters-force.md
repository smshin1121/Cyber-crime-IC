## Source Summary

DOJ announced that the U.S.-UK Data Access Agreement entered into force on 3 October 2022. The agreement, authorized by the CLOUD Act, permits service providers in one country to respond to qualifying lawful orders for electronic data issued by the other country, improving timeliness compared with ordinary MLA channels.
