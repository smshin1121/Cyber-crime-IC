## Source Summary

WAFF Huntsville covers Carver's guilty plea and frames it as part of the Welcome To Video international takedown — the largest darknet CSAM marketplace, operated by South Korean national Jong Woo Son and dismantled through coordinated arrests in 38 countries.

## Relevance to IC

Strong IC coverage: the Welcome To Video takedown is one of the most significant international cybercrime cooperation cases, involving Korean, U.S., U.K., German, Canadian, Australian, and other authorities. Carver is one of the U.S. downstream prosecutions resulting from that international operation. This source repositions the case from "domestic-only" to "downstream of major IC operation."
