## Source Summary

On 12 April 2019, DOJ announced that Brian Gutierrez-Villasenor was sentenced to 120 months in prison for distributing cocaine and methamphetamine for a darknet vendor site called `JetSetLife`. The release described him as having been apprehended as part of a nationwide crackdown on darknet illicit market sites.
