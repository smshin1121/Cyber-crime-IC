# Operation Cyber Guardian: Asia 6-Country CSAM Crackdown

## Source Overview

The Korean National Police Agency press release (via Korea Policy Briefing) and Seoul Shinmun reported on a coordinated 5-week operation involving South Korea, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, and Hong Kong targeting CSAM distribution through information communication networks. Initiated by the Singapore Police Force.

## Key Data Points

- Operation period: February 24 - March 28, 2025
- 544 arrests across 6 countries; Korea: 435, Japan: 111, Singapore: 21, Malaysia: 4
- Offense breakdown (Korea): 258 possession, 74 production, 42 distribution
- 49% of Korean suspects were teenagers (age 10-19)
- AI-generated deepfake CSAM cases prosecuted
