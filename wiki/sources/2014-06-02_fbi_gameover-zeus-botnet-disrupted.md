## Source Summary

The FBI story explains the Gameover Zeus disruption and frames it as a collaborative effort among international law-enforcement, private-sector, and U.S. government partners. It describes the botnet's peer-to-peer structure, the related CryptoLocker infrastructure seizure, and the victim-remediation process using CERTs and private-sector partners.

## Relevance to IC

This source corroborates the operational mechanics and international-cooperation rationale for [[operation-tovar|Operation Tovar]], especially the technical reason sinkholing and global remediation were needed.
