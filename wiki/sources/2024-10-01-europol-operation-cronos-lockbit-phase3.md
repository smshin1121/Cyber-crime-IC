## Summary

Official Europol press release announcing the third phase of **Operation Cronos**, continuing enforcement actions against the LockBit ransomware group. This phase featured new arrests, server seizures, and a significant expansion into financial sanctions against Evil Corp affiliates.

The source provides details on 4 new arrests across France, the UK, and Spain, plus coordinated sanctions by Australia, the UK, and the US. It describes the continuing multi-country investigation involving 12+ countries and 14 agencies, coordinated by [[europol-ec3|Europol]] and [[eurojust|Eurojust]].
