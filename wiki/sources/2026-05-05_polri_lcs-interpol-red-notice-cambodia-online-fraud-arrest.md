## Source Profile

This is an Indonesian-language press release on **Tribrata News**, the official news portal of the **Indonesian National Police** (Kepolisian Negara Republik Indonesia, "Polri"), at `tribratanews.polri.go.id`. It is dated **2026-05-05 09:59 WIB** and is maintained by Polri's Public Relations Division (Divisi Humas Polri). Verbatim mirror copies appear on regional Polri sub-domains (e.g. `tribratanews.jatim.polri.go.id`, `tribratanews.resbatu.jatim.polri.go.id`, `tribratanews.sultra.polri.go.id`), confirming the canonical national release.

The release announces a single coordinated arrest action conducted at **Soekarno-Hatta International Airport** in Jakarta on **Sunday, 2026-05-03**, against an Indonesian national designated by the initials **LCS**. LCS had been on INTERPOL's Red Notice fugitive list and on the wanted-persons list (DPO) of Polri's Cyber Crime Directorate (Dittipidsiber, Bareskrim). He is alleged to have run a cross-border online fraud network operating from **Cambodia** (Kamboja), using a branded fraud platform called "abbishopee".

> [!note] Translation note
> The release uses both "INTERPOL Red Notice" (English term) and "daftar buronan internasional" (Indonesian periphrase) interchangeably for the same instrument. "Bareskrim" is the standard short form for *Badan Reserse Kriminal*. "Dittipidsiber" is the standard short form for *Direktorat Tindak Pidana Siber*, the Cyber Crime Directorate within Bareskrim — distinct from the broader Bareskrim umbrella, and the appropriate sub-unit attribution for cyber-specific operations.
