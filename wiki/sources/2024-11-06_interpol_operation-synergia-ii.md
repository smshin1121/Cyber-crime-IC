## Source Summary

INTERPOL's Operation Synergia II ran from 1 April to 31 August 2024 and specifically targeted phishing, ransomware, and information stealer infrastructure. The operation was a joint effort involving law enforcement agencies from 95 INTERPOL member countries alongside private sector partners Group-IB, Trend Micro, Kaspersky, and Team Cymru. Of approximately 30,000 suspicious IP addresses identified, 76% were taken down. Additionally, 59 servers and 43 electronic devices (laptops, mobile phones, hard disks) were seized, and 41 individuals were arrested with 65 others still under investigation. - **Operation name:** Operation Synergia II - **Date:** 1 April - 31 August 2024
