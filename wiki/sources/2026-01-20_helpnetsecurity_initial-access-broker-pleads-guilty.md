Help Net Security's editor-in-chief reports on the guilty plea by Feras Khalil Ahmad Albashiti in the District of New Jersey, providing investigative detail about how the FBI identified the defendant through monitoring of underground forums and an OPSEC mistake involving an EDR bypass demo. The article ties Albashiti's broker activity to downstream ransomware harm.
