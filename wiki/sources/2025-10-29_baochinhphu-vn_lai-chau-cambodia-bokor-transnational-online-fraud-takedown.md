## Source Summary

Tier-1 Vietnamese-language primary press article published by **Báo Chính phủ (Báo điện tử Chính phủ)** — the electronic newspaper of the Government Office of the Socialist Republic of Việt Nam — on 29 October 2025 at 16:46 (GMT+7) (modified 16:49 the same day), authored by **Linh Anh**. The article reports a Việt Nam–Cambodia joint operation on 28 October 2025 that took down a transnational online-fraud cell operating from the **Bokor Hill area, Bokor Ecological Park complex, Tuek Chhou ward, Bokor city, Kampot province, Kingdom of Cambodia**, and resulted in the arrest in Cambodia of **59 Vietnamese-national suspects** (44 male, 15 female), with **66 arrests in total** in the case to date.

Operational lead is identified as a special working group of **Lai Châu Provincial Police** of Việt Nam, in coordination with the **Representative Office of the Ministry of Public Security of Việt Nam in Cambodia**, the **Criminal Police Department of the MPS** (Cục Cảnh sát Hình sự — Bộ Công an), and **Cambodian functional forces**. Direct command authority is named as **Senior Lieutenant General Nguyễn Văn Long, Deputy Minister and Head of the Police Investigation Agency of the Ministry of Public Security of Việt Nam**.

The named ringleader is **Sùng Thị Mải** (alias "Vi"), born 1999, resident of Lào Cai province (Việt Nam). The article reports the modus operandi as impersonation of shippers, Police, and Military, plus romance-fraud (lừa đảo tình cảm), with **more than 8,000 victims nationwide** in Việt Nam and an estimated appropriated amount of **more than 300 billion VND**.

After Cambodian-side triage, **Cambodian functional forces handed over all 59 suspects** plus seized physical evidence to the Police Investigation Agency — Lai Châu Provincial Police, for Vietnamese-domestic prosecution. The article frames the operation as a demonstration of close Việt Nam–Cambodia police cooperation in the immediate diplomatic context of the **Signing Ceremony of the UN Convention against Cybercrime (Hà Nội Convention)** hosted by Việt Nam in Hà Nội on 25-26 October 2025.

## Relevance to IC

This is a primary-source attestation, in Vietnamese, of three IC-relevant features:

1. **MPS Representative Office in Cambodia as a routinised liaison node.** The article names the **Representative Office of the Ministry of Public Security of Việt Nam in Cambodia** (Cơ quan đại diện Bộ Công an Việt Nam tại Campuchia) as one of the three coordinating Vietnamese-side entities, alongside the Criminal Police Department and the lead provincial police. This evidences a permanent, institutionalised Vietnamese-MPS liaison presence in Cambodia handling criminal-investigation cooperation, distinct from ad-hoc Joint-Investigation-Team setups.
2. **Cambodian-host hand-over of suspects to the requesting state's investigative agency.** The article explicitly states that after on-the-ground triage by Cambodian functional forces, the 59 suspects (all Vietnamese nationals) and seized items were handed over to the Police Investigation Agency of Lai Châu Provincial Police of Việt Nam. This is a host-state hand-over for home-state prosecution pattern, parallel in form (though not in legal basis) to comparable Korea–Cambodia, Korea–Philippines, and China–Cambodia repatriations of co-national scam-compound suspects documented elsewhere in the wiki.
3. **Diplomatic-cooperation framing tied to the Hà Nội Convention.** Báo Chính phủ explicitly frames the takedown as a deliverable of close bilateral police cooperation, framed against the immediate aftermath of the **Signing Ceremony of the UN Convention against Cybercrime in Hà Nội (25-26 October 2025)** — itself hosted by Việt Nam. This is a useful primary attestation of the Hà Nội-signing → bilateral-takedown sequencing.
