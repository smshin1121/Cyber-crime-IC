## Source Summary

The Malabo Convention was adopted by the African Union in June 2014 and entered into force on 8 June 2023 after obtaining the required 15 ratifications. It addresses cybersecurity, cybercrime, and personal data protection across African Union member states. It is considered a regional alternative and complement to the Budapest Convention.
