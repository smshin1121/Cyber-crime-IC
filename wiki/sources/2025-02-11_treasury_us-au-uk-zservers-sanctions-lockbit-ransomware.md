## Summary

OFAC press release SB-0018 (2025-02-11) announces a coordinated trilateral cyber-sanctions action by the United States, Australia, and the United Kingdom against Zservers, a Russia-based bulletproof hosting provider that supplied infrastructure to LockBit ransomware affiliates. OFAC designated Zservers and two Russian administrators (Mishin and Bolshakov); the UK FCDO supplementally designated XHOST Internet Solutions LP (UK front) and four further employees the same day. The release establishes the LockBit nexus through three concrete incidents (2022 Canadian LE search, 2022 IP purchase, 2023 affiliate lease) and grounds the designations in E.O. 13694 as further amended by E.O. 14144. The action is highly likely the opening move in a deliberate 2025 trilateral track targeting Russian BPH infrastructure, with later entries (Aeza Group designations in July 2025; Media Land + Hypercore/SB-0319 in November 2025) following the same trilateral template.
