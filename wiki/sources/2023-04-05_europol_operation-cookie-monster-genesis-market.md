## Source Summary

Europol's public release reported that a 17-country operation took down Genesis Market on 4 April 2023. It records 119 arrests, 208 property searches, 97 knock-and-talk measures, FBI and Dutch National Police leadership, and Europol command-post coordination at The Hague.

## Relevance to IC

This source supplies the main operational metrics and coordination model for [[operation-cookie-monster|Operation Cookie Monster]]. It establishes Europol EC3's investigative support since 2019 and confirms the action-day command-post model used for simultaneous enforcement across jurisdictions.
