## Source Summary

Joint KNPA + Chinese MPS announcement of the signing on 2025-11-01 of the bilateral 「보이스피싱·온라인 사기 범죄 대응 공조 양해각서」 (MOU on Cooperation in Response to Voice Phishing and Online Fraud Crime). Acting KNPA Commissioner Yoo Jae-sung 유재성 and Chinese Ambassador to Korea Dai Bing 다이 빙 signed the MOU in Korea during the APEC Korea 2025 summit, with both heads of state observing. The MOU commits both police authorities to share information on Southeast Asian scam-crime bases, conduct joint operations and investigations, support victim rescue / protection / repatriation, and trace / freeze scam-crime proceeds. The agreement formally establishes a 「한·중 공동대응 협의체」 (Korea-China Joint Response Council) with designated KNPA and Chinese MPS departments operating the standing cooperation network.

## Relevance to IC

This is the principal tier-1 Korean-language source for the 2025 Korea-China bilateral anti-scam-crime MOU and the establishment of the 「한·중 공동대응 협의체」 standing cooperation council. It supplies (a) the named-officials and signing-context detail (heads-of-state-observed APEC venue), (b) the explicit scope language including victim rescue, evidence sharing, and asset tracing, (c) the establishment of a standing bilateral council with designated cooperation departments inside both police agencies, and (d) the Cambodia / Southeast Asia operational frame that aligns with the bilateral Korea-Cambodia Joint Task Force established 9 days later (2025-11-10). This is the third wiki public-record Korea-led bilateral cooperation instrument for cybercrime IC, complementing the [[korea-cambodia-joint-task-force-mou-2025|2025-11-10 Korea-Cambodia MOU]] and the [[korea-philippines-police-mou-2026-revision|2026-03-03 Korea-Philippines MOU revision]].
