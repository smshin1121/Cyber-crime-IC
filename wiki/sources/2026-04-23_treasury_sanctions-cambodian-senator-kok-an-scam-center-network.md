## Source Summary

OFAC press release **SB-0469** dated **23 April 2026** designating **Cambodian Senator Kok An plus 28 individuals/entities (29 total)** under E.O. 13694 as amended. The action targets a Cambodia-based, casino-and-scam-compound network that uses **digital asset investment fraud + romance-friendship lures + human-trafficking forced labor** to defraud Americans, with a documented underlying loss of **USD 10 billion in 2024** to Southeast Asia-based scam operations (66% YoY increase).

The action is **coordinated with the newly-named Scam Center Strike Force** (joint initiative of USAO-DC, DOJ Criminal Division, FBI, USSS) focused on **Burma, Cambodia, and Laos**, and is undertaken in furtherance of **E.O. 14390 of 6 March 2026 ('Combating Cybercrime, Fraud, and Predatory Schemes Against American Citizens')**. Same-day Strike Force actions include charges against 2 Burma-Cambodia scam-compound operators, seizure of a social media messaging app used to recruit trafficking victims, and **seizure of 503 fraudulent web domains** used for cryptocurrency investment fraud.

The release also chains to prior OFAC actions:
- **12 Nov 2025**: Burmese armed group + 7 others (Tai Chang scam compound, Karen State, Burma)
- **14 Oct 2025**: [[us-uk-prince-group-tco-huione-group-sanctions-2025|Prince Group TCO]] + 146 associates (US-UK joint)
- **8 Sep 2025**: 12 companies + 7 individuals (Cambodia/Burma)
- **12 Sep 2024**: Ly Yong Phat, L.Y.P. Group + 4 hotels

Same-day **State Department rewards**: USD 10M for info on Tai Chang scam-compound proceeds; USD 4M for arrest of fugitive Daren Li (convicted in C.D. Cal., removed ankle monitor, fled US).

The release establishes:

1. The **Scam Center Strike Force** as the formal US multi-agency cooperation framework for SE-Asia scam-compound enforcement (USAO-DC + DOJ-Crim + FBI + USSS), focused on Burma + Cambodia + Laos.
2. **E.O. 14390 (6 March 2026)** as the new presidential authority specifically directed at foreign-backed cybercrime / cyber-enabled fraud / extortion targeting Americans.
3. **Cambodian-political-protection layer** as a structural feature of SE-Asia scam-compound enforcement — Kok An is a *sitting senator*, not just a businessman.
4. **503 fraudulent crypto-investment web domains seized** as a discrete operational metric.
5. **Casino-as-laundering-vehicle pattern** explicitly cited: "Nearly all major scam compounds in Cambodia are connected to casinos, which serve to launder the proceeds of scams."
