## Source Summary

Official press release issued by the **Polícia Federal (PF)** of Brazil announcing **Operação Dark Bet** on **2025-12-16**. The operation targets a Brazilian organization that lured Brazilian nationals through false online-betting employment offers, trafficked them to Nigeria, and forced them under armed surveillance to operate cybercrime/scam schemes.

A mirror announcement on the Ministério da Justiça e Segurança Pública (MJSP) site (2025-12-17) adds an attributed quotation from Marina Bernardes, MJSP coordinator for human trafficking enforcement, characterizing trafficking via false BET-sector job offers as "a concrete and extremely serious reality."
