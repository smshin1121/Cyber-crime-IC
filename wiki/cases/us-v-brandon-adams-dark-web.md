## Summary

United States v. Brandon Adams is a darknet counterfeit-pill case involving millions of fake Xanax tablets sold under the name `BenzoBoys`.

## Facts

According to DOJ, Adams sold millions of counterfeit Xanax pills on the darknet under the name `BenzoBoys`. The case focuses less on hidden-service administration and more on counterfeit-pill manufacturing, branding, and online distribution at industrial scale.

### Evidence Context

No foreign procedural step is identified in the public source. The case's relevance instead lies in how darknet distribution channels were used to move counterfeit pharmaceuticals through domestic commerce while obscuring the origin of the pills.
