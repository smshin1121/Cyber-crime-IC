Dark Web Drug Trafficker Sentenced to 160 Months in Federal Prison is documented in the linked source material. On 11 January 2022, DOJ announced that Jose Rodolfo Barraza Flores was sentenced to 160 months in prison for selling methamphetamine, cocaine, fentanyl-laced pills, and other narcotics on dark web markets such as White House Market, Empire Market, and Yellowbrick Road.
