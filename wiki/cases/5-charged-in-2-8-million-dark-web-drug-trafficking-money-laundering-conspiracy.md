- US DOJ USAO, 2020-01-15: Attorney's Office, Southern District of Ohio CINCINNATI – A federal grand jury has indicted five individuals alleged to be members of one of the most prolific online drug trafficking organizations in the United States.
- US DOJ USAO, 2020-01-15: According to the 21-count indictment, since 2013, co-conspirators used several Dark Web marketplace accounts and encrypted messaging apps in order to sell illegal drugs online.
- US DOJ USAO, 2020-01-15: Each of the five defendants was arrested in December and has appeared in federal court in Cincinnati.
- US DOJ USAO, 2020-01-15: The investigation was coordinated out of the Cincinnati Field Office of the FBI and is part of an Organized Crime Drug Enforcement Task Force (OCDETF) joint investigation conducted by the Southern Ohio Digitized Organized Crime Group.
