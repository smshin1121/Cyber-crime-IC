## Summary

United States v. Fedir Hladyr is the principal U.S. criminal case publicly tied to the FIN7 prosecution track. DOJ announced in April 2021 that Hladyr, described as a systems administrator for FIN7, was sentenced to 10 years' imprisonment.

## Disposition

- Sentence: 10 years' imprisonment
- Offense posture: Conspiracy and fraud-related counts tied to FIN7 intrusions and payment-card theft
