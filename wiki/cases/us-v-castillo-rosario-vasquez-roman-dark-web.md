## Summary

United States v. Castillo-Rosario et al. is a multi-defendant darknet case involving counterfeit Adderall pills containing methamphetamine.

## Facts

DOJ said the defendants manufactured and shipped counterfeit Adderall through darknet aliases including "MrJohnson," "NuveoDeluxe," and "AllStateRx." The case stands out because it is organized around a small production-and-distribution group rather than a lone vendor, making it a good example of team-based darknet supply.
