## Summary

United States v. Adam Miles is a dark web vendor case involving narcotics trafficking, money laundering, and forfeiture of weapons and cash.

## Facts

DOJ said Miles used the moniker "NoStress" to traffic narcotics through dark web channels and then launder the proceeds. The sentencing summary also highlights the seizure and forfeiture of firearms, ammunition, and cash, showing how marketplace drug sales often intersect with broader public-safety concerns.
