## Summary

In 2023, Europol and Eurojust coordinated an international law enforcement operation that took down botnet infrastructure used for malware distribution, data theft, and DDoS attacks. Law enforcement agencies from Germany, the Netherlands, the United States, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, France, Romania, and Ukraine cooperated to neutralize the botnet and disrupt the associated cybercrime operations.

## Participating Parties

**Coordinating Bodies:**
- Europol
- Eurojust

**Participating Countries (8):**
Germany, Netherlands, United States, United Kingdom, Switzerland, France, Romania, Ukraine

## Results

- Botnet infrastructure neutralized through international cooperation
- Malware distribution and data theft operations disrupted
