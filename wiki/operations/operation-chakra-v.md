## Summary

Operation Chakra-V was a CBI-led action against two illegal tech-support scam call centers targeting Japanese citizens. CBI coordinated with Japan's National Police Agency and Microsoft, searched **19 locations** across Delhi, Haryana, and Uttar Pradesh, and arrested **six** alleged operatives.

## International Cooperation Details

The source states that CBI worked closely with Japan's National Police Agency and Microsoft Corporation. That public-private and cross-border cooperation helped identify the perpetrators and trace the operating structure of the syndicate before the India-based searches and arrests.
