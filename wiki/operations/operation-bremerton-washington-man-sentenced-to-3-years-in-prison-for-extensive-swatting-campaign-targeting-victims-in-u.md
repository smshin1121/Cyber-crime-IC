## Summary

This page captures the defendant-specific enforcement sequence against Ashton Connor Garcia for a swatting campaign that threatened victims in the United States and Canada. The public record shows a progression from indictment in March 2023, to a guilty plea in January 2024, to sentencing in June 2024.

## Background

The case involved repeated false emergency reports, or swatting calls, designed to trigger armed police responses against victims. DOJ and AP reporting describe at least 20 incidents affecting people in the United States and Canada. Because the case included cross-border victims but a U.S. federal prosecution, it fits the repository's follow-on operation model: a discrete enforcement action anchored to one case with international impact.
