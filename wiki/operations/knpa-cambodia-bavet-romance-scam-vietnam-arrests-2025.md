## Summary

On **2025-10-28**, the [[knpa|Korean National Police Agency]] (경찰청), in joint operation with **Vietnamese Public Security** (베트남 공안청 / *Bộ Công an Việt Nam*), simultaneously arrested **five Korean-national cadre-level members** of a Cambodia-Bavet-based web **romance-scam** (로맨스스캠) organisation across three Vietnamese cities — **Da Nang, Ho Chi Minh City, and Khanh Hoa Province**. The KNPA announced the arrests on **2025-11-04** via the official Korea.kr 정책브리핑 channel (newsId 156724852).

The criminal organisation operated from **Bavet, Cambodia** (a border town adjacent to Vietnam), targeting Korean victims through dating-app and SNS-based romance lures and monetising through cryptocurrency-investment manipulation. **192 Korean victims** suffered cumulative losses of approximately **₩4.6 billion (~USD 3.5 million)**.

KNPA International Cooperation Officer **Lee Jae-young (이재영 경찰청 국제협력관)** is quoted in the primary source explicitly attributing the outcome to Korea–Vietnam bilateral cooperation (full quote in the [Cooperation Mechanisms Used](#cooperation-mechanisms-used) section below).

## Background

The romance-scam corridor in Cambodia–Vietnam is a recognised pattern in 2024-2025 Korean LE reporting: organisations recruit Korean-language-capable cadre, set up operating compounds in Cambodia (notably Bavet, Sihanoukville, Poipet, and the Taizi 태자 단지 compound), and monetise via app/SNS-mediated romance lures targeting Korean victims. When operational pressure increases on the Cambodian operating base, cadre frequently transit into Vietnam (geographic adjacency, large Korean expatriate community in Da Nang and Ho Chi Minh City, weaker visibility into immigration databases for Korean LE).

This operation is one of the discrete Vietnam-side arrest events within the broader 2025-Q4 / 2026-Q1 / 2026-Q2 KNPA campaign architecture against Southeast Asian scam compounds. It precedes the embedded [[knpa-cambodia-korea-task-force-redcorner-arrests-2026-02|Korea-dedicated Squad operations inside Cambodia (2025-12 to 2026-02)]] and the [[korea-cambodia-philippines-73-extradition-2026|73-person mass extradition from Cambodia and the Philippines (2026-04-27)]], and is part of the same KNPA strategic response to scam-compound transnationalisation that organises the [[knpa-breaking-chains-2nd-international-operation-meeting-2026|22-country/5-IO multilateral coordination meeting (2026-02-05)]].

## Results and Impact

- **Arrests**: 5 Korean-national cadre-level members of the Cambodia-Bavet romance-scam organisation, in three Vietnamese cities, on a single day.
- **Victims identified**: 192 (Korean nationals).
- **Financial damage**: ~₩4.6 billion (~USD 3.5 million).
- **At-large**: Organisation leader (총책) and additional cadre; INTERPOL Red Notice pursuit ongoing per primary source.
- **Separate parallel arrest**: One Korean-national leader-class subject in Bac Ninh Province (별건; reported in same release window).

## Challenges and Friction Points

- **Origin-state vs. destination-state enforcement**: Cambodia is the criminal organisation's operating base, but the arrest event was achieved through the destination-state (Vietnam) channel rather than the origin-state (Cambodia) channel. Per LESSONS L24, Cambodia is recorded as prose context only and excluded from `participating_countries`, since the primary source identifies no Cambodian LE counterpart in this discrete operation.
- **Balloon-effect / 풍선효과**: Cadre relocation from Cambodia into Vietnam is itself the displacement pattern that necessitated bilateral Korea–Vietnam cooperation; single-corridor (Korea–Cambodia) cooperation cannot foreclose the displacement vector.
- **Leadership at-large**: The organisation leader (총책) was not arrested in this event, and field-cadre arrests do not by themselves dismantle the operating compound.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Layered Southeast Asia cooperation architecture**: Korea simultaneously operates (a) embedded bilateral co-location with Cambodia ([[knpa-cambodia-korea-task-force-redcorner-arrests-2026-02|Korea-dedicated Squad inside Cambodia]] from 2025-11) and (b) attaché-mediated bilateral cooperation with Vietnam (this operation). The two channels are complementary against the mobile-cadre pattern, not substitutes.
2. **Embassy police attaché as operational layer**: The Korean police attaché stations (경찰주재관) in Da Nang and Ho Chi Minh City function as a real working operational layer for time-sensitive joint arrests with Vietnamese provincial public security, distinct from formal MLA pipelines and faster on operational timelines.
3. **Romance-scam + cryptocurrency-investment**: The combined romance-scam (로맨스스캠) plus cryptocurrency-investment-manipulation modus operandi is now a recurring KNPA-tracked pattern in the Cambodia–Vietnam corridor, alongside voice-phishing and online-investment fraud. See [[online-fraud-ic]].
4. **Per LESSONS L24 handling of origin-states**: The page records Korea + Vietnam as the participating jurisdictions and reserves Cambodia for prose attribution as the operating base. This avoids the L24 anti-pattern of conflating origin/destination/adversary states with cooperating LE jurisdictions.
