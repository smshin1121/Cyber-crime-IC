# Operation Contender 3.0

## Summary

Operation Contender 3.0 was an INTERPOL-coordinated pan-African cyber-enabled fraud operation focused on romance scams and sextortion. The action ran from 28 July to 11 August 2025 and involved 14 African countries.

The operation targeted networks exploiting social media and dating platforms, using fake profiles, forged identities, stolen images, secretly recorded intimate material, and money-transfer channels to defraud or extort victims.

## Cooperation Model

- **Coordination:** INTERPOL coordinated the operation under the African Joint Operation against Cybercrime project.
- **Private-sector support:** Group-IB and Trend Micro provided intelligence and operational support.
- **National action examples:** Ghana, Senegal, Cote d'Ivoire, and Angola were publicly identified as major action locations.
