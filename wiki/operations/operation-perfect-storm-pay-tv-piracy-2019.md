## Summary

Operation Perfect Storm was an Italian-led operation against a trans-European criminal network selling unauthorized pay-TV and streaming access. Eurojust's 2021 publication supplies the operation name and connects the 2019 action day with continued investigations into 2020.

The 2019 official record describes illegal rebroadcasting of pay-per-view products and services, special servers used to generate illegal IPTV signals, more than 200 servers taken offline, and 22 suspects identified. The 2020 Eurojust record describes a further action in Italy and ten other countries that took down more than 5,550 servers used for television broadcasts, live sports, and films.

## Results and Impact

- The 2019 action took more than 200 servers offline and blocked more than 150 PayPal accounts.
- The 2020 continuation took down more than 5,550 servers.
- Authorities arrested one alleged leader in the 2020 action and seized computer equipment, payment instruments, and luxury assets.
- The operation is retained as an illegal IPTV/pay-TV streaming record because the official sources describe unauthorized audiovisual streaming infrastructure rather than only civil copyright enforcement.
